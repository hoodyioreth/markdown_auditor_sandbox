Diocese of Falsridge
====================

_“All that passeth west is weighed. All that returneth is known.”_

### Overview

The Diocese of Falsridge standeth as the western gate of the Archclericy of Veluna—a land where road, law, and coin bind together in iron certainty. From the outer reaches of Veluna City unto the narrow throat of the Fals Gap, this diocese commandeth all overland passage into the realm from the west.

 

Unlike the river-fed dioceses of the east, Falsridge draweth its strength not from water, but from stone and distance. Here, movement is not free—it is directed, observed, and taxed. Every caravan, every wagon, every traveler that would enter Veluna by land must pass beneath its scrutiny.

 

Thus, Falsridge is not merely a border. It is Veluna’s western lock upon the world.

### Geography & Structure

The diocese is divided both by nature and by purpose:

*   **The Eastern Fields** — Vast agricultural lands feeding Veluna City, broken only by the Great Western Road
*   **The Western Corridor** — A hardened line of road, fortification, and toll control stretching toward the Fals Gap

The Great Western Road is the spine of the diocese, running from Mitrik through Falsridge and onward into Veluna’s heart. Along its length are placed toll gates, patrol stations, caravanserais, and fortified checkpoints—each positioned to ensure that no movement escapeth notice.

 

At the far western edge, the land tightens into the Fals Gap—a natural choke point where hills and rising stone constrain the road into a narrow passage. Here, Veluna’s authority is absolute.

 

### Trade & Commerce

If Devarnish is the artery of flowing trade, then Falsridge is the hand that measureth and collecteth it.

 

The diocese derives immense wealth from its control of overland commerce. Caravans from Bissel, Ket, and lands beyond must pass through its gates, and all are subject to inspection, levy, and record.

*   Tariffs on all goods entering and leaving Veluna
*   Mandatory caravan registration and inspection
*   Storage, redistribution, and bonded warehousing
*   Licensing of merchants and transport factors

Unlike the fluid and decentralized river trade of the Velverdyva, Falsridge’s economy is rigid, structured, and highly controlled. This hath made it one of the wealthiest dioceses in Veluna—and one of the most tightly governed.

 

### Government & Power Structure

Falsridge is governed through the Velunan dual authority, yet here that balance is strained by wealth and necessity.

*   **The Bishop of Falsridge** — Spiritual authority, representing the will of Rao and the Celestial Circle
*   **The Baron of Falsridge** — Secular ruler, overseeing taxation, military presence, and road control

Yet neither ruleth alone in truth.

 

The immense wealth of the road hath elevated merchant guilds and trade factors into powers that rival both bishop and baron. Decisions within Falsridge are rarely made without consideration of coin—and those who control its flow.

 

The Celestial Circle maintaineth particular interest in Falsridge, for instability here would choke Veluna’s western lifeline.

### Major Settlements & Sites

##### Falsridge (City)

The seat of the diocese and master of the western road. Its gates are never still, its counting houses never silent. Here caravans are broken, taxed, and reorganized before continuing east.

##### Fals Keep

A fortified stronghold guarding the western approach. It serves as both military bastion and administrative checkpoint, where goods are recorded and levies enforced.

##### Triplegate

A major toll and inspection point where roads divide and traffic is filtered. Known for delays, disputes, and the ever-present threat of inspection.

##### The Fals Gap

The defining geographic feature of the diocese—a narrow pass through which all western traffic must flow. Control of the Gap is control of the road itself.

##### Krag Spire

A watchtower overlooking the approaches, used for signaling, surveillance, and early warning of threats from the west.

### Political Tensions

##### The Weight of Coin

The immense wealth generated by tariffs hath created friction between the bishop, the baron, and merchant guilds. Each seeketh greater control over the revenues of the road.

##### Smuggling & Evasion

Despite strict control, illicit routes and bribery persist. Entire networks exist to bypass tolls, undermining authority and enriching shadow powers.

##### The Western Uncertainty

Beyond the Gap, the world groweth less predictable. Rumors of instability, shifting alliances, and distant threats place constant pressure upon Falsridge to remain vigilant.

##### Celestial Oversight

The Celestial Circle quietly monitors Falsridge, wary that its wealth might unbalance the careful harmony of Veluna’s dioceses.

### Role Within Veluna

Falsridge is the western counterweight to Veluna’s river dominion.

*   Where the Velverdyva bringeth speed and breadth, Falsridge bringeth control and certainty.
    
*   Where Devarnish contendeth with politics and shifting influence, Falsridge dealeth in fixed law and measured coin.
    
*   Together, they form the twin pillars of Veluna’s strength—river and road, movement and measure.
    

 

### DM Notes & Adventure Hooks

*   Caravan escort missions through the Fals Gap
*   Smuggling rings attempting to bypass toll systems
*   Political intrigue between bishop, baron, and guildmasters
*   Celestial Circle agents investigating corruption
*   Western threats attempting to breach Veluna’s border

 

In Falsridge, none pass unseen—and none pass without consequence.

##### Demographics

Population: Approx. 95,000

*   Oeridians — Majority population (farmers, officials, soldiers)
*   Suloise — Merchant and minor noble presence
*   Flan — Rural laborers and long-established communities
*   Dwarves — Engineers, road-builders, and caravan specialists
*   Gnomes — Trade logistics, accounting, and mechanical craft
*   Halflings — Caravan support and agricultural roles

Population density is highest along the Great Western Road, with vast stretches of farmland to the east and fortified infrastructure to the west.

##### SIDEBARCONTENTBOTTOM

**WHY THE DIOCESE OF FALSRIDGE MATTERS FOR YOUR CAMPAIGN**

 

Falsridge is one of the most useful western dioceses in Veluna because it joins together several strong campaign functions at once:

*   **Road Play:** caravans, escorts, tolls, inspections, and fortified choke points
*   **Trade Pressure:** tariff income, smuggling, guild interference, and warehousing disputes
*   **Political Conflict:** bishop, baron, and merchant interests all struggle to shape the same revenue stream
*   **Strategic Geography:** the Fals Gap turns terrain itself into law and leverage
*   **Velunan Contrast:** it shows the overland, controlled face of Veluna in contrast to the river-borne, more fluid trade of Devarnish

Use Falsridge when thou wantest Veluna to feel measured, fortified, and economically vigilant. This is the diocese where the road itself becomes a political instrument, and where every wagon that creaketh westward doth so beneath the eye of church, lord, and ledger alike.
