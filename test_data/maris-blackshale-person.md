Maris Blackshale
================

Maris is one of Romag’s most trusted field agents in southern Verbobonc. A hardened warrior with a blunt, utilitarian worldview, Maris oversees the Earth Temple’s raider‑training operations in a hidden vale between the The Sepulchre - Monastery & Tomb of Heironeous and Feathergale Spire. His current mission—outlined in _VER1‑04 How Greenway Was My Valley_—is to destabilize relations between the Kron Hills gnomes and the Viscounty of Verbobonc through impersonation, false‑flag raids, and engineered massacres.

Maris is not a zealot. He is a professional. He fights because he is good at it, follows orders because it keeps coin in his pocket, and spends that coin as fast as he earns it. His loyalty is transactional, but his reliability is absolute.

*   **Motivation:** Maris is driven by three simple principles:
*   **Warrior First:** He views the world through the lens of combat. Strength, discipline, and decisive action are the only currencies he respects.
*   **Knowledge Should Be Universal:** Maris believes secrets are a weakness. He freely shares tactics, techniques, and information with subordinates—so long as it serves the mission.

**No Moral Complications**

He does what he is told. He does not question orders, hesitate at cruelty, or concern himself with consequences. If the Earth Temple wants a village burned, he burns it. If they want gnomes framed for murder, he trains the killers.

**Fleeting Indulgence**

Maris spends his pay immediately—ale, gear, gambling, or fleeting comforts. He has no long‑term plans and no desire for them.

##### **Personality**

*   **Direct, blunt, and unflinching**
*   **Honest in his own way**—he says exactly what he means
*   **Frank with superiors, straightforward with peers, commanding with subordinates**
*   **Emotionally flat** but not cruel for cruelty’s sake—cruelty is simply a tool

##### **Maris →** Caidax Nightshade

**Role:** Earth Temple Agent (Important)  

Maris and Caidax were recruited together by Romag as field agents. They train raiders, lead strikes, and coordinate operations across southern Verbobonc and the Kron Hills. Their partnership is professional, efficient, and free of rivalry.

##### **Maris →** [Romag](romag-person.md)

**Role:** Field Agent (Vital)  
Romag personally recruited Maris for his reliability and martial discipline. Maris respects Romag more than anyone else in the cult and follows his orders without hesitation.

##### **Maris →** [Lakash Quallad](lakash-quallad-person.md)

**Role:** Verbobonc Greater Temple Agent (Important)  
Lakash recruits mercenaries in Verbobonc and funnels them to Maris’s training camp. Their relationship is purely transactional.

##### **Maris →** [Gimble Jillink](gimble-jillink-person.md)

**Role:** Raider Leader for the Earth Temple (Important)  
Gimble was sent to assist Maris and Caidax with southern Verbobonc raids. Both men lie to each other constantly, each assuming the other is hiding something.

##### **Maris →** [Nypnic](nypnic-person.md)

**Role:** Earth Temple Leader (Important)  
Nypnic admires Maris as a commander. Maris barely notices Nypnic except as a useful tool.

#### **Current Operations**

*   **Training Camp Commander:** Oversees a mixed force of human mercenaries from Verbobonc and half‑orcs from Nulb.
*   **False‑Flag Specialist:** Directs raids meant to appear as gnomish aggression.
*   **Earth–Air Conflict Mediator:** Occasionally clashes with Feathergale Knights who interfere with Earth Temple operations.
*   **Southern Verbobonc Disruptor:** Works with Caidax Nightshade to destabilize the region and supply Romag with prisoners, loot, and chaos.

* * *

##### **Adventure Hooks**

**1\. The False Gnome Massacre**

Maris prepares to unleash a raid disguised as gnomes from Greenway Valley. PCs may intercept scouts, uncover forged gnomish gear, or confront Maris directly.

**2\. The Raider Training Camp**

A hidden valley between the The Sepulchre - Monastery & Tomb of Heironeous and Feathergale Spire houses Maris’s mixed mercenary force. PCs might infiltrate, sabotage, or negotiate with defectors.

**3\. Earth vs. Air Tensions**

Feathergale Knights sometimes interfere with Maris’s operations. PCs could exploit this rivalry to turn the cults against each other.

**4\. The Jillink Connection**

Gimble Jillink’s involvement ties Maris to a larger criminal network. PCs may uncover smuggling routes, blackmail material, or political corruption.

##### **GM Notes**

*   Maris is **not** a mastermind—he is a soldier executing someone else’s plan.
*   He fights with brutal efficiency and retreats only when ordered.
*   He is honest in conversation but never volunteers information.
*   He respects strength; intimidation works better than persuasion.
*   If captured, he will reveal everything except Romag’s location.
