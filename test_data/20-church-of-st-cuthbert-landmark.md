20\. Church of St. Cuthbert
===========================

The Church of Saint Cuthbert of Hommlet stands as a magnificent testament to faith and resilience, overlooking the village from its perch atop a gentle hill. Built to honor the aid rendered by the Archcleric of Veluna in the war against the Temple of Elemental Evil, this newly constructed edifice is both a spiritual haven and a fortress of hope for the people of Hommlet.

### Exterior Description

Perched on the gentle rise of a hill, the Church of St. Cuthbert stands as a beacon of faith and resilience, its two-story stone edifice exuding an aura of strength and tranquility. The exterior walls are crafted from expertly hewn stone blocks, their surfaces weathered yet steadfast, whispering tales of endurance against the ravages of time and nature. The main entrance, flanked by solid stone pillars, opens to a beautifully carved wooden door adorned with symbols of [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md) — the cudgel and starburst. This gateway invites the devout and the curious alike into its hallowed halls. Above the entrance, the church’s facade is crowned with a modest yet elegant rose window, its stained glass depicting scenes of wisdom and justice, casting colorful reflections on the cobblestone path leading to the door.   The Church of Saint Cuthbert is a grand two-story stone structure, exuding an aura of strength and tranquility. ##### Structural Layout

*   **Cruciform Design:** The church's architecture features a central nave culminating in a high, vaulted ceiling, inspiring awe and reverence among visitors.
*   **Transepts and Chapels:** Extending outward, the transepts provide balance and house chapels dedicated to the saint's teachings.
*   **Roof and Windows:** The slate-tiled roof channels rainwater into stone gutters, and small arched windows allow streams of natural light to illuminate the interior.
*   **Circular Apse:** The rear apse encloses the sacred altar and sanctuary, where the faithful gather for prayer and reflection.

##### The Round Bell Tower

The church's iconic round bell tower, rising majestically above the rear of the building, serves as a symbol of vigilance and hope. Its dome-shaped roof echoes the architectural grandeur of the church and houses the bells that call the faithful to worship.

##### Interior Description

Inside, the church is a sanctuary of peace and devotion. The high-vaulted ceilings and cruciform layout create an expansive, airy space where the faithful gather for worship. Sunlight filters through the stained-glass windows, casting vibrant hues across the polished stone floor and bathing the interior in a serene glow. The central nave leads to the sacred altar, where the priests conduct ceremonies and offer guidance to the congregation. To the sides, the transepts house chapels dedicated to the teachings of Saint Cuthbert, where visitors can seek solace and reflection. The interior decor is simple yet elegant, reflecting the church's commitment to humility and devotion.

### Lore and History

Built by Viscount Wilfrick of Verbobonc to honor the alliance with the Archcleric of Veluna, the church has become a symbol of hope and unity for the people of Hommlet. Its construction marked a turning point in the battle against the Temple of Elemental Evil, serving as a reminder of the village's resilience and determination. The church's strategic location on the hill also makes it a watchful guardian over Hommlet, offering protection and spiritual guidance to its inhabitants.

##### Influence of the Church

Under the leadership of [Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md), the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) in Verbobonc has grown into the most influential religious institution in the region. The Hommlet church is a critical outpost in the church's network, providing spiritual guidance and protection against evil forces.

##### Guardian of Faith

The church is part of a broader guardianship network that includes abbeys strategically positioned across Verbobonc. These guardianships serve as bastions against heresy and raiding evils, with the church in Hommlet acting as both a place of worship and a defensive stronghold.

### Leadership and Clergy

#### Canon Terjon

*   **Role and Background:** Canon Terjon, a member of the Order of the Chapeaux, has been assigned by Bishop Haufren to oversee the church in Hommlet. Previously serving as an adventurer, Terjon's appointment reflects his deep commitment to the church's mission and its role in safeguarding the village.
*   **Personality and Motivation:** Though not particularly friendly, Terjon is driven by a sense of duty to protect the faithful and uphold the teachings of St. Cuthbert. He secretly yearns for adventure but remains bound by his obligations to the church.
*   **Assignment by Bishop Haufren:** Terjon's assignment to Hommlet is part of Bishop Haufren's strategy to strengthen the church's presence in the region and ensure the safety of its followers. The bishop's directive emphasizes vigilance against the growing threat of banditry and evil influences.

> _"The rise in banditry is a direct threat to our community's safety. We must remain vigilant and united in our efforts to combat this menace."_

#### Acolyte Calmert

Acolyte [Calmert](calmert-person.md) is a devoted member of the Order of the Cudgel and serves as a key figure in the Church of Saint Cuthbert in Hommlet. Tasked with maintaining the spiritual welfare of the village's inhabitants, he plays an instrumental role in guiding the flock and ensuring adherence to the church's teachings. Known for his fervor in collecting donations, Calmert's presence is both respected and occasionally scrutinized by the villagers.

> _"Every coin given in faith strengthens our shield against the darkness."_

##### Secret Rumor

**Rumor of Embezzlement:** There are whispers among the villagers that Calmert may be diverting some of the church's funds for personal gain. However, these claims are unfounded. In truth, Calmert remains committed to the church's mission and directs the majority of donations toward the construction of the fortress, ensuring the safety and prosperity of Hommlet.

##### Role and Responsibilities

*   **Tending to the Flock:** As the lesser cleric of the Church of Saint Cuthbert, Acolyte Calmert is responsible for addressing the needs of the congregation. He provides spiritual guidance, conducts religious ceremonies, and offers counseling to those seeking solace and understanding.
*   **Promoting the Teachings:** Calmert is committed to upholding the teachings of Saint Cuthbert and encourages the villagers to live by the principles of justice, fortitude, and wisdom. His sermons emphasize the importance of these values in overcoming the challenges faced by the community.
*   **Collecting Contributions:** Calmert is renowned for his zeal in obtaining contributions from both long-time residents and newcomers to Hommlet. These donations are crucial for the church's operations and its charitable endeavors within the village.
*   **Supporting the Fortress Construction:** Calmert is eager to contribute to the construction of the nearby fortress, seeing it as a vital step in securing Hommlet against the threats of banditry and evil. Most of the funds he collects are intended to support this project, although rumors occasionally suggest otherwise.

### Current Events

The Church of Saint Cuthbert is actively engaged in addressing the bandit problems plaguing the region. With the construction of the nearby fortress, the church has become a center of coordination and support for the militia's efforts to secure the village and its trade routes.

#### Conclusion

The Church of Saint Cuthbert in Hommlet stands as a testament to the enduring power of faith and community. Under the leadership of Canon Terjon and Acolyte Calmert, it continues to provide spiritual guidance, protection, and hope to the villagers in their ongoing struggle against the forces of darkness.

A passionate and zealous acolyte, [Calmert](calmert-person.md) is a member of the Order of the Cudgel. Known for his fervent collection of donations, he aims to contribute to the construction of the nearby fortress. Although rumors circulate about his handling of funds, Calmert is honest and dedicated to the church's mission.

*   **Zealous and Passionate:** Calmert approaches his duties with fervor and dedication. His passionate nature drives him to ensure the church remains a strong and guiding force within the community.
*   **Distant but Caring:** While Calmert can seem somewhat aloof at times, his dedication to the village is genuine. He takes his responsibilities seriously and is committed to the spiritual growth of the congregation.
*   **Unwaveringly Honest:** Despite rumors to the contrary, Calmert is an honest and honorable man. His intentions are pure, and his actions are guided by a deep sense of duty to both the church and the people of Hommlet.

##### Melda, Serving Woman

Melda is a devoted and kind-hearted servant who manages the day-to-day operations of the church. Her gentle demeanor and attention to detail ensure that the church remains a welcoming sanctuary for all who visit.

##### Jorge, Handyman

Jorge is a hardworking and resourceful handyman responsible for the upkeep of the church grounds. His skills and dedication ensure that the church remains in pristine condition.

*   Foolishness can be beaten.
