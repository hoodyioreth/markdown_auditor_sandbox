Hummings End
============

Humming's End, last village in the viscounty before the Gnarley Forest. This woodsman, hunter and logger's community is under the rule of Viscount’s Sheriff Baron Corbin Deleveu, Warden of the South, who resides in Ostverk.

#### Residence

The mayor, Albion son of Utred, and his (charming) wife Jelwyna. The local Druid, Culinn, the villagers being followers of the Old Faith.   Ranger Knight Wyn has a cottage here from which he trains his junior rangers in the ways of the forest.

#### Druids of the Gnarley

As this village is at the edge of the viscounty and the deepest of the forest, the Druids of the Gnarley Forest have a few groves nearby. They revere Ehlonna , and for this reason have some friends among the wood elves. Druids do not care for woodsmen, but they know that they respect the forest. They welcome The Gnarley Rangers
