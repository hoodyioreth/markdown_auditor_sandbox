Garghuk
=======

The ogre chieftain is a particularly crafty individual. Ogre chieftains are what passes for cunning in ogre "society," though this often simply means they are somehow more brutal than the average ogre. Still, they have enough charisma to impel their kin or any lesser creature to do their bidding, and enough strength to enforce that writ.   In addition to their monetary treasure (3d6 gp and 2dlO sp each), the ogres' tribe possesses the following magic items, which are divided among the males and the chieftain: boots of speed, handaxe +2, potion of healing, potion of sweet water, ring of protection +2, stone of good luck.   The ogres are the leaders of Blackthorn simply because they are the Strongest creatures there.
