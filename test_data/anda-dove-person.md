Anda Dove
=========

### Mistress

Mistress Anda Dove is the graceful yet world-weary leader of the **Domain of the Thrice-Kissed Lady**, a sanctuary devoted to **Myhriss, goddess of love, beauty, and romance**. Once an ambitious reformer who sought to shape Twilight Falls into a haven for artists and dreamers, she has since tempered her ideals, focusing instead on **preserving beauty amid the frontier’s hardships**. Wise and compassionate, she nurtures love and art in all forms, though she carries **quiet regrets for the dreams left unrealized**.

* * *

> “Twilight Falls may not be the haven I once dreamed of, but as long as there is art, there is hope.”

##### **Description**

###### **Physical Appearance**

*   **Age:** Mid-50s, still exuding a timeless beauty touched with silver at her temples
*   **Build:** Tall, slender, and graceful, moving with a dancer’s poise
*   **Hair & Eyes:** Long auburn hair streaked with silver, deep blue eyes filled with longing and wisdom
*   **Attire:** Flows in **soft gowns embroidered with floral patterns**, adorned with a **silver locket of Myhriss**, always carrying the fragrance of roses and lavender

###### **Personality**

*   **Charismatic & Enigmatic** – Anda speaks in soft, poetic tones, effortlessly drawing others into her presence.
*   **Tired of Politics** – Once heavily involved in village governance, she has since stepped away, preferring art and faith over power.
*   **Determined yet Melancholy** – Still fights to protect beauty, but mourns the dreams that faded with time.
*   **Respected but Controversial** – Loved by artists and druids, tolerated by merchants, and dismissed by pragmatists who see her ideals as impractical.

* * *

> “I do not seek power, nor wealth, nor war. I seek only to remind this world that beauty still exists.”

### **History**

*   **566 CY:** Arrived in Twilight Falls with a band of poets, musicians, and sculptors, seeking to **build an artists' retreat**.
*   **570 CY - 574 CY:** Served **two terms as mayor**, attempting to balance the town’s rugged economy with cultural development.
*   **574 CY:** Lost political influence to **Robleth Tyne**, a powerful lumber merchant who saw little value in artistic endeavors.
*   **Post-574 CY:** Retreated from governance, **focusing on the Domain of the Thrice-Kissed Lady**, preserving a small but devoted circle of artists, priests, and romantics.
*   **Present:** Advocates for Twilight Falls’ beauty and heritage, quietly supporting figures like **Mahrli Wildbriar and Halwys Thorne**.

* * *

> “The Rangers watch over the land, the druids guard the wilds, and I… I keep the light of inspiration from fading.”

### **Motivations & Political Relationships**

*   **Seeks to protect beauty and culture** in Twilight Falls, despite the town’s focus on industry and survival.
*   **Supports Mahrli Wildbriar**, the druidic leader, in her mission to preserve the wild lands around the village.
*   **Maintains a delicate alliance with the Gnarley Rangers**, who appreciate her reverence for the land but remain skeptical of her pacifist ideals.
*   **Avoids conflicts with the Mounted Borderers**, preferring to work through diplomacy and persuasion.
*   **Distrusts the Church of Zilchus**, seeing its influence as a threat to the town’s artistic and spiritual soul.
*   **Engages in trade with elven merchants**, bringing **silver jewelry, woven silks, and rare perfumes** to the Domain’s small marketplace.

* * *

> “I was once a woman of ambition, of politics. Now I am simply a woman who refuses to let the world forget how to love.”

### **Acolytes of Mistress Anda Dove**

Mistress Anda Dove is not alone in her devotion. Two of her most trusted followers, **Elyndra Vale** and **Taron Deveris**, serve as key figures within the **Domain of the Thrice-Kissed Lady**.

##### **Elyndra Vale – The Devoted Caretaker** _(Medium humanoid, Neutral Good)_

A gentle and nurturing cleric of Myhriss, Elyndra **oversees the gardens, blesses couples, and tends to the wounded**. She is deeply devoted to **spreading love and healing**, believing that even in the roughest lands, beauty can take root.

*   **Appearance:** Wears **simple yet elegant robes embroidered with floral patterns**, her auburn hair tied back in a loose braid.
*   **Personality:** Warm and motherly, though firm in her beliefs. She sees love as a **force of nature**, capable of healing even the most broken souls.
*   **Role:** Acts as a **healer, counselor, and keeper of rituals**, ensuring the Domain remains a sanctuary for those in need.

##### **Taron Deveris – The Cynical Poet** _(Medium humanoid, Chaotic Neutral)_

A bard-priest with a sharp wit and a jaded heart, Taron writes **poetry and music dedicated to Myhriss** but often questions the sincerity of love. He stays in the Domain, not out of faith, but because **he secretly admires Mistress Anda Dove**.

*   **Appearance:** Wears **dyed ceremonial vestments**, slightly unkempt, always carrying a lute and parchment scrolls.
*   **Personality:** Witty and charismatic but **cynical about love**, seeing it as fleeting and often misguided.
*   **Role:** Acts as a **scribe, performer, and reluctant advisor**, drawing visitors into Myhriss’ fold even as he questions his own faith.

* * *

#### **Conclusion**

Mistress Anda Dove is a figure caught between **dreams and reality**. She has long abandoned the idea of transforming Twilight Falls into a paradise, yet she remains **a guardian of beauty, love, and art**. Though age and experience have made her wiser—and perhaps more resigned—her presence in the village still serves as a beacon for those who seek solace, inspiration, and devotion. In the heart of a land where **pragmatism and survival reign**, she stands as a quiet, enduring testament to the idea that beauty and love will always have a place, even in the wildest of frontiers.
