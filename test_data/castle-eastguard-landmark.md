Castle Eastguard
================

Currently under construction and scheduled to be completed by 590 CY. This walled castle will two eastward-facing towers, ready to spot any unusual activity on the Greyhawk Road. It will be able to sleep one hundred fifty people, although it will be normally garrisoned with only a hundred soldiers of the Free Army. Ten swift horses are stabled within the outer wall, not for cavalry but for carrying messages to Maraven and Dyvers, The Free Lands . Eastguard will be well stocked with weapons and have facilities to repair and create more. The Rovers are welcome at the castle, and there are one or two Rovers present at any particular time, resupplying and trading news.
