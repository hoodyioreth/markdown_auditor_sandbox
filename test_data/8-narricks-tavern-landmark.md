8\. Narrick's Tavern
====================

At the heart of Twilight Falls, where the forest shadows flirt with cobblestone streets and music drifts through the evening mist, stands **Narrick’s Tavern**. Known among locals and travelers alike as the **Wine Hall**, this refined establishment is more than a tavern—it is a cultural hub for **artisans, nobles, and devotees of Myhriss**. Favored by Mistress Anda Dove’s collective and held in high regard by the refined circles of the village, Narrick’s Tavern prides itself on exclusivity, elegance, and exceptional entertainment.

> "I’ve seen drunken knights fall in love by their third glass—makes my job easier when I sing them into their fourth."
> 
> — Thalren Merris

* * *

### The Wine Hall of Twilight Falls

The tavern is housed in a **well-appointed timber and stone building**, with ornate iron lanterns glowing softly at twilight and flower boxes blooming with lavender and night-roses.

Within, the **main music hall** opens into a **vaulted chamber of polished wood**, draped in rich fabrics and filled with the scent of spiced wine and fresh herbs. A **raised stage** stands opposite a collection of **round tables and cushioned benches**, inviting guests to dine, drink, and bask in performance.

*   **Exterior:** Ivy-covered timber, stained-glass windows, hand-carved signage.
*   **Interior:** Warm lighting, rosewood beams, plush seating, wine shelves, and a central music stage.
*   **Ambience:** Elegant, quiet, romantic; scented candles and live music nightly.

> "Myhriss teaches us to find beauty in the simple act of serving. Let me show you."
> 
> — Serel Winnow

* * *

#### **Clientele & Culture**

Narrick chooses his patrons with care. The tavern caters to:

*   **Artists, lovers, and aesthetes** aligned with the **Domain of the Thrice-Kissed Lady**.
*   **Visiting nobles, druids, and refined rangers** seeking a reprieve.
*   **Members of Anda Dove's inner circle** and **Gnarley Rangers** who appreciate art and peace.

Rowdy folk or loud drunks are pointed toward the **Weary Woodsman Tavern** instead.

> "Let the Weary Woodsman have the lumber-brawlers. My floor is for the gentle-footed and sweet-voiced."
> 
> — Narrick

#### **Adventure Hooks & Campaign Use**

*   **The Missing Bard:** Ivenel vanishes during a lunar celebration; her harp left behind, strings torn.
*   **Poisoned Wine:** A shipment from Celene arrives tainted, leading to illness and suspicion of sabotage.
*   **Secret Rendezvous:** A noble couple meets in secret at Narrick's, and players are drawn into their scandal.
*   **Divine Visitation:** Myhriss speaks through Ivenel during a song, delivering a cryptic prophecy.

#### **Summary for Dungeon Masters**

*   **Narrick's Tavern** is a high-class, wine-focused music hall in **Twilight Falls**.
*   Owned by **Narrick**, a cultured half-elf aligned with **Anda Dove and Myhriss**.

##### **Narrick – Proprietor and Curator**

Narrick is a **wiry half-elf of indeterminate age**, always impeccably dressed in **embroidered forest-green vests and deep crimson cloaks**. His sharp eyes miss nothing, and his voice carries the smoothness of aged wine.

###### **Personality**

*   Cultured, discerning, and slightly aloof.
*   Holds high standards for both patrons and performers.
*   Passionate about preserving a refined space for the appreciation of wine, art, and beauty.

###### **Background & Affiliations**

*   Former vintner and court entertainer from Dyvers, retired after a scandal involving a noble affair.
*   Moved to Twilight Falls and partnered with **Mistress Anda Dove**, aligning the tavern with the **Thrice-Kissed Lady's ideals**.
*   **Member of Anda Dove's Collective**, prefers patrons who embody the values of **Myhriss: beauty, love, and refinement**.

###### **Motivations**

*   To maintain an **exclusive sanctuary for elegance and artistic expression**.
*   To **elevate the cultural standing of Twilight Falls**.
*   To **defend against the creeping coarseness** of merchant and lumberjack taverns.

##### **Tavern Staff**

###### **Serel Winnow (Server)**

*   **Race:** Human, female, mid-30s
*   **Appearance:** Pale-haired, elegantly dressed in green and silver with floral motifs.
*   **Personality:** Calm, quick-witted, and graceful. Devotee of Myhriss, wears a rose pendant.
*   **Role:** Oversees floor service and guest needs, trained in etiquette and tea ceremonies.

##### **Thalren Merris (Server)**

*   **Race:** Half-elf, male, young adult
*   **Appearance:** Chestnut curls, bright hazel eyes, nimble build.
*   **Personality:** Playful, charming, and flirtatious.
*   **Role:** Serves drinks, entertains with stories, often sings elven verses while pouring wine.

##### **Ivenel Duskwhisper (Entertainer, Bard of** [Myhriss](myhriss-person.md)**)**

*   **Race:** Elf, female, appearing in her 120s
*   **Class:** Bard (Level 5)
*   **Appearance:** Radiant, with long raven-black hair, gold-threaded robes, and a necklace of rose quartz.
*   **Performance Style:** Sings in Elvish and Common, plays harp and lute, dances with flowing silks.
*   **Personality:** Passionate, romantic, and enigmatic.
*   **Faith:** **Cleric of Myhriss (Multiclassed)**, channels divine emotion in her art.
*   **Role:** The star of the tavern, she brings lovers together and calms weary hearts.

##### **Seasonal Dishes**

Delicately plated and locally sourced from the Gnarley Forest and surrounding farmlands.

*   **Elven Lavender & Honey-Glazed Pheasant  
    **Roasted in wine and forest herbs, served with sweetroot mash.
*   **Twilight Meadow Salad  
    **Wild greens, moonberries, candied nuts, and elven cheese with rose-petal vinaigrette.
*   **Petal-Folded Pastries  
    **Light puff pastries stuffed with roasted mushrooms, goat cheese, and herb sauce.
*   **Gnarled Root Stew** _(Vegan)  
    _Hearty blend of forest tubers, wild onion, and savory broth, served with hearthbread.
*   **Rose-Apple Tarts  
    **Thinly sliced apples folded into blooming rose shapes over sugared almond crust.

##### **Drinks & Elixirs**

Wines, meads, and special brews favored by lovers, poets, and dreamers.

*   **Myhriss’ Kiss  
    **A delicate rosé wine infused with jasmine and cherry blossom—said to soften hearts.
*   **Blushing Thorn Mead** _(House Specialty)  
    _Made with wildberries, rose hips, and a kiss of mint—refreshing and aromatic.
*   **Lover’s Cider (Dyvers)

*   **Greenleaf Sparkle (Clan Sherendyl)  
    **Effervescent herbal wine, pale gold with a crisp finish—served chilled.

    **Smoky and deep with a honey finish; imported from the Wild Coast for bold palates.
*   **Golden Hart Ale (Verbobonc)  
    **Earthy, traditional ale brewed with golden barley from the Viscounty.
