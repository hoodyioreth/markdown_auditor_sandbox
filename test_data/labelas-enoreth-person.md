Labelas Enoreth

### Eternal Wisdom and Time's Guardian

Labelas Enoreth, a deity transcending gender norms, shines with silver eyes and hair, adorned in tranquil robes reflective of time's passage. As a philosopher and mentor, he nurtures wisdom across the ages, wielding the power to manipulate the aging process, ensuring that his interventions are judicious and sparing.

*   **Description:** Labelas Enoreth embodies both male and female aspects, a testament to the fluidity and continuum of time. With silver eyes and hair, he dons robes of serene colors that symbolize the calm passage of time. As a philosopher and mentor, Labelas imparts wisdom across ages.
*   **Guardian of Ages:** His dominion over time allows him to halt or hasten the aging process, a power he wields with great discretion.

#### Cosmic Relations

Within the celestial family of the Seldarine, Labelas maintains harmonious relationships, especially with Sehanine Moonbow. His role as a deity opposes the forces of decay and entropy, fostering a unique bond with Hanali Celanil due to his protection of elven beauty against time's ravages.

*   **Divine Kinship:** Within the Seldarine, Labelas maintains a harmonious relationship, especially with [Sehanine Moonbow](sehanine-moonbow-person.md), his presumed sibling. [Corellon Larethian](corellon-larethian-person.md) is regarded as the progenitor of this timeless entity.
*   **Alliances and Enmities:** While fostering good relations within the elven pantheon, Labelas stands against deities of decay and entropy. His decree safeguarding elven beauty from the ravages of time endears him to Hanali Celanil. He is opposed to the gods of entropy and decay and Clangeddin Silverbeard the dwarven god of battle has a long-standing grudge against him.

#### Sanctuary in the Stars

Labelas resides in Arvandor on Arborea, a realm that epitomizes the ethereal beauty and continuity of existence. From this celestial domain, he oversees the flow of time, guarding the universe against temporal disruptions.

*   **Celestial Abode:** Dwelling in the ethereal beauty of Arvandor on Arborea, Labelas oversees the continuum from this celestial realm.

### Followers of the Continuum

Devotees of Labelas, typically sages, historians, and philosophers, embark on quests to recover ancient artifacts, demonstrating their dedication to preserving knowledge and history. Although direct interventions from Labelas are rare, he occasionally grants rejuvenation to those whose deeds align with his divine mission.

*   **Worshipers' Profile:** Labelas appeals to those who cherish knowledge, history, and the philosophical musings of existence. His followers embark on quests to recover lost artifacts, a testament to their devotion.
*   **Temporal Grace:** Although seldom invoked for temporal interventions, Labelas might grant rejuvenation to those who have proven their worth through monumental quests.

#### Clerical Keepers of History

The clergy of Labelas Enoreth, clad in light gray, serve as the educators, historians, and preservers of elven lore. Through daily rituals in sacred groves, they honor the passage of time, sharing prayers and wisdom to celebrate another day’s conclusion.

*   **The Duties of the Clergy:** Labelas' clerics are the educators, historians, and guardians of elven culture and wisdom. Their attire reflects the lightness of their divine charge, wearing robes of light gray as they preserve the annals of time.
*   **Rituals of Dusk:** Each day's end is marked by gatherings in sacred groves, where the passage of time is honored through prayer and the exchange of knowledge.

#### Conclusion

Through the teachings and influence of Labelas Enoreth, the elves are reminded of the preciousness of time and the importance of wisdom that transcends ages. His followers are custodians of history, ensuring that the legacy of elvenkind is preserved for eternity.

##### Labelas Clergy Heirarchy

*   **Observer:** Novices who are just beginning their journey into the lore of the elven kind, focusing on observation and learning.
*   **Recorder:** Clerics tasked with documenting new findings, experiences, and knowledge gained from the world.
*   **Librarian:** Guardians of sacred texts and knowledge, managing vast collections of historical records and magical tomes.
*   **Lorist:** Experts in specific fields of elven history or magical lore, often sought for their specialized knowledge.
*   **Scholar:** Advanced practitioners who contribute significantly to the collective understanding of the elven past and magical practices.
*   **Historian:** Esteemed clerics who have mastered the art of historical analysis, providing insights into the progression of elven society and the wider world.
*   **Sage:** Wise clerics recognized for their deep understanding of multiple disciplines, often acting as advisors.
*   **Philosopher:** Those who ponder the metaphysical implications of time and fate, offering guidance on the moral and ethical dilemmas of life.
*   **Time Sentinel:** The highest rank, reserved for those who have shown an unparalleled commitment to preserving the continuum and safeguarding the future from temporal disruptions.
