2\. Glopna’s Stone Inn
======================

_A Cozy Refuge for Gnomes and Halflings in the Heart of Kron_

**Introduction**
----------------

Glopna’s Stone Inn is one of the most well-known establishments in the village of Kron, a welcoming haven for gnomes, halflings, and the occasional adventurous traveler small enough to fit comfortably through its rounded doors. Carved directly into a natural rock formation, this **gnome and halfling-sized inn** exudes warmth and tradition, offering **fine brews, hearty meals, and a roaring hearth** to those seeking shelter and camaraderie. With its **low ceilings, carved stone furniture, and burrow-like charm**, Glopna’s is a beloved gathering place for locals and a rare but delightful curiosity for visitors who can squeeze inside.

> "Mind your head and mind your manners—both will get you in trouble if you’re not careful."
> 
> — Glopna Nimblecap, Innkeeper

* * *

**Exterior and Structure**
--------------------------

### **An Inn Built into Stone**

_Glopna’s Stone Inn is a unique architectural wonder, blending seamlessly with the surrounding rock formations of Kron._

*   **Carved into a large boulder**, the inn’s entrance is marked by a rounded wooden door reinforced with iron bands.
*   A **stone chimney** juts out of the top, always puffing with the scent of roasting meats and burning oak.
*   The **roof is covered in moss and creeping ivy**, giving it the appearance of a natural formation rather than a built structure.
*   Small **round windows** dot the walls, letting in a soft glow of daylight while keeping the cozy interior private.

> "A mug of Nutmeg Ale costs a few coppers. The stories that come with it? Priceless."
> 
> — Jori "Tallmug" Tealeaf, Bartender

### **The Courtyard and Stables**

_Despite its modest size, Glopna’s Stone Inn has a small but well-maintained courtyard._

*   **A fenced-off area for ponies, goats, and burrowing mounts** provides accommodations for gnome and halfling travelers.
*   A **covered patio with stone benches and wooden tables** allows guests to enjoy their drinks in the open air.
*   Lanterns with **soft, glowing magical fireflies** light the courtyard at night, adding to the inn’s rustic charm.

* * *

**Interior and Atmosphere**
---------------------------

### **A Cozy and Intimate Space**

_The inside of Glopna’s Stone Inn is designed for comfort, with warm lighting, sturdy furnishings, and a welcoming atmosphere._

*   **Low-beamed ceilings** ensure that human visitors must crouch, while gnomes and halflings move about freely.
*   **Stone walls carved with intricate gnomish patterns** depicting old tales and legends give the inn a historical touch.
*   The **main hearth** is an impressive carved fireplace, with a roaring fire that provides warmth and a central gathering point.
*   **Round tables with thick wooden chairs** are arranged in cozy clusters, encouraging conversation and camaraderie.
*   A **bookshelf filled with gnome and halfling folk tales** stands in the corner, available for guests to peruse over a mug of cider.

> "A traveler once told me our chairs are too small. I told him his backside was too big."
> 
> — Glopna Nimblecap

### **Rooms and Accommodations**

_Glopna’s offers small but comfortable lodgings for weary travelers._

*   **Five guest rooms**, each with rounded doors, soft feathered mattresses, and handwoven blankets.
*   A **communal sleeping area for passing merchants and adventurers**, filled with soft cots and thick curtains for privacy.
*   **A hidden burrow-style room** reserved for long-term guests or those needing complete discretion.

> "A warm hearth, a full belly, and a locked door—that’s all the safety you’ll get in Kron."

### **Politics and Intrigue**

##### **A Gathering Place for the Kron Council**

*   The **elders of Kron** frequently meet at Glopna’s Stone Inn to discuss trade, security, and political affairs.
*   Deals between **Clan Keeleene and Clan Tulvar** are often brokered over mugs of Nutmeg Ale.

##### **Rumors and Secrets**

*   Some claim there is a **hidden tunnel beneath the inn** leading to a secret gnome hideout.
*   A **few well-placed bribes** can convince Jori Tallmug to let someone use the private back room for discreet meetings.
*   Travelers whisper that Glopna **knows more about the bandit raids** on the Greenway than she lets on.

* * *

### **Adventuring Hooks**

##### **The Missing Merchant**

_A well-known gnome jeweler staying at Glopna’s has vanished overnight. Did he flee? Or was he taken?_

##### **The Ghost in the Cellar**

_Guests claim to hear strange whispers from the basement. Is it haunted, or is something—or someone—hiding below?_

##### **The Smuggler’s Map**

_A halfling adventurer left behind an old map before he disappeared. Jori Tallmug might know what it leads to…_

##### **The Bandit’s Mark**

_Glopna herself has received a cryptic message carved into the tavern door. A warning? Or an invitation?_

* * *

#### **Conclusion**

Glopna’s Stone Inn is more than just a place to sleep—it is the heart of **Kron’s social life, political intrigue, and whispered rumors**. Whether adventurers are looking for a hot meal, an interesting story, or a mystery to unravel, this cozy inn remains a welcoming (or perilous) stop on their journey through the Greenway Valley.

##### **Glopna Nimblecap**

**The Innkeeper**

*   A **stout, middle-aged gnome woman** with curly silver hair and a warm smile.
*   Known for her **quick wit and sharp tongue**, she takes no nonsense from rowdy guests.
*   A former **adventurer turned innkeeper**, she has a collection of **odd relics** displayed behind the bar.
*   Deeply involved in **village politics**, often providing a neutral ground for meetings and discussions.

##### **Jori "Tallmug" Tealeaf**

**The Halfling Bartender**

*   A **cheerful and round halfling** who can pour a perfect ale even in the middle of a tavern brawl.
*   Loves **gossiping with travelers** and often trades drinks for **interesting tales**.
*   Secretly runs **a side business in smuggling rare herbs and potions**, using the inn as a cover.

##### **Mibbin Oakwhistle**

**The House Bard**

*   A **gnome bard** who plays the **whistlepipe and lute**, performing in exchange for free food and lodging.
*   His repertoire includes **old gnomish songs, bawdy drinking tunes, and eerie forest ballads**.
*   Once a thief, he now uses his **silver tongue to diffuse fights before they escalate**.

##### **Food and Drink**

###### **The Menu at Glopna’s**

_The inn serves hearty, traditional gnome and halfling cuisine, renowned for its rich flavors and satisfying portions._

*   **Brambleberry Stew** – A thick, meaty stew made with root vegetables, mushrooms, and wild berries.
*   **Puffroot Pies** – Small, flaky pastries filled with cheese, onions, and herbs.
*   **Crispgriddle Trout** – Freshly caught fish from the Greenway Valley, pan-seared with butter and lemon.
*   **Nutmeg Ale** – A warm, spiced ale brewed in-house, popular during the colder months.
*   **Honeyglow Cider** – A sweet, fermented apple cider with a hint of cinnamon, favored by halflings.
*   **Gnomefire Spirits** – A fiery, potent drink known to make even dwarves cough, best taken in small sips.
