Halvand Deyr
============

### Strider

> Standing there is a large man of baklunish Suel mix, great of girth, with a booming voice and a friendly demeanor. His large face frames a brown beard and friars hat which covers his baldness. He seems friendly and exuberant and full of mirth. With just traveling clothes he wields a a shiny and smooth quarterstaff from years of use and travel. Hanging from a leather cord around his neck is a wooden disc carved with a curved line .

_Guardian of the Open Road_

Halvand is the jovial and robust guardian of the [18\. Fharlanghn Chapter House](18-fharlanghn-chapter-house-landmark.md) in [Ostverk](ostverk-settlement.md). Known for his large stature and equally large heart, Halvand embodies the spirit of hospitality and safe passage that Fharlanghn, the god of roads, espouses. His chapter house not only serves as a waypoint for travelers but also as a symbol of the journeys that shape our lives.

> _“Fharlanghn’s wisdom is simple: Put one foot in front of the other, and the world will show you where to go.”_

#### Background

*   **Origins:** Halvand is of Baklunish-Suel descent, his heritage evident in his robust build and distinctive features.
*   **Early Life:** Before dedicating his life to Fharlanghn, Halvand was a caravan leader, known for his lucky escapes and cheerful disposition despite the dangers of the road.

> _“The horizon is no end; it’s a promise. The journey is what makes it worthwhile.”_

### Role in Ostverk

**Halvand Deyr** is the **Chapter House Keeper** of Fharlanghn’s sanctuary in Ostverk—a vital waystation for travelers on the Long Road, near the dangerous frontier of Celene and the Kron Hills. He provides:

*   **Sanctuary and Aid** for weary travelers, pilgrims, and merchants
*   **Blessings of Fharlanghn** before journeys through the Gnarley or into elven lands
*   **Spiritual counsel** and caravan route warnings
*   **Divine scrying**, used to detect ambushes or unrest along the major roads
*   **Spiritual Guide:** He offers blessings and guidance to those who seek to travel, especially those venturing into dangerous territories like the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md) or crossing into the Faerie Kingdom of [Celene](celene-organization.md).
*   **Community Support:** Halvand’s duties extend beyond spiritual guidance; he helps maintain the stability and safety of the local travel routes.

#### Divine Gifts of Fharlanghn

Halvand is blessed with visions that **reveal danger on the road ahead**. These visions come through dreams, subtle wind shifts, or shadows across a trail map. He uses this knowledge to:

*   Warn travelers of bandits or beast tracks near the forest edge
*   Provide alternate paths, rest stops, or nightwatch timings
*   Share information with **Lord Deleveu**, **the Gnarley Rangers**, and **gnomish scouts**
*   Send **emergency alerts** to outposts via trained kestrels or whispering prayers

#### Services Offered

*   **Traveler's Aid:** Halvand provides various services such as care for horses, lodging, and even baths for weary travelers.
*   **Blessings and Rituals:** Regular blessings for caravans and individual travelers commence under Halvand’s watchful eye, invoking Fharlanghn’s favor for safe and successful journeys.

### Relationships and Interactions

*   Tiriel: Halvand shares a special bond with [Tiriel Mossheart](tiriel-mossheart-person.md), admiring her patience and wisdom. He often turns to her for advice and assistance in spiritual matters.
*   **Travelers and Pilgrims:** Halvand interacts daily with a diverse array of travelers, offering them a warm welcome and practical assistance.
*   **Glarian (Innkeeper):** Halvand often enjoys a warm meal and ale at the Laughing Moon. The two share a deep mutual respect, as Glarian occasionally sends troubled travelers to Halvand for guidance.

> _“I’ve seen more danger in a man’s heart than I ever did on the open road. Choose your traveling companions well.”_

#### Challenges and Threats

*   **Regional Dangers:** The vicinity around Ostverk, including the menacing [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md) and the volatile border with [Celene](celene-organization.md), poses significant dangers due to bandit raids and orcish warbands, making Halvand’s role crucial for many.
*   **Overseeing Safety**: With threats from Blackthorn Orcs and other malevolent forces, Halvand’s leadership is pivotal in safeguarding the roads and paths leading to and from Ostverk.

#### Personal Traits and Qualities

*   **Affectionate and Protective:** Known for his bear-hug greetings, Halvand’s affectionate nature makes him a beloved figure among those who know him.
*   **Lucky Yet Clumsy:** His success often attributed to sheer luck rather than skill, Halvand's clumsy yet fortunate nature has become legendary along the roads he travels.

> _“Bless your steps, friend. Fharlanghn teaches us that every road leads somewhere worth knowing – even if it’s just back to where you started.”_

##### Future Aspirations

*   **Enhanced Security:** Halvand aims to strengthen the defenses of the Chapter House and improve the safety of the travel routes under his watch.
*   **Expand Outreach:** He is motivated to extend the services of the Chapter House to support more travelers and become a beacon of hope and safety for all who venture through Ostverk.

### Divine Augery of Fharlanghn

Halvand, the affable cleric of Fharlanghn, plays a vital role in aiding travelers and sharing his divine insights into the often perilous routes of the Viscounty of Verbobonc and the Gnarley forests. His unique ability to scry travel information is invaluable, particularly in an area fraught with the dangers posed by bandits, slavers from the Wild Coast, and marauding bands of Blackthorn Orcs and raiding goblins. Here's how Halvand's divine powers enhance his service to the community:

*   **Scrying for Safety:** Halvand uses his divinely granted scrying abilities to foresee potential dangers along the travel routes. This proactive approach helps in warning travelers and merchants about possible threats, allowing them to prepare or choose safer paths.
*   **Sharing Crucial Information:** He shares this crucial travel information with travelers and merchants stopping at the Fharlanghn Chapter House, ensuring they are well-informed before they continue their journeys.
*   **Strategic Advice:** For those heading into particularly dangerous areas, Halvand provides strategic advice on how to avoid or handle potential threats based on his scrying results.
*   **Collaboration with Local Forces:** Halvand's insights are also shared with local militia and rangers, aiding them in patrolling and securing the roads and forests more effectively.
*   **Support for Merchant Caravans:** Merchant caravans benefit significantly from Halvand's abilities, as he can predict safe travel times and routes, reducing their risks of ambushes and losses.
*   **Emergency Alerts:** In cases of imminent threats, such as orc raids or slaver attacks, Halvand can send out emergency alerts to nearby settlements and travelers, potentially saving lives and livelihoods.

Halvand’s role extends beyond that of a typical cleric; he is a guardian of the pathways, ensuring that the roads and trails of the Viscounty of Verbobonc remain as safe as possible for those who travel them. His dedication to Fharlanghn’s tenets of travel and discovery is demonstrated daily through his commitment to the safety and well-being of all who pass through his chapter house.

### For Dungeon Masters (DM-Only Section)

*   **Hidden Knowledge:** Halvand's extensive travels make him a font of hidden lore. He knows paths through the Gnarley Forest, contacts among the Knights of Luna, and even whispers of ancient ruins hidden in the Lortmil Mountains.
*   **Treasure Map Hook:** Halvand possesses a torn map hinting at a forgotten shrine to Fharlanghn. If players earn his trust, he may share it with them as part of a quest.
*   **Potential Ally:** If treated well, Halvand can act as an ally to the party, offering safe refuge, healing, or crucial information for their travels.
*   **Neutral Mediator**: Halvand has a secret understanding with both local gnome merchants and elven emissaries, making him uniquely positioned to broker peace between factions in Ostverk.
*   **Quest Hook:** Bandits or monstrous incursions threatening travelers on the roads may prompt Halvand to seek adventurers for aid, tasking them to clear out dangers in the nearby Hingnora Waterside Mills or beyond.

#### Conclusion

Halvand’s life and work at the Fharlanghn Chapter House epitomize the ideals of journey and discovery. His dedication not only serves those who seek respite within his walls but also embodies the spirit of adventure that Fharlanghn cherishes.
