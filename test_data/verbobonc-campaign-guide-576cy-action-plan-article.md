Verbobonc Campaign Guide 576CY Action Plan
==========================================

### **Project Overview**

The **Verbobonc Campaign Guide 576CY** is an ongoing world-building project focused on detailing cities, villages, locations, and organizations within the Viscounty of Verbobonc, Gnarley Forest, Wild Coast, Dyvers, and the surrounding regions. This action plan will organize and track the progress of content creation by assigning specific tasks to content creators with set deadlines and work-in-progress updates.

**Tracking & Workflow**
-----------------------

Each content creator will be responsible for:

1.  **Researching** relevant material from existing world lore.
2.  **Outlining** key details such as history, geography, politics, and notable figures.
3.  **Writing** and formatting the article according to the **Verbobonc Campaign Guide 576CY** structure.
4.  **Submitting drafts** for review and revision.
5.  **Finalizing and publishing** the content to World Anvil.

Each assignment will be monitored in a shared document, and progress will be updated weekly. Deadlines can be adjusted based on workload and complexity.
