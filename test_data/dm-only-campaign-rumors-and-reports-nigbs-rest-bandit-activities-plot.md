DM-only Campaign Rumors and Reports: Nigb's Rest Bandit Activities
==================================================================

The merchant roads connecting the [Verbobonc, City](verbobonc2C-city-settlement.md) and the village of Ostverk have become perilous due to increasing bandit activities. These attacks have disrupted trade routes, affecting both human and gnome merchants. This article delves into the recent surge in banditry, detailing locations, notable characters, and political relationships within the Viscounty of Verbobonc.

### The Bandit Menace

The bandit attacks have primarily targeted the merchant roads from Etterboek to Nigb's Rest, extending south to the village of Hommlet and Sheernobb, and reaching Ostverk on the border of the Fey Kingdom of Celene. These roads, critical for trade between human and gnome settlements, have become increasingly perilous.

##### Character and Personality of Bandits

The bandits, typically human brigands in greasy leathers with disheveled appearances, are well-organized and daring. They operate in the dense forests, laying ambushes along the narrow, winding roads, and showing little regard for the law.

##### Political Tensions

The political landscape of the Viscounty of Verbobonc has been strained by these bandit activities. Trade disruptions affect the economies of Verbobonc, Nigb's Rest, and the gnome settlements, creating tension among the local leaders and residents.

### Locations Affected by Bandit Activities

**The Long Road  
**The Long Road is a vital trade route connecting Verbobonc to Ostverk. It passes through several key locations, including:

*   [Etterboek](etterboek-settlement-1.md): Northern starting point of the troubled trade route.
*   [Nigb's Rest](nigb-s-rest-settlement.md) : A central hub impacted by the bandit raids.
*   [Village of Hommlet](village-of-hommlet-settlement.md): Southern village experiencing increasing bandit presence.
*   [Sheernobb](sheernobb-settlement.md): Gnome settlement affected by the attacks.
*   [Ostverk](ostverk-settlement.md) : Southern border village near the Fey Kingdom of [Celene](celene-organization.md).

##### The Viscounty Road

This road connects various settlements within the Viscounty of Verbobonc, facilitating trade and travel for both humans and gnomes.

### Economic Strains

Local businesses, especially the vineyards and ranches, have suffered significant losses due to the bandit raids. Vartia Vineyard, Tiera Vineyard, Grape Caravan Traders, Cienega Valley Wine Market, Tornio Ranch, and Tumasarson Farm have all reported missing shipments.

*   **Wine and Cheese Thefts**: Common targets include barrels of wine and cheese shipments, with losses impacting local trade and production.
*   **Merchant Rewards:** Merchants like those from Vartia Vineyard have offered rewards for recovering stolen goods, indicating the severity of the situation.

### Detailed Reports and Rumors

**Bandit Attacks and Incidents**

*   **Etterboek to Nigb's Rest:** Increasing reports of ambushes on merchant caravans.
*   **Nigb's Rest to Hommlet:** Notable hijackings, particularly of wine shipments.
*   **Hommlet to Sheernobb:** Steady rise in fully-fledged robberies.
*   **Sheernobb to Ostverk:** Reports of bandits and rumors of involvement with Fey creatures.

##### Local Comments and Concerns

*   **Merchants Arming Themselves:** Growing need for self-defense among traders.
*   **Noble Intervention:** Some nobles increasing military presence due to bandit threats.
*   **Druid Gatherings:** Reports of druids meeting in the Etters, possibly related to the bandit activities.

##### Notable Events

*   **Merchant Convoys Attacked:** Several incidents reported by Vartia and Tiera Vineyards.
*   **Skyrider Attacks:** Vicious skyriders mounted on large winged birds attacking caravans.
*   **Haunted Keep Gatherings:** Druids rumored to gather at a haunted keep in the Etters.

#### Conclusion

The rise in bandit activities on the Long Road and Viscounty Road poses a significant threat to trade and stability in the Viscounty of Verbobonc. Key figures in Nigb's Rest are working together to address these challenges, but the situation remains tense. Merchants and residents alike are urged to stay vigilant and report any suspicious activities.   For more information, stay tuned to local announcements and updates from the Cienega Valley Vintner's Guild Hall and the Asbury House Guards.
