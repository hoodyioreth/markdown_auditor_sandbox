Tallfellow
==========

The Tallfellow Halflings of Greyhawk are a unique and esteemed sub-race within the hobniz communities. These beings, known for their affinity with nature, their skilled craftsmanship, and their valor in defense of their homelands, offer a rich tapestry of culture and tradition that intertwines closely with the sylvan realms they inhabit.

### History and Lore

Tallfellows share a history deeply connected with the forests they call home. Their origins are intertwined with the ancient woodlands, where they have long formed symbiotic relationships with the elves. Their societies have evolved in the shadow of these towering guardians, learning the ways of the forest and the art of living in harmony with the land. This deep connection to nature has imbued Tallfellows with a profound understanding of woodland lore and an inherent ability to navigate and protect their forested realms.

### Society and Culture

Tallfellow society is marked by a profound respect for nature and a close kinship with elves. Their villages are typically nestled near elven populations, fostering strong bonds and mutual trade between the two races. Tallfellows live in harmony with their environment, crafting homes from wood and living above ground to stay connected with the natural world.

#### Community Life

*   **Occupations:** Tallfellows excel in occupations that require a keen understanding of nature and craftsmanship. They are renowned carpenters, loggers, musicians, and artisans, producing goods that are highly sought after by other races for their quality and beauty.
*   **Farming and Foraging:** While proficient farmers, Tallfellows are particularly adept at foraging, harvesting the forest's bounty with a gentle hand that ensures the continued health and balance of their ecosystem.
*   **Riding and Combat:** Unique among halflings, Tallfellows are skilled riders, breeding and training small ponies for travel and combat. They are also proficient with spears, capable of forming defensive formations that can stand against larger foes.

#### Locations

Tallfellows are found in many temperate woodland regions across Greyhawk, with a significant presence in areas like Highfolk. Their communities are integrated into the landscape, with homes and structures that complement the natural beauty of their surroundings.

### Politics

Tallfellow halflings are generally apolitical, focusing on the wellbeing of their communities and the protection of their homes. However, their alliances with elven kingdoms and their strategic importance as guardians of forested realms often place them at the heart of regional politics, especially in matters concerning the defense against encroaching threats.   In conclusion, the Tallfellow Halflings of Greyhawk embody the spirit of the sylvan lands they inhabit. With their deep-rooted history, unique culture, and natural affinities, Tallfellows stand as a testament to the diversity and richness of the world's halfling sub-races.

##### Physical Characteristics

*   **Height and Build:** Tallfellows stand taller than their Harfoot and Stout counterparts, averaging a little over 4 feet in height. They possess a slender and light-boned physique, making them appear almost elf-like in stature.
*   **Appearance:** They are distinguished by their long hair, often worn under small, brightly-colored caps. Their facial features are delicate, with clear eyes that reflect a deep-seated wisdom.
*   **Lifespan:** Notably, Tallfellows are the longest-lived among the halfling sub-races, boasting an average lifespan of 180 years, with some individuals reaching beyond 250 years.
*   **Attire:** Preferring the hues of the forest, Tallfellows dress in shades of brown, yellow, and green, often employing unique dyes to create vibrant clothing that blends seamlessly with their woodland surroundings.
