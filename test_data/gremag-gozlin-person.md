Gremag Gozlin
=============

Gremag Gozlin, co-proprietor of D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile in Hommlet, is a tall and thin merchant known for his fussy demeanor and sharp features. His tendency to dither and fuss at customers and his partner, Rannos Davi, gives him a somewhat comical presence. Despite his seemingly harmless eccentricities, Gremag plays a key role in the daily operations of the trading post, ensuring that the shop remains well-stocked and organized to meet the needs of villagers and travelers alike.

#### Role in the Community

As a co-owner of D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile, Gremag is an integral part of Hommlet's commercial scene. He interacts with customers daily, offering assistance and advice on purchases. His knowledge of the shop's inventory is extensive, allowing him to make recommendations and fulfill special orders.

### Background and Relationships

Gremag partnered with Rannos Davi to establish D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile, seeing an opportunity to capitalize on the growing trade in Hommlet. His sharp business acumen and attention to detail have contributed to the shop's success, attracting a steady stream of customers.

##### Relationships

*   [Rannos Davl](rannos-davl-person.md): Gremag's business partner and collaborator in managing the shop. Though he often fusses at Rannos, the two work well together, balancing each other's strengths and weaknesses.
*   **Customers and Villagers:** Gremag maintains cordial relationships with his customers, using his fussy nature to ensure they receive the best service possible. His eccentricities are tolerated, if not appreciated, by many in the community.

##### Motivations

*   **Profit:** Gremag is motivated by a desire to increase the shop's profitability, using his business skills to attract and retain customers.
*   **Reputation:** He strives to maintain the shop's reputation as a reliable source of goods and services, ensuring it remains a cornerstone of Hommlet's economy.

### Current Activities

##### Shop Operations

Gremag is actively involved in the management of D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile, overseeing the day-to-day operations and ensuring the shop runs smoothly. He is meticulous in tracking inventory and fulfilling customer orders, often going out of his way to accommodate special requests.

##### Community Engagement

Gremag participates in local events and gatherings, using these opportunities to network and promote the shop. He enjoys engaging with customers, sharing stories and insights about the latest products and trends.

### Secret Motivations and Plans

##### True Allegiances

Gremag is a servant of the Temple of Elemental Evil, using his position in Hommlet to further the Temple's dark goals. He and Rannos Davi report local activities to a brigand courier, ensuring that the Temple remains informed of potential threats and opportunities.

##### Evil Tendencies

Sociopathic Urges: Gremag is a sociopathic serial killer who struggles to control his urges. Once a month, he stalks and kills someone, ritually cutting out their brains and eating them. This gruesome habit has led some to speculate that a mind flayer is on the loose. Manipulation of Adventurers: Gremag is particularly interested in adventurers who uncover hidden treasures or dungeons. He gathers information from them under the guise of friendly conversation, then passes this intelligence on to a group of evil adventurers for a cut of the profits.

##### Plans for Hommlet

Gremag seeks to destabilize the region by fostering distrust and chaos, all while maintaining his facade of a fussy merchant. He uses his influence to sow discord, ensuring that the Temple's interests are served.

##### Partnership with Rannos

Together with Rannos, Gremag plots to increase their control over Hommlet, using the shop as a base of operations for their nefarious activities. They aim to extend their influence beyond the village, manipulating events to benefit the Temple and their own ambitions.

##### Exploitation of Bibi and Garent

Gremag uses Bibi and Garent as spies and agents, employing them to monitor adventurers and report any valuable discoveries. This allows Gremag to stay informed and maintain control over potential threats and opportunities in Hommlet.

### Temple Command Chain — Who Gremag Reports To

**Gremag Gozlin does not operate independently. He is a managed Temple asset under the direct oversight of **Lucius Graeme**, the Greater Temple's courier and intelligence handler.** The "brigand courier" Gremag and Rannos send reports through is part of Lucius's encrypted communication network — every piece of intelligence Gremag gathers flows up through Lucius to **Hedrack** and the Greater Temple leadership.

#### Gremag's Reporting Chain

*   **Direct handler:** **Lucius Graeme** — Greater Temple courier and intelligence handler. Lucius recruited Gremag, provides operational direction, and evaluates his intelligence product. All of Gremag's reports pass through Lucius before reaching Greater Temple leadership
*   **Communication method:** Encrypted courier network. Gremag passes written reports to a brigand courier who carries them to Lucius at his staging points along the Nulb–Hommlet corridor. Lucius evaluates the intelligence and forwards actionable items to **Hedrack**
*   **Operational partner:** [Rannos Davl](rannos-davl-person.md) — Gremag's co-proprietor at D&G Dry Goods and his co-conspirator. Both report to Lucius Graeme through the same courier network. Lucius considers them a single intelligence cell
*   **Strategic authority:** **Hedrack** (Greater Temple). Gremag does not know Hedrack personally — all orders from the Greater Temple leadership come down through Lucius. Gremag knows only that his handler serves a powerful Temple superior

#### Gremag's Place in the Hommlet Spy Network

Gremag and [Rannos Davl](rannos-davl-person.md) are two of four Temple agents embedded in [Hommlet](village-of-hommlet-settlement.md). All four report to **Lucius Graeme**:

*   **Rannos Davl & Gremag Gozlin** — D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile. Intelligence gathering and smuggling coordination. Primary recruitment contacts for civilian sympathizers
*   **Zert** — Mercenary agent at the Inn of the Welcome Wench. Monitors adventurers, sabotages Doomwatch Keep construction. Lucius's enforcer in Hommlet
*   **[Joseph Millar](joseph-millar-person.md)** — Saboteur at the Doomwatch Keep construction site. Delays fortification construction under Lucius's direction. Also has a separate link to the [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md) through [Romag](romag-person.md)

This spy network was originally built to support [Lareth](lareth-person.md) the Beautiful's Moathouse operation. Now that Lareth has been captured or killed, the network continues to operate under Lucius Graeme's direct control — the agents' loyalty is to the Greater Temple, not to Lareth personally.

#### DM Notes: Why the Command Chain Matters

*   **If the party captures Gremag:** Interrogation is complicated by Gremag's sociopathic nature. He will lie reflexively (DC 18 Insight to detect deception). Under sustained pressure (DC 16 Intimidation), he reveals his "handler" — a man who travels between Nulb and Hommlet. Gremag knows Lucius by name and face but does not know Lucius's connection to **Hedrack** or the Greater Temple hierarchy
*   **If the party kills Gremag:** Lucius loses one half of his primary D&G Dry Goods intelligence cell. [Rannos Davl](rannos-davl-person.md) panics and may attempt to flee Hommlet within 48 hours unless Lucius sends a courier ordering him to hold position. If both Rannos and Gremag are eliminated, Lucius falls back on Zert as his sole Hommlet intelligence source
*   **Gremag's serial killing complication:** Gremag's monthly murders are a personal compulsion, not a Temple operation. **Lucius Graeme** is aware of Gremag's "hobby" and considers it a security liability — if Gremag is caught for the killings, the entire Hommlet spy network is compromised. Lucius has warned Gremag to control himself. This tension is exploitable by the party
*   **If Lareth's diary is recovered:** The diary implicates Rannos, Gremag, and Zert — but does NOT name Lucius. The diary only references a "courier" who delivers Hedrack's orders. Connecting the courier to **Lucius Graeme** requires additional investigation (DC 18 Investigation to cross-reference courier descriptions from multiple sources)

##### Gremag: The Fussy Trader

Gremag is tall and thin, with sharp features and protruding eyes that give him a hawkish appearance. He often dresses in neat, practical clothing that reflects his role as a meticulous and detail-oriented merchant.

##### Personality

Gremag is known for his fussiness and attention to detail. He can often be seen flitting about the shop, adjusting displays and fussing over minor details to ensure everything is in order. His dithering mannerisms, though occasionally exasperating, endear him to some customers, who view him as a harmless eccentric.
