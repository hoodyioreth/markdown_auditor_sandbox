Acknowledgements
----------------

This fan work builds on the imagination and scholarship of many creators. Primary inspiration flows from E. Gary Gygax, with major contributions and developments by Roger E. Moore and Carl Sargent across multiple editions. Sources include AD&D, 2e, 3E/3.5—and, where helpful for mechanics or lore parallels, select material originating in the Forgotten Realms and Eberron product lines.

Special thanks: the Greyhawk Online **Wiki (Fandom)** proved invaluable for quickly locating publication data across editions. Greyhawk Grognard, Greyhawk Musings

If a relevant source is missing below, that’s on me—I’ll update this page promptly as needed.
