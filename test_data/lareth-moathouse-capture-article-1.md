#### Recruitment

The [Cult of the Elder Elemental God](cult-of-the-elder-elemental-god-organization.md) have hateful principle that take into a primary form in the world. The first is corruption. Elemental Evil tempts the broken, the wronged, and the wicked with the power to strike back. It represents the worm in the apple, a hidden corruption that grows stronger over time as the natural order decays. We work as a network cult, the Viscounty Lords are debased, and the fundamental laws governing the Viscounty begin to weaken. You will be able to strike back at the greedy, malicious, wrong doing Lords. Revenge will be at hand for those that join the brethren. Until this world becomes an everlasting war of raging elements.

#### Questioning

We are supposed to turn any captives over to the Hangmen for questioning and sacrifice on the altar, but sometimes an attractive merchant, guard, or passenger catches Lareth's eye and is held back for a time as a "potential recruit" until he tires of the prisoner. Sometimes we hold a prosperous-looking captive for ransom, promising to free them once the coin arrives. We have Lubash watch them. But by that time the [The Crimson Reapers](the-crimson-reapers-organization.md) or TOEE agent @luchave discovered the captive's existence and demand them for their altars-a request we dare not refuse. Commander Gattas brings the prisoners and supplies to Demien's farm then on up the High road to the hidden cabin. If he is cross with someone, he will order them to help bring the supplies to the cabin. But Gattas Void always returns alone. \*shiver\*

#### What is the Cabin in the Woods

It is a log [55\. Hunting Cabin - off map](55-hunting-cottage-landmark.md) just south of Nulb. [Gattas Void](gattas-void-person.md) says it is a base run by the most ruthless butcher and his pack of Hyena's and a giant Gnoll sent by the King Ga’awootarr. We are to leave the bodies in the back shed then leave as fast as we can!
