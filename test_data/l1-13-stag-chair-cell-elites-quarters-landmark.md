L1-13. Stag-Chair Cell 'Elite’s Quarters'
=========================================

The number 13 is slapped above the lintel in crude whitewash. A single door admits a cramped cell: on the far wall a faded mural of everyday scenes—children learning letters, a woman weaving, farmers exchanging bread—glows in torchlight. Closer inspection shows Suel order marks woven through the composition. A bed, a low chest, and a straight-backed chair carved with a proud stag’s head crowd the room. Grease and smoke cling to the air.

### Suel features & lore keys

*   **Everyday Mural:**
*   From a distance: domestic calm.
*   **DC 12 Religion/History:** spot **Lydia**’s teaching glyphs etched into the border and **Phyton**’s stylized field-scrolls in the background. Reading this correctly grants **advantage on one Investigation or Insight check** made in this room (the “order in the ordinary” clicks for the players).
*   A faint ledger rune appears on **every sixth floor tile**—the temple’s cadence of order.
*   **Stag-Carved Chair:** Hard oak, straight arms, antler finials. **DC 11 History/Nature:** a [Phyton](phyton-person.md) motif adopted by Suel households honoring tended woodland over wilderness. The antler finials can **snap off** (action) to become two improvised **daggers** (1d4 piercing) if needed.

Whitewash “13” crowns the door. Inside, a bed, a chest, and a straight-backed chair carved with a stag’s head. A mural of everyday life—lessons, weaving, trade—softens the stone. An orc sits in the chair, gnawing a greasy haunch and using the chest as a table.

#### Features & checks

*   **Bed:** functional; the underside slat is loose (can be palmed as a club).
*   **Chest:** plain hasp, **unlocked**. He uses it as a table while he eats.
*   **Whitewash “13”:** fresh; orc tally paint.

#### Tactics & response

*   **If no earlier combat:** Brughan sits in the stag chair, **gnawing a gamy haunch**, chest pulled up as a table.
*   **Opener:** He **hurls the greasy haunch** (treat as improvised club, +5 to hit, 1d4+3 bludgeoning; on a hit, target must make **DC 10 Dex** or slip, falling **prone**), then closes with his **greataxe**.
*   **Style:** Uses the chair as a **shove tool** (object interaction to tip; creatures behind it must succeed **DC 12 Str** or be **pushed 5 ft**). He fights to hold the doorway so help can arrive.
*   **Response clock:** Noise here brings—
*   **Rooms 10, 11, 12:** **next round**
*   **Chief + bodyguard:** **3 rounds**
*   **Barracks warriors:** **6 rounds**

#### Treasure (chest)

*   **Coins:** **12 ep, 27 sp, 10 cp**.
*   **Fine set of orc-carved knucklebones** (engraved crescents and scars; **5 gp** to a collector of orc games).
*   **Small box** with **chalk & charcoal sticks** used for rites and body marks (orc blue crescent, Suel ledger lines).
