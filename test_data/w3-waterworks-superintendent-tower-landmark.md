W3 Waterworks Superintendent Tower
==================================

_**The Lifeline of Verbobonc**_

Verbobonc’s wells do not simply draw from the Velverdyva. Water must be filtered, channeled, measured, and monitored. The Superintendent Tower regulates flow from upstream intake gates and oversees the city’s modest but essential sewer system. When trade increases, water demand increases. When the docks are busy, waste increases. When disease threatens, sanitation becomes political.

And politics in Verbobonc always finds its way upward. Lord Mayor Marakios Willem Haxx understands this well. Infrastructure is leverage. Control the water, and you control the city’s health, its labor force, its trade capacity. The Tower answers administratively to City Hall. But in practice, its survival depends on House Haxx funding and oversight.

##### **Key Structural Features**

*   Five full stories plus a sub-level water control chamber
*   Rooftop cistern and gravity-fed distribution system
*   Canal access for water testing and waste control
*   Subterranean sluice tunnels connect to sewer grates
*   Reinforced stone foundations to withstand flooding
*   Direct coordination channel with W2 Harbormaster’s Office
