Mayaheine
=========

### The Shield Maiden

##### Mayaheine demigoddess of Protection, Justice, and Valor

##### Announcement for the Mayaheine Article

Please be aware that in the context of our Verbobonc campaign set in the year 576 CY, the demigoddess Mayaheine does not officially exist within the pantheon, as her first recorded appearance and divine ascension occur in 583 CY, following the events of the Greyhawk Wars. This article on Mayaheine has been prepared with future campaigns in mind, specifically for Dungeon Masters and players who may choose to explore the realms of Greyhawk in the aftermath of the year 583 CY. For those running campaigns set in the year 576 CY, the inclusion of Mayaheine offers an enriching opportunity to weave future narratives or to incorporate elements that align with the evolving lore of Greyhawk.

Mayaheine embodies the virtues of Protection, Justice, and Valor, serving as a beacon of light in a world threatened by darkness and evil. As a demigoddess in the pantheon of Greyhawk, her influence extends across battlefields and bastions, inspiring those who seek to uphold the forces of good. This article delves into the history, religion, culture, and lore surrounding Mayaheine, exploring her church's organization, the locations where she is worshipped, her clergy, and the political implications of her faith.

### History

*   **Ascension:** Mayaheine was once a mortal paladin of [Pelor](pelor-person.md), whose valorous deeds and unwavering commitment to justice elevated her to demigoddess status.
*   **Divine Mission:** Brought to the world of Greyhawk from another plane, her purpose is to combat darkness and evil alongside her followers.

### Religion

*   **Symbolism:** Mayaheine's holy symbol, a shield with a bastard sword surrounded by sunburst, signifies her role as a protector against evil.
*   **Doctrine:** The faith of Mayaheine emphasizes the virtues of protection, justice, and valor, encouraging her followers to act as beacons of light in the world.

#### Culture and Lore

*   **Divine Protector:** Mayaheine is depicted as a towering woman clad in battle attire, with her divine artifacts repelling evil and tyranny.
*   **Beliefs:** Her followers believe in the sanctity of protection, advocating for the defense of the innocent and the punishment of the wicked.

### Church Organization

*   **Clergy:** Clerics of Mayaheine dedicate themselves to training others in self-defense, leading community projects for protection, and serving as judges due to their intrinsic sense of justice.
*   **Paladins:** The church sponsors many paladins, known for their bravery and commitment to eradicating evil, often working alongside clerics of [Pelor](pelor-person.md) and paladins of other faiths.

#### Locations

*   **Worship Sites:** Temples and shrines dedicated to Mayaheine are found in fortified cities and along the frontiers, serving as bastions of hope and defense against encroaching darkness.

#### Clergy and Politics

*   **Community Leadership:** Mayaheine's clergy often hold positions of authority within communities, leveraging their divine mandate to enforce justice and protect the weak.
*   **Alliances:** The church maintains friendly relations with other faiths that share its ideals, particularly the followers of Pelor, [Heironeous](heironeous-person.md), and other deities of the Common pantheon.
