Lenny Valoran
=============

### Sir

> "I am a great Paladin, named Lenny the Turbulent. Of course you have heard of me!"

Lenny Valoran, also known as “Lenny the Turbulent,” is a prominent figure in the Viscounty of Verbobonc and beyond. A Paladin of [Heironeous](heironeous-person.md), Lenny embodies valor, justice, and an unyielding sense of duty. Known for his prowess on the battlefield and his overwhelming personality, Lenny is a complex character whose strengths and flaws make him a captivating addition to any campaign.

##### The Turbulent Paladin of Heironeous

Lenny is a striking figure who commands attention wherever he goes.

*   **Height and Build:** Standing over six feet tall, his lean but muscular frame is a testament to years of rigorous training.
*   **Features**: His long, flowing golden hair gleams like sunlight, framing an angular face with piercing amber eyes that burn with righteous fervor.
*   **Armor and Weaponry**: Lenny’s plate armor is polished to a mirror shine, adorned with the silver lightning bolt of Heironeous. He wields a finely-crafted longsword, the pommel set with a sapphire. His shield is emblazoned with the emblem of his god, symbolizing protection and justice.

_

> “The gods will be watching this battle, and I intend to give them a show.”

_

##### Personality

*   **Charismatic but Narcissistic:** Lenny’s charisma inspires devotion among his allies, but his inflated ego can alienate others.
*   **Chivalrous but Arrogant:** He upholds the virtues of honor and justice, often delivering dramatic speeches before combat, but his self-importance sometimes overshadows his noble deeds.
*   Zealous and Single-Minded: Lenny is deeply committed to the eradication of evil, sometimes to the point of recklessness.

_

> “Sometimes the gods need more than sermons; they demand a sword.”

_

### Background and Lore

Raised in the bustling City of Verbobonc, Lenny’s life has been shaped by devotion to Heironeous.

##### Early Life

*   Born to Oeridian merchants, Lenny showed an early aptitude for combat and leadership.
*   As a boy, he dreamed of knighthood, often reenacting battles with wooden swords.

##### Training and Ascension

*   He entered the Temple of Heironeous as an acolyte but quickly transitioned to the martial path when his temperament proved ill-suited for priesthood.
*   Mentored by the revered Abbot Rodolfus at the Abbey of the Valorous Knight, Lenny excelled in swordsmanship, strategy, and the sacred rites of the Archpaladin.

##### Rising Through the Order

*   Lenny’s prowess earned him a place in the Order of the Shining Sword, an elite cadre of paladins dedicated to eradicating evil and defending the innocent.
*   His dramatic exploits, including defeating a band of fiends and defending a village from marauding undead, earned him the moniker “Lenny the Turbulent.”

##### Role and Responsibilities

Lenny is not just a warrior but also a moral compass and a symbol of hope.

##### Champion of the People

*   Provides counsel and aid to the residents of Ostverk, often at the Laughing Moon Inn.
*   Mediates disputes and advises Captain Stone on law enforcement.

_

> “Heironeous walks beside me. Who can stand against us?”

_

##### Guardian of the Viscounty

*   Patrols the borders of Verbobonc, defending against bandits, cultists, and monstrous threats.
*   Protects holy sites of Heironeous and ensures the safety of pilgrimages.

##### Motivations and Philosophy

*   Devotion to Heironeous: Lenny views himself as the physical embodiment of his god’s virtues, striving to uphold justice and chivalry.
*   Crusade Against Evil: Driven by a desire to cleanse the world of malevolence, Lenny seeks out evildoers with unrelenting determination.
*   Quest for Legacy: Beneath his bravado lies a need to be remembered as a hero of legend.

#### Political Relationships

##### With the Viscounty of Verbobonc

*   Lenny maintains a tenuous relationship with the nobility, who find his self-righteous demeanor grating.
*   However, the common folk revere him as a champion of justice.

##### With Other Gods

*   Supports the allied faiths of Pelor and Rao, often coordinating efforts against shared threats.
*   Shares a bitter enmity with followers of [Hextor](hextor-person-1.md), the arch-enemy of Heironeous, and opposes cults of [Erythnul](erythnul-person-1.md) and [Vecna](vecna-person.md).

##### With the [9\. The Laughing Moon Inn](9-the-laughing-moon-inn-landmark.md) 

A frequent guest, Lenny enjoys engaging in philosophical debates with the innkeeper, Glarian, and other travelers.

#### Challenges and Threats

*   **Isolationist Policies:** The growing political tensions in Verbobonc hinder Lenny’s efforts to unify the region against external threats.
*   **Cultist Schemes:** Lenny is actively targeted by cults of Hextor and Vecna, who view him as a significant threat to their plans.
*   **Internal Struggles:** His narcissism and impulsive nature occasionally lead to friction with allies and superiors.

#### Campaign Role

Lenny is an invaluable NPC for Dungeon Masters running campaigns in the World of Greyhawk. His larger-than-life personality, moral complexities, and connections to key factions provide numerous opportunities for character interactions, quests, and dramatic moments.
