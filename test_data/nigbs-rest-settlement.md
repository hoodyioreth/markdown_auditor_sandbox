Nigb's Rest
===========

> You see a small village at a distance nestled on both sides of the Nigb just before the mounds of the Kron Hills. The farmlands have changed from grain and beans to unending rows of sun-drenched vinyards. Commoners in long sleeve blouses and wide brim straw hats pull small carts up and down multitude of parcels. You notice that the topsoil is a deep rich black. It seems the regular showers of the Lortmils makes this a very fertile land. Nigb's Rest, a picturesque township located 11 leagues south of the city of Verbobonc, lies along the Long Road and the [Nigb’s Run](nigbE28099s-run-location.md) river in the Asbury Demesne. This article delves into the township's detailed description, lore and history, background, motivations, and relationships, highlighting its significance within the [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md).

### Description

Nigb's Rest is a small village nestled in the middle of Cienega Valley on both sides of the Nigb's Run river, just before the mounds of the [Kron Hills](kron-hills-location.md). The farmlands have shifted from grain and beans to unending rows of sun-drenched vineyards. Commoners in long-sleeved blouses and wide-brimmed straw hats pull small carts up and down the multitude of parcels. The topsoil is a deep, rich black, nourished by regular showers from the Lortmils, making this a fertile land. Many vines here have been in the ground for half a century or more, with wineries being family-owned for more than a dozen generations. A large ornate stone bridge spans the river, accompanied by wood docks and a crane busy with trade. On the eastern side, a small church presides over the faithful from a knoll, while a large stone fort, resembling a Mounted Borderer fort, stands guard on a high ridge at the village's edge.

### History

Nigb's Rest gained prominence when travelers discovered that the soil was perfect for growing wine grapes. Lorien Vinter is credited with founding the Nigb's Rest Wine-Cooperative, a place for wine-growers to showcase their labor's fruits. The Cooperative, built where the trade road crosses the river, became a sturdy and essential part of the hamlet, reflecting the independent spirit of the local farmers and wine makers.

##### Key Historical Events

*   **Founding of the Wine-Cooperative:** Established by Lorien Vinter, the Cooperative became the focal point for the valley’s wine industry.
*   **Claim by** **Lord** [Simon Milinous](simon-milinous-person.md): Recently, Lord Milinous's claim over a portion of Lady Asbury's lands turned Nigb's Rest into a tense border town. This dispute was settled by an election in which Marcus Asbury was elected leader, maintaining the valley’s alignment with House Asbury.
*   **Election of** Marcus Asbury: Amidst tension, Marcus Asbury was elected, bringing some stability and reaffirming the valley’s loyalty to House Asbury.

### Geography

Nigb's Rest is characterized by rolling hills, fertile fields, and dense forests. The Long Road and Nigb's Run river are vital for trade and travel. The strategic location includes Fort Emridy, which offers a vantage point and enhances the valley’s defense. The presence of a natural area known as the [13\. Village Green](13-the-gallows-table-landmark.md), with its semi-tamed beauty and the consecrated grove dedicated to Lady Charmalaine, highlights the town’s commitment to preserving its natural surroundings amidst growth.

### Society and Culture

The community of Nigb's Resty is tight-knit, with a strong sense of tradition and adherence to [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md). Festivals and rituals celebrating the agricultural cycle are common, reflecting the agrarian roots of the town. The influence of deities such as Beory and Obad-Hai is significant, with the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) also playing a vital role in the spiritual life of the inhabitants.

##### Cultural Highlights

*   **Festivals and Rituals:** Celebrations tied to the agricultural calendar, honoring Beory and Obad-Hai.
*   **Village Green:** A central gathering place for feasts, town announcements, and ceremonies, maintaining its natural beauty thanks to the efforts of a wandering druid.

#### Government and Laws

Nigb's Rest is under the jurisdiction of House Asbury, with Lady Asbury at the helm. The local governance is supported by a contingent of house guardsmen and the Mounted Borderers. The Vigil Wardens of Verbobonc ensure law and order, upholding the viscounty’s legal framework.

##### Governance Structure

*   **Lady** [Elinor Asbury](elinor-asbury-person.md): Head of House Asbury, overseeing local governance.
*   [Marcus Greensward](marcus-greensward-person.md) : Recently elected leader of Nigb's Rest, maintaining the town's allegiance to House Asbury.
*   Asbury Guard and [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md): Key enforcers of law and order, ensuring peace and security.

### Politics

The political landscape is shaped by the ongoing rivalry between House Asbury and House Milinous. The recent dispute over land claims brought the valley into the political spotlight, with tensions still simmering beneath the surface despite the resolution through elections. The presence of the Gnome Free Alliance of the Kron Hills adds another layer of complexity, particularly concerning trade and resource management.

##### Political Dynamics

*   [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md) vs. [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md): A rivalry impacting local governance and stability.
*   Gnome [Free Alliance of the Kron Hills](free-alliance-of-the-kron-hills-organization.md): Influences trade and resource management, contributing to the valley’s economic and political complexity.

#### Religion

Religion is central to life in Nigb's Rest, with [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md) and the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) being the primary influences. The Old Faith, particularly the worship of [Beory](beory-person.md) and [Obad-Hai](obad-hai-person.md), is integral to the town’s traditions and rituals. The Church of St. Cuthbert emphasizes law and order, complementing the spiritual landscape.

##### Religious Practices

*   [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md): Worship of Beory and Obad-Hai, deeply rooted in agricultural practices.
*   [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md): Advocates for law and order, playing a significant role in community life.

#### Military

The military presence in Nigb's Rest is robust, anchored by Fort Emridy and the [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md). These forces ensure the town's defense and maintain order. The fort serves as a training ground and strategic outpost, reflecting the valley’s importance in regional defense.

##### Military Units

*   [1\. Fort Emridy](1-fort-emridy-landmark-1.md): A strategic military outpost, key to local defense.
*   **Mounted Borderers**: Elite military unit ensuring safety and law enforcement.
*   **House Guardsmen:** Supporting local governance and security under Lady Asbury's command.

#### Adventures and Points of Interest

Nigb's Rest offers numerous opportunities for adventure and exploration. Key locations include Fort Emridy, the Iron Hearth Forge, and the Village Green. These sites provide rich backdrops for quests and interactions, reflecting the town’s vibrant history and dynamic present.

##### Key Locations

*   [1\. Fort Emridy](1-fort-emridy-landmark-1.md): Military outpost with opportunities for exploration and defense missions.
*   [4\. The Iron Hearth Forge](4-the-iron-hearth-forge-landmark.md): A smithy in need of a blacksmith, presenting potential quests for skilled characters.
*   [13\. Village Green](13-the-gallows-table-landmark.md): Central gathering place with the Hearth of the Lady Charmalaine, a consecrated grove highlighting the town's natural beauty and spiritual significance.

#### Conclusion

Nigb's Rest, with its rich history, strategic importance, and vibrant community, offers a dynamic and immersive setting within the Viscounty of Verbobonc. From political intrigue and religious devotion to military strategy and agricultural prosperity, this township encapsulates the diverse and complex nature of life in the region, providing a compelling backdrop for any campaign.
