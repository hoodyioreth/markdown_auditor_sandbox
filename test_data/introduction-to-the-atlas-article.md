Introduction to the Atlas
=========================

The atlas is intended as a place to explore and write about your physical locations, like universes, galaxies, solar systems, planets, continents, islands, mountain ranges, forests, rivers, cities, ruins, buildings other important landmarks of your world.

It might make sense to keep all the locations together in one category, but as you add more locations you should find your own style that works best for you!

> ### Tips & Tricks: Artemis (Quick Navigation)
> 
> *   Press CTRL + Enter to activate the Artemis quick navigator.
> 
> *   Write "edit" (or just "e"), space, and the name of any of your articles to go to the edit page.
> 
> *   Write "list" (or just "l"), space, and then "articles", "maps", "timelines" etc. to go to your list of items in less than a second.
>
