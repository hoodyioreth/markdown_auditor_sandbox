Stout
=====

Delving into the essence of the Stout Halflings, it's imperative to appreciate their rich history, unique culture, and distinctive characteristics that set them apart within the diverse world of Greyhawk. Known for their robustness and a fondness for craftsmanship, Stouts carve a distinct identity in the tapestry of halfling sub-races.

### Introduction

Stouts, akin to their Hairfoot cousins, play a vital role in the societal fabric of Greyhawk. Despite their relative scarcity compared to the Hairfeet, Stouts have made their presence known through their resilience, craftsmanship, and remarkable longevity. Drawing inspiration from Tolkien's Stoors, Stouts offer a blend of halfling cheerfulness with a dwarvish penchant for craftsmanship and sturdiness.

### Society and Community Life

*   **Segregation and Companionship:** Stouts show a preference for segregation from human society, finding solace and companionship among dwarves. This relationship is characterized by mutual respect, military alliances, and flourishing trade.
*   **Dwelling Preferences:** Stouts predominantly reside in underground burrows in hilly or rocky regions. These abodes, cooler and somewhat damper than those of Harfeet, reflect the Stout's affinity for subterranean living.
*   **Economic Pursuits:** Stouts excel in various trades, including jewelry, stonemasonry, smithing, and particularly mining. Their communities often thrive on the extraction and trade of minerals, showcasing their industrious nature.

#### Distinctive Traits

*   **Industriousness:** Renowned as the most industrious among halflings, Stouts are capable of accomplishing substantial work swiftly. Their craftsmanship in metalwork and stonework often rivals that of their dwarven allies.
*   **Military Prowess:** In addition to their craftsmanship, Stouts are formidable soldiers, utilizing their darkvision and agility to excel in nocturnal warfare. Their expertise in swimming and boating further amplifies their tactical versatility.

#### Conclusion

Stout Halflings of Greyhawk embody a unique blend of halfling joviality and dwarvish resilience. Their robust nature, skilled craftsmanship, and close-knit communities contribute significantly to the rich cultural mosaic of Greyhawk. As defenders, artisans, and unwavering friends to dwarves, Stouts continue to forge their legacy across the lands, rivers, and under the hills of this vibrant world.

##### Physical Characteristics

*   **Stature and Build:** Stouts stand just shy of 3 feet tall but possess a stocky build, with a body mass significantly greater than that of Hairfeet. This robustness lends them an innate muscular strength, setting the stage for their prowess in physical contests.
*   **Complexion and Hair:** With ruddy complexions, Stouts easily blush or flush, adding to their expressive nature. Their hair color spectrum includes blondes and sandy reds, while their eyes shimmer in shades of blue, grey, and green.
*   **Facial Hair:** Uniquely among halflings, male Stouts can grow facial hair, primarily thick sideburns or muttonchops, with the occasional rare moustache being a source of great pride.

##### Clothing and Appearance

*   **Practical Attire:** Favoring durability over flair, Stouts predominantly wear leather-based clothing suited to their terrain. However, they cherish having at least one brightly colored outfit for special occasions.
*   **Footwear:** Unlike other halfling sub-races, Stouts regularly don boots resembling robust moccasins, safeguarding their feet across diverse terrains.
