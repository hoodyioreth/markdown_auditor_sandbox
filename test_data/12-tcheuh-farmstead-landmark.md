12\. Tcheuh Farmstead
=====================

Tcheuh Farmstead is a robust farming household located in the center of Hommlet by the Nigb's Run. Home to the formidable Auld Tcheuh and his large family, the farm is known for its hearty crops and strong [Flannae Flan](flannae-flan-ethnicity.md) lineage. The Tcheuh family plays a significant role in the community, contributing to both the village's defense and its agricultural output.

### Description

Situated by the serene [Nigb’s Run](nigbE28099s-run-location.md), the Tcheuh Farmstead is a large and bustling farmstead at the heart of Hommlet. The farm comprises sprawling fields of crops, a sturdy farmhouse, and a barn for livestock. The proximity to the river provides ample water for irrigation, making it one of the more fertile plots in the village.

##### Farmhouse

The Tcheuh farmhouse is a rustic two-story structure made of stone and timber, with a thatched roof. It exudes a welcoming aura, often filled with the sounds of family life and the aroma of hearty meals.

##### Fields and Livestock

The fields are well-tended, yielding crops such as turnips, carrots, parsnips, and brussels sprouts. A few cattle and goats roam the pasture near the barn, adding to the farm's productivity.

### Lore and History

The Tcheuh family has lived in Hommlet for generations, embodying the Flan heritage through their strong builds and close ties to the land. Auld and Wagh's participation in the siege of the moathouse seven years ago is a point of pride for the family.

##### Historical Significance

*   **Siege of the Moathouse:** After [The Battle of Emridy Meadows](the-battle-of-emridy-meadows-myth.md) both Auld and Wagh fought valiantly in the siege, earning respect within the community for their bravery and skill.
*   **Farming Legacy:** The Tcheuh farmstead has been a cornerstone of Hommlet's agricultural success, providing vital produce for the village and surrounding areas.

### Motivation and Relationships

##### Auld Tcheuh

*   **Motivation:** Auld is motivated by his pride in his family and his role in the community. He thrives on recounting tales of past battles and maintaining the farm's prosperity.
*   **Relationships:** Auld has a strong bond with his family and a camaraderie with fellow militia members and villagers, especially at the [7\. The Inn of the Welcome Wench](7-the-inn-of-the-welcome-wench-landmark.md).

##### Wagh Tcheuh

*   **Motivation:** Wagh is driven by a longing for adventure and a desire to explore beyond the confines of Hommlet. He is, however, deeply committed to his family and hesitant to leave them.
*   **Relationships:** As a student of Canoness Y’dey, Wagh has honed his mind and martial skills. His relationship with his family is strong, though his aspirations often lead to internal conflict.

#### Religion

The Tcheuh family are steadfast followers of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md), honoring the cycles of nature and the wisdom of their ancestors. Despite Wagh's interest in the teachings of [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md), the family maintains traditional practices and rituals.

### Current Events

##### Farm Activities

The Tcheuh Farmstead is in full swing, with the family working tirelessly to manage the fields and livestock. Their produce is in high demand at the local market, and they are known for their fresh, high-quality vegetables.

##### Militia Involvement

Both Auld and Wagh remain active members of the militia, prepared to defend Hommlet should the need arise. Their experience and skills are invaluable assets to the village's defense strategy.

##### Family Treasure

[Show spoiler](#spoiler-vaksrn)

Hidden among dried flowers in a clay pot, the Tcheuh family treasure consists of 26 gold pieces and a gold ring valued at 150 gp. This secret stash is kept safe within their home, a testament to their humble but prudent lifestyle.

#### Conclusion

Tcheuh Farmstead represents the heart and resilience of Hommlet. With its rich history, dedicated family, and unwavering commitment to both community and cultivation, the farmstead stands as a pillar of strength amidst the challenges facing the village. As the Tcheuhs continue to balance their roles as farmers and defenders, their contributions to Hommlet remain invaluable.

### Tcheuh Family

##### Auld Tcheuh

**Age:** 42

*   **Role:** Head of the Tcheuh family and a farmer.
*   **Appearance:** Auld is a large, muscular man with sun-weathered skin, brown hair, and a booming voice. He often wears practical farming clothes and, when needed, dons chainmail armor.
*   **Personality:** Boisterous and proud, Auld is known for his tales of bravery and feats of strength. He is a reliable and dedicated member of the militia.

##### Sahny Tcheuh

**Age:** 44

*   **Role:** Auld's wife and the heart of the household.
*   **Appearance:** Sahny is a sturdy woman with a kind face, often seen wearing simple dresses and an apron.
*   **Personality:** Caring and nurturing, she manages the household with efficiency and grace.

##### Wagh Tcheuh

**Age:** 20

*   **Role:** Auld's son and aspiring adventurer.
*   **Appearance:** Wagh is a burly young man with keen eyes and a determined expression. He wears leather armor adorned with the symbol of [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md).
*   **Personality:** Thoughtful and introspective, Wagh is torn between his duty to the farm and his desire for adventure.
