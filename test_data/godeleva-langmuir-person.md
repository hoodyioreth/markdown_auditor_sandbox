Godeleva Langmuir
=================

### Lady (a.k.a. "Iron Lady")

House Langmuir stands as a pillar of tradition and power within the Viscounty of Verbobonc, marked by its historical lineage and the formidable presence of its current matriarch, Lady Godeleva Langmuir, also known as the "Iron Lady." Despite the outward appearance of solidarity, the house navigates complex internal and external political landscapes, striving to maintain its influence amid rivalry and intrigue.

### Historical Background

House Langmuir traces its origins back to the Vice Royalty of Ferrond, established by Diptherius Langmuir, a relative of the ruling family in Furyondy. Over the centuries, the house has played a pivotal role in shaping the political and social fabric of Verbobonc.

*   **Founding Era:** House Langmuir was established during the formative years of Verbobonc’s establishment, gaining a foothold through strategic marriages and alliances.
*   **Rise to Power:** The house rose to prominence by playing a pivotal role in local governance and securing influential marriages.
*   **Historical Conflicts:** House Langmuir has a history of rivalries with other noble families and involvement in regional politics, often at odds with Viscount [Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) due to historical claims and disputes.

#### Personality of Lady Godeleva Langmuir

Lady Godeleva Langmuir, known as the "Iron Lady," is a formidable and shrewd leader.

*   **Physical Description:** Godeleva is an imposing figure, with a stern countenance that reflects her reputation.
*   **Personality Traits:** She is known for her tactical acumen, resolve, and ability to command respect and fear. Godeleva is a master manipulator, using her influence to navigate complex political landscapes.

### Political Influence

House Langmuir wields significant political influence through a combination of tradition, strategic alliances, and shrewd maneuvering.

*   **Support for the Viscount:** Despite internal ambitions, House Langmuir publicly supports Viscount [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) and pays its taxes in full and on time.
*   **Strategic Alliances:** Godeleva is actively pursuing alliances in [Veluna](veluna-organization.md) , balancing these ambitions with public loyalty to Verbobonc.
*   **Internal Power Dynamics:** The real power within the house lies with Godeleva, who deftly manages both internal and external politics to her advantage.
*   **Strategic Maneuvering:** Godeleva’s political maneuvers in Veluna reflect her ambitions to expand the house’s influence beyond Verbobonc.

> _"In the game of politics, cunning and intellect triumph over mere strength."_

#### Estates and Holdings

House Langmuir's estates reflect their enduring legacy and wealth.

*   **Langmuir Estate:** Located in [Etterboek](etterboek-settlement-1.md) , the estate is a sprawling complex with robust stone constructions and elaborate gardens, symbolizing their readiness to preserve heritage and adapt to new challenges.
*   **City Manor:** House Langmuir also maintains a [C17 Lord Langmuir’s Manor](c17-lord-langmuirE28099s-manor-landmark.md) within the [C1-16 Civic Centre](c1-16-civic-centre-settlement.md) of Verbobonc City, facilitating their involvement in urban affairs and trade.

#### Relationships

House Langmuir maintains a network of relationships that balance alliances and rivalries.

*   [House Haxx](house-haxx-organization.md): Maintains a wary but respectful relationship due to differing political views.
*   [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md): Strained relations due to historical conflicts and differing military strategies.
*   [House Chondell](house-chondell-organization.md): Cooperative in economic and trade matters, benefiting from mutual interests.
*   **Viscount** [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md): Publicly loyal, though underlying tensions exist due to historical disputes and Godeleva’s ambitions.

#### Current Affairs

House Langmuir is actively engaged in various political and economic activities.

*   **Veluna Alliances:** Godeleva is currently tantalizing factions in [Veluna](veluna-organization.md) with prospects of alliance, a move that has made her unpopular in some Verbobonc circles.
*   **Public Support:** Despite her maneuvers, Godeleva ensures that House Langmuir maintains a public face of support for Viscount Wilfrick, securing their position within Verbobonc.

#### Godeleva Langmuir's Influence and Tactics

Lady Godeleva Langmuir, known as the "_Iron Lady_," is the driving force behind House Langmuir's strategies and ambitions.

*   **Tantalizing Factions:** Godeleva is currently engaging factions in Veluna with prospects of alliance, playing a dangerous game with objectives known only to her.
*   **Public Face of Support:** Despite her secretive maneuvers, she ensures House Langmuir maintains a public face of support for Viscount [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md), allowing her significant latitude in her actions.
*   **Control over Ludovic:** Rumors suggest that [Ludovic Langmuir](ludovic-langmuir-person.md), the nominal head of the house, is merely a puppet controlled by his mother. Godeleva’s influence ensures that the house’s strategies align with her vision.

#### Current Affairs

House Langmuir is actively engaged in various ongoing projects and political maneuvers.

*   **Veluna Alliances:** Godeleva’s maneuvers to secure alliances in Veluna are a testament to her strategic foresight.
*   **Economic Expansion:** Enhancing trade routes and leveraging their urban and rural holdings to boost economic influence.
*   **Political Maneuvering:** Balancing public loyalty to Viscount Wilfrick with secretive strategies to expand their power.

#### Conclusion

Lady Godeleva Langmuir and House Langmuir stand as formidable forces within the Viscounty of Verbobonc. Through strategic alliances, economic power, and shrewd political maneuvering, they maintain a delicate balance of influence and ambition. Under the leadership of the "Iron Lady," House Langmuir continues to navigate the complexities of Verbobonc’s political and economic landscape with resilience and cunning.

##### Secret Political Plans

Lady Godeleva Langmuir is not only a master manipulator but also a traitor whose ambitions threaten the stability of the Viscounty.

*   **Viscount's Awareness:** Viscount [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) has begun employing old friends from his shadowy past to act as his eyes and ears, uncovering the true loyalties of his nobles.
*   [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md): The Viscount is aware that Lord Milinous’ allegiance is waning.
*   **House Langmuir:** The Viscount suspects Lady Godeleva's lack of support but does not yet realize she is a traitor.
*   **Strategic Moves:** House Langmuir, under Godeleva’s manipulation, sees an opportunity to gain power by weakening House Milinous and [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md).

##### Marriage to House Haxx

Lady Godeleva’s plans extend to marital alliances, seeking to strengthen her position through a marriage arrangement with House Haxx.

*   [Savra Haxx](savra-haxx-person.md): Godeleva has discussed a marriage between her son Ludovic Langmuir and Savra, the daughter of Lord [Marakios Willem Haxx](marakios-willem-haxx-person.md).
*   **Savra’s Rebellion:** Savra finds the arrangement insufferable and runs away to join the Feathergale Society, following Thurl Merosska to Feathergale Spire.
*   **Godeleva’s Response**: Enraged by Savra's defection, Godeleva will stop at nothing to retrieve her, even hiring mercenaries or adventurers to bring her back.
