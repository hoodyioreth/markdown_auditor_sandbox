Thornblade Rocksplitter
=======================

### Clan Elder of Rockhall and Priest of Moradin

Thornblade Rockspitter stands as a pillar of strength and tradition within the Dwarven community of Rockhall, nestled within the rugged terrains of the [Lortmil Mountains](lortmil-mountains-location.md). As the revered Clan elder of Clan Rockhall and a devout priest of [Moradin](moradin-person.md), Thornblade embodies the stoic virtues and leadership qualities that have long defined the noble lineages of his people. His life and leadership are deeply interwoven with the spiritual and cultural fabric of his clan.

### Biography

Thornblade Rockspitter, son of Ulgar the Defender and Gunleada, a priestess of [Berronar Truesilver](berronar-truesilver-person.md), was born into the esteemed 4th house of Ulthak-Nor, a lineage known for its brave warriors and wise priests. From a young age, he was steeped in the rich history of [Clan Rockhall](clan-rockhall-organization.md), a knowledge that has profoundly shaped his worldview and leadership.

##### Early Life and Education

Raised in the shadow of the ancient citadel of Ulthak-Nor, Thornblade was educated in the deep lore of the [Dwarves](dwarves-species.md), learning the sacred rites of Moradin under the tutelage of his mother. His father, a celebrated defender of Rockhall, instilled in him a sense of duty and resilience.

##### Clerical Ascendancy

Thornblade’s devotion and fervor in his worship of Moradin led him to ascend as a priest. His spiritual guidance has been pivotal in upholding the clan's traditions and in the continual blessing of their endeavors, be it in mining, crafting, or defending their homestead.

### Leadership and Legacy

As leader of [Clan Rockhall](clan-rockhall-organization.md), Thornblade Rockspitter has seen his people through times of prosperity and peril. His leadership is characterized by his frankness and strategic mindset, attributes that have earned him deep respect both within and beyond his clan.

##### Key Achievements

*   **Spiritual Guidance:** His role in reviving and maintaining the worship practices of Moradin has reinvigorated the clan’s spiritual life.
*   **Defender of the Realm:** Under his leadership, Rockhall has fortified its defenses, ensuring the safety and stability of his people.

#### Political Influence and Leadership in Clan Rockhall

Thornblade Rockspitter, as the Clan elder of Rockhall and a dedicated priest of Moradin, holds a pivotal role not only in spiritual and cultural spheres but also in the intricate politics of his Clan. His leadership comes during a critical period in Clan Rockhall's history, marked by endeavors to reclaim lost territories and fortify against external threats.

##### The Strategic Role of Thornblade Rockspitter

Thornblade's leadership extends beyond religious duties, encompassing significant political and strategic responsibilities vital to the survival and prosperity of Clan Rockhall. His deep-rooted knowledge of dwarven lore and history, combined with his spiritual guidance, makes him a cornerstone of the clan's resilience and determination.

##### Reclaiming Ancestral Lands

Under Thornblade's guidance, Rockhall has faced the daunting challenge of reclaiming its ancient lands, which were lost after the inadvertent release of an ancient evil five centuries ago. This quest has not only been a physical battle but also a spiritual journey, restoring the clan's honor and rightful place in the Lortmil Mountains.

##### Alliances and Diplomacy

Recent times have seen Thornblade overseeing diplomatic endeavors, such as the negotiations with Prince Corond of Ulek. These talks aim to gather support for Ulek's reclamation efforts against the orcish hordes of Turrosh Mak in the [Pomarj](pomarj-organization.md), reflecting Rockhall's readiness to extend their influence and aid to neighboring regions in mutual struggles.

#### Conclusion

Thornblade Rockspitter remains a central figure in the daily life of Rockhall, embodying the virtues of a priest and a leader. His life’s work continues to ensure that the stone of the mountain remains solid under the feet of his people, as he carries forth the legacy of the Rockspitters and the divine guidance of Moradin.
