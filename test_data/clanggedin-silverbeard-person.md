Clanggedin Silverbeard
======================

**Clanggedin Silverbeard, god of Battle, War  
**_The Father of Battle, Lord of the Twin Axes, the Giantkiller, the Goblinbane, the Wyrmslayer, the Rock of Battle_   Clangeddin Silverbeard is a central figure within the dwarven pantheon, epitomizing the martial spirit and indomitable courage of the dwarves. Known as the Father of Battle, he is worshipped as a god of war, valor, and strategic warfare. Clangeddin's followers idolize him for his unwavering bravery and his expertise in combat, especially against giants. His presence is a rallying point for dwarves across all clans, urging them to embrace the call of battle as a noble endeavor.

#### The Warrior's Form

_Physical Manifestation  
_Clangeddin is depicted as a robust, middle-aged dwarf with a shining silver beard and a bald head, symbolizing both wisdom and battle-hardened experience. He typically wears chain mail and wields paired battle axes, a testament to his readiness for combat at any moment. His demeanor is fierce yet jubilant, always found smiling amidst the chaos of battle, reveling in the test of strength and strategy.

#### Relationships and Political Intrigue

_Divine Alliances and Rivalries, Ancestral Bonds_

*   **Allies:** within the dwarven pantheon include [Moradin](moradin-person.md), [Berronar Truesilver](berronar-truesilver-person.md), and [Dugmaren Brightmantle](dugmaren-brightmantle-person.md), among others.
*   **Enemies:** Clangeddin's most significant adversary is Grolantor, the hill giant god, highlighting a perpetual conflict against threats to dwarfkind.
*   **Mutual Respect:** He enjoys a mutual respect with gods who favor the strategies of war and defense, extending occasional support to non-dwarven deities aligned with his principles.

#### The Creed of Battle: Clangeddin's Dogma

_Core Teachings  
_Clangeddin’s dogma revolves around the sanctity of combat and the importance of facing adversity with courage and strategic insight. He teaches:

*   The moral duty to protect dwarfkind through strength of arms.
*   That true honor in battle is achieved through valor and the willingness to face great risks.
*   Encouragement of tactical knowledge and the continuous preparation for warfare as a way of life.

#### Defenders of the Faith: Worshipers of Clangeddin

_Community and Devotion_

*   Predominantly worshipped by dwarf warriors, blacksmiths, and strategists.
*   Viewed as a cultural icon in dwarven society, embodying the highest ideals of courage and endurance.
*   His followers often hold significant military or leadership roles within dwarf communities, acting as the primary defenders against external threats.

### Battle-Ready Ministry: The Clergy of Clangeddin

_Roles and Responsibilities  
_Known as the alaghor, Clangeddin’s clergy are both spiritual leaders and warrior trainers. They wear silver war helms and chain mail, symbolizing their readiness for battle. Clerics are often involved in the planning and execution of military campaigns and are revered as elite members of dwarf society.

#### Strongholds of Valor: Temples of Clangeddin

_Sacred Grounds  
_

*   Temples are fortified structures, often situated in strategic locations to serve both religious and military functions.
*   These temples are equipped with armories and training grounds to prepare dwarves for the rigors of combat.
*   Altars within these temples are typically adorned with symbols of battle, such as crossed axes or armor.

#### Rituals of Steel: Sacred Practices

_Ceremonial Acts_

*   Clangeddin is honored through rituals that mimic the preparations for war, including weapon consecrations and warrior blessings.
*   Before major battles, dwarves gather in his temples to offer prayers for strength and victory.
*   Weapons sacrificed in his name are believed to be imbued with divine favor, providing spiritual and physical reinforcement to his followers.

#### Conclusion

Through these facets of worship and devotion, Clangeddin Silverbeard continues to inspire and protect his followers, ensuring that the spirit of battle and the defense of the dwarven people remain steadfast and unyielding.

##### Clerical titles for Clangeddin Silverbeard's clergy

*   **Unblooded:** The novices who are yet to prove themselves in battle.
*   **Axe Cutter:** Warriors who have demonstrated basic combat skills.
*   **Squire:** Apprentices to more experienced knights, learning the ways of war and worship.
*   **Knight of the Third Rank:** Initiated knights proving their valor and dedication to battle.
*   **Knight of the Second Rank:** Seasoned warriors recognized for bravery and tactical acumen.
*   **Knight of the First Rank:** Elite knights who have shown extraordinary prowess and leadership.
*   **Knight Commander:** Leaders of military orders, overseeing multiple knights and strategic operations.
*   **Axe Brother:** Veteran warriors deeply respected for their wisdom and battle experience.
*   **War Prince:** The highest rank, these are the legendary heroes and leaders of Clangeddin's clergy.
