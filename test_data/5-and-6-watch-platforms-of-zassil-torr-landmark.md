5\. & 6. Watch Platforms of Zassil’torr
=======================================

The lizardfolk village of Zassil’torr is surrounded by treacherous terrain and hidden dangers, yet its inhabitants remain ever-vigilant. Two watch platforms, strategically positioned on the village’s periphery, serve as essential structures for safeguarding the community from threats.

### Sentinels of the Swamp

The watch platforms are elevated structures built on sturdy stilts of swampwood, designed to rise above the dense foliage and murky waters. These platforms blend seamlessly into the environment, their reed-thatch roofs and woven walls camouflaged with vines and moss.   Each platform is large enough to accommodate three to four sentries, providing ample space for patrol rotations or additional defenders during an emergency. Ladders made from lashed-together branches lead up to the platforms, while below, the water teems with reeds and the occasional lurking predator.

### Role in Village Defense

The platforms serve as Zassil’torr’s first line of defense. Positioned at key vantage points overlooking the surrounding swamp, they offer unparalleled visibility, ensuring that no intruders approach unnoticed.

##### Primary Functions

*   **Surveillance:** Guards stationed atop the platforms watch for approaching dangers, including rival tribes, swamp predators, or foreign intruders.
*   **Alarm Systems:** Each platform is equipped with primitive yet effective signaling devices, such as:
*   Drums crafted from hollow logs stretched with swamp beast hide.
*   Reed-crafted horns that emit deep, resonating sounds across the swamp.
*   **Strategic Response:** The platforms allow sentries to relay information to the central village quickly, giving Zassil’torr time to mobilize its warriors.

### Construction and Features

##### Design and Build

*   **Materials**: Constructed from swampwood stilts driven deep into the marshy ground, reinforced with woven reed bindings.
*   **Roofing**: Reed-thatched coverings offer protection from rain and harsh sun.
*   **Flooring**: Slatted wooden planks allow water to drain through, reducing rot.

##### Unique Features

*   **Camouflage**: The platforms are adorned with natural swamp elements, making them nearly invisible to outsiders.
*   **Defensive Tools:** Small armories hold spears, bows, and quivers of crude arrows. A cauldron of swamp pitch is stored for potential use as a flaming deterrent.
*   **Escape Measures:** Each platform has a hidden slide rope leading into the swamp for quick evacuation.

#### Conclusion

The watch platforms of Zassil’torr stand as silent guardians of the swamp village, embodying the lizardfolk’s resilience and cunning. These elevated sentry posts are not only a physical barrier against threats but also a testament to the lizardfolk’s mastery of their environment. They offer a wealth of narrative opportunities for Dungeon Masters to weave into their campaigns.

### Guards and Operations

##### Rotating Shifts

The platforms are manned by two guards at all times, with rotations occurring every six hours. Guards are equipped with swamp-forged weapons and basic reed armor.

##### Personalities of the Sentries

**Gorrath (LG male lizardfolk)**

*   A diligent and stoic sentry, Gorrath views his post as a sacred duty.
*   Quote: _"Thessse eyeess see all. Outsidersss will not ssstep where they are unwelcome."_

**Skithra (CN female lizardfolk)**

*   Quick-witted and sharp-tongued, Skithra relishes the power of her vantage point.
*   Quote: _"Let the ssswamp protect itssself. I’ll enjoy watching them flail in the water firssst."_

##### Emergency Protocols

When an intruder is spotted, the sentries:

*   Sound an alarm (drumbeat for predators, horn for humanoid threats).
*   Signal the nearest hunting party or the central village.
*   Engage enemies only if outnumbered or outmatched.
