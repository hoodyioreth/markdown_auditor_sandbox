Religion in Verbobonc
=====================

### Verbobonc: A Tapestry of Faith

_An Ancient Bastion of Belief_

Verbobonc is a melting pot of diverse faiths and religious practices. The city's spiritual landscape is shaped by its inhabitants' devotion to various deities, each contributing to the cultural and political fabric of the region. This article covers the key religious institutions of the Viscounty, their historical significance, and the influence they wield in Verbobonc as of the year 576 CY.

For the DM, religion in Verbobonc is never merely spiritual. Every high priest is a political actor. Every temple is a power base. And when the Second Rising begins, each faith will be forced to choose where it stands — and what it is willing to sacrifice.

 

### Echoes of Time: A Sacred History

The religious history of Verbobonc is deeply intertwined with the city's development. From ancient elven practices centered around the [D7 Ash Horn Stream](d7-ash-horn-stream-landmark.md) to the more recent establishment of human-centric temples, each faith has left an indelible mark. Key historical events, such as [The Battle of Emridy Meadows](the-battle-of-emridy-meadows-myth.md) and the subsequent rise of [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md), have shaped the religious dynamics and power structures within the city.

 

#### Sanctified Grounds

_The Geography of Faith_

Verbobonc's religious sites are strategically located throughout the city and its surroundings. The [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md) dominates the business district, symbolizing the close ties between commerce and faith. In contrast, the Ash Horn Stream, nestled in the [D1 - D7 Elven Dawn Quarter](d1---d9-elven-dawn-quarter-settlement.md), reflects the naturalistic and harmonious aspects of elven worship. The [Church of Pelor](church-of-pelor-organization.md) and the [W4 Temple of the Velverdyva River](w4-temple-of-the-velverdyva-river-landmark.md) serve the faithful near the waterfront, offering blessings for safe voyages and prosperous trade.

 

#### Rituals of Daily Life

_Society and Culture_

Verbobonc's society is a mosaic of traditions and customs influenced by its religious diversity. Festivals, rituals, and daily practices reflect the teachings of various deities. The monthly processions at the Ash Horn Stream, the grand ceremonies at the [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md), and the seasonal celebrations at the [B20 Church of Pelor](b20-church-of-pelor-landmark.md) all contribute to the city's rich cultural tapestry. These religious practices foster a sense of community and continuity among the citizens.

 

#### Government and Laws

_Edicts and Judgments_

The governance of Verbobonc is a delicate balance of secular and religious influences operating across all four layers of the Viscounty's [government](the-government-of-verbobonc-organization.md). Religious leaders are not passive observers of civic life — they are embedded actors with specific access points into the system.

*   At **Castle Greyfist**, [Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) is advised directly by [Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md) of [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) on matters of justice and morality — the most direct clerical access to supreme executive authority in the Viscounty.
*   At **City Hall**, the [Church of Pholtus the One True Way](church-of-pholtus-the-one-true-way-organization.md) supplies many of the Advocates who argue before the Chamber of Thirteen. Pholtus's doctrine of strict legal adherence makes his clergy the natural inhabitants of the judicial machine.
*   At **the Gathering Hall**, temple delegations — Zilchus, Pelor, Trithereon, the river priests — appear regularly at public hearings to argue guild interests, advocate for vulnerable communities, or apply pressure on pending legislation.
*   Among **the Council of Lords**, every major noble house has a preferred confessor, patron saint, or temple alliance that shapes their political loyalties. See [Council of Lords of Verbobonc](the-council-of-lords-of-verbobonc-organization.md) for the full factional breakdown.

The [Vigil Wardens of Verbobonc](vigil-wardens-of-verbobonc-organization.md) and the Billets of St. Cuthbert collaborate to maintain order and uphold both civic and religious law. The city's laws reflect the moral and ethical codes of the dominant faiths — and when those faiths disagree, the law buckles.

 

The Faiths of Verbobonc
-----------------------

_Power and Piety_

The political landscape of Verbobonc is shaped by its religious institutions, each wielding significant influence. The [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md) plays a crucial role in economic and political negotiations, leveraging its wealth and connections to shape policy. The [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md), under [Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md), has a strong alliance with the Viscount, ensuring that its doctrines influence key decisions. Meanwhile, [The Order of Rao](the-order-of-rao-organization.md) and the [Church of Pholtus the One True Way](church-of-pholtus-the-one-true-way-organization.md) advocate for peace, order, and strict adherence to the law.

 

### Main Faith | [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md)

**Political Alignment:** Greyfist (executive), Militarist faction (Council), Vigil Wardens (law enforcement)

[Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md) holds significant sway within the Viscount's court, often advising [Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) on matters of justice and morality. His cooperation with the Vigil Wardens and the Mounted Borderers ensures a strong alliance between the church and the city's law enforcement.

*   **Dominant Religion:** The worship of [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md) stands as the cornerstone of religious life in Verbobonc.
*   **Temple:** A grand temple dedicated to St. Cuthbert, rivaling only the temple of Trithereon, has been erected in the heart of the commercial market of Verbobonc, reflecting the deity's burgeoning worship post the Elemental War.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Justice and Order:** Haufren is deeply committed to upholding law and order, viewing the rise of evil as a direct threat to the stability of Verbobonc.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** The Bishop is aware of the tensions between the gnome merchants and the human lords, advocating for fair treatment and protection of gnome caravans.
*   **Lost Delegation:** He is concerned about the lost diplomatic delegation from Veluna, fearing it could destabilize regional politics and peace.
*   **Druid's Gift:** Supports the Great Druid's efforts to send the 'Gift of Beauty' to Queen Yolande, recognizing the importance of maintaining good relations with the Fey Kingdom of Celene.

 

### Worship of [Church of Trithereon](church-of-trithereon-organization.md)

**Political Alignment:** Cultural Moderate faction (Council — Lord Rhynehurst), community self-defense, anti-authoritarian

Known for their advocacy of individual liberty and personal justice, the High Priest of Trithereon often stands in opposition to overly restrictive policies, promoting freedom. [Cornelius Arx](cornelius-arx-person.md), the High Priest of Trithereon's Cathedral, is revered for his wisdom, humor, and approachability. Following St. Cuthbert, the [Church of Trithereon](church-of-trithereon-organization.md) enjoys significant worship in Verbobonc.

*   **Community Empowerment:** Focus on teaching communities to depend on their own strength for protection.
*   **Collaboration and Caution:** They work with local militias, magistrates, and the [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md) but maintain a cautious stance towards official authorities.
*   **Relationship with Other Factions:** While they cooperate with [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md) and the Church of [Ehlonna](ehlonna-person.md), their activities are mostly autonomous.
*   **Presence in Verbobonc:** Established in Rhynehurst due to tensions with the Church of St. Cuthbert in the city, operating from a warehouse known as "Summoner's Keep." This geographic exile is itself a political statement — and why Lord [Wendell Rhynehurst](wendell-rhynehurst-person.md) is Trithereon's natural Council ally.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Liberty and Justice:** Committed to fighting oppression and injustice, seeing the rise of evil as a direct challenge to personal freedoms.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Supports the gnome merchants in their struggle for fair treatment, advocating for their right to trade freely.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the implications for personal freedoms and justice, supports efforts to locate the diplomats.

 

### Rao's Declining Worship

**Political Alignment:** Cultural Moderate faction (fragile alliance with Rhynehurst), peacemaking, neutral voice

Initially viewed with skepticism, Legeum's prudent alliances have somewhat restored Rao's standing in Verbobonc. He maintains delicate relationships with other religious leaders, advocating for peace and reason in times of conflict.

*   **Shift in Faith:** The worship of [Rao](rao-person.md) has dwindled, with many followers of [The Order of Rao](the-order-of-rao-organization.md) turning to St. Cuthbert during times of conflict.
*   **Patriarch [Martiolus Legeum](martiolus-legeum-person.md):** Although initially viewed with skepticism, Legeum's prudent alliances have somewhat restored Rao's standing in Verbobonc.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Peace and Reason:** Dedicated to promoting peace and reason, Legeum views any rise of evil as a direct threat to these principles.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Supports fair treatment and protection of gnome merchants, recognizing their role in maintaining economic stability.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the lost diplomatic delegation, understanding the potential for conflict and disruption of peace.
*   **Druid's Gift:** Supports the diplomatic gesture to Queen Yolande, seeing it as an essential step in fostering peace and understanding with Celene.

 

### Worship of Zilchus

**Political Alignment:** Economic Powers faction (Council — Galans, House Haxx, Vaswell), Gathering Hall influence, financial infrastructure

The Fillmyer brothers are key players in Verbobonc's economic and political scenes. They provide financial services to the nobility and merchants, and their opinions are highly valued in economic policy discussions. The church's presence in Verbobonc is significant, with clerics acting as currency exchangers, investment solicitors, merchandise appraisers, and moneylenders.

*   The church's headquarters in Verbobonc, [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md), the Office of Enumeration, is a hub for financial record-keeping and monitoring.
*   [Abrac Fillmyer](abrac-fillmyer-person.md) the High Enumerator, a short and thin man, serves as the High Priest of Zilchus in Verbobonc, renowned for his supernatural ability with finances.
*   The church's influence extends throughout Verbobonc's merchant quarters, with the Office of Enumeration serving as a critical financial institution.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Economic Prosperity:** Focused on ensuring Verbobonc's economic growth, they see the rise of evil and instability as major threats to trade and commerce.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** They have a pragmatic approach to the gnome merchant problems, often mediating disputes to keep trade flowing smoothly.

 

### Worship of Ehlonna

**Political Alignment:** Nature faction (allied with Old Faith and Gnarley Rangers), rural communities

The High Priestess of [Ehlonna](ehlonna-person.md) works closely with the druids of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md), promoting the harmony between nature and civilization. Her influence extends to rural communities, hunters, and rangers who seek to preserve natural beauty.

*   **Devotion to Nature:** Clerics of [Ehlonna](ehlonna-person.md) are deeply committed to protecting forests, woodlands, and the beauty of nature.
*   **Educational Role:** They see themselves as educators, spreading knowledge about the importance of flora, fauna, and natural ecosystems.
*   **Rivalries and Alliances:** Ehlonna has a rivalry with [Obad-Hai](obad-hai-person.md) but maintains friendly relations with elven deities and most good-aligned gods.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Nature and Balance:** Committed to maintaining the balance between civilization and nature, viewing any rise of evil as a threat to the natural world.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the potential disruption to natural and regional balance, supports efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats.
*   **Druid's Gift:** Strongly supports the diplomatic gesture to Queen Yolande, seeing it as a step towards reinforcing the bonds between nature and the Fey.

 

### Worship of Fharlanghn

**Political Alignment:** Economic Powers faction (intelligence network), road and trade infrastructure

Father Pilchus of [Fharlanghn](fharlanghn-person.md) leads the [Striders of Fharlanghn](striders-of-fharlanghn-organization.md) and maintains a network of connections with various travelers, merchants, and city officials, ensuring the needs of itinerant clerics and travelers are met. His influence is subtle but profound, affecting many aspects of daily life in Verbobonc.

*   **Clergy Composition:** Clerics of Fharlanghn comprise wandering clerics in green and settled clerics in brown. Wandering clerics travel and bless journeys; settled clerics minister to those on the roads.
*   **Duties and Beliefs:** Clerics are encouraged to explore, assist in construction of roads and bridges, and act as translators and diplomats. They are defenders of travelers and oppose any force that restricts freedom of movement.
*   **Church Activities:** Involves blessing caravans, exploring unknown territories, updating maps, and serving as scouts. They also gather politically important information passively within Verbobonc.
*   **Church Structures:** No traditional churches, but chapterhouses that function like inns for priests and travelers, offering lodging, stables, and extensive map collections.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Guidance and Exploration:** Committed to aiding travelers and promoting the exploration of new horizons, viewing any rise of evil as an obstacle to freedom of movement and discovery.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Supports the fair treatment and protection of gnome merchants, recognizing their role in facilitating trade and exploration.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the impact on travel and diplomatic routes, supports efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats to ensure safe and open pathways.

 

### Worship of Heironeous

**Political Alignment:** Greyfist (martial legitimacy), Militarist faction (Council — Velysin, Milinous), military and knightly orders

The High Priest of Heironeous commands significant respect, especially among the city's military and defenders. Known for their valor and honor, the followers of Heironeous often collaborate with the city's knights and guards.

Abbot [Rodolfus](rodolfus-person.md) oversees the Abbey of the Valorous Knight in the Viscounty of Verbobonc. His efforts to form the Council of Abbots mark a significant move towards unified religious governance in the region.

[Tanner BrightBlade](tanner-brightblade-person.md) at the [B21 Temple of Heironeous](b21-temple-of-heironeous-landmark.md) in the city is more than just a military leader — he is a pivotal figure whose work impacts the fabric of Verbobonc's religious and secular communities.

##### Motivations and Concerns

*   **Valor and Honor:** Committed to upholding principles of valor, honor, and justice, viewing any rise of evil as a direct threat to these ideals.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Supports the protection and fair treatment of gnome merchants, recognizing their contributions to the city's economy and defense supplies.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Actively supports efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats, understanding the potential impact on regional security and alliances.

 

### Worship of Pholtus

**Political Alignment:** City Hall (judicial dominance — supplies the Advocates), law enforcement infrastructure

[Daton Brightblade](daton-brightblade-person.md) navigates the complex web of Verbobonc politics from a position of institutional strength. The High Priest of [Church of Pholtus the One True Way](church-of-pholtus-the-one-true-way-organization.md) holds a position of respect among the city's rulers due to the church's emphasis on law, order, and inflexibility. The church often collaborates with secular authorities to enforce justice and maintain public order. Many of the Advocates who argue before the Chamber of Thirteen at [C2 City Hall](c2-city-hall-landmark.md) are trained or sanctioned by the Church of Pholtus — giving the faith a direct institutional stake in civic law.

*   **Valued Advisor and Arbitrator:** Frequently called upon to arbitrate political matters.
*   **Law and Order Advocate:** Supports the Viscount's efforts to maintain law and order in the city.

**Church of St Cuthbert relationship**

*   **Collaborative Guardianships:** Collaborates with the eight guardianships anchored by abbeys of St. Cuthbert.
*   **Training Grounds for Cudgellers:** Supports the Vigil Wardens of Verbobonc in combating heresy and raiding evils.

**Church of Trithereon relationship**

*   **Philosophical Clash:** Clash between Trithereon's chaotic individualism and Pholtus' rigid doctrines.
*   **Base in Rhynehurst:** The Church of Trithereon strategically avoids direct contention in Verbobonc — but the tension remains.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Law and Order:** Committed to upholding Pholtus's strict doctrines, viewing any rise of evil and disorder as direct challenges that must be eradicated.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Advocates for the protection of gnome merchants, ensuring they adhere to strict trading laws while receiving fair treatment.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the implications for regional stability, supports efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats and ensure their safety.

 

### [Church of Pelor](church-of-pelor-organization.md)

**Political Alignment:** Gathering Hall (public trust, healing), moral advisory to the Viscount and local lords

The High Priest of Pelor [Charles Evertide](charles-evertide-person.md) is a respected figure, often consulted on matters of health, healing, and moral guidance. The [Church of Pelor](church-of-pelor-organization.md)'s influence extends into political realms, advocating for benevolence and light.

*   **Collaborative Beacon of Light:** Works with [Clerics and Inquisitors of Pelor](clerics-and-inquisitors-of-pelor-organization.md) to promote light and hope across Verbobonc.
*   **Inquisitors and Doctrinal Purity:** Supports Pelor's inquisitors in maintaining doctrinal purity.
*   **Liaison Functions:** Acts as a moral and ethical advisor to the Viscount and local lords, influencing governance with the teachings of Pelor.
*   **Support and Influence:** The church's support is often sought in civic matters, from social welfare programs to legislative counsel.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Healing and Benevolence:** Dedicated to combating evil through healing and benevolence, seeing any rise of darkness as a direct opposition to Pelor's teachings.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Advocates for fair and compassionate treatment of gnome merchants, providing healing services to those in need.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Actively involved in the efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats, understanding the potential for broader conflict and suffering.

 

### Worship of Delleb

**Political Alignment:** Academic and advisory circles, intellectually essential but politically quiet

[The Order of the Enlightened Mind](the-order-of-the-enlightened-mind-organization.md) maintains strong ties with academic and intellectual circles, often advising on matters of reason, knowledge, and governance. High Priestess [Julia Fairfriend](julia-fairfriend--person.md) of [Delleb](delleb-person.md) is dedicated to promoting reason and knowledge, viewing any rise of evil as a threat to enlightenment and intellectual freedom. Her influence extends beyond the spiritual realm, as she leverages her position to advocate for the importance of education and knowledge in the city's governance.

*   **Gnome Merchants:** Advocates for the protection and fair treatment of gnome merchants, recognizing their contributions to knowledge and trade.
*   **Lost Delegation:** Concerned about the implications for diplomatic and intellectual exchange, supports efforts to locate the Velunan diplomats.

 

### Temple of the Velverdyva River

**Political Alignment:** Economic Powers faction (maritime commerce), Harbormaster relations

Cedric Talus, Nadia M'karr, and Leif Vand of the [W4 Temple of the Velverdyva River](w4-temple-of-the-velverdyva-river-landmark.md) hold strong ties with the maritime community and the Office of the Harbormaster. They also maintain influence over merchants and sailors, ensuring their voices are heard in city governance.

**Positions:** Priests of Xerbo, Geshtai, and Osprem respectively

*   [Xerbo](xerbo-person.md) is the Suel god of the Sea, Sailing, Money, and Business.
*   [Osprem](osprem-person.md) is the Suloise goddess of sea voyages, ships, and sailors.
*   Geshtai is the Baklunish god of Lakes, Rivers, Wells, and Streams.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Maritime Safety and Prosperity:** Each priest is committed to ensuring the safety and prosperity of Verbobonc's maritime activities. The rise of evil is seen as a threat to safe voyages and trade.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** They advocate for the protection of gnome merchant caravans, understanding their integral role in maritime commerce.

 

### Worship of Merikka

**Political Alignment:** Rural agricultural communities, farm village governance

The High Priest of [Merikka](merikka-person.md) works closely with agricultural communities and has significant influence over rural policies and practices. There are no chapels within the City of Verbobonc but there are small shrines throughout the farm villages of the Viscounty.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Agriculture and Fertility:** Focused on ensuring the prosperity of farming and rural communities, seeing any rise of evil as a threat to these values.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Supports fair trade practices that benefit agricultural communities and gnome merchants alike.
*   **Conflict with the Old Faith:** The Oeridian High Priest of Merikka often clashes with the druids of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md). The focus on agriculture and maximizing crop yields frequently leads to environmental changes that the druids view as harmful.

 

### Worship of the Old Faith

**Political Alignment:** Rural Viscounty, allied with Viscount through St. Cuthbert, nature faction

The druids maintain a cautious but influential relationship with the political leaders, focusing on the balance of nature and the protection of ancient traditions.

*   **Foundational Beliefs:** [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md) venerates natural spirits, focusing on fertility, creation, protection, sustenance, and death, rooted in ancient Flan cults worshipping [Beory](beory-person.md) and [Obad-Hai](obad-hai-person.md).
*   **Historical Presence in Verbobonc:** The Old Faith was brought by early human settlers, with Druidic counselors advising clan chiefs and Druidic customs permeating ceremonies.
*   **Contemporary Practice:** Despite the Viscount being advised by [Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md) of St. Cuthbert, rural folk in the Viscounty still adhere to Old Faith practices, especially in farming and childbirth ceremonies.
*   **Integration with Local Governance:** The Viscount and the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) recognize the Old Faith's influence and have chosen to ally with its practitioners rather than oppose them, ensuring peace and mutual respect.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Balance of Nature:** Dedicated to maintaining the natural balance, viewing any rise of evil as a threat to the world's equilibrium.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** Advocate for the fair treatment of gnome merchants, recognizing their role in maintaining economic and ecological balance.
*   **Conflict with the Church of Merikka:** The Old Faith's druids often find themselves at odds with the Oeridian worshipers of [Merikka](merikka-person.md). The druids prioritize the protection of nature, opposing extensive agricultural practices that disrupt the natural landscape.
*   **Druid's Gift:** Strong proponents of sending the 'Gift of Beauty' to Queen Yolande, seeing it as a crucial step in maintaining the natural and political balance.

 

### Demi-Human Deities

*   **Shrines:** Numerous shrines dedicated to gnomish and elven gods dot the city, managed by the respective communities.
*   **Integration:** These shrines, while primarily for demi-humans, are also frequented by humans, particularly those traveling through the Viscounty's diverse lands.

##### [Resheph](resheph-person-1.md), High Councilor of the Ash Horn Stream

**Position:** Druid and High Councilor of the [D7 Ash Horn Stream](d7-ash-horn-stream-landmark.md)

*   **Political Relationships:** Resheph works closely with the elven community and maintains a respectful but cautious relationship with the human leaders of Verbobonc. His counsel is sought on matters of nature and elven traditions.

**Motivations and Concerns**

*   **Nature and Tradition:** Dedicated to preserving elven traditions and the natural world, Resheph sees any rise of evil as a direct threat to these values.
*   **Gnome Merchants:** He advocates for the gnome merchants, recognizing their importance in maintaining a balanced and prosperous ecosystem.
*   **Druid's Gift:** Resheph is a strong proponent of sending the 'Gift of Beauty' to Queen Yolande, viewing it as a crucial step in reinforcing the bonds between the elves and their Fey kin.

 

### The Glittering Grotto of the Golden Hills

**Political Alignment:** Gathering Hall (gnome rights advocacy), Free Alliance of the Kron Hills

**High Priest:** Glimmer Gemheart

Located in the [G1 - G8 Gnomesberg Gnome Quarter](g1-10-gnomesberg-gnome-quarter-settlement.md), the Glittering Grotto of the Golden Hills is dedicated to the Gnome Pantheon. High Priest Glimmer Gemheart, known for his wisdom and resilience, leads this sacred sanctuary. He tirelessly advocates for the rights of the gnome community, navigating complex political challenges.

*   **Human Distrust and Tensions:** Glimmer works tirelessly to counter stereotypes about gnomes, promoting understanding and cooperation between the communities.
*   **Conflict Over Gem Mines:** Glimmer staunchly defends the gnomes' ancestral rights to the Kron Hills gem mines, leveraging political alliances and rallying support from sympathetic factions.
*   **Withdrawal of Patrols:** The recent withdrawal of the Mounted Borderer Patrols has left the Kron Hills vulnerable. Glimmer is actively lobbying the Viscount to reinstate the patrols.
*   **Bandit Raids:** With increased bandit activity, Glimmer has organized additional protection for the caravans, employing gnome scouts and mercenaries to ensure safe passage.

 

### Shadow Faiths: Wee Jas and Hextor

Not all faiths in the Viscounty operate in the open. Two churches hold recognized status in the Flanaess but maintain a deliberately low profile in Verbobonc — and their quiet presence here is itself a campaign-relevant fact.

##### [Church of Wee Jas](church-of-wee-jas-organization-1.md)

**Political Alignment:** Manipulator faction (Council — Lady Langmuir), arcane nobility, long-game power

Wee Jas governs Magic, Death, Vanity, and Law. Her clergy — the Karuth — are drawn to arcane power, meticulous legal order, and the secrets of the afterlife. In Verbobonc, her followers cluster among the arcane nobility and those with access to old money and older knowledge. The church does not advertise itself. It does not need to. Its members are already in the rooms that matter.

**DM Note:** A noble with connections to Wee Jas is not necessarily an enemy — but they are playing a longer game than anyone else at the table. Arcane manipulation, contract law as a weapon, and access to undead intelligence make the church's practitioners dangerous political actors.

##### [Hextorian Church](hextorian-church-organization.md)

**Political Alignment:** None declared — mercenary contracts, military advisors, institutional fracture indicator

Hextor is the god of War, Discord, Massacres, and Tyranny — the dark mirror of Heironeous. His church has no public temple in Verbobonc. It has no need of one. Hextor's priests operate through mercenary contracts, military advisors, and the whispered counsel of those who believe that strength and fear are the only honest governing principles.

**DM Note:** The Hextorian Church's presence in the Viscounty is an early warning sign. Wherever Hextor's clergy appear in strength, social fabric is fraying. In a city braced for a Temple resurgence, Hextorian agents are worth tracking — and worth asking who is paying them.

Faith as Political Currency: The Religious Power Map of 576 CY
--------------------------------------------------------------

_The Missing Connection — How Religion Drives Viscounty Politics_

The faiths of Verbobonc do not exist alongside politics. They **are** politics — expressed in vestments rather than votes. Every major faction in the [Council of Lords of Verbobonc](the-council-of-lords-of-verbobonc-organization.md) has a corresponding religious power base. Every layer of [The Government of Verbobonc](the-government-of-verbobonc-organization.md) has a church that dominates it. And as the Temple of Elemental Evil begins its Second Rising, those religious alignments become the most important intelligence a Dungeon Master can have.

For the full political context — factions, crises, and the Temple's exploitation strategy — see [Verbobonc Politics in 576 CY](verbobonc-politics-in-576-cy-article.md).

#### How the Map Connects

 

##### The Viscount's Inner Circle — Greyfist

[Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) governs through two primary religious relationships:

*   **St. Cuthbert** ([Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md)) is the Viscount's primary confessor and moral advisor. Haufren's counsel shapes decrees on justice, order, and how the city responds to Temple activity. St. Cuthbert's alliance with the Vigil Wardens means the church has direct reach into law enforcement. This is the most powerful single clerical-secular relationship in the Viscounty.
*   **Heironeous** ([Tanner BrightBlade](tanner-brightblade-person.md), Abbot [Rodolfus](rodolfus-person.md)) provides the martial-honor framework that legitimizes military action. When the Viscount needs to muster knights and call a crusade, Heironeous's clergy supply the spiritual authority. This church is the sword to St. Cuthbert's scales.

**DM use:** Access to Greyfist is easiest through St. Cuthbert (justice angle) or Heironeous (military valor angle). A party with enemies at Greyfist can sometimes work around them by approaching through the other church.

 

##### The Militarists — Lords Milinous and Velysin

[Simon Milinous](simon-milinous-person.md) and [Claviger Velysin](claviger-velysin-person.md) are the Council's hawks. Their natural religious allies are:

*   **Heironeous** — valor, martial honor, aggressive action against evil. The Temple of Heironeous is where the Militarists pray before making their arguments at Greyfist.
*   **St. Cuthbert** — law-enforcement and order doctrine supports their calls for decisive crackdowns on Temple cells and bandit activity.

**DM use:** The Militarists will advocate for any party that brings hard evidence of Temple activity to the Council. Approaching them through their religious allies — especially Heironeous — adds credibility to that advocacy.

 

##### The Economic Powers — Galans, House Haxx, Vaswell

[Roland Galans](roland-galans-person.md), [House Haxx](house-haxx-organization.md), and Baron [Drenton Vaswell](drenton-vaswell-person.md) move money. Their religious relationships follow the gold:

*   **Zilchus** ([Abrac Fillmyer](abrac-fillmyer-person.md)) is the Economic Powers' institutional church. The [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md) holds financial records, manages investment portfolios, and brokers merchant disputes. Abrac Fillmyer has more leverage over House Haxx than the Lord Mayor does — because Fillmyer knows the numbers.
*   **Fharlanghn** (Father Pilchus) provides the Economic Powers with intelligence from the road network — which trade routes are safe, where caravans are being raided, and what is moving through the Viscounty without tax stamps.
*   **Velverdyva River Temple** — maritime blessings and Harbormaster relationships make the river priests natural allies of any lord with shipping interests.

**DM use:** To influence the Economic Powers on the Council, the party can work through Zilchus (financial angle), use Fharlanghn intelligence about trade route disruption (which directly threatens their wealth), or demonstrate that Temple activity is costing them money. Gold is the argument. Faith is the framing.

 

##### The Manipulators — Lady Langmuir

Lady [Godeleva Langmuir](godeleva-langmuir-person.md) is the most dangerous member of the Council. Her religious alignment is deliberately obscured. However, the church whose doctrine most closely mirrors her methods is:

*   **Wee Jas** — Magic, Death, Vanity, Law. A faith that prizes meticulous record-keeping of secrets, long-game manipulation through legal instruments, and the quiet exercise of power that leaves no visible fingerprints. The Karuth do not advertise their adherents. Neither does Godeleva.

**DM use:** If the party ever discovers Godeleva's religious affiliation, it is a significant intelligence find. A connection to Wee Jas explains her methods and opens the question of what she knows about death magic, undead intelligence networks, and the Temple's upper tier operations.

 

##### The Cultural Moderates — Lord Rhynehurst

Lord [Wendell Rhynehurst](wendell-rhynehurst-person.md) is the Council's voice of patience and diplomacy. His domain of Rhynehurst is significant for one key reason:

*   **Church of Trithereon** ([Cornelius Arx](cornelius-arx-person.md)) is _based in Rhynehurst_ precisely because it was squeezed out of Verbobonc City by St. Cuthbert. Rhynehurst gave the church a home. In return, Trithereon's advocacy for individual liberty and community self-defense aligns closely with Rhynehurst's moderate, anti-authoritarian politics.
*   **The Order of Rao** (Patriarch [Martiolus Legeum](martiolus-legeum-person.md)) — peace and reason above all. Rao's declining influence means Legeum needs Rhynehurst-style Moderates to even get a hearing in the Council chamber. They are natural, if fragile, allies.

**DM use:** The Moderates are most useful to the party when the Militarists and Economic Powers are deadlocked. Rhynehurst can be the swing vote — but he can only be moved by arguments that combine principle with pragmatism, the kind that Trithereon and Rao together can supply.

 

##### City Hall and the Judicial Layer

The Chamber of Thirteen at [C2 City Hall](c2-city-hall-landmark.md) is where Pholtus rules:

*   **Church of Pholtus** ([Daton Brightblade](daton-brightblade-person.md)) supplies the majority of trained legal Advocates who argue before the Chamber of Thirteen. Pholtus's doctrine — rigid, procedural, inflexible — makes his clergy the natural masters of civic law. If you want a writ issued, a warrant challenged, or a charter enforced, you need a Pholtine advocate in your corner.
*   **Implication:** The party can use the Church of Pholtus to navigate City Hall — or find themselves stonewalled by it. Pholtus does not bend. But he does follow procedure exactly — which means he can be beaten on procedural grounds if the party knows the law.

 

##### The Gathering Hall — Public Pressure

The [C16 The Gathering Hall of Verbobonc](c16-the-gathering-hall-of-verbobonc-landmark.md) is where temples bring delegations to apply public pressure. The most active are:

*   **Pelor** — benevolence, healing, light. The most politically palatable church at a public hearing. [Charles Evertide](charles-evertide-person.md) is the face guilds, temples, and commons trust.
*   **Zilchus** — merchant interests. [Abrac Fillmyer](abrac-fillmyer-person.md)'s guild connections mean the chapel speaks for much of Verbobonc's commercial class at civic hearings.
*   **Fharlanghn** — traveler and trade route concerns. Father Pilchus turns up quietly at hearings that affect road safety or merchant access.
*   **Gnome Pantheon** (Glimmer Gemheart) — the most politically charged delegation at the Gathering Hall. Gemheart is fighting for patrol reinstatement, mine rights, and caravan protection. His appeals go unanswered — until the party gives him a reason to hope.

 

#### What the Temple Exploits

The Temple of Elemental Evil does not attack Verbobonc's religious institutions directly. It does not need to. It exploits the existing fault lines:

*   The **St. Cuthbert / Trithereon tension** prevents the city's two largest congregations from coordinating a unified response.
*   The **Pholtus / Trithereon philosophical war** clogs City Hall with procedural disputes that delay action.
*   The **Zilchus / Heironeous divide** — commerce vs. crusade — paralyzes the Economic Powers and Militarists from agreeing on a military budget.
*   The **Wee Jas shadow network** may already be feeding intelligence to Temple-aligned actors. Lady Langmuir's real loyalties remain unknown.
*   **Rao's decline** means there is no effective peacemaking voice that all factions trust.
*   The **Gnome Pantheon's unanswered pleas** push the Free Alliance closer to redirecting trade to Dyvers — removing a critical military and economic ally from any coalition response.

The party that understands this map can play the churches against the Temple. The party that ignores it will find that when they bring evidence to the Council, every lord is waiting for a different priest to tell him what to do about it.

##### Divine Patrons: Faith and Worship

_The religious diversity in Verbobonc includes  
_

*   [D7 Ash Horn Stream](d7-ash-horn-stream-landmark.md): A spiritual hub for elves dedicated to Corellon Larethian, featuring monthly processions and floral offerings.
*   [B3 Chapel of Zilchus](b3-chapel-of-zilchus-landmark.md): A center of commerce and faith, crucial for business dealings and political influence.
*   [Church of Pelor](church-of-pelor-organization.md): Known for its healing services and missionary work, promoting light and benevolence.
*   [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md): The strongest religious institution, influential in both spiritual and political spheres, focusing on law and order.
*   [W4 Temple of the Velverdyva River](w4-temple-of-the-velverdyva-river-landmark.md): Dedicated to maritime deities, providing blessings and rituals for sailors and merchants.
*   [Church of Pholtus the One True Way](church-of-pholtus-the-one-true-way-organization.md): Emphasizes strict adherence to law and order, with a well-organized clerical hierarchy and militant arm.
*   [The Order of Rao](the-order-of-rao-organization.md): Advocates for peace and reason, with a strong presence in academic and diplomatic circles.
*   [Church of Trithereon](church-of-trithereon-organization.md): Champions individual liberty and retribution against oppression, with a focus on freedom and personal justice.
*   [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md): Centers around the natural world and the worship of Beory and Obad-Hai, with druidic traditions and practices.
*   [Merikka](merikka-person.md): Goddess of agriculture, home, and fertility, with her followers emphasizing farming, family, and community well-being.
*   [The Order of the Enlightened Mind](the-order-of-the-enlightened-mind-organization.md): A monastic order dedicated to [Delleb](delleb-person.md), promoting knowledge, reason, and scholarly pursuits.
*   [Church of Wee Jas](church-of-wee-jas-organization-1.md): Magic, Death, Law. _Manipulator faction — Lady Langmuir._
*   [Hextorian Church](hextorian-church-organization.md): War, Discord, Tyranny. _Institutional fracture indicator._
