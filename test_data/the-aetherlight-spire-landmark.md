The Aetherlight Spire
=====================

##### _Spire of Dreams and Mirrors, Keeper of the Veil_

_As you ascend the high rope bridges into the loftiest reaches of Oakvein’s canopy, the forest fades into soft murmurs and distant winds. Suspended like a dewdrop between three ancient branches, the Aetherlight Spire reveals itself as a glimmering dome woven from moonlight and living silverleaf. Ethereal lights swirl across its curved walls, and faint melodies echo from no visible source. The air here is cool, tinged with the scent of night-blooming orchids and something stranger—like the memory of a dream you've half-forgotten._

**Sanctum of Dream and Planar Insight**
---------------------------------------

The **Aetherlight Spire** is the most enigmatic of Oakvein’s six sacred halls. Home to **Loremaster Nemoi**, it serves as a bridge between realms—part temple, part laboratory, and part dream-archive. Here, **planar boundaries thin**, and the veil between the waking world, the Feywild, and even the Astral Plane becomes translucent.

Where others craft, heal, or debate, **Nemoi listens**—to the hum of the planes, the whisper of dreams, and the subtle ripples left behind by gods, fey, and ancient beings long departed.

* * *

#### **History and Lore**

The Spire’s origin is tied to an event known only as the **Veil-Walk**, when Nemoi vanished from Oakvein for seven days and seven nights beneath a moonless sky. When he returned, he bore the **seed of a fey dream-spire**, gifted by a starborn archfey known as **The Mirror Bloom**.

With the guidance of Faeluneth and the blessing of the tree itself, the spire was coaxed into being—**grown, not built**, through dream-shaping and planar resonance. Since then, the Aetherlight Spire has become a locus of **dream-touched convergence** in the Gnarley Forest, attracting fey entities, dreamwalkers, and vision-priests from across the Flanaess.

* * *

#### **Function and Role in Oakvein**

*   **Planar Mapping and Observation  
    **The Spire functions as a **lighthouse for planar currents**, charting fluctuations in the Feywild, Astral Plane, Shadowfell, and the Dreaming.
*   **Dreamgate Navigation  
    **Through lucid trancework and the use of starlit pools, Nemoi and his aides **navigate and map the dream-threads** that touch Oakvein and nearby realms. These are used to detect incursions, foresee emotional disturbances, or **access lost elven memories**.
*   **Fey Concords and Visitors  
    **The Aetherlight is a neutral site where **fey courts occasionally send envoys**. These guests may arrive unannounced, often in unusual forms—some with warnings, others with riddles.
*   **Mystical Research  
    **Nemoi pursues a personal quest to **catalog the metaphysical echoes** left behind by lost gods, dream-walkers, and planar beings.

* * *

### **Key Features and Spaces**

##### ✦ **The Dream Orb Array**

Suspended from the canopy, a constellation of floating orbs rotates slowly through color and shape. Each tracks a known or suspected **dreamgate or planar anchor point** across Oerth.

##### ✦ **The Reflection Pool**

A shallow basin of silver-clear water that mirrors **not the body, but the soul’s unrest**. Used for dream scrying and lucid entry into shared dreamscapes.

##### ✦ **Fey Tether Rings**

Three crescent-shaped thrones carved from alabaster and luminous crystal, bound with vines and hung with wind chimes. These are used to host **fey guests** or anchor visitors during dream-walks.

##### ✦ **The Veil Archive**

An ethereal chamber of memory-silk scrolls, bound with dream-ink. Some are readable only during trance or under moonlight. Contains records of past Feywild alignments, astral flares, and nightmare incursions.

#### **Activities and Mystical Phenomena**

*   **Trance Vigils**: Conducted on solstices, where participants enter guided lucid dreams
*   **Fey Migrations**: During alignments, flocks of fey spirits pass through the spire
*   **Nightmirror Sickness**: Rare affliction where mortals mirror the dreams of outer planar entities—Nemoi oversees treatment and cleansing

* * *

### **Adventure Hooks**

*   A **star has vanished** from the Dream Orb Array—its last echo leads to the ruins near Nulb
*   **A nightmare storm** sweeps through Hommlet, infecting dreamers; Nemoi believes a cultic portal has reopened
*   A **fey emissary goes missing** after leaving the spire—last seen speaking with a silver-eyed druid who never cast a shadow
*   A stolen **dream-scroll** appears on the black market in Dyvers, whispering forgotten names from the Flannae's lost pantheon
