6\. Tanner’s Leatherworking
===========================

Tanner’s Leatherworking is a bustling hub of leather craftsmanship located in the heart of Hommlet. Run by Bogan and Telia Tanner, this family business specializes in creating a variety of leather goods, from shoes and boots to saddles and armor. Despite their skills and contributions to the village, the Tanners prefer to stay out of adventuring, focusing instead on their craft and community.

### Location and Description

Tanner’s Leatherworking is a modest yet well-maintained establishment. The workshop is an extension of their home, situated conveniently on the Long Road, adjacent to the Inn of the Welcome Wench. The shop’s exterior is adorned with various leather samples and completed works, showcasing the Tanners' expertise. The scent of tanned leather and beeswax pervades the air, drawing customers in.

##### Workshop and Home

*   **Workshop:** Filled with tools, hides, and finished products.
*   **Home:** Attached to the workshop, with living quarters for the family.
*   **Display Area:** Showcases boots, saddles, harnesses, and leather garments.

### Lore and History

The Tanner family has been a fixture in Hommlet for generations. Bogan Tanner, 31, learned the trade from his father, who was also a renowned leatherworker. Bogan’s wife, Telia, age 30, is an accomplished leather garment and armor maker, known for her skill and precision. Their business is essential to the village, providing essential goods and repairs.

> _"In leather, as in life, it's the small details that make the difference."_

##### Current State

Bogan and Telia work tirelessly to maintain their reputation and support their three children. Their eldest son, Bogan Jr., is a 16-year-old apprentice, eager to follow in his parents' footsteps. The family lives modestly but comfortably, their home and workshop a testament to their dedication and hard work.

### Motivation and Relationships

##### Motivations

The Tanners are motivated by their passion for their craft and the desire to provide for their family. They have no interest in adventuring, preferring the stability and satisfaction of their trade.

##### Relationships

*   **Community:** Well-respected by villagers, often contributing to the militia.
*   **Family:** Close-knit, with each member playing a role in the business.
*   **Religion:** Devout worshippers of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md), participating in local ceremonies.

### Current Events

##### Business Struggles

The Tanners face the occasional challenge of supply shortages and the need for skilled labor. However, their reputation and quality of work ensure a steady stream of customers.

##### Community Involvement

Bogan sometimes serves as a scout for the militia, using his skills in hunting and trapping to aid the village in times of need.
