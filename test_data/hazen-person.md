Hazen
=====

### His Venerable Reverence, Canon

Canon Hazen is the living embodiment of Veluna’s divine authority—a man in whom faith and governance are not separate, but wholly entwined. As Canon of Veluna, he standeth not merely as high priest, but as the axis upon which the Archclericy itself turneth.

 

Through him floweth the will of Rao, and through that will the direction of the realm. Kings may rule elsewhere—but in Veluna, it is Hazen who shepherdeth both soul and state.

* * *

##### Appearance & Presence

Hazen is an elderly man, his years plain upon his form, yet no weakness clingeth to him. His head is shorn smooth, his eyes deep and unwavering, blue as still water beneath a cloudless sky.

 

When he entereth a chamber, voices do not rise—they fall silent. His presence commandeth not through force, but through certainty. Men do not fear him. They yield to him.

 

### The Archclericy of Veluna

Veluna is not a kingdom of crowns, but of faith ordered into governance. The Archclericy is structured through dioceses, bishops, and noble authorities, all bound beneath the guiding doctrine of Rao. Archclericy of Veluna – Religion

 

At its pinnacle standeth Canon Hazen.

He doth not issue commands as a tyrant, nor rule through decree alone. Instead, he shapeth the realm through guidance, doctrine, and quiet authority. His voice setteth the tone by which bishops govern, nobles rule, and councils deliberate.

*   He appointeth and confirmeth bishops across Veluna
*   He guideth the moral and political direction of the realm
*   He acteth as final arbiter in matters where faith and law intertwine

Thus, Hazen is not merely a leader—he is the foundation upon which Veluna’s unity is maintained.

### The Celestial Circle

Though the Celestial Circle deliberateth, it is Hazen who giveth its purpose form.

 

He doth not sit as a constant voice within its debates, yet his influence permeateth every chambered word. The High Prelate and the assembled seats look ever toward his guidance, whether spoken or withheld.

 

When Hazen speaketh on matters before the Circle, discussion endeth soon thereafter—not by command, but by inevitability.

### The Flight of Fiends

*   In Coldeven of 586 CY, when fiendish forces plagued the Flanaess, Canon Hazen took up the Crook of Rao and wrought a miracle that hath no equal in living memory.
    
*   With its power, he banished thousands—demons, devils, yugoloths, and darker things—from the world, driving them from Oerth itself.
    
*   This act, now known as the Flight of Fiends, secured his place not merely as a ruler, but as a figure of legend.
    
*   Yet since that day, he hath withdrawn.
    

### Withdrawal from Public Life

*   Since the Flight of Fiends, Hazen hath appeared but rarely. When he doth, it is only at moments of great consequence—when the balance of Veluna, or perhaps all the Flanaess, standeth at risk.
    
*   In his absence, governance continueth through bishops, diocesan rulers, and the Celestial Circle—but always beneath the shadow of his authority.
    
*   Some say he prepareth for another great act. Others whisper that such power demandeth a cost not yet paid.
    

### Personality & Doctrine

Hazen is a man of profound calm, embodying the teachings of Rao—peace, reason, and measured action.

 

Yet beneath that calm lieth unyielding resolve. When he acteth, he doeth so with finality.

*   Prefers diplomacy and balance over force
*   Believes in unity through guidance, not domination
*   Acts decisively when the world itself is threatened

He is neither distant nor warm. He is… certain.

 

### Political Significance

Hazen’s authority extendeth beyond Veluna.

*   His influence shapes relations with Verbobonc and Furyondy
*   His approval lends legitimacy to rulers and councils
*   His silence can unsettle even the most powerful lords

In matters of faith, he is unquestioned. In matters of politics, he is unavoidable.

### Current Questions & Rumors

*   Why hath Hazen withdrawn so completely since the Flight of Fiends?
*   Doth the Crook of Rao still remain in his possession—or is it hidden?
*   Will he intervene again should chaos rise anew?
*   Who, if any, could succeed him?

These questions are not spoken loudly—but they are spoken often.

##### WHY CANON HAZEN MATTERS

Canon Hazen is not merely a powerful cleric—he is a campaign-defining figure.

*   A distant yet ever-present authority shaping Veluna’s direction
*   A legendary figure tied to one of the greatest events in the Flanaess
*   A potential patron, judge, or final arbiter for high-level play
*   A mystery—withdrawn, watchful, and perhaps preparing for what cometh next

To stand before Hazen is to stand before certainty itself—and to know that thy actions shall be weighed not in coin or crown, but in the balance of the world.
