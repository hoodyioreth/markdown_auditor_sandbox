Sulian
======

### High Priest

_Hierophant of Corellon’s Hall of Seven Vows, Düalarn (Clan Sherendyl)_

**Sulian** presides over the largest temple of [Corellon Larethian](corellon-larethian-person.md) in the Sherendyl heartlands. In the lakeside capital of [Düalarn, the Hidden Fortress](dualarn2C-the-hidden-fortress-settlement.md), his airy, silver-and-gold-chased oak halls shelter both worship and war-readiness: beneath the nave lies a fortified **granary and armory** sized to equip the Gnarley’s elves for a campaign. Gentle in public and exacting in private, Sulian reads troubling omens—**Iuz’s resurgence, Hierarch intrigues, and organized banditry near the forest roads**—and quietly urges **Las’törla Aerith** and fellow clergy to prepare for the worst.

> “Art that cannot endure a march is decoration. Corellon asks for art that stands.”

* * *

#### Appearance & Bearing

Slender and ageless, with pale sun-browned skin, **silver-threaded russet hair** bound in a coronet braid, and **clear green eyes** that brighten when he invokes Corellon. He wears a long white-leaf vestment edged in moon-blue silk over travel-ready scale beneath. His voice is calm, measured, and carries across halls without effort.

**Roleplay cues:** folds hands at the wrist when listening; blesses with two fingers and the thumb; rarely raises his voice—silence does the work.

* * *

#### Offices & Duties

*   **High Priest of Corellon’s Hall of Seven Vows (Düalarn):** Oversees sacraments, oath-witnessing, investitures of officers, and artists’ guild blessings.
*   **Keeper of Stores:** Below the temple, Sulian maintains the **Armory of Seven Seasons** and **Nuralin Vaults**—sealed by dual-key protocol (priest + quartermaster elder).
*   **Strategic Advisor:** Sits on Aerith’s inner council; signs readiness audits; coordinates festival rites with inspection days (worship tied to stewardship).
*   **Inter-Clan Liaison:** Exchanges letters and scryings with priests in [Alaeduri](alaeduri-settlement.md) and [Oakvein](oakvein-settlement.md), aligning doctrine, signals, and relief plans.

* * *

#### Beliefs & Private Warnings

Publicly, Sulian projects **cautionary optimism** about neighbors around the western **Nyr Dyv**: cooperation when possible, firmness at need. Privately, his divinations and courier reports convince him that **war will touch the borderlands soon**. He counsels: stock the vaults, rotate officers, test bells at night, and teach the young to sing **oaths that hold at spear-length**.

> “Hope is a ration; pack enough, but never mistake it for bread.”

* * *

#### Relationships

*   **Las’törla** [Aerith Sherendyl](aerith-sherendyl-person.md)**:** Mutual trust. He supports her drills and diplomacy, but urges faster stock rotations and wider ritual training.
*   [Azaethus Ilaro](azaethus-ilaro-person.md)**:** Strategic ally; Sulian provides **abjurations** and **warding rites** that let Azaethus shape blast-lanes without scarring the canopy.
*   [Ruael Y’kashiral](ruael-yE28099kashiral-person.md)**:** Respects her resolve; tempers her impatience by insisting on **witnessed writs** for every seizure on the Forest Road.
*   **Meldarin/Enlanefel clergy:** Regular exchange of liturgical texts and relief schedules; plans for sanctuary overflow if southern routes fail.

* * *

#### Temple & Underhalls

*   **The Seven Vows Nave:** Open-raftered hall with leaf-lattice windows; acoustics tuned for chorus and command.
*   **Armory of Seven Seasons (sub-level):** Standardized kits by season (rain, frost, drought, flood, blight, march, siege racks of bronzewood shields and spear-bundles; sealed oil jars; bow-cord spares.
*   **Nuralin Vaults (sub-level):** Grain silos, smoked fish caches, herbal draughts; clay tags and leaf-wax seals; tracked like scripture.
*   **War-Bell:** A wooden bell hung in the crossing; one peal summons muster captains, three peals sanctify a defensive stand.

* * *

#### Personality

*   **Ideal:** _“Beauty and readiness are the same discipline.”_
*   **Bond:** The choristers and quartermasters who keep the hall honest.
*   **Flaw:** Pessimism masked as prudence—he can underplay political windows that require boldness.

* * *

#### Notable Blessings & Relics

*   **Diadem of the First Stroke (wondrous, attunement):** +1 to spell save DC for **abjuration** or **light** spells; once/day cast _dispel magic_ without slot.
*   **Seven-Vows Censer:** When swung during a formation blessing, grants allied units advantage on the first **Wisdom save vs. fear** taken within 1 hour.
*   **Scroll-Ark of Arvandor:** Holds rotating emergency liturgies (ritual _water walk_, _purify food and drink_, _ceremony_ variants for oaths).

#### Adventure Hooks

1.  **The Bell That Shouldn’t Ring:** The war-bell peals once at midnight; Sulian asks the party to trace a saboteur in the tunnel loops before dawn.
2.  **The Quiet Muster:** He tasks the PCs with escorting a hidden **grain convoy** to a drought-struck hamlet while Dyvers factors probe for stores.
3.  **Signs in the Basin:** Sulian’s scrying bowl shows cult traffic on the Nyr Dyv road; he wants **witnessed names** before he pressures Aerith for a preemptive cordon.
4.  **Seven Vows, Sixth Key:** One vault key goes missing during a festival—recover it without panicking the village or revealing the armory’s scale.

* * *

#### Using Sulian at the Table

*   **As Patron:** Offers clear objectives paired with logistical support (rations, poultices, sanctified rest).
*   **As Moral Compass:** Frames choices in terms of **oath, stewardship, and readiness**—not sentiment.
*   **As NPC Anchor:** After hard temple delves, a night in Sulian’s hall feels safe, ordered, and restorative—at a price of one honest report.
