Findiser
========

### Professor of Transmutation, Abjuration

Where other mages pursue power, Findiser pursues **survival**.

A veteran of half a century of hard travel, cult wars, and near-fatal mistakes, Findiser teaches magic as something meant to _hold the line_. At Wrinkle Academy, she is both instructor and shield—responsible not only for classrooms, but for the quiet wards, sigils, and defensive protocols that keep the academy standing when politics, cults, or catastrophes come knocking.

* * *

#### Appearance & Manner

Findiser is a **grey elf**, tall and spare, with weathered grace rather than elegance. Her **ash-gray hair** is usually bound tight, revealing sharp **green eyes** that rarely miss movement in a room. She favors **simple cloth armor**, a practical **staff**, and a **dagger worn within easy reach**.

She positions herself carefully in any space and **dislikes having anyone behind her**—a habit earned, not learned.

Her voice is calm, direct, and rarely raised. Praise is sparse. Correction is immediate.

* * *

#### Background

Findiser’s early arcane training was **self-taught**, learned the hard way among the defenders of the [Elven Enclave](elven-enclave-organization.md) **of Verbobonc**, where survival often mattered more than theory.

For nearly **fifty years**, she lived as an itinerant adventurer, serving with multiple companies across the Flanaess. She faced the cults of **Orcus** and **Iuz**, surviving ambushes that wiped out better-armed companions. Those experiences taught her a brutal truth: _magic without preparation is just another way to die._

Recognizing her own limits, Findiser left the road and formalized her studies at the **School of Magic** in the Free City of Greyhawk. Her aptitude for defensive theory and applied abjuration earned her notice—and eventually a teaching offer from **Obble Quillkeeper Humbleburrow Har**.

She accepted, and relocated to the **Wrinkle Academy of Magic**, where her experience filled a gap no amount of theory could replace.

* * *

#### Role at Wrinkle Academy

Findiser teaches both **foundational** and **advanced** coursework, including:

*   _Introduction to Magic Circles_ (required for all first-year students)
*   _Principles of Arcane Transmutation_
*   _Building Arcane Defense Systems_ (advanced seminar)
*   Emergency defensive response drills (mandatory attendance)

Beyond the classroom, Findiser spends much of her time coordinating with **campus security**, reviewing wards, reinforcing weak points, and quietly correcting student habits that might get them killed later.

Her philosophy is simple:

_Every mage should know how to defend themselves—even if they never intend to fight._

As a result, she is widely respected by students across **all schools**, not just Abjuration.

* * *

#### Personality

*   **Protective**, but never indulgent
*   Deeply invested in student survival
*   Pragmatic to the point of bluntness
*   Values preparation over brilliance

Her affection for students is evident not in warmth, but in vigilance.

* * *

#### Relationships

*   **Wrinkle Academy Faculty**: Trusted specialist in defensive doctrine
*   **Campus Guards**: Acts as advisor and magical coordinator
*   **Students**: Widely respected, quietly beloved

She maintains professional ties with the **Elven Enclave** and keeps discreet watch for cult activity in Verbobonc—old habits never die.

**Findiser — First Day Lecture:** _**Introduction to Magic Circles**_
---------------------------------------------------------------------

##### **Minimal Narration (DM Read-Aloud)**

You take your seat in the defensive lecture hall. Chalk lines already score the stone floor in concentric rings. Every circle is unbroken. Every angle exact.

A tall, grey-haired elf stands near the center. Her back is to the wall. It stays that way.

She waits until the room is silent.

* * *

##### **Findiser’s Opening Address (Dialogue)**

“Good. You arrived on time. That tells me two things already.”

“First—you can follow instructions.”

“Second—you intend to survive.”

She lets her gaze pass over each of you, slow and measuring.

“My name is **Findiser**. I teach **Abjuration** and **Transmutation**, but today you will learn something simpler.”

She taps the floor with the ferrule of her staff.

“You will learn how **not to die**.”

* * *

“A magic circle is not a spell.”

“It is a _promise_.”

She crouches, tracing one line with two fingers.

“A promise that power will stay where you tell it to stay.”

“A promise that what is summoned will remain where it belongs.”

“And a promise that when something goes wrong—and it will—only one of us pays the price.”

She looks up.

“That will not be me.”

* * *

“Some of you believe circles are tedious.”

“Some of you believe they are beneath you.”

“And some of you believe you can ‘feel’ when a ward is strong enough.”

She straightens.

“Those students tend to leave the Academy early.”

A pause.

“Sometimes vertically.”

* * *

“In this room, there are three rules.”

She raises one finger.

“Rule one: **Never step into a circle you did not draw yourself.**”

Another finger.

“Rule two: **Never erase a line you do not understand.**”

A third.

“Rule three: **If you feel watched, assume you are correct.**”

Her eyes narrow slightly.

“Magic notices hesitation.”

* * *

“Today, you will not cast.”

“You will draw.”

“You will measure.”

“You will learn why a circle that is _almost_ perfect is worse than no circle at all.”

She gestures to the chalk laid out before you.

“Perfection is not required.”

“Respect is.”

* * *

She steps aside, keeping the wall at her back.

“Begin.”

“And if you hear something breathing that should not be—”

A thin smile.

“Step away slowly.”

“We will discuss your error _after_ the wards hold.”

* * *

##### **Optional Closing Line (If Needed)**

If the room grows tense, she adds quietly:

“Remember. Power forgives nothing.”

“But preparation forgives almost everything.”

##### Tactics (DM Guidance)

*   Findiser **avoids direct combat** unless absolutely necessary
*   She opens with **mage armor** and positioning
*   Uses **counterspell, hold person, and protection spells** first
*   Retreats behind allies or terrain rather than pressing an attack
*   Will not pursue fleeing enemies—_survival over pride_

##### DM Notes

Findiser is an excellent NPC for:

*   Warning players about magical threats without exposition
*   Introducing ward mechanics, protective magic, or counter-spells
*   Serving as a calm authority during magical crises
*   Quietly opposing reckless magical experimentation

She does not seek adventure—but she will _prepare others for it_.
