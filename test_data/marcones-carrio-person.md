Marcones Carrio
===============

### Captain

> Older Suel / Oeridian mix, Captain Marcones bald mien is tanned lean and strong from years on the horse patrolling the viscounty. His weather worn face and hands has soon more days in the borders than in a barracks. He has the full respect of his men and cares much for them as his own family. The safety of the Viscounty, Ostverk, the Viscounty border is paramount. Then the safety of his men and the viscounty folk. He takes his oath as serious as any cleric of any of the gods.

Captain Marcones Carrio, a seasoned leader of the Mounted Borderers stationed at Deleveu Manor, plays a pivotal role in maintaining the security of the [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md). Serving under the command of Knight Captain Lord [Corbin Deleveu](corbin-deleveu-person.md), Marcones embodies the dedication and resilience required to oversee the challenging southern borders of the realm. This article delves into his background, motivations, and the complexities of his duties amidst the political and martial challenges facing the region.

#### Physical Description

Marcones Carrio is characterized by his lean, strong build, a testament to years of rigorous patrol duties on horseback. His bald head and tanned, weather-worn features reflect a life spent outdoors, marking him as a man shaped by the elements and the demands of his role.

#### Background

*   **Early Life and Military Service:** Captain Carrio hails from a mixed Suel-Oeridian heritage, bringing a blend of cultural traits that enrich his leadership style. His early years were marked by a stern military discipline that prepared him for a life of service.
*   **Notable Battles:** He is a veteran of [The Battle of Emridy Meadows](the-battle-of-emridy-meadows-myth.md), where his strategic prowess and courage left a lasting impression on his peers and superiors. This battle also personal losses that have driven his fervent commitment to the security of his charges and the lands they protect.

#### Motivation and Personality

*   **Dedication to Duty:** Marcones is fiercely dedicated to the safety of the Viscounty and its borders, valuing the security of the land and its people above all else.
*   **Protective Nature:** He views the men under his command as family, prioritizing their welfare and maintaining a morale-boosting camaraderie among his troops.
*   **Personal Loss and Resolve:** The loss experienced during the Battle of Emridy Meadows fuels his resolve to prevent such tragedies from befalling his homeland again.

#### Political Relationships and Opinions

*   **Lord Corbin Deleveu:** Marcones holds Lord Deleveu in high esteem, appreciating his balanced approach to leadership and his efforts to maintain peace with the elven realms.
*   **Bitterness Over Policy:** He harbors a deep frustration with Viscount Wilfrick’s decision to pull back patrols from the [Kron Hills](kron-hills-location.md), feeling it undermines the security of the Viscounty and exposes his men to greater risks.
*   **Alignment with Local Leadership:** Despite political frustrations, he maintains strong ties with the leaders of Ostverk, supporting mutual goals of stability and safety.

##### Current Challenges

*   **Depleted Forces:** The recent reduction in manpower has left his command, the 3rd Company of the Mounted Borderers, dangerously understaffed, struggling to cope with increasing threats from bandits, slavers, and marauding monsters.
*   **Border Security:** With the growing presence of Blackthorn Orcs and other hostile entities, Marcones faces the dual challenge of safeguarding the borders and ensuring the safety of his diminished forces.

##### Role and Responsibilities

*   **Strategic Defense:** Overseeing the strategic deployment of the Mounted Borderers to effectively patrol and protect the Viscounty from external threats.
*   **Training and Leadership:** Providing training and leadership to ensure his men are well-prepared for the challenges they face.
*   **Community Relations:** Engaging with local communities to foster a sense of security and mutual support between the Borderers and the citizens of the Viscounty.

#### Conclusion

Captain Marcones Carrio remains a steadfast guardian of Verbobonc, his life dedicated to the service of its people and the protection of its lands. Despite facing significant challenges, his leadership and unwavering commitment continue to inspire those around him, embodying the spirit of resilience and duty that defines the best of the Mounted Borderers.
