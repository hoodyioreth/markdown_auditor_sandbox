Sir Goodwyn
===========

Sir Goodwyn knight in command of the Mounted Borderers region. He is the knight responsible for the safety and defense of this remote area of the Viscounty. The outpost is part of Sir Simon Milinous holdings and is under expansion to help accommodate the increased numbers of borderers being posted here.
