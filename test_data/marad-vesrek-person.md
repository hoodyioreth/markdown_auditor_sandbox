Marad Vesrek
============

### Captain (a.k.a. The Crimson Trader)

Captain Marad Vesrek is a ruthless pirate and slaver whose vessel, [The Crimson Trader](the-crimson-trader-organization.md), serves as a critical logistical link between the pirate trade networks and the dark ambitions of the Temple of Elemental Evil. With deep connections to the Slave Lords and clandestine ties to the Scarlet Brotherhood, Vesrek is one of Nulb's most feared and influential figures. Through careful manipulation and strategic brutality, he maintains significant influence over the region's shadow economy.

##### Physical Description

Vesrek is an imposing, predatory presence with distinctive Suel features. His tall frame, stark white hair, and piercing steel-gray eyes command attention. He proudly wears crimson-dyed leather armor trimmed with silver accents, a shark-tooth-hilted rapier at his side, and a weathered black tricorn hat, emphasizing both elegance and intimidation. Sea-serpent tattoos winding around his forearms openly mark his piratical and Brotherhood affiliations.

##### Personality

Captain Vesrek is a cold, calculated manipulator. Every interaction is weighed for advantage; loyalty, compassion, and sentimentality have no place in his ruthless worldview.

*   **Manipulative & Ruthless**: Unflinchingly cruel when necessary.
*   **Strategic Charm**: Can switch seamlessly from charismatic to intimidating.
*   **Narwell Origins**: Frequently uses rough slang from his Wild Coast roots.
*   **Intimidation Tactic**: Constantly sharpens his weapons during negotiations.

### Background

Born in the slums in **Safeton** of the [The Wild Coast](the-wild-coast-organization.md), Vesrek rose from a life of destitution by sheer will and brutality. The son of a dockworker and a washerwoman, Vesrek grew up in squalor, learning early the value of wit and ruthlessness. His early years as a dockside enforcer taught him the value of leveraging fear and alliances. A fateful encounter aboard the Iron Tide, where he saved his captain from mutiny, marked the beginning of his rise in the ranks. After years of service, Vesrek commandeered his first ship, the Crimson Trader, which became his ticket to wealth and infamy.

*   **Early Life**: Born to [Suloise Suel](suloise-suel-ethnicity.md) immigrants associated with the Scarlet Brotherhood; survived by wit and violence on Safeton’s dangerous docks.
*   Spent his childhood in Safeton's dangerous docks, running errands for smugglers and thieves.
*   **Rise to Power**: Swiftly moved from smuggling to piracy, capturing _The Crimson Trader_, transforming it into a feared slaving ship.
*   **Slave Lords Connection**: Established critical trade routes, supplying slaves from Woolly Bay and Selintan River areas to distant markets and Temple buyers.

* * *

#### Motivations

Captain Vesrek is driven by power and wealth. While he serves the Slave Lords and the Temple of Elemental Evil, he harbors ambitions to expand his influence further. The discovery of a powerful artifact like the Yellowskull could become an obsession if he learns of its existence. Vesrek’s ambitions extend beyond Nulb and the Slave Lords. His ultimate goal is to establish his own dominion, using his connections to the Temple and the Scarlet Brotherhood to secure power.

##### Primary Goals

*   Maintain and expand the Slave Lords’ operations.
*   Amass wealth, power and retrieve powerful artifacts.
*   Establish a base of operations beyond the reach of the Viscounty of Verbobonc.

* * *

#### **Operations and Activities**

Captain Vesrek’s operations revolve around piracy, slave trading, and smuggling. He specifically targets valuable human cargo for sale to the Temple of Elemental Evil and Nulb’s underground market.

*   **Slave Raiding**: Targets merchants, isolated communities, and travelers along waterways.
*   **Contraband Smuggling**: Secretly delivers luxury goods, weapons, and exotic items into Nulb’s market through intermediaries like Lorveth "The Broker."
*   **Temple Logistics**: Regularly supplies slaves and special cargo to Canon Belsornig (Water Temple) and Barkinar (Greater Temple) for their rituals.

#### Role in Nulb

Captain Vesrek is central to Nulb’s illicit economy, supplying slaves and contraband essential to the town’s survival and the Temple’s operations.

*   **Economic Influence**: Drives commerce in Nulb’s black market and local establishments such as the Boatman's Tavern and the Brothel above it.
*   **Supply Chain**: Essential conduit for slaves and goods, working closely with Lorveth Thrask to manage slave distributions and profit maximization.
*   **Temple Agent**: Carefully maintains good standing with Temple leadership, ensuring continued protection and profitability.

* * *

### **Relationships and Alliances**

Captain Vesrek carefully navigates Nulb’s dangerous power dynamics, maintaining cautious alliances and deep rivalries:

*   **Captain** [Tolub](tolub-person.md) **(Iron Dagger)**: Collaborative yet tense; rivals for control over river piracy and smuggling operations.
*   [Lorveth Thrask](lorveth-thrask-person.md) **“The Broker”**: Close but volatile relationship; frequent negotiations for slave quality and prices.
*   **Madame Selentis (**[Nysera Krivaltis](nysera-krivaltis-person.md)): Mutually beneficial but wary relationship; she taxes his land operations and monitors his Brotherhood connections closely.
*   **Canon** [Belsornig](belsornig-person.md) **(Water Temple)**: Crucial partnership, regularly providing slaves for rituals, securing Temple protection.
*   [Romag](romag-person.md) **(Earth Temple)**: Occasional business associate; wary of Belsornig's influence, sometimes seeks slaves directly from Vesrek to strengthen the Earth Temple’s rituals.
*   [Gefreid Patris](gefreid-patris-person.md) **& Commander** [Feldrin](feldrin-person.md): Limited direct contact but occasionally delivers slaves and contraband indirectly through Nulb’s logistics managed by Madame Selentis.

* * *

#### **Captain Vesrek’s Secrets**

*   **Scarlet Brotherhood Operative**: Uses his piracy to secretly funnel intelligence and resources back to the Brotherhood; keeps meticulous hidden ledgers documenting this relationship.
*   **Hidden Ledger**: Detailed records of his illicit operations with Lorveth, the Temple, and the Scarlet Brotherhood—potentially devastating if exposed.
*   **Artifact Ambition**: Obsessed with finding powerful relics like the Yellowskull to leverage his dominance and expand his personal power.

#### **Connections to the Brothel**

Captain Vesrek maintains a profitable and personal alliance with Elvanna Thorne, the mistress of the brothel above the Boatman’s Tavern.

*   **Supply Line**: Provides carefully selected captives for the brothel's operations.
*   **Information Exchange**: Trades valuable intelligence with Elvanna gathered from his travels and captives.
*   **Personal Patronage**: Regular visitor; invests significantly, ensuring preferential treatment and continued influence.

#### **Role in Slave Trade Logistics**

The Crimson Trader is uniquely outfitted for slave transportation, making Vesrek a pivotal figure in the Nulb slave market. Captain Vesrek of the [The Crimson Trader](the-crimson-trader-organization.md) maintains a lucrative and secretive relationship with the 'Broker' at the Nulb Brothel in the Boatman's Tavern. This partnership forms one of the darkest aspects of Nulb’s economy, fueling the slave trade that supports the brothel, and the broader criminal enterprises in the region.

*   **Selection and Capture**: Carefully selects high-value targets from raids for optimal profit or Temple utility.
*   **Negotiations and Sale**: Works closely with Lorveth to tailor slave shipments to specific buyers, maximizing profits and influence.
*   **Slave Quality Management**: Ensures slaves are delivered alive and in good condition for maximum resale value.

### Captain Marad Vesrek — Updated Adventure Hooks

##### 1) Reckoning on the Docks

**Patron:** _Madame Selentis (“Nysera”)  
_**Pitch:** Vesrek’s Scarlet Brotherhood ties and his habit of keeping his own ledgers make him useful—but dangerously independent. Selentis wants leverage or a leash. She’ll pay for proof.  
**Objective:** Infiltrate the **Crimson Trader** or the **Boatman’s Brothel** offices to copy or plant evidence tying Vesrek to the Scarlet Brotherhood and slave consignments; alternatively, replace one ledger page with a forged “double-book.”  
**Complication:** Elvanna’s loft is a web—hush-wards, muscle (Mardun), and the Broker’s paper trails will notice a missing page; Selentis wants Vesrek **close, yet compartmentalized**, not openly toppled.  
**Outcome:** Delivering copies (not originals) lets Selentis squeeze Vesrek without sparking a war; turning the proof over to **Belsornig** or **Tolub** shifts the balance in the river-faction triangle.

* * *

##### 2) Redhand’s Bait

**Patron:** _Smigmal Redhand  
_**Pitch:** Smigmal hates Temple liabilities. Vesrek is profitable, but his Brotherhood stink draws attention. She wants him **shamed**, not necessarily dead.  
**Objective:** Frame Vesrek as a leak to Verbobonc by planting a coded courier token (or “witness” name) aboard the **Crimson Trader**, then seed the rumor through the Waterside/Boatman’s grapevine.  
**Complication:** **Lady Selentis’s watchers** haunt the brothel and tavern; a clumsy rumor frames the wrong rival (Tolub), igniting pirate reprisals.  
**Outcome:** If the party pulls it off, Smigmal owes a quiet favor (maps, patrol times near the ruins). If they bungle it, Vesrek tightens security and starts hunting informants.

* * *

##### 3) The Brotherhood’s Brand

**Patron:** _Zilchus priest (Gilded Shackle) or Mother Screng (indirect)  
_**Pitch:** Proof exists—**hidden ledgers and scarlet-threaded scrolls**—that Vesrek runs Brotherhood intelligence through Nulb and the Brothel. Fetch them, and the whole river changes course.  
**Objective:** Lift the **hidden ledger** and cipher scrolls from Vesrek’s cabin (or Lorveth “the Broker’s” room across from Elvanna’s) without alerting the scribe network.  
**Complication:** The Brothel tracks secrets like coin; Elvanna’s crawlspace to the warehouse makes pursuit quick if the alarm goes.  
**Outcome:** Handing the proof to Selentis gives her a velvet garrote on Vesrek; selling it to **Belsornig** buys the party river immunity—until it doesn’t.

* * *

##### 4) Tides of the Past

**Patron:** _An old Wild Coast contact (Safeton/Narwell)  
_**Pitch:** Someone from Vesrek’s **Safeton/Wild Coast** rise wants old sins aired—mutiny, betrayal, and the bloody birth of the **Crimson Trader**.  
**Objective:** Gather testimony and proofs (tattoo marks, charter fragments, former crew accounts) that Vesrek betrayed his captain to the Brotherhood to seize the ship.  
**Complication:** Vesrek buys silence fast; witnesses vanish into Elvanna’s **Prison Room** or onto Tolub’s boats.  
**Outcome:** Exposing his Wild Coast crimes cracks his alliances; **Selentis** keeps him “close,” **Belsornig** calls him **expendable**, and **Tolub** circles for turf.

* * *

#### Optional escalator (drop-in)

**5) Countermanded by the Dark  
Patron:** _Feldrin, on Hedrack’s leash  
_**Pitch:** Hedrack keeps Vesrek **valued but expendable**, timed by **Feldrin** so shipments pass unseen. A rival has learned the timetable; plug the leak—or become it.  
**Objective:** Identify the informant using the Brothel’s blackmail ledgers; swap the rendezvous hour to ambush a rival captain instead.  
**Outcome:** If successful, Vesrek “owes” the party—and collects—later. If not, shipments stall and **Selentis** tightens her grip on Nulb’s riverside.

* * *

#### DM Running Notes

*   Vesrek is the **Temple’s profitable conduit**—but he’s running his **own** game (Brotherhood ledgers, selective sales, keeping options open). Push that tension.
*   **Selentis** wants proof and pressure, not a bonfire on the docks; she’ll keep Vesrek **close and compartmentalized** while taxing his land-side operations.
*   **Belsornig** treats Vesrek as **profitable, expendable**; **Tolub** is the practical rival; either can be flipped into short-term allies against the other.
*   The Brothel (Elvanna/Lorveth) is the **paper nexus**—contracts, hush-wards, crawlspaces to warehouses—perfect for heists and counter-heists.

##### **Scene: Docks of Nulb — Twilight, as fog rolls in from the river**

The Crimson Trader rocks gently at anchor. The smell of tar, brine, and something less wholesome wafts from her hull. From the mist steps a tall half-elf in a dark captain’s coat trimmed in crimson braid, silver chains glinting at his chest. A rapier, elegant and coiled like a viper, rides his hip.

He walks toward you with the gait of a man who owns the dock beneath his boots. His dark eyes flick over your gear, your posture—calculating.

* * *

**Marad pauses a short distance away, hands clasped behind his back.**

**“Ah. New faces in Nulb. And so few know the way here by accident.”  
**His voice is calm, rich, and polished, each word deliberately chosen.

* * *

**He takes a slow step closer, the butt of his dagger tapping against his thigh.**

**“I am Captain Marad Vesrek. I command** _**The Crimson Trader**_**—and oversee certain… delicate exchanges in this harbor.”  
**A faint smile plays on his lips, unreadable and practiced.

* * *

**His gaze sharpens, head tilting slightly as if studying a rare coin for imperfections.**

**“You wear steel, but not like river guards. You’ve coin enough for gear, but no trade tokens. And you walk like people used to trouble. So…”  
**He gestures with a gloved hand toward you.

**“Who, precisely, are you? What stirs your boots through Nulb’s mud—and whose cause do you march for?”**

* * *

**He lets the silence linger just a little too long, watching who meets his eyes and who flinches.**

**“Is it coin you seek? Revenge? Or some deluded crusade wrapped in good intentions and bad maps?”**

* * *

**He steps to your side and gazes down the misted docks toward the inland path.**

**“Nulb is no place for the uninvited. The Talons, the Crimson Reapers, the Temple’s errand-men… they all keep watch, in one form or another. And I assure you—so do I.”**

* * *

**He turns back, that faint smirk returning.**

**“Now. Impress me.”  
**His voice hardens just enough to chill the air.  
**“Tell me your names. Or lie. But either way, I’ll know which it is.”**
