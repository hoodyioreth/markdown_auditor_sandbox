22\. Bambus the Basket Maker
============================

Bambus Eldarsson, a respected fisherman and basket maker, resides on the east bank of the [Nigb’s Run](nigbE28099s-run-location.md) in Nigb's Rest. His quaint cottage, surrounded by nature, stands near the Vartia Vineyard. Known for his craftsmanship and contributions to the local market, Bambus plays a significant role in the community, balancing his duties with his dedication to [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md)  and his relationships with key figures in the township.

### Location

A small vine-covered cottage surrounded by rock and brush sits on the northern edge of Nigb's Rest. A fisherman’s dock, hidden in the turn of the [Nigb’s Run](nigbE28099s-run-location.md), features a small fishing boat and racks of drying fish. The serene setting offers a picturesque view of the river and the lush Vartia Vineyard to the east.

### Background

Bambus Eldarsson has lived in Nigb's Rest his entire life. He served in The Battle of Emridy Meadows, an experience that shaped his worldview and deepened his dedication to St. Cuthbert. After the battle, he returned to his peaceful life by the river, focusing on his crafts.

##### Significant Events

*   Served in [The Battle of Emridy Meadows](the-battle-of-emridy-meadows-myth.md)
*   Became a vocal member of the Church of the Cudgel

### Politics of Verbobonc

Bambus plays a vital role in the local economy and social fabric. His baskets are a staple at the Nigb's Rest Square, and his fish supply local taverns and homes. His involvement in community discussions, especially those concerning the environment and land use, highlights his commitment to sustainable living.

##### Political Relationships

*   Mayor [Marcus Greensward](marcus-greensward-person.md): Respects the mayor’s leadership and collaborates on community projects.
*   [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md): Supports House Asbury’s efforts to maintain stability and prosperity in the region.
*   [Yvan Cross](yvan-cross-person.md), Priest of Pelor: Shares a mutual respect despite differing religious views.
*   Cannon [Austol Pengelly](austol-pengelly-person.md) at the Chapel of the Cudgel: A vocal supporter and active member.
*   Commander [Olean Rodrigo](olean-rodrigo-person.md) of Fort Emridy: Holds high regard for the commander’s strategic mind.
*   [Tanithil Mornala](tanithil-mornala-person.md) the Druid: Engages in discussions about land use and environmental preservation.
*   [Tibor Timason](tibor-timason-person.md) of the Black Griffon Inn: Enjoys sharing stories and fish over a pint.
*   [Gigur Nónason](gigur-nonason-person.md) , Guild Master: Collaborates on market strategies and trade opportunities.

##### Community Ties

Bambus’s influence extends beyond Nigb's Rest. His baskets and fish are well-known in nearby villages, contributing to the region’s economy and cultural heritage.

*   Provides baskets and fish to local markets and taverns
*   Participates in trade discussions and market strategies

#### Conclusion

Bambus Eldarsson, the esteemed fisherman and basket maker of Nigb's Rest, embodies the values of hard work, tradition, and community. His contributions to the local market and his dedication to the Old Faith and St. Cuthbert highlight his multifaceted role in the township. Through his relationships and political engagement, Bambus ensures that Nigb's Rest remains a prosperous and harmonious community.

##### Bambus Eldarsson

Bambus is a robust man in his late fifties with a kind face weathered by years of outdoor labor. His hands are calloused from both fishing and weaving, showcasing his dedication to his crafts. He has a calm demeanor, often seen whistling a tune as he works.

*   **Personality:** Kind-hearted and patient, Bambus enjoys the simple pleasures of life. His love for nature and his community is evident in his actions and interactions.
*   **Motivation:** Bambus is driven by a desire to preserve the traditions of basket making and fishing. He finds joy in sharing his skills and knowledge with others, ensuring these crafts are passed down
*   **Relationships:** Bambus maintains strong connections with various figures in Nigb's Rest. His relationships are built on mutual respect and a shared commitment to the community’s well-being.
