Rom Khavernus
=============

### (a.k.a. Hollow Jack)

Rom Khavernus is **Zelt’s right-hand enchanter**, a battle-mage with a sharp tongue and sharper mind. A product of Dyvers’ **New Gentry**, he was born to privilege but now thrives in the underworld, carving a name for himself in the **Circle of Shadows**. While other wizards bury themselves in dusty tomes, Rom embraces the thrill of combat, the dance of diplomacy, and the allure of power. His magical skills are formidable, but it’s his **charisma, wit, and ruthlessness** that make him a rising star.

Among the Crimson Reapers, the men whisper a nickname behind his back—**"Hollow Jack,"** a mocking yet fearful title that suggests his deals and promises are as empty as his conscience.

> "I may not be the strongest mage you’ll meet, but I guarantee I’ll be the last one you cross."
> 
> — Rom

* * *

#### **Description**

Rom Khavernus is a **strikingly handsome Oeridian**, always impeccably dressed in **fine leathers and velvets**, the very image of a noble turned scoundrel. His **well-groomed beard and meticulously styled hair** set him apart from the rougher elements of the Circle. Rings, pendants, and jeweled clasps adorn him—not just for vanity, but because **each is enchanted** in some way. Despite his refined appearance, he’s no soft noble; his **lean, athletic build** suggests a man accustomed to both training and real combat. His weapons—**a rapier, dagger, and quarterstaff**—are all exquisitely made, their craftsmanship betraying both wealth and experience.

> "I don’t need to kill you. I just need to convince you that breathing is optional."
> 
> — Rom

#### **Personality & Motivations**

Rom is **charming, clever, and utterly self-assured**. A gifted conversationalist, he is equally at ease **negotiating a contract, seducing a noblewoman, or incinerating an enemy with a fireball**. While he enjoys the finer things in life—wine, music, the company of intriguing people—he has a **pragmatic ruthlessness** that makes him dangerous. To him, loyalty is **transactional**, and survival is about keeping the right people happy while staying one step ahead of the blade.

Rom’s ultimate goal? **Power. Influence. Freedom.** He’s tired of playing second fiddle to Dyvers’ elites. If Zelt offers him a path to something greater, he’ll follow—for now. But Rom is always watching, always calculating. He respects Zelt, even admires him, but he is **not above replacing him** should the time come.

* * *

### **Role in the Circle of Shadows**

Rom is not just another mage—he is a **battlefield sorcerer, enchanter, and alchemist** who wields both spells and politics with equal finesse. His primary duties include:

*   **Zelt’s Mouthpiece:** Rom is often the **public face of the Circle**, smoothing over disputes, striking deals, and keeping the organization’s interests protected in Dyvers’ upper echelons.
*   **Battlefield Mage:** Unlike most wizards, Rom fights **in the thick of battle**, weaving destruction with **fireballs, lightning bolts, and conjured blades.**
*   **Crafter of Wonders:** Rom is **skilled in alchemy and magical item crafting**, specializing in **wands, scrolls, and potions** that keep the Circle armed and dangerous.
*   **The Circle’s “Whip”** – When the underlings get too ambitious or sloppy, it’s Rom who **delivers the warnings, the punishments, or the final blows**.

* * *

### **Relationships & Alliances**

##### [Zelt](zelt-person.md) **the Bat (Master of the Circle of Shadows)**

*   **Mentor, Patron, and Possible Rival** – Rom owes Zelt **everything**, from his magical training to his place in the Circle. But loyalty only lasts as long as opportunity.
*   Rom handles **Zelt’s diplomatic affairs**, often **sweet-talking the nobility** while Zelt pulls the strings from the shadows.

> "Zelt saved my life once. That means he gets one favor. He’s lucky I still owe him."

##### **Serenda the Ice Witch (Fellow Mage, Rival & Ally)**

*   The **only wizard in the Circle who rivals Rom’s talents**—and they both know it.
*   Their relationship is a mix of **friendly competition, mutual respect, and unresolved tension.**

##### [Felix Fahnor](felix-fahnor-person.md) **(Enforcer, Hitman, and Unsettling Presence)**

*   Rom provides **magic and strategy**, Felix provides **violence and efficiency**. Together, they’re a deadly team.
*   They respect each other, but Rom **keeps a wary eye on Felix**—assassins have a way of **removing obstacles**.

##### [Maldrith](maldrith-person.md) **the Hexed (Priest of Orcus, Circle Occultist)**

*   Rom **doesn’t trust necromancers**, but Maldrith is **useful**.
*   He ensures Maldrith **doesn’t overstep**, knowing that **dead men should stay dead**—unless their souls are worth something.

### **Adventure Hooks**

*   **A Sorcerer’s Gambit:** Rom approaches the adventurers, offering them **a job on behalf of the Circle**. But is it a test—or a trap?
*   **The Circle’s Coup:** Zelt is missing. Rom sees an opportunity. **Will he take control of the Circle, or will others stand in his way?**
*   **A Duel of Magic & Wits:** A noble wizard has slighted Rom. Now, he’s **hunting for spellcasters** to help him **settle the score**.
*   **The Mage’s Vault:** Rom has **discovered a lost vault of magical artifacts** and **needs a few expendable bodies** to retrieve its treasures.

* * *

### **Final Thoughts**

Rom Khavernus is a **spell-wielding rogue in noble’s clothing**, a man caught between his aristocratic past and his criminal present. He is not a mindless killer nor a power-hungry sorcerer—he is a **pragmatic survivor**, always watching, always scheming. Whether as a **formidable ally, a dangerous rival, or a potential betrayer**, Rom adds **depth, intrigue, and danger** to any campaign.

After all, magic is power—and power is everything.

> "You think because I wear velvet and drink fine wine that I’m soft? Let’s see if you still think that after I set you on fire."
> 
> — Rom the Wizard
