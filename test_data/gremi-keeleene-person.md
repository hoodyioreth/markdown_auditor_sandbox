Gremi Keeleene
==============

Keeleene family wizard assigned to caravan delivers to Dyvers and Verbobonc. One of the few Keeleene family wizards. He is young yet and his father Habooni Keeleene wants him to learn the business and to get to know the dealings of humans of Verbobonc and Dyvers and Keoland. He accompanies most merchant deliveries from Tulvar and assists with Meldreeb Keeleene in the business of trade.
