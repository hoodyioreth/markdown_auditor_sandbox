Fens of Tor
===========

The Fens of Tor is a sprawling, foreboding swamp that separates the eastern Viscounty of Verbobonc from the dense [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md). With its winding waterways, ancient secrets, and lurking dangers, the Fens hold a vital role in regional trade, ecological diversity, and dark prophecies tied to the rise of ancient powers. At its heart lies the [Imeryd's Run](imeryd-s-run-location.md) , a navigable river that threads through the swamp, drawing merchants, pirates, and adventurers alike.

##### Lizardfolk Village

Amidst the misty expanse of the [Fens of Tor](fens-of-tor-location.md), the lizardfolk village of Dawnmarsh serves as a beacon of survival, neutrality, and trade. The teachings of [Semuanya](semuanya-person.md), the lizardfolk god of survival and propagation, shape the culture and spiritual life of the settlement. Yet, beneath the surface of this seemingly peaceful village, a shadow grows as tensions between the tribe’s traditionalist leader, Lightkeeper Kassarin the Wise, and the ambitious shaman, Ssarkosh, threaten to engulf Dawnmarsh in chaos.

### Geography and Ecology

The Fens of Tor are an expansive, low-lying wetland shaped by seasonal flooding and the meandering flow of the Imeryds Run.

*   [Imeryd's Run](imeryd-s-run-location.md): This vital tributary flows north from the [Velverdyva River](velverdyva-river-location.md), cutting through the swamp and feeding its labyrinth of pools, bogs, and streams.
*   **Borders:** The swamp sprawls between Verbobonc’s fertile farmlands to the west and the shadowy Gnarley Forest to the east.

##### Ecological Features

*   **Flora:** The swamp teems with bald cypress, tupelo trees, swamp grasses, and creeping vines like trumpetvine and honeysuckle. Parasitic plants such as mistletoe add to its ominous beauty.
*   **Fauna:** Amphibians, reptiles, and fish thrive in its waters, while predatory creatures—both natural and monstrous—lurk in its shadows.

### Dawnmarsh: The Lizardfolk Trade Village

[Dawnmarsh](zassil-torr--torfen--settlement.md) is an enigmatic lizardfolk settlement nestled among the swamp’s islands. Unlike most hostile lizardfolk tribes, Dawnmarsh is a rare bastion of trade and cautious diplomacy, though tensions simmer beneath its stilted houses.

##### Features of Dawnmarsh

**Trade Goods**

*   **Regenerative Elixirs:** Mysterious potions brewed from swamp herbs, said to accelerate healing.
*   **Riding Lizards:** Techniques for taming these agile mounts are offered to trusted outsiders.
*   **Poisons and Guides:** Extracted from the swamp’s deadly flora and fauna, these are valuable tools for navigation or combat.
*   **Culture Clash:** Foreign traders spark tension, as young lizardfolk question the old ways while warmongers scheme to dominate.

##### Points of Interest

*   **Inn of the Emerald Scale:** Run by a friendly lizardfolk innkeeper, this gathering place is where adventurers can glean useful information.
*   **Visionary of Qalnossk:** An elderly prophet warns of the prophesied return of Rasskarth, the Devourer the Great Lizard King and his connection to the ancient holy place of Zyssaneth.
*   **Tensions of Faith**: Young lizardfolk, enamored with dreams of power, plot to transform Dawnmarsh from a sanctuary into a warlike cult under Rasskarth and the Fens’ Black Dragon Vorthlyxx.

### Merchant Trade and Illicit Activity

##### Navigating the Imeryds Run

The Imeryds Run is a lifeline for regional commerce and criminal enterprises alike.

##### Legitimate Trade

*   Merchants transport timber, grains, and textiles from Verbobonc and Dyvers to Nulb.
*   Barges loaded with crafted goods and exotic wares often stop in Nulb or Dawnmarsh before continuing southward.

##### Illicit Activity

*   **Piracy**: The Fens are a haven for river pirates, who prey on poorly defended vessels.
*   **Smuggling**: Contraband, including stolen goods and slaves, is ferried covertly to the Temple of Elemental Evil.

##### Lightkeeper Kassarin the Wise

*   **Appearance:** A tall, aged lizardfolk woman with weathered scales and bright orange-fringed robes. She carries a staff adorned with sun symbols.
*   **Personality:** Wise and patient, Kassarin is a diplomat at heart, though her age and exhaustion sometimes leave her vulnerable to the village’s growing tensions.

**Role and Background**

*   **Leader of Dawnmarsh:** Kassarin has guided the village for over six decades, mediating disputes and expanding trade with human merchants in Ashton.
*   **Reluctant Reformer:** While Kassarin values Dawnmarsh’s neutrality and traditions, she has made compromises—such as restricting cannibalistic funerals—to maintain good relations with outsiders.
