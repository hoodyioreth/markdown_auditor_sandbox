Caltaran
========

Caltaran sits on the bank of the Serault River. It is ruled by a noble family, the Grandhearths, who also look after farmers, riverfolks, and woodsmen in the “westlands” across the Serault, some of the most remote portions of the Dyvers, The Free Lands .   A small stone bridge crosses the Serault, and a toll is collected from all mounted, cart, and wagon traffic that wishes to cross that fern-lined river.   Across the Velverdyva River is the Furyondian town of Dianrift. The two are linked by a ferry, with Dyvers collecting the toll of those departing from Caltaran, and Furyondy receiving the payments of those from Dianrift.
