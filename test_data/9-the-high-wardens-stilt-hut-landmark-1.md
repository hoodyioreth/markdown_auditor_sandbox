9\. The High Warden’s Stilt Hut
===============================

A central structure in Zassil’torr, the stilt hut of High Warden Ssalissar the Vigilant is both a commanding vantage point and an administrative hub for the lizardfolk village. Perched on tall wooden stilts above the swamp, the hut symbolizes authority, tradition, and survival.

### Location Description

The High Warden’s stilt hut is an imposing yet practical structure, constructed from swampwood reinforced with clay and woven reeds. The walls are adorned with symbols of Semuanya, the lizardfolk god, carved with meticulous care. A roof of tightly bound reeds provides shelter, and the platform is guarded by railings made of lashed branches.

*   **Raised Platform:** Accessible by a ladder or crude ramp, the elevated hut keeps the High Warden safe from swamp predators and offers a clear view of the village and surrounding marshlands.
*   **Symbolic Decor:** The hut is adorned with symbols of survival and vigilance, including trophies from past hunts and ceremonial totems.
*   **Interior:** Inside, the hut contains a simple wooden table for council meetings, maps drawn on stretched skins, and a modest sleeping area for High Warden Ssalissar.

### Role in the Village

The High Warden’s stilt hut is the nerve center of Zassil’torr. From here, Ssalissar oversees the village’s defense, coordinates hunting parties, mediates disputes, and ensures adherence to the will of Semuanya.

*   **Defense Coordination:** Monitoring threats and directing sentries from the watch platforms.
*   **Village Council:** A place where lizardfolk elders and leaders gather to discuss pressing matters.
*   **Ceremonial Space:** Occasionally used for religious observances honoring [Semuanya](semuanya-person.md).

### Background

The stilt hut has served generations of High Wardens as a place of leadership and sanctuary. High Warden Ssalissar expanded its purpose to include council meetings and the storage of critical resources like swamp maps and ceremonial relics.

##### Politics and Relationships

High Warden Ssalissar wields considerable authority over the village, balancing traditional worship of Semuanya with the rising tensions from Ssarkosh, the Swamp Seer, and his followers of Sess’innek.

##### Conflict with Ssarkosh

While Ssalissar remains focused on the survival of the village, Ssarkosh’s growing influence threatens to fracture the community. The High Warden uses the hut to strategize ways to counteract the shaman’s demonic allure while avoiding open conflict.

##### Loyalists and Allies

Many lizardfolk warriors and elders regularly visit the hut to seek guidance or pledge their loyalty to Ssalissar. The High Warden’s measured leadership has garnered respect from most of the village, though some younger lizardfolk are tempted by Ssarkosh’s promises of power.

##### Secrets of the Stilt Hut

*   **Hidden Cache:** Beneath a loose plank in the hut’s floor is a hidden cache containing rare swamp herbs, emergency provisions, and a ceremonial dagger used for sacred rites.
*   **Ssalissar’s Dilemma:** The High Warden secretly doubts whether Semuanya’s passive tenets of survival can protect the village from the growing dangers posed by Sess’innek’s followers and external threats.

##### Adventure Hooks

*   **Call to Action:** The High Warden enlists the adventurers to infiltrate Ssarkosh’s inner circle and gather intelligence.
*   **Sacred Relic:** A relic of Semuanya is stolen from the stilt hut, and Ssalissar tasks the adventurers with its recovery.
*   **Loyalty Test:** Ssalissar tests the adventurers' loyalty by assigning them a dangerous mission, such as escorting a supply caravan through hostile swamp territory.

#### Conclusion

The High Warden’s stilt hut is a cornerstone of Zassil’torr’s leadership and culture. It embodies the lizardfolk’s resilience and dedication to survival while serving as a backdrop for political intrigue, spiritual conflict, and potential adventure.

##### High Warden Ssalissar the Vigilant

Ssalissar is a regal, older lizardfolk with dark green scales streaked with white. Her piercing yellow eyes are sharp with intelligence, and her imposing posture radiates authority. She wears a ceremonial bone crest on her head and wields a staff topped with a polished obsidian egg, symbolizing Semuanya’s blessing.

##### Role and Personality

*   Role: High Warden of Zassil’torr, protector of its traditions and people.
*   Personality: Calm, decisive, and deeply reverent of Semuanya. Ssalissar is fiercely protective of her village and prioritizes its survival above all.

##### Motivations

*   Maintain the village’s neutrality and traditions.
*   Counteract Ssarkosh’s divisive influence without alienating his followers.
*   Preserve the lizardfolk’s way of life amid growing outside threats.
