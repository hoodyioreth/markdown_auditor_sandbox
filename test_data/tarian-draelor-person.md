Tarian Draelor
==============

### (a.k.a. Dashblade)

Tarian "Dashblade" Draelor is an enigmatic figure residing at the Laughing Moon Inn in the village of [Ostverk](ostverk-settlement.md). This shadowy yet charismatic ranger-fighter straddles the line between ruthless mercenary and loyal ally. As a potential recruit for adventuring parties, he offers unparalleled skill with his twin blades, a keen eye for danger, and an unyielding hatred for goblinkind.

##### Overview of Tarian "Dashblade" Draelor

*   **Height & Build:** Tarian stands at an athletic 5'11", his lean but muscular frame betraying a life of hardship and battle.
*   **Distinctive Features:** He sports dark brown, shoulder-length hair, a sly grin that hints at his cunning nature, and piercing, storm-gray eyes that seem to measure everyone they meet.
*   **Clothing and Gear:** Dressed in a blackened chain shirt and a weathered leather cloak, Tarian blends into the shadows with ease. His weapons, two battle-worn short swords, remain sheathed at his hips, and a longbow is slung across his back. A scar across his left cheek adds to his aura of danger.

##### Role at the Laughing Moon Inn

Tarian is a regular fixture at the [9\. The Laughing Moon Inn](9-the-laughing-moon-inn-landmark.md), often found seated in a dimly lit corner nursing a tankard of ale. He trades stories of adventure with other patrons, occasionally offering his services for a price. Despite his sharp tongue, he is a trustworthy source of local lore and an adept dungeon guide.

### Background and History

##### Origins in the Gnarley Forest

Tarian hails from a remote settlement deep within the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md), where he learned the ways of survival and combat from a young age. His village was overrun by goblins, leaving him orphaned and instilling in him a lifelong hatred for goblinkind.

##### Life as a Mercenary

After the fall of his village, Tarian wandered the Viscounty of Verbobonc, honing his skills as a ranger and mercenary. His reputation for completing dangerous contracts earned him the nickname "Dashblade," a moniker derived from his lethal efficiency and the jet-black hilts of his short swords._

> "Why fight fair when you can fight smart? I prefer to do both."

_

### Personality and Motivation

##### Personality Traits

*   **Charismatic and Sly**: Tarian’s wit and charm make him an engaging storyteller, though his sharp tongue can occasionally land him in trouble.
*   **Wary but Loyal:** While slow to trust, once he commits to a cause, Tarian becomes a dependable and resourceful ally.
*   **Fearless Yet Restless:** He is driven by an unquenchable wanderlust, rarely staying in one place for long.

_

> "Rules? I prefer guidelines... and I ignore those too."

_

#### Motivations

*   **Vengeance**: Tarian seeks every opportunity to eradicate goblinkind, viewing them as the ultimate scourge of the Gnarley Forest.
*   **Exploration**: His insatiable curiosity drives him to explore forgotten ruins, hidden dungeons, and untamed wilds.
*   **Self-Preservation:** Above all, Tarian values survival and will act pragmatically when necessary to ensure it.

_

> "I’ve seen dark corners of dungeons you wouldn’t dare whisper about. And guess what? I came out richer."

_

##### Relationships at the Laughing Moon Inn

*   **Glarian (Innkeeper):** Tarian shares a mutual respect with Glarian, often exchanging tales of adventure and danger over drinks.
*   [Baris Stone](baris-stone-person.md) (Captain of the Guard): Tarian’s skill as a ranger has earned him Feath’s grudging respect, though their interactions are often tinged with tension.
*   **Local Patrons:** Tarian is well-known among the inn’s regulars, who admire his bravery and skill while remaining wary of his mercurial nature.

##### Abilities and Skills

*   **Proficiencies**: Exceptional with short swords, longbow, and stealth.
*   **Tracking**: Highly skilled at tracking creatures, especially goblins and other humanoids.
*   **Dungeon Expertise:** Adept at navigating and surviving in hostile environments, including dungeons and wilderness.
*   **Stealth**: Tarian’s blackened chain shirt and natural agility make him a master of moving unseen.

_

> "Goblins are vermin, and I’m just the exterminator."
