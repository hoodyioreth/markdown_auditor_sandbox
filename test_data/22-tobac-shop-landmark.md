22\. Tobac Shop
===============

> The rich smell of burned pipeweed can be detected from down the block, no doubt owing to the proprietor of the tobac shop, and their clientele, indulging in their favorite vice on the premises. Behind its doors, the crowded shelves are stocked with pipes, hookahs, cigars, cigarettes, cigarillos, and pouches of chewing tobacco. The bounty of rich smokables wait to be purchased and sampled, preferably after strong drinks

Nestled within the quaint gnome village of Sheernobb, the Tobac Shop offers a sensory journey into the world of pipeweed, cigars, and other smokables. Owned and operated by the eccentric yet knowledgeable Bunzbart, this cozy shop is a haven for those who appreciate the finer nuances of tobacco and its many herbal companions. The rich aromas of burning pipeweed linger in the air, welcoming all who enter.

### Location and Atmosphere

Located on a bustling side street in Sheernobb, the Tobac Shop is impossible to miss due to the ever-present cloud of aromatic smoke wafting from its open windows. The exterior is modest, with a wooden sign carved with swirling vines and leaves, depicting a gnome puffing on an elaborate pipe. Inside, the shop is dimly lit, with shelves overflowing with pipes of various shapes and sizes, hookahs, cigars, cigarettes, cigarillos, and pouches of finely cut chewing tobacco. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, with the air thick with the sweet and earthy scents of premium pipeweed.

*   **Herbal Blends:** Bunzbart also offers a range of herbal smoking blends, each with unique properties. These blends are often used by those seeking the medicinal or calming effects of certain herbs, including blends for relaxation, focus, and minor pain relief.

#### Security and Guard Patrols

Given the value of some of the more exotic and rare tobacco blends in the shop, Bunzbart has taken steps to ensure its protection. The shop is magically warded with gnomish illusions that obscure its interior from prying eyes. Additionally, a small enchanted bell hangs by the door, alerting Bunzbart to any customers or intruders. The village militia also keeps a close eye on the shop, recognizing its importance as a cultural and social hub in Sheernobb.

### Relationships

##### Community Bonds

*   **Customers:** Bunzbart has built a loyal customer base in Sheernobb, with many gnomes and visiting halflings frequenting his shop to enjoy the quality tobacco and the relaxed atmosphere.
*   **Headgnome** [Glennddarc Keeleene](glennddarc-keeleene-person.md): Bunzbart and Glennddarc share a mutual respect, with the Headgnome often visiting the shop for a relaxing smoke and conversation about village matters.

##### Regional Connections

*   **Herbalists and Traders:** Bunzbart maintains strong connections with herbalists in the [Greenway Valley](greenway-valley-settlement.md), ensuring a steady supply of rare herbs for his blends.
*   [Druids of the Gnarley Forest](druids-of-the-gnarley-forest-organization.md): Bunzbart has a cordial relationship with the local druids, who occasionally provide him with unique herbs in exchange for his tobacco blends.

#### Bandit Raid Opinions

Bunzbart is concerned about the increasing bandit activity on the roads leading to Sheernobb. He worries that the disruption of trade could affect the supply of rare herbs and tobacco coming into the village.

##### Opinions on Bandit Raids

> _“These bandits, they’re a nuisance to everyone. Disrupting trade and making life harder for us all.”  
> __“I’ve always said, we need more protection on those roads. It’s the lifeline of Sheernobb.”  
> __“Every lost shipment means fewer herbs for my blends. It’s time someone dealt with these scoundrels.”  
> __“The guards do what they can, but they’re spread thin. I wish we had more support from Verbobonc.”_ _“If these raids continue, we might have to start growing our own herbs again. It’s been decades since we did that.”_

#### Conclusion

The Tobac Shop in Sheernobb is more than just a place to purchase fine tobacco; it’s a sanctuary for those who appreciate the art of smoking and the rich history behind it. Under Bunzbart’s care, the shop has become a cornerstone of the community, providing both a product and a gathering place for the gnomes of Sheernobb. With his vast knowledge of tobacco lore and herbalism, Bunzbart continues to enrich the lives of his customers, one pipe at a time.

#### Products and Services

##### Pipeweed and Tobacco Varieties

*   **Greenleaf Gold:** A premium pipeweed known for its smooth, mellow flavor with subtle hints of pine and earth. Price: 2 gp per pouch.
*   **Mountain's Breath:** A robust tobacco blend with a sharp, invigorating taste that leaves a refreshing aftertaste. Price: 3 gp per pouch.
*   **Sweetroot Cigarillos:** Small, tightly rolled cigars infused with honey and cinnamon, perfect for a quick smoke. Price: 1 gp per pack of five.
*   **Gnarleywood Hookah Blend:** A mix of herbs and tobacco designed for use in hookahs, with a rich, smoky flavor and a hint of spice. Price: 5 gp per jar.
*   **Traveler’s Chew:** A popular chewing tobacco among adventurers, known for its long-lasting flavor and mild stimulating effects. Price: 1 gp per pouch.

##### **Proprietor:** Bunzbart

Bunzbart, the aged gnome who runs the Tobac Shop, is a character as rich in history as the products he sells. With a long, braided beard tinged with yellow from years of smoking, he is often seen with a pipe in his mouth, a steady stream of smoke drifting from his nose and ears. His eyes, slightly glazed, give the impression of a gnome who is always deep in thought or perhaps lost in the memories of long-gone days.

*   **Personality:** Bunzbart is known for his laid-back demeanor and seemingly distant focus when conversing, often gazing past those he speaks to as if contemplating something far away. However, beneath this dreamy exterior lies a sharp mind and an unparalleled expertise in tobacco lore. He is passionate about the history and effects of various herbs, often discussing their origins, uses, and potential health benefits with customers. Despite his relaxed nature, Bunzbart is meticulous in his craft, ensuring that only the finest products are sold in his shop.
*   **Motivation:** Bunzbart's primary motivation is to share his love of pipeweed and tobacco with others. He sees his shop as a sanctuary for like-minded individuals who appreciate the art of smoking and the relaxation it brings. Additionally, Bunzbart has a deep respect for the history of tobacco and its ties to herbalism, and he enjoys educating his customers on these connections.
*   **Political Relationships:** While Bunzbart stays out of most political affairs, he maintains a friendly relationship with the leaders of Sheernobb, including Headgnome Glennddarc Keeleene. He provides the village militia with calming herbal blends to help them relax after long patrols. His shop is also a favorite among the local gnomes, who see it as a place of camaraderie and relaxation.
