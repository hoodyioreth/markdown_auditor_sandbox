L1-5B. Stew-Hearth Barracks
===========================

Both doors stand spiked open. The room smells of grease and smoke—once a kitchen and mess, now a den. Pallets line the walls; most orcs snore in heaps while two squat by a blackened stove, cleaving meat into a bubbling stew pot. A wall-length mosaic shows a fantastic city: curtain-walls bristling with tall spires, and yet taller needles rising from the city’s heart. Along the east wall, five battered chests sit in a row; one chest is missing a plank, the loose board propped against the wall. Flecks of rust spot the floor.

Former **kitchen/mess** converted into an **orc barracks**. Both doors are **spiked open** for fast response. **Eight orcs** are assigned here—six asleep on wall-lined pallets, **two awake cooking** at the blackened stove. A long **Suel mosaic** of a fantastical walled city runs the length of one wall (useful lore cue). Five **battered chests** sit along the east wall: common kit, bones and leather scraps, a small crate of **coin**, a chest with **acid/rust** traces (echoing 5A), and one holding [Thuldon Rockspitter](thuldon-rockspitter-person.md)**’s dwarf-sized chain mail**. Any loud noise or a horn/howl wakes the room; one orc will try to sprint to **Room 4** to blow the bray-horn while the rest form a line using kitchen tables as **half cover**. This room will reinforce any nearby fight on your response clock.

#### Chests & contents (east wall)

All are iron-banded and dented.

*   **Chest A (unlocked):** **soup bones**, knuckle piles, butcher’s cleaver; **bits of damaged leather** cut into thong.
*   **Chest B (locked, DC 10 / STR DC 14):** mixed **loot** in a small **crate inside**: **17 ep, 37 sp, 52 cp** bound with gut string.
*   **Chest C (unlocked):** **field kit**—bowls, ladles, 2 oil flasks, 1 wineskin of rot-spirits (see 5A effect).
*   **Chest D (plank missing):** the **loose board** leans nearby; interior stains and **acid pitting** match the **rust flecks** on the floor (Perception DC 11). Once held caustic jars (removed).
*   **Chest E (locked, DC 12 / STR DC 16):** a neatly folded **dwarf-sized chain mail**—**Thuldon’s work** (superb rings; fits a dwarf, counts as **chain mail**, 55 lb; sale value **75 gp** to a collector, **150 gp** if returned to the right dwarves).

**Clue:** The acid/rust traces mirror the melted hinge observed in **Room 5A**—Vhakra once stored caustics here.

* * *

#### Tactics & response

*   The two cooks grab **cleavers (treat as handaxes)** and bark: “Huh? A noise?” → “Found you! Now try and hide from this!”
*   If a round passes, one orc sprints to **Room 4** to grab/blow the **bray-horn** while the others form a **kitchen line** (two behind the table as half cover).
*   Use your dungeon clock: fighting here triggers **5A** in **2 rounds**, **5C/5D** in **4**.

**Orc barks:** “Bleed! Bleed and die!” “Die, metalman!” “Stop moving, stupid human!”

* * *

#### Checks & clues

*   **Survival DC 10:** The stew is mostly **boar and bone**; a few **eel pieces** (marsh fare).
*   **Investigation DC 12:** Hidden **border glyphs** in the city mosaic—rune-inlays of **Wee Jas** worked into the architectural scrolls.
*   **Perception DC 12:** A small **jawbone howl-whistle** tucked on a stove hook (functions as horn).

* * *

#### Treasure summary

*   Coins: **17 ep, 37 sp, 52 cp** (Chest B).
*   **Thuldon’s dwarf chain** (Chest E).
*   **4 rations** (stew if saved 2 **oil flasks**; **rot-spirits** wineskin.

* * *

#### Quick 5e plugs

*   **Orcs (8)** as MM. Optional spice: once/encounter **Moon-Frenzy** (+2 damage on next hit when first bloodied).
*   **Kitchen cover:** Flipping the prep table (object interaction) grants **half cover** to two orcs until moved (AC +2).

* * *

#### Hooks

*   Returning Thuldon’s chain earns advantage with dwarven smiths and may soften **Thuldon Rockspitter** if rescued later.
*   The mosaic’s city script can be used as a **puzzle key** or as proof of Suel provenance for **Verbobonc** authorities.

##### What’s here

*   **Orcs (8).** Six asleep; **2 awake** at the stew. Loud speech wakes the two by the stove; steel-on-stone or any horn/howl wakes all.
*   **Doors spiked open.** Nails and splinters jam the hinges; **DC 12** to pull free quietly, **DC 8** to kick loose (noisy).
*   **Kitchen relics.** Cracked kettles, hanging hooks, a brick stove. The stew is **edible** (counts as 4 rations if salvaged).
*   **City mosaic.** Suel artistry in lapis and malachite; **History/Religion DC 12** identifies pre-Cataclysm Suel style (possible **Wee Jas / Lendor** script hidden in borderwork).
*   **Light/Sound.** A brazier by the stove; heavy snoring elsewhere—stealth is feasible if the party stays quiet.
