7\. Greensward Manor
====================

Greensward Manor is a historic and influential estate of the Cienega Valley area is located in the township of Nigb's Rest. Home to Mayor [Marcus Greensward](marcus-greensward-person.md) and his family, the manor has been a cornerstone of the community since the Greenswards were granted lordship over Asbury Meadows. The manor serves as both a family residence and a council meeting hall, playing a crucial role in the governance and development of the village.

### Description

_Location  
_Greensward Manor is situated just west of the open market square, providing easy access to the heart of the village. The manor is surrounded by lush gardens and farmland, reflecting the Greensward family's agricultural roots and commitment to the community.

##### Manor and Grounds

The manor itself is an impressive two-story stone building with a stately facade. The front entrance is framed by tall columns and a large oak door, opening into a spacious foyer. The interior is elegantly decorated, featuring polished wooden floors, high ceilings, and large windows that allow natural light to flood the rooms. The grounds include a stable, servant quarters, and expansive farmland that supports the household and the community.

### The Greensward Family

The manor is home to Mayor Marcus Greensward, his wife, their two grown sons, one of whom is married with three children, and the household staff. The family is well-respected in the community, known for their leadership and dedication to the well-being of Cienega Valley.

### History

Greensward Manor has been in the family since the Greenswards were given lordship over Asbury Meadows. Over the years, the manor has witnessed significant events and developments, playing a central role in the village's history and governance.

##### Key Historical Events

*   **Founding:** Granted lordship over Asbury Meadows, establishing Greensward Manor.
*   **Election of Marcus Greensward:** Elected as the Land-steward, solidifying the family’s influence in the community.
*   **Community Hub:** The manor has served as a meeting hall and refuge during times of trouble.

### Politics of Verbobonc

Greensward Manor is a significant player in the politics of Verbobonc, influencing local and regional dynamics through its leadership and strategic alliances.

##### Political Influence

*   [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md): Close ties with House Asbury, benefiting from their protection and support. His familial ties to Lady [Elinor Asbury](elinor-asbury-person.md) strengthen his position and influence within the region. As a family member beholden to House Asbury, Marcus works closely with Lady Elinor to ensure the prosperity and stability of Nigb's Rest.
*   [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md): Navigates a delicate relationship with House Milinous, maintaining neutrality and diplomacy.
*   **Local Governance:** Central to the governance and development of Nigb's Rest, working closely with key figures like Olean Rodrigo, Austol Pengelly, Yvan Cross, Tanithil Mornala, and Gigur Nónason.

### Relationships

_Key Figures and Allies_

*   Commander [Olean Rodrigo](olean-rodrigo-person.md) (Fort Commander): Collaborates on security and defense matters.
*   Canon [Austol Pengelly](austol-pengelly-person.md) (Church of the Cudgel): Works together on community and spiritual affairs.
*   Yvan Cross (Shining Temple of Pelor): Coordinates on religious and community initiatives.
*   Druid [Tanithil Mornala](tanithil-mornala-person.md) (Hearth of Obad-Hai): Balances traditional and religious influences in the community.
*   [Gigur Nónason](gigur-nonason-person.md) (Vintner's Guild): Collaborates on economic and trade matters.

##### Community Relationships

The Greensward family is deeply involved in the community, supporting local initiatives and fostering a sense of unity and cooperation.

*   **Community Support:** Provides leadership and guidance, acting as a central figure in village affairs.
*   **Militia Involvement:** The sons and hired hand are active members of the militia, ensuring the village’s security.
*   **Agricultural Leadership:** Promotes agricultural development and prosperity, benefiting the entire community.

##### Politics Throughout the Viscounty of Verbobonc

Greensward Manor's influence extends beyond Nigb's Rest, impacting regional politics and trade throughout the Viscounty of Verbobonc.

##### Regional Impact

*   **Economic Development:** Drives economic growth and stability in the region through strategic alliances and trade initiatives.
*   **Political Alliances:** Maintains strong relationships with key political figures and houses, ensuring the village’s interests are protected.
*   **Social Stability:** Promotes social cohesion and stability through community leadership and support.

#### Conclusion

Marcus Greensward stands as a pillar of Nigb's Rest, guiding the community with wisdom, dedication, and a deep sense of duty. His strong familial ties to House Asbury, coupled with his fair-minded leadership, ensure the prosperity and stability of the village. Through strategic alliances, community support, and a commitment to the well-being of Cienega Valley, Marcus Greensward continues to be a central and influential figure in the region.

**Mayor** [Marcus Greensward](marcus-greensward-person.md)  
Marcus Greensward is a wise and respected leader, known for his dedication to the community and his fair-minded approach to governance. He is a skilled farmer and a shrewd politician, navigating the complex dynamics of Nigb's Rest with grace and determination.

*   **Personality:** Wise, fair, and dedicated.
*   **Motivation:** Ensuring the prosperity and stability of Nigb's Rest.
*   **Background:** Elected as the Land-steward for the enfranchised peasants of the valley, cousin to Lady Baroness [Elinor Asbury](elinor-asbury-person.md).

### The Greensward Family Members

*   **Mrs. Greensward:** A supportive and compassionate woman, deeply involved in community affairs.
*   **Sons and Family:** Proud of their status, involved in the militia, and dedicated to the family’s agricultural endeavors.
*   **Servants:** Two servant girls and a hired farmhand, all quartered in the stable loft and members of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md).

##### Butler Radu Platon

Radu Platon has served the Greenswards for [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md) for 20 years. He is known for his meticulous organization and dedication to the manor's operations.

*   **Personality:** Proud, organized, and slightly avaricious.
*   **Motivation:** Maintaining the manor's reputation and ensuring smooth operations.
*   **Background:** Highly organized, with a planner full of events and responsibilities.
