9\. The Jewel of Asbury Guardhouse
==================================

> The two story stout stone structure stands 30 feet on a side with few narrow braced windows. Its front wide entrance mills half a dozen vigilant guards ready to address any sound of alarm. Well prepared, they wear chain over leather with House Asbury surcoats, spears and short swords. A few have crossbows within hands reach. Above, the House Asbury banner proudly stands as a symbol that these lands are under the protection of House Asbury. One of the guardsmen steps forward to the edge of the landing. Often, guardsmen, local craftsmen, landowners and couriers come and go.

The Jewel of Asbury Guardhouse is a stout stone structure in the township of [Nigb's Rest](nigb-s-rest-settlement.md), serving as a critical symbol of security and authority under [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md). As tensions rise due to territorial disputes, this guardhouse has become a focal point of protection and order in the region.

### Description

The Jewel of Asbury Guardhouse is strategically located in the heart of Nigb's Rest, near the Black Griffon Inn and the central market square. Its position ensures maximum visibility and accessibility for both the guards and the townsfolk.

##### Exterior Description

The guardhouse is a two-story stone structure, 30 feet on each side, with few narrow, braced windows. Its front entrance is wide, often milling with half a dozen vigilant guards. The guards are well-prepared, wearing chain mail over leather with House Asbury surcoats, and are equipped with spears, short swords, and crossbows. Above the entrance, the House Asbury banner proudly flies, symbolizing the protection and authority of the house over these lands.

##### Interior Description

Inside, the guardhouse is functional and well-organized. The ground floor houses the armory, barracks, and a common room for the guards. The upper floor includes offices, a briefing room, and private quarters for the captain and lieutenant. The interior is sparsely decorated, focusing on utility and efficiency.

### History

The Jewel of Asbury Guardhouse was established to reinforce the security of Nigb's Rest amidst rising tensions between House Asbury and House Milinous. Lady Elinor Asbury's investment in the guardhouse signifies her commitment to protecting the region and maintaining order.

##### Significant Events

*   **Establishment:** Built to secure Nigb's Rest and assert House Asbury's authority.
*   **Rising Tensions:** Played a crucial role during territorial disputes with House Milinous.
*   **Current Role:** Continues to serve as the primary bastion of security in the village.

### Politics of Verbobonc

The guardhouse plays a pivotal role in the political landscape of the Viscounty of Verbobonc, balancing the interests of House Asbury, House Milinous, and the local community.

##### Regional Impact

*   [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md): Reinforces their authority and protection in the region.
*   [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md): Serves as a deterrent to their territorial ambitions. While they both serve as leaders and protectors of their respective houses, the rivalry between House Asbury and House Milinous often places them on opposing sides.
*   **Local Community:** Ensures peace and order, gaining the trust of villagers.

##### Balancing Relationships

The guardhouse navigates complex political relationships, maintaining neutrality while ensuring the security and prosperity of Nigb's Rest.

*   **Neutrality:** Avoids direct conflicts while asserting authority.
*   **Security and Prosperity:** Focuses on the well-being of the community.

#### Conclusion

The Jewel of Asbury Guardhouse stands as a bastion of security and order in Nigb's Rest. Under the leadership of Captain Jareth Thorne and Lieutenant Brander Fenn, the guardhouse ensures the safety and prosperity of the village. Through strategic alliances and careful diplomacy, the guardhouse navigates the complex political landscape of the Viscounty of Verbobonc, maintaining peace and asserting the authority of House Asbury.
