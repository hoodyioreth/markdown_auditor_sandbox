37: Pasmaries Beehives
======================

Pasmarie's Beehives, located in the village of Sheernobb, is renowned for producing exceptionally sweet honey. Run by the dedicated Pasmarie Clee, this establishment ensures the bees are well cared for, producing copious amounts of honey that supports both the local community and the Honey Haven Inn. Run by the dedicated druid Pasmarie Clee, this establishment ensures the bees are well cared for and protected by swarms of bees that see Pasmarie as their queen. The honey produced here is of divine nature, capable of creating a special honey water potion that removes curses and heals the heart and soul.

### Location and Ambiance

Pasmarie's Beehives are situated a quarter mile southeast of Sheernobb, surrounded by verdant fields and wildflowers. The hives are neatly arranged in rows, with the buzzing of bees creating a harmonious backdrop. The area is well-maintained, with paths leading to each hive and small benches for visitors to observe the bees at work.

##### Interior and Layout

*   **Beehives:** A dozen artificial hives, most constructed by Pasmarie's grandmother, stand in orderly rows. These hives are home to large honeybees known for their exceptional honey production.
*   **Processing Area:** A small building adjacent to the hives houses the equipment for processing and storing honey. Shelves are lined with jars of honey, beeswax, and other bee-related products.
*   **Bee Protection:** The bees are kept docile by nature clerics and druids who work with Pasmarie, ensuring visitors can safely observe the hives without disturbing the bees.

### Pasmarie's Beehives: Special Features

##### Bee Protection

The beehives are as well-hidden as the gnome silver mines and are fiercely protected by swarms of bees. Pasmarie, a druid of nature specializing in the harmony and love of bees, is seen as the queen bee by the hive. The bees will fiercely defend her and obey her commands.

##### Druidic Magic

Pasmarie can cast her druidic magic through the bees, enchanting them with divine nature powers. This bond between Pasmarie and her bees is a testament to her deep connection with nature. Pasmarie's Beehives stand as a testament to the Clee family's dedication to the well-being of Sheernobb. Despite the challenges posed by bandit activity and regional unrest, the beehives remain a cornerstone of the village, providing essential honey and fostering a sense of unity and resilience.

##### Divine Honey

The honey harvested from Pasmarie's bees is of divine nature. It is said that no human-made honey can match its taste. Pasmarie can create and enchant a special honey water potion, known as Elixir of the Sacred Hive, that removes any curse and heals the heart and soul.

##### Elixir of the Sacred Hive

*   Price: 250 gold pieces
*   **Effects:** Removes any curse, heals the heart and soul, providing a sense of peace and rejuvenation.
