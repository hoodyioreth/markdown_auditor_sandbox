Alber Guldenstern
=================

Alber Guildenstern is a **shadow within the darker corners of Verbobonc**, a name whispered in the backrooms of taverns, in hushed exchanges between criminals, and in the desperate prayers of those who have crossed him. A man of **plain features, small stature, and unassuming presence**, Alber has made anonymity his most powerful weapon. He is **never seen, never heard, yet always succeeds**.

Arriving in **Verbobonc City from** [Dyvers, Free Lands of](dyvers2C-free-lands-of-organization.md) **seven years ago**, he established himself as a **freelance operative for the city’s criminal underworld**, selling his services to those with coin. But unlike common thugs or mercenaries, Alber is **a professional**—his work is precise, his reputation unblemished, and his fees exorbitant. Now, he serves **Lakash Quallad**, a merchant and secret agent of the **Temple of Elemental Evil**, using his skills to **sow chaos and dissent within the Viscounty**​.

* * *

### **Appearance & Demeanor**

##### **Physical Description**

*   **Small and forgettable:** Standing **just below average height**, Alber is the type of man whose **face fades from memory the moment you look away**.
*   **Plain features:** Neither ugly nor handsome, his expression is **perpetually neutral**, making it difficult to read his thoughts.
*   **Dark, practical clothing:** Always dressed in **muted tones of brown and grey**, designed to **blend into a crowd and disappear**.
*   **Soft, deliberate movements:** Every step is measured, every gesture controlled—**Alber wastes no motion**.

##### **Personality**

*   **Coldly professional:** Alber **never acts on impulse** and **never kills without purpose**. He does **only what is required** by his contract.
*   **Detached and pragmatic:** He has **no personal loyalties** and sees the world as **a web of contracts, debts, and obligations**.
*   **Speaks only when necessary:** Alber believes words are **as dangerous as weapons**, and uses them sparingly.
*   **Favors efficiency over cruelty:** He is **not sadistic**, but **not merciful either**. The job gets done, nothing more, nothing less.

_"Violence is a tool. I prefer not to use it. But make no mistake—it is a tool I know well."_

* * *

### **Background & History**

##### **Origins in Dyvers**

*   Alber's **early life is a mystery**, even among those who employ him.
*   He likely **honed his craft in Dyvers**, a city rife with **thieves’ guilds, political intrigue, and assassins for hire**.
*   Some suspect he was once **a failed noble**, others believe he was **a common street rat who learned to survive**.

##### **Arrival in Verbobonc**

*   **Seven years ago**, Alber appeared in Verbobonc, taking **high-risk, high-paying contracts** for local thieves' guilds and corrupt merchants.
*   He quickly gained a **reputation for precision and discretion**, making him one of the **most sought-after fixers in the city**.
*   Unlike other criminals, he has **no known allies or crew**—Alber **works alone**.

* * *

### **Motivations & Methods**

##### **Why He Works for Lakash Quallad**

*   Lakash Quallad, **a noble-turned-merchant with ties to the Temple of Elemental Evil**, has **hired Alber to spread discord within Verbobonc**​.
*   Alber sees Lakash as a **means to an end**, an employer **willing to pay handsomely for his unique skills**.
*   Unlike other criminals, Lakash **thinks long-term**, which suits Alber’s **calculated approach to his work**.

##### **His Work in Verbobonc**

*   **Political destabilization:** Alber discreetly **blackmails officials, sabotages trade deals, and spreads misinformation** to weaken Verbobonc’s leadership.
*   **Eliminating threats:** Those who **pose a danger to Lakash’s plans** often disappear—Alber ensures their **deaths appear accidental or untraceable**.
*   **Fomenting unrest:** He subtly **pushes rival factions into conflict**, ensuring that Verbobonc’s **thieves’ guilds, merchants, and nobles remain divided**.

##### **His Code**

*   **"The job is the job."** Alber takes **no unnecessary risks** and does **only what he is paid for**.
*   **"I am not your friend, nor your enemy."** Alber remains **emotionally detached**, seeing his work as **pure business**.
*   **"I do not leave loose ends."** He ensures **there are no witnesses, no evidence, no betrayals**.

* * *

### **Relationships & Factions**

##### [Lakash Quallad](lakash-quallad-person.md) **– The Mastermind Employer** _(Employer & Potential Rival)_​

*   Alber is **one of Lakash’s most valuable assets**, used for **high-level manipulation and covert operations**.
*   Though **he respects Lakash’s intelligence**, Alber **trusts him no more than necessary**.
*   He suspects that **Lakash is using him as a disposable asset**—a mistake Lakash will only make **once**.

_"You have grand ambitions, Lakash. That’s good. Just remember—ambition makes men reckless."_

##### **The Thieves' Guilds of Verbobonc** _(Both Clients & Enemies)_

*   The **various thieves' guilds and criminal factions** in Verbobonc **either admire or fear Alber**.
*   Many would **love to employ him**, but **none can control him**.
*   Some **suspect he is working against them**, but can never prove it.

_"A guild is only as strong as the weakest thief. You should hope I never find yours."_

##### [The Council of Lords of Verbobonc](the-council-of-lords-of-verbobonc-organization.md) _(Unwitting Victims)_

*   Through Lakash’s schemes, Alber is **slowly eroding the stability of the Viscounty**, ensuring **leaders remain suspicious and divided**.
*   Several lords and merchants have **fallen to scandal and misfortune**, unaware that Alber was the **true hand behind their ruin**.
*   If the **Council ever discovers his existence**, **every spy, bounty hunter, and assassin in Verbobonc will be after him**.

* * *

### **Adventure Hooks & Encounters**

##### **The Invisible Hand** _(Mystery & Investigation Hook)_

*   The adventurers **stumble upon a political scandal or assassination**, with **no clear evidence of the perpetrator**.
*   Clues lead them to **whispers of a faceless killer**, but tracking him is **nearly impossible**.

##### **A Contract on the Players** _(Thriller & Chase Hook)_

*   Lakash Quallad **orders Alber to eliminate the party** after they interfere in his schemes.
*   The players must **evade a hunter who leaves no trace**, or **turn the tables on the assassin**.

##### **Turning Alber Against Lakash** _(Intrigue & Negotiation Hook)_

*   If the players **can prove Lakash is planning to betray him**, they may be able to **turn Alber against his employer**.
*   But **what price will he ask** in exchange for his aid?

* * *

#### **Conclusion**

Alber Guildenstern is **a ghost in the underworld of Verbobonc**, a man whose **name is never spoken except in whispers**. He exists **between the shadows**, a professional **fixer, assassin, and manipulator**, tied to **Lakash Quallad’s grand designs** yet **beholden to none**.

To those who cross his path, one truth remains certain:

**If Alber Guildenstern wants you dead, you’ll never see it coming.**
