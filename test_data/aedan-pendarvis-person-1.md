Aedan Pendarvis
===============

Mounted patrol lieutenant stationed at 2\. Deleveu Manor under the command of Captain  

> Suel / Oeridian mix, Aedan is tanned lean and strong from years on the horse patrolling the viscounty. His weather worn face and hands has soon more days in the borders than in a barracks. He has the full respect of his men and cares much for them as his own family. The safety of his captain is paramount. Then the safety of his men and the viscounty folk. He takes his oath as serious as any cleric of any of the gods.

#### Who are the Mounted Borders

The Borderers is a highly elite force wholly devoted to the Viscounty and its citizens, upholding the law and defending the populace. The individual Borderer makes use of whatever means they deem most efficient at achieving their mission. As an organization well respected and trusted among many throughout the Viscounty. We make extensive use of our own personal combat skills and at the same time take advantage of any help afforded to us by locals.  

#### The life of a Verbobonc Lancer

The life of Verbobonc Lancer is not comfortable, safe or easy. We patrol the borders of the Viscounty, often pursuing enemies of Verbobonc far afield. We are found more often riding an endless circuit of the villages and towns than the city and sleep in the saddle or on the cold ground with a thick horse blanket for bedding than beneath a roof and in a real bed.
