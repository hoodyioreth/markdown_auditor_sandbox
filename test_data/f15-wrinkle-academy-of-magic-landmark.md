F15 Wrinkle Academy of Magic
============================

The Wrinkle Academy of Magic is Verbobonc’s boldest experiment in arcane education—an ambitious, newly founded academy housed within a renovated manor at the edge of the Foreign Quarter. Where the Silver Consortium prizes tradition, pedigree, and internal politics, the Wrinkle Academy offers practical wizardry, strict discipline, and carefully managed access to arcane power.

To some, it is a beacon of opportunity. To others, it is a dangerous concession to magic in the hands of the unproven.

To the city’s power brokers, it is something more troubling still: a school that answers to no single guild.

##### Approaching the Academy

The manor before you bears the marks of recent change—new stone set beside older walls, windows freshly glazed, sigils still sharp with unweathered paint. Lanterns glow with a steadiness that speaks of practiced magic rather than showmanship.  
  
A carved sign hangs beside the gate: Wrinkle Academy of Magic — Instruction with Discretion. Beyond the iron fence, robed students cross the grounds in small groups, voices low, spellbooks clutched close. Whatever lessons are taught here, they are not meant for spectacle.

### **Founding & Purpose**

The Wrinkle Academy was founded in **592 CY** by **Obble Quillkeeper Humbleburrow Har**, formerly a senior illusionist and instructor at the **Silver Consortium School of Magic**. Obble’s departure from the Consortium was polite, public, and permanent—a quiet schism that signaled a shift in Verbobonc’s arcane landscape.

Obble believed the city required:

*   a school **less bound by guild lineage**,
*   instruction focused on **control and responsibility**,
*   and a place where _non-elite_ students could receive structured magical training without becoming liabilities.

The Academy’s mission is explicit:  
**magic must serve society, not unsettle it.**

* * *

#### **Relationship with the Silver Consortium (C5)**

The Silver Consortium officially recognizes the Wrinkle Academy as _legitimate but unproven_.

*   Consortium magisters view Obble’s methods as **efficient but risky**
*   Several Consortium instructors quietly **refer problem students** to Wrinkle
*   Guild politics remain strained, but professional courtesy holds—for now

The Academy exists in a **carefully tolerated space**, watched by guild agents, city officials, and rival spellcasters alike.

* * *

#### **Facilities & Grounds**

The Academy occupies a sprawling manor under ongoing renovation.

**Key Features Include:**

*   **Eight Lecture Halls:** Used for theory, spell structure, and magical ethics
*   **Two Laboratories:** One operational, one still under construction
*   **The Library:** Sparse but growing—many shelves remain intentionally empty
*   **Administrative Suite:** Offices for Obble Har and senior staff
*   **Warded Grounds:** Subtle containment enchantments prevent uncontrolled casting

Construction is constant. Dust, scaffolding, and fresh stone are part of daily life here.

* * *

#### **Educational Philosophy**

Wrinkle Academy instruction is:

*   **Time-intensive**
*   **Strictly supervised**
*   **Focused on discipline before power**

Students are taught that _casting is a privilege_, not a right.

Unauthorized spell use—especially illusion, evocation, or enchantment in public spaces—is punished swiftly and quietly.

### Political Influence

The Wrinkle Academy has faced suspicion from the common folk of Verbobonc but has garnered support from professional organizations. Magister Har emphasizes control and discretion in the use of magic to maintain the academy's reputation.

##### Relationships with Verbobonc Politics

*   **Common Folk:** Generally suspicious and fearful of the academy
*   **Professional Organizations:** View the academy as a potential boon and support its establishment
*   **Magister Har's Strategy:** Foster cooperation among prestigious groups to enhance the academy's reputation

#### Politics and Relationships

*   **Public Perception:** Met with suspicion by some, but seen as a potential asset by professional organizations.
*   **Silver Consortium:** Magister Har was previously affiliated with the [Mages Guild of the Silver Consortium](mages-guild-of-the-silver-consortium-organization.md), teaching there until he founded the Wrinkle Academy. The Silver Consortium, established in 562 CY, has grown into a prestigious institution with a complex political landscape. Magister Har's departure to start his own academy highlights a shift in the dynamics of magical education within Verbobonc.
*   **Strategic Alliances:** Magister Har promotes cooperation with other prestigious groups to enhance the academy’s reputation.
*   **Professional Organizations:** Viewed favorably by those who see the benefit of a well-run magic academy.

### Facilities and Resources

The Wrinkle Academy is located within a sprawling, newly-renovated manor house in Verbobonc City. Many areas still sport fresh paint and construction is ongoing in parts of the building.

##### Lecture Halls and Laboratories

*   **Number of Lecture Halls:** Eight well-equipped rooms designed for theoretical and practical instruction.
*   **Number of Laboratories:** Two labs, one fully operational and another under construction, serve as the centers for magical experimentation.
*   **Library:** A mostly-empty library that is still being stocked with tomes and scrolls.
*   **Administrative Suite:** Offices for the administrative staff, including Magister Har and Artemis Kurl, who oversees construction and administrative duties.

### Educational Approach

*   **Courses:** Time-intensive classes designed to produce skilled wizards
*   **Instruction:** Open to anyone with magical knowledge, offering irregular classes for skill improvement
*   **Magister Har's Emphasis:** Control and discretion in magic use

### Academy Operations

The academy operates with a strong emphasis on control and discretion in magic use. Students are encouraged to study diligently, and the academy has strict policies regarding the use of magic, especially in ways that could harm its reputation. Refer to [The Student's Wrinkle Academy Handbook](the-student-s-wrinkle-academy-handbook-article.md)

##### Student Body

*   **Initial Class:** Over 80 students of varying skill levels, mostly Commoners who will take one level in Wizard upon graduation, alongside some adventurers.
*   **Curriculum:** Time-intensive classes designed to produce skilled wizards.
*   **Extracurricular Learning:** Irregular classes available for those with prior magical knowledge, for a fee.

#### Benefits and Requirements

The Wrinkle Academy offers various benefits to students and instructors, including educational resources and influence within the Viscounty of Verbobonc.

Requirements for Admission

*   **Session Class Fee:** 500 gp
*   **Library Fee for Students:** 150 gp
*   **Library Fee for Instructors:** 300 gp

##### Student Benefits

*   **Education's Bounty:** +1 bonus to Knowledge
*   **Arcane Lore:** Ability to research and scribe 2 additional spells at half cost

##### Instructor Benefits

*   **Education's Bounty:** +2 bonus to Alchemy, Knowledge, and Spellcraft
*   **Arcane Research:** Access to the research center and labs

### Current Affairs

The Wrinkle Academy continues to grow and develop, with new students enrolling and additional facilities being constructed. Magister Har is dedicated to maintaining the academy's reputation and fostering a cooperative relationship with the city's professional organizations.

* * *

### Membership Benefits

##### General

**Permanent Influence:** One permanent influence with/from the Wrinkle Academy of Magic, which can be used in any way a normal influence can be used in the Viscounty of Verbobonc. This benefit exists as long they maintain membership in good standing with the Academy.

##### Student

**Education’s Bounty** – A Student of the Wrinkle Academy of Magic is provided with excellence in instruction granting a +1 bonus to Knowledge (Arcana) and Spellcraft.

##### Instructor (Magister)

*   **Arcane Lore** – The Magister may upon gaining a level of wizard research and scribe 2 additional spells into their spellbook for half cost.
*   **Education’s Bounty** – A Magister of the Wrinkle Academy of Magic has access to numerous resources to research and seek knowledge. These resources grant the Magister a +2 bonus to Alchemy, Knowledge (Arcana) and Spellcraft. This bonus is granted as long as the student is within the boundaries of the Verbobonc allowing the Magister to be close to the Academy’s resources.
*   **Arcane Research** – A Magister is granted access to the research center and labs.
