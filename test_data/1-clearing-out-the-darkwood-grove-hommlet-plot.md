1\. Clearing Out the Darkwood Grove - Hommlet
=============================================

**Quest Giver:** Lenerd Kolter, the [3\. Woodcutter’s Cottage](3-woodcutterE28099s-cottage-landmark.md)  
Lenerd, a local woodcutter, has discovered a grove of rare darkwood trees in the nearby woods, but the area is overrun with dangerous creatures. He approaches the party, offering a deal: if they clear out the grove, he will reward them with finely crafted darkwood shields or bucklers.

*   **Location:** The grove is a short journey from the village, deep within a secluded part of the forest.
*   **Creatures:** Initially believed to be infested by giant spiders, the grove is actually tainted by the Temple of Elemental Evil’s
*   **influence.** Blights have sprung up around a blood-stained altar with remnants of ritualistic offerings, indicating the presence of Temple agents.
*   **Reward:** After a few days, Lenerd will deliver the darkwood shields or bucklers to the party, each enhanced with the natural resilience of the wood.

##### Possible Modifications

Instead of clearing out the grove immediately, the party might choose to investigate the source of the corruption, leading them on a deeper quest that ties back to the Temple of Elemental Evil.

##### Rumors Tied to the Quest

> _"I heard some strange chanting coming from the woods last night. Whatever it was, it gave me the chills." - A villager in the Welcome Wench.  
> __"Tarim's been talking about a new grove he's found, but no one’s brave enough to venture there with him." - Ostler Gundigoot._

Instead of spiders, to add interest perhaps the PCs can see signs of the Temple's influence, a group of blights have cropped up around a blood-stained pile of stones on which a few melted candles and a holy symbol bearing the elder elemental eye symbol have been left. Tatters of ochre cloth suggest that the Temple of Earth is at play here.

#### Darkwood Shield

armour (shield), uncommon  
This shield is made of a surprisingly light, coffee-coloured wood. It weighs only three pounds, but is incredibly durable: attempts to break or damage it are at disadvantage, and saving throws made to prevent damage to it are at advantage.
