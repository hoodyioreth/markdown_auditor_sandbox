Aerith Sherendyl
================

### Las’törla (Clan-Lady)

_Las’törla (Clan-Lady) of Sherendyl, Companion Guard veteran — Age 304_

**Aerith Sherendyl** has ruled [Clan Sherendyl](clan-sherendyl-organization.md) since **514 CY**, elected las’törla in the uneasy quiet after the **Hateful Wars**. A soldier first and last, she runs Düalarn like a peaceful barracks: drills regular, stores tallied, borders watched. She is openly **pro-Celene**, intent on renewing formal fealty to Enstad, and **deeply suspicious of Dyvers** and its ship-timber appetites along the northern Gnarley. Aerith works hand-in-hand with [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md) and **druids** to hold the line while keeping firm, cordial terms with Verbobonc.

> “Las’törla Aerith Sherendyl—soldier-queen of a village that trains like a fortress, smiles seldom, and keeps every promise sharpened.”

* * *

#### Appearance & Demeanor

Tall, spare, and rangy from a lifetime in armor. Ash-brown hair braided for campaign, river-green eyes that miss little. Aerith moves with rangemaster economy; even her courtesy has edges. She speaks plainly, favors few words, and expects the same.

**Roleplay cues:** direct eye contact; hands behind back when listening; taps two fingers against vambrace before issuing an order; praise is brief—“Good”—and never wasted.

* * *

#### Early Life & Service

*   **Companion Guard of** [Celene, Faerie Kingdom of](../c/celene2C-faerie-kingdom-of-category.md)**.** Aerith served in Queen **Yolande’s** elite guard in her youth; she still glows when recalling the court’s discipline and art.

*   **Border Captain.** Returned to the Gnarley to command Sherendyl patrols during the late raids and through [The Hateful Wars](the-hateful-wars-article-1.md); earned her reputation for **clean logistics, hard marches, and zero panic**.
*   **Election (514 CY).** Succeeded cousin **Turleval**, who abdicated to heal from war-trauma; Aerith won a decisive feudal election by laying out a 20-year readiness plan and a sober treaty posture.

* * *

#### Mandate & Policies (579 CY)

*   **Renew Fealty to Celene.** Publicly argues that the Gnarley elves still owe **feudal loyalty** to the grey-elf crown; seeks a written compact naming shared wardens and posted envoys—not just polite letters.
*   **Check Dyvers’ Timber Drives.** Blocks inner-forest logging, tightens bonded-caravan rules, and funds joint ambushes with Rangers when slaver coin “escorts” timber.
*   **Practical with Verbobonc.** Prefers **oath-bound traffic** and ranger liaisons; supports Mounted Borderers when they respect canopy law.
*   **Readiness Culture.** Tunnels drilled, stores inventoried, officers rotated; “predictability kills” is policy for patrol routes and bell hours.

* * *

#### Alliances & Rivalries

*   **Allies:** [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md) (shared calendars), circles (firebreaks, coppice plans), [Clan Meldarin](clan-meldarin-organization.md) (oath-law and picket signals).

*   **Contentious:** [Dyvers, Free Lands of](dyvers2C-free-lands-of-organization.md) factors pressing ship-timber; opportunist Woodsmen who ignore cutting writs; smugglers working [The Wild Coast](the-wild-coast-organization.md) lines.
*   **Mixed:** [Clan Fealefel](clan-fealefel-organization.md) (she respects their ley wardens but dislikes secrecy [Clan Enlanefel](clan-enlanefel-organization.md) (she values their arbitration yet pushes them to share more intelligence).

* * *

#### Personality & Ideals

*   **Ideal:** Duty before comfort; oaths before convenience.
*   **Bond:** The Sherendyl dead of the Hateful Wars; the tunnels of Düalarn must never be needed—but will be ready.
*   **Flaw:** Impatience with “pretty policy”—if it cannot be held at spear-length, it is not law.

#### Seats of Power

*   **Corellon’s Hall of Seven Vows (Düalarn):** Aerith’s council chamber and oath-hall; armory vault beneath.
*   **Listening Hill:** Where she meets Ranger captains before dawn to plan road traps.
*   **Under-Step Galleries:** Inspection loops through granaries, cisterns, and muster rooms; Aerith walks a different loop each tenday.

### Relationships

*   [Yolande, Her Fey Majesty](yolande2C-her-fey-majesty-person.md) **(Celene):** Devotion bordering on reverence; Aerith frames policy to be worthy of Yolande’s standards.
*   [Verbobonc, Viscounty](../c/verbobonc2C-viscounty-category.md)**:** Respectful, businesslike; supports Mounted Borderers when they honor green law.

*   [Dyvers, Free Lands of](dyvers2C-free-lands-of-organization.md)**:** Distrustful; will not yield inner-forest timber for hulls.
*   [Clan Meldarin](clan-meldarin-organization.md)**:** Warm alignment on patrols and oaths.
*   [Clan Fealefel](clan-fealefel-organization.md)**:** Mutual respect; wants clearer intelligence lanes.
*   [Clan Enlanefel](clan-enlanefel-organization.md)**:** Uses them as arbiters; nudges for less secrecy in wartime.

* * *

#### Adventure Hooks

1.  **The Timber Charter:** A Dyvers envoy arrives with a flawless charter; Aerith asks the party to authenticate seals without provoking a rupture—or to catch the hidden clause.
2.  **The Missing Captain:** One of Aerith’s best patrol captains vanishes near Sobanwych; recover the captain or prove treachery before the council vote on tightening the road.
3.  **Fealty or Friction:** Celene sends a formal request for oaths. Aerith wants an escort to [Enstad](enstad-settlement.md) —and discreet troubleshooters if rivals in the clan attempt to stall the compact.
4.  **Bell at Midnight:** Düalarn’s wood-bell rings once, then dies. Aerith deputizes the PCs to hunt a saboteur through roof lanes and cellar doors before dawn.

#### Using Aerith at the Table

*   **As Patron:** She gives precise objectives, crisp timelines, and material support—then expects results and a short debrief.
*   **As Counterweight:** When politics drift into speeches, Aerith demands **numbers, maps, and names**.
*   **As Foil:** For reckless PCs, she offers one chance to earn trust by fixing what they broke—fast.
