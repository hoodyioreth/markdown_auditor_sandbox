Demien Trevils
==============

Old man Demien Trevils, a cultist of the Cult of the Elder Elemental God. Was recruited by Lareth as this location is the perfect place off the High Pass trail and the edge of the swamp near the Moathouse.   The former Druids of the Old Faith worshiper lost his sons during The Battle of Emridy Meadows. Since the forces of good pulled back and decided to stop the horde of Elemental Evil at Emridy Meadows to the north the the chaotic humanoids caught and trapped his sons outside and captured them as slaves. Since them Demien took to drink and resentment grew for the forces of good to protect his farm and family. He bought into the idea of "balance" and realized "chaos" needs to spread. Lareth converted him and been using his downtrodden unproductive farm as a staging area for the Moathouse bandit raids.
