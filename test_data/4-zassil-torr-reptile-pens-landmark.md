4\. Zassil'torr Reptile Pens
============================

The Reptile Pens are a sprawling network of reed-woven enclosures and wooden platforms perched on the swamp’s edge. The pens are built to accommodate the various needs of the reptiles, with some sections submerged in murky swamp water for amphibious creatures and others raised above the waterline to keep the dry-habitat reptiles comfortable. The air here is thick with the pungent aroma of raw fish and swamp vegetation, mingling with the guttural croaks and hisses of the animals.

*   **Submerged Pens:** These water-filled enclosures house the giant frogs and semi-aquatic reptiles, providing an environment to mimic their natural swamp habitat. Wooden walkways crisscross these pens, allowing handlers to move safely above the water.
*   **Dry Pens:** Elevated enclosures filled with packed dirt and vegetation provide space for training the giant lizards, from riding mounts to combat-ready beasts.

_

> Where the strength and agility of Zassil’torr’s lizardfolk mounts are honed and nurtured.

_

#### Role in the Village

The Reptile Pens are integral to the daily life and defense of Zassil’torr. Managed by Huntmaster Tazzirik and his team of handlers, they ensure the village remains supplied with loyal, trained creatures for work, travel, and combat.

*   **Training Ground:** The pens serve as a training area for young lizardfolk learning to tame and ride these creatures.
*   **Strategic Asset:** The lizards and frogs are vital for scouting, patrols, and combat, offering a unique advantage in the swamp terrain.

##### Notable Features

*   **Feeding Stations:** Large wooden troughs and nets filled with raw fish and swamp vegetation are scattered throughout the pens. Feeding the creatures is a careful task requiring precision and patience.
*   **Training Tools:** Sturdy poles, harnesses, and padded lizardfolk dummies are used to train the creatures for battle and riding.
*   **Observation Platforms:** Raised platforms give handlers and visitors a clear view of the pens, ensuring safety and facilitating training.

### DM-Only Secrets

[Show spoiler](#spoiler-YrGgDO)

*   **Hidden Creatures:** A few pens house rare and exotic reptiles that Tazzirik and Ssarkosh use in their dealings with outsiders, including creatures infused with minor magical abilities by the Swamp Seer.
*   **Training Challenges:** A particularly unruly giant lizard named Rasshklaw has been causing problems, injuring several handlers. Unknown to the village, Ssarkosh has been secretly experimenting on it, infusing it with demonic energy.
*   **Potential Encounter:** Adventurers might be tasked with calming or subduing Rasshklaw as part of their dealings with the village.

##### Adventure Hooks

*   **Sabotage:** Rivals or pirates attempt to poison or steal the creatures in the pens, requiring adventurers to investigate and defend the village’s assets.
*   **Taming the Wild**: The adventurers are asked to help tame a particularly aggressive creature, testing their patience and skills.
*   **Demonic Influence:** A mysterious illness spreads through the reptiles, linked to Ssarkosh’s experiments. The adventurers must uncover the cause before it spreads further.

##### Trained Giant Lizard (Combat Mount)

_Large Beast, Unaligned_

*   Armor Class: 12 (natural armor)
*   Hit Points: 37 (5d10 + 10)
*   Speed: 30 ft., climb 30 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA 15 (+2) 12 (+1) 14 (+2) 2 (-4) 10 (+0) 5 (-3)

*   Skills: Perception +2
*   Senses: Darkvision 30 ft., Passive Perception 12
*   Languages: —

**Features**

*   Loyal Mount: The lizard is trained to obey its rider’s commands in combat and remains calm in chaotic situations.
*   Swamp Camouflage: The lizard has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made in swampy terrain.

**Actions**

*   Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage.
*   Tail Swipe (Recharge 5–6): The lizard sweeps its tail in a 10-foot radius. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 10 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.

  **Equipment**: Combat harness and saddle, pack straps for carrying gear.

**Type**

Room, Animal, Pen
