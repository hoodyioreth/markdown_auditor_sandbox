14\. Wolog’s Teamsters
======================

Wolog’s Teamsters is a critical component of Hommlet’s infrastructure, providing transport and logistical support to the village and surrounding areas. Located next to D&G Dry Goods & Mercantile Exchange along [Nigb’s Run](nigbE28099s-run-location.md), the teamster business is run by Wolog and his family. Known for their honest work and dedication to [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md), Wolog and his team are trusted by many but harbor suspicions about the traders at D&G Dry Goods.

### Location and Description

Wolog’s Teamsters is situated at the base of the hill presided over by the Church of St. Cuthbert, nestled alongside Nigb's Run. The strategic location ensures easy access to both the village and the main roads leading to the greater Verbobonc region, making it an ideal spot for transportation services.

##### Building Description

The establishment consists of a sturdy wooden house with an attached barn. The barn is large enough to house two wagons and two carts, while a loft provides living quarters for two drivers. A smaller barn at the rear shelters a dozen mules, all trained and ready for transport duties. A wooden sign depicting a cart and horse hangs prominently, signaling the teamster’s services.

##### House Interior

*   **Ground Floor:** Living space for Wolog, his wife Grina, and their five children. The area is modest but well-kept, reflecting the family’s hardworking nature.
*   **Barn:** The barn is a bustling hub of activity, with drivers preparing for their next assignments. Tools, spare parts, and harnesses line the walls, ensuring the team is always ready for the road.

### Lore and History

Wolog originally hails from the town of Devarnish in Veluna. Seeking new opportunities and a fresh start, he moved to Hommlet with his family. His experience and dedication quickly established Wolog’s Teamsters as a trusted service provider in the area.

##### Historical Significance

*   **Founding:** Established soon after Wolog’s arrival in Hommlet, the business has grown to become an essential part of the village’s economy.
*   **Challenges:** Recently, Wolog lost a wagon and team to road bandits, a significant blow that has increased his wariness and need for vigilance on the roads.

### Motivation and Relationships

*   **Honesty and Service:** Driven by a sense of duty and honesty, Wolog aims to provide reliable transportation services while upholding the tenets of Saint Cuthbert.
*   **Family Legacy:** Wolog is committed to building a lasting business that his children can inherit and continue.

##### Relationships

*   **Community:** Wolog maintains positive relations with most villagers, seen as a trustworthy and hardworking member of the community.
*   **Distrust of Traders:** Although unsure why, Wolog distrusts the traders at D&G Dry Goods, sharing his concerns with customers who inquire.

### Current Events

##### Operations

Wolog’s Teamsters continues to operate despite recent setbacks. The team remains busy with transport contracts, ensuring goods and people reach their destinations safely.

##### Bandit Concerns

The increasing bandit activity around Hommlet has heightened Wolog’s vigilance. He shares his concerns with fellow villagers, advocating for greater protection and cooperation to safeguard the roads.

#### Secrets and Treasure

*   **Hidden Treasure:** Despite his honest reputation, Wolog has 19 silver pieces hidden under a loose floorboard, a small reserve for emergencies.
*   **Ambition of Wolog Jr.:** Wolog Jr. dreams of adventure and seeks to join an adventuring party, driven by a desire to carve out his own path.

 

#### Conclusion

Wolog’s Teamsters stands as a pillar of integrity and service in Hommlet, providing essential transport solutions amid growing challenges. Wolog and his family remain committed to their community, driven by honesty and faith in Saint Cuthbert. As bandit threats loom, they continue to advocate for vigilance and cooperation, ensuring that the village of Hommlet thrives despite adversity.
