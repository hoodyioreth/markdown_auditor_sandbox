23\. Tumasarson Farm
====================

Tumasarson Farm, located in the southern part of the township of Nigb's Rest, between the Druids Grove, the Vintners Guild Hall, and the Hidden Vine Mill, is a prominent farm that contributes significantly to the local economy with its swine and sheep herds. Owned by Vagn Tumasarson and his family, this farm provides meat and wool to the community and the southern Viscounty of Verbobonc.

### Location

Tumasarson Farm is nestled in the fertile lands of Nigb's Rest, strategically positioned between notable landmarks. To the north lies the Druids Grove, a serene and sacred area dedicated to the Old Faith. To the east is the Vintners Guild Hall, the heart of the valley's winemaking industry, and to the west is the Hidden Vine Mill, where the farm's produce is often processed.

### History

The Tumasarson Farm has been in the family for generations. Originally established as a small homestead, it has grown into a significant agricultural enterprise under Vagn's stewardship. The farm's history is intertwined with the development of Nigb's Rest, contributing to its reputation as a prosperous agricultural hub.

### Economics

_Market Day  
_Every market day, Vagn and his family set up stalls in the Nigb's Rest marketplace, selling fresh meat and wool. Their high-quality products are sought after by both locals and visitors. The Tumasarson Farm also supplies [1\. Fort Emridy](1-fort-emridy-landmark-1.md)  and the [4\. Black Griffon Inn](4-black-griffon-inn-landmark.md) with meat, ensuring that the soldiers and patrons are well-fed.

##### Regional Trade

The meat and wool from Tumasarson Farm are staples in the region, reaching as far north as the City of Verbobonc. While the cheese from the Gnome cheesemakers of [Greenway Valley](greenway-valley-settlement.md)  is highly regarded, Tumasarson Farm's products are affordable and accessible to the common folk.

##### Vagn Tumasarson

*   **Description:** A tall, robust man with a thick beard and weathered skin from years of working under the sun.
*   **Personality:** Hardworking, dedicated, and deeply connected to the land. Vagn is known for his straightforwardness and sense of duty.
*   **Motivation:** Driven by a desire to provide for his family and community, ensuring the farm's prosperity and sustainability.
*   **Relationships:** Vagn maintains strong ties with local leaders and is respected by his peers.

##### Iselle Tumasarson

Iselle, Vagn's wife, is a nurturing and resourceful woman. She manages the household and assists with the farm's daily operations. Her kind nature and sharp intellect make her the perfect partner for Vagn.
