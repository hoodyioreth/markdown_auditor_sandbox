AO-14. Chapel of Heironeous
===========================

_The great chapel of the knights of the_ _**Azure Oath.**_

Once the spiritual heart of the Sepulchre-Priory, this great chapel was where the knights of the **Azure Oath** gathered to swear loyalty to Heironeous and guard the sacred tombs beneath the monastery. Sunlight once shone through narrow windows upon polished mosaics and rows of kneeling warriors as priests blessed their blades before battle.

That sanctity has been violently broken.

Romag’s Earth Temple forces have deliberately defiled the chamber, smashing its statues and scarring the floor with crude symbols of the **Black Earth cult**. Torn banners now hang over the heraldry of the knights, and the once-sacred altar has been desecrated into a ritual focus for dark ceremonies.

The cult uses this hall as a **ceremonial center for corruption rituals**, mocking the faith that once protected the monastery. If the party enters at the wrong moment, they may stumble directly into one of these rites — where the earth itself is called to swallow the memory of Heironeous.

##### Enter the Chapel of Heironeous

The heavy oak doors groan open upon a once-sacred hall now fallen into ruin. The long chapel stretches before you beneath a broken roof where pale daylight spills through jagged beams. Rain and wind have carved dark stains across the stone floor.  
  
Rows of shattered pews lie overturned like the bones of a fallen congregation. The cracked mosaic floor still bears fragments of a once-glorious image: a mailed knight raising a lightning-wreathed sword — the ancient symbol of Heironeous.  
  
At the far end of the chamber stands the stone altar, scarred and defaced. The statues of armored knights that once guarded it lie smashed upon the ground, their faces shattered.  
  
Crude black banners now hang over the walls where noble heraldry once stood. Their sigils twist into jagged shapes like broken mountains — the mark of the Black Earth. The air is heavy here. Dust drifts slowly in the dim light, and somewhere beneath the stones you feel a faint trembling… as though the earth itself listens.

### Stone Altar of Heironeous

At the far end of the chapel stands a heavy altar carved from pale granite. Its surface once bore the lightning bolt symbol of Heironeous, but the sacred mark has been gouged away and replaced with jagged cult sigils.

Scratches and gouges in the altar show where cultists have used it as the focus of their rituals, staining the stone with soot, oil, and ash.

**Key Details**

• Large granite altar (2 × 2 squares)  
• Defaced lightning-bolt symbol of Heironeous  
• Earth Cult runes carved into the stone surface  
• Functions as the ritual focus for defilement ceremonies  
• A successful **DC 12 Religion check** reveals the altar could be reconsecrated

* * *

#### Cracked Mosaic Floor

The chapel floor is covered in a once-magnificent mosaic depicting the legendary **Triumph of Heironeous** — a knight raising a lightning-blessed sword against the forces of chaos.

Time, neglect, and deliberate damage have fractured the artwork into jagged cracks that now resemble fault lines spreading across the floor. Cultists have scratched crude sigils of the Black Earth into these fractures, twisting the once-holy image into something darker.

**Key Details**

• Mosaic depicts the Azure Oath knights honoring Heironeous  
• Cult sigils etched into cracks across the floor  
• Several cracked tiles create uneven footing  
• **DC 12 Investigation:** reveals ritual markings used in summoning magic  
• Minor tremors sometimes ripple through the stone during rituals

* * *

#### Smashed Knight Statues

Four statues once stood along the walls of the chapel, depicting armored knights standing guard over the sacred hall. Each statue once held a sword pointed downward in a symbol of vigilance.

The cultists have smashed them.

Fragments of stone armor and broken shields litter the floor. One statue lies in several pieces beneath fallen roof beams while another has been shattered so thoroughly that only its pedestal remains.

**Key Details**

• Four statues originally guarded the chapel  
• All have been destroyed by cultists  
• Broken fragments provide scattered cover  
• One statue bears a faint lightning symbol on its shield  
• **DC 13 Investigation:** reveals signs the statues were smashed deliberately with hammers

* * *

#### Ruined Pews

Rows of wooden pews once filled the chapel, allowing knights and priests to kneel in prayer. Most have been overturned or hacked apart for firewood by the occupying cultists.

Some broken benches remain upright enough to form crude barricades.

**Key Details**

• Six original rows of pews  
• Many broken or overturned  
• Provide **half cover** during combat  
• Splintered wood scattered across the floor  
• Cultists occasionally stack them as defensive barriers

* * *

#### Collapsed Roof Section

A portion of the chapel roof has collapsed inward near the altar end of the chamber. Jagged beams and broken stone lie in a heap beneath the opening, allowing sunlight and rain to fall into the room.

During the day, shafts of light illuminate the altar area. At night, moonlight pours through the broken roof and casts eerie shadows across the shattered mosaics.

**Key Details**

• Rubble occupies a **10-foot area** near the altar  
• Area counts as **difficult terrain  
**• Daylight enters through the broken roof  
• Rain occasionally drips into the chamber  
• Earth mephits can enter or escape through the opening

* * *

#### Cult Banners

Dark banners hang from the chapel walls where noble heraldry once stood. Each bears the jagged symbol of the **Black Earth cult**, painted in crude black pigment.

Behind several banners, fragments of the original heraldry of the Azure Oath can still be seen — faded lightning symbols and knightly crests.

**Key Details**

• Black Earth cult banners cover original chapel symbols  
• Removing a banner reveals the heraldry of the Azure Oath knights  
• Cultists often kneel before these banners during rituals  
• A banner can be torn down with an action

* * *

#### Defilement Ritual

The chapel is now used for ceremonies meant to corrupt the sanctity of the monastery.

When such a ritual occurs, cultists gather around the altar chanting prayers to the **Elder Elemental God**, invoking the power of the earth to crush the memory of the fallen knights.

The cracks in the mosaic floor tremble during the ritual as earth magic spreads through the chamber.

**Key Details**

• Ritual focus is the defaced altar  
• Cultists chant prayers to the Elder Elemental God  
• Earth energy causes faint tremors in the floor  
• Ritual may summon minor elemental creatures

### Hidden Altar Puzzle — **The Restored Lightning of Heironeous**

The cultists of the Black Earth have already discovered the crypt stair below the monastery and use it freely. Yet they have overlooked the **true sacred mechanism hidden within the altar itself**—a safeguard created by the original knight-priests of the priory.

The altar was designed not merely as a place of worship, but as a **guardian reliquary**. Only those who restore the **symbol of Heironeous** within the chapel can unlock the hidden chamber concealed within the altar.

To the Earth cultists, the relics beneath the altar remain inaccessible. They lack the reverence—and the knowledge—to restore what they have broken.

* * *

##### What the Players See

The cracked mosaic floor still shows fragments of a once-glorious image: a mailed knight raising a lightning-wreathed sword, the sacred symbol of Heironeous. The cultists have defiled the image. Several mosaic plates have been shifted, breaking the sacred geometry of the lightning blade.  
  
The altar bears a carved relief of the same symbol, but the lightning bolt across the front has been scratched and crudely defaced with the jagged triangle of the Earth Cult. A faint seam runs along the back of the altar stone.

##### Puzzle Overview

The altar mechanism is tied to the **alignment of the lightning symbol in the mosaic floor**.

Three large mosaic plates remain partially intact:

• **The Knight’s Gauntlet** (base of the image)  
• **The Lightning Blade Fragment  
**• **The Radiant Halo Circle**

These plates can be **rotated or slid slightly within their stone frames**.

When aligned correctly, the lightning blade forms a continuous line pointing toward the altar.

* * *

##### Investigation Clues

**Investigation DC 12**

Players notice the mosaic fragments are **movable stone plates rather than fixed tiles**.

**Religion DC 10**

The image represents **Heironeous raising the lightning sword of justice**.

**Religion DC 13**

The lightning blade should point **toward the altar**, symbolizing divine power descending into the sacred place.

* * *

##### The Hidden Reliquary Chamber

Inside the altar lies a **stone reliquary chest** sealed with the ancient sigil of Heironeous.

The compartment contains the relics of the fallen knights of the priory.

Possible contents include:

• **Reliquary of the Azure Oath** (holy relic)  
• **Knight-Abbot’s Signet Ring  
**• **Scroll of the Azure Vigil  
**• **Sanctified Lightning Dagger**

The interior chamber is lined with polished stone and silver inlay designed to protect sacred objects from corruption.

* * *

##### Failed Attempts

If players attempt to force the altar open:

• the stone will not budge  
• the internal mechanism remains locked

After **three incorrect mosaic alignments**, a dormant ward activates.

* * *

##### Sacred Defense Mechanism

Two spectral guardians of the fallen order manifest.

Use:

• **2 Specters** representing the spirits of the fallen knights  
or  
• **2 Animated Helms**

The guardians attack only those attempting to break or desecrate the altar.

If the lightning symbol is restored correctly, the spirits instead **bow silently and fade away**, recognizing the restoration of the sacred sign.

###### 1\. Reliquary of the Azure Oath

A small silver reliquary containing a fragment of bone said to belong to **Sir Althen Var**, the first knight-abbot of the monastery.

**Effects**

• Holy symbol of Heironeous  
• Clerics or paladins of lawful good alignment gain **+1 to Channel Divinity save DCs while holding it  
**• Once per day the bearer may cast **Bless (1st level)**

Value: 250 gp (religious relic)

* * *

###### 2\. Knight-Abbot’s Signet Ring

A heavy electrum ring engraved with a **raised lightning sword over a mailed gauntlet**.

**Effects**

• Advantage on **Persuasion checks with lawful knights or clergy of Heironeous  
**• Functions as a **holy symbol**

Value: 150 gp

* * *

###### 3\. Scroll of the Azure Vigil

A preserved vellum scroll containing the original **oath sworn by the knights of this priory**.

The script reads:

_"Stand thou unyielding against tyranny.  
Let thunder answer wickedness.  
Let the sword be drawn for justice alone."_

**Effects**

• A cleric who studies the oath for 10 minutes gains **Inspiration  
**• Can be used once to grant **Advantage on a saving throw vs fear or charm**

Value: priceless to the Church of Heironeous
