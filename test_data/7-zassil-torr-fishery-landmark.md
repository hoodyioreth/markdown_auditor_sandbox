7\. Zassil’torr Fishery
=======================

The Zassil’torr Fishery is a vital and bustling location in the lizardfolk village, serving as a central hub for processing, preserving, and preparing the aquatic bounty of the swamp. It reflects the lizardfolk's resourceful nature and their mastery of swamp-based survival techniques.  

#### Structure and Layout

The fishery is an open-air platform elevated on thick wooden stilts above the swamp's murky waters. Its roof is constructed from reed thatch, offering shade and partial shelter from rain while allowing airflow to dry the fish. Beneath the main platform, partially submerged tunnels connect to the village’s underground chambers, providing a quick escape route or storage area during flooding or danger.

*   **Fish-Drying Racks:** Rows of reed-woven racks hold fish and small swamp creatures to dry under the sun.
*   **Clay Chimneys:** Simple clay chimneys used for smoking meats rise from enclosed areas at the platform's edges.
*   **Pickling Area:** Large clay jars are arranged in neat rows, filled with brined fish preserved using swamp herbs and naturally occurring swamp gases.
*   **Tools and Equipment:** Nets, woven reed baskets, spears, and fish traps are neatly stored in designated areas or hung on wooden hooks.

### Role in the Village

The fishery plays a crucial role in the life of Zassil’torr, providing sustenance and trade goods. Its products are consumed by the villagers or bartered at Zis’drek’tor: The Trader’s Hollow for steel weapons and other resources.

*   **Daily Food Supply**: The fishery provides the village with its primary source of protein, supplemented by amphibians and swamp creatures.
*   **Trade Goods:** Pickled and smoked fish are highly valued by neighboring swamp denizens and merchants.
*   **Community Hub:** The fishery serves as a gathering place for lizardfolk, particularly those involved in hunting and fishing.

### Operations

##### Preservation Techniques

The lizardfolk of Zassil’torr use innovative preservation methods unique to their swampy environment:

*   **Pickling with Swamp Herbs:** Rare herbs and minerals extracted from the swamp are combined with brine to pickle fish, ensuring long-term preservation.
*   **Smoking:** Meats are smoked using dried swampwood and moss, giving the fish a distinct, earthy flavor.
*   **Sun-Drying:** Smaller fish are strung on reed cords and left to dry in the humid but breezy air.

##### Key Tasks and Tools

*   **Fishing Nets and Traps:** Reeds and vines are expertly woven into durable fishing tools, ensuring a steady supply of swamp creatures.
*   **Clay Jar Crafting:** Clay gathered from nearby deposits is shaped and fired to create storage jars for pickling.
*   **Herb Preparation:** Swamp herbs are gathered, dried, and crushed for use in preservation and flavoring.

#### Defensive Measures

While the fishery is not a combat-focused location, its workers are capable of defending themselves.

*   **Escape Routes:** Submerged tunnels beneath the fishery allow for quick retreats into the safety of the village's underground chambers.
*   **Defensive Gear:** Workers are equipped with spears, tridents, and bone knives, which double as tools for fish processing and weapons in emergencies.
*   **Alarm Systems:** Reed horns and strategically placed drums are used to alert the village in case of danger.

### DM Only: Secrets and Adventure Hooks

##### Secrets

*   **Hidden Supply Cache:** Beneath the fishery, a secret chamber holds an emergency supply of weapons and food for use during sieges or disasters.
*   **Unusual Visitors:** On rare occasions, emissaries from Ssarkosh, the Swamp Seer, visit the fishery to trade fungi or observe operations, causing tension among the workers.

##### Adventure Hooks

*   **Sabotage at the Fishery:** Rival swamp denizens or pirates seek to sabotage the fishery to weaken Zassil’torr.
*   **Mystery of the Pickling Jar:** A strange artifact or magical ingredient is discovered in one of the clay jars, sparking interest from both allies and enemies.
*   **Recruiting the Fishery Workers:** Zarrek seeks adventurers to defend the fishery during an anticipated attack or to escort a vital trade delivery to Zis’drek’tor.

##### The Fishery in the Eyes of the Village

For the lizardfolk of Zassil’torr, the fishery is not just a source of sustenance but a symbol of their ingenuity and ability to thrive in the harsh swamp. It stands as a testament to their adaptability, resourcefulness, and deep connection to their marshy homeland.
