2\. Keenleene Family Statue
===========================

Located in the heart of the gnome village Sheernobb, the [Clan Keeleene](clan-keeleene-organization.md) Family Statue is a celebrated landmark that embodies the rich history and vibrant culture of the gnome community. This enchanted statue, situated in the festival social grounds before the Moot Hall, serves as a central hub for social and political events, bringing the village together in joyous celebration and solemn remembrance.

### Location and Description

**Festival Social Grounds  
**The statue stands in the wide, 100-foot area known as the festival social grounds, directly in front of the Moot Hall. This space is surrounded by lush, verdant hill homes deep in the dense forest of the [Kron Hills](kron-hills-location.md). The area features 3-tiered audience stands, perfectly sized for the 3-foot-tall gnomes, and is used for the village's social and political gatherings.  
**Statue Description  
**

*   **Appearance:** The ancient stone statue depicts the original founder of Sheernobb, Keenleene, in a joyous pose, playing the lute with a captivating smile.
*   **Enchantment:** The statue is enchanted with permanent gnome illusion magic, bringing it to life with lifelike mischief whenever there is smiling and dancing nearby. The illusion shows Keenleene laughing and dancing, playing the pipes with a grin and a wink.
*   **Base:** The base of the statue is overgrown with a thick blanket of green moss, adding to its ancient and revered appearance.

### Character and Personality

**Keenleene's Legacy  
**Keenleene, the founder of Sheernobb, is remembered as a joyful and charismatic leader. The statue captures his spirit, serving as a reminder of the playful and community-focused nature of the village. His love for music and celebration is immortalized in the enchanted statue, reflecting the values he instilled in Sheernobb.

### History

**Founding of Sheernobb  
**Keenleene established Sheernobb as a haven for gnomes, fostering a community based on unity, craftsmanship, and joy. The statue commemorates his vision and leadership, standing as a testament to the enduring legacy of his work.

##### Enchantment

The statue was enchanted with illusion magic by the village's skilled spellcasters, ensuring that Keenleene's joyful spirit would continue to inspire and entertain future generations.

##### Significant Events

*   **Community Gatherings:** The festival social grounds have hosted numerous significant events, from political gatherings to vibrant festivals, with the statue playing a central role in these occasions.
*   **Historical Celebrations:** The statue has been a focal point during key historical moments, such as the anniversary of the village's founding and commemorations of significant battles and alliances.

### Politics of Verbobonc

_Symbol of Unity  
_The Keenleene Family Statue serves as a symbol of unity for the gnomes of Sheernobb. It represents their collective identity and their bond with the larger political landscape of the [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md).

##### Political Gatherings

The festival social grounds in front of the Moot Hall are often used for important political events, where decisions impacting the village and its relationships with neighboring territories are discussed.

### Relationships

_Community Bond  
_The statue fosters a strong sense of community among the gnomes, serving as a gathering point for celebrations and discussions. It symbolizes the shared heritage and collective spirit of the villagers.

##### External Relations

*   **Neighboring Communities:** The statue and the festival grounds play a role in fostering relationships with neighboring gnome families and other races in the Kron Hills, such as dwarves and humans.
*   **Diplomatic Symbol:** The statue is also a symbol of Sheernobb's diplomatic relationships, representing the village's commitment to peace and collaboration with surrounding territories.

### Cultural Significance

_Festivals and Celebrations  
_The statue comes to life during festivals and celebrations, adding to the festive atmosphere with its enchanting illusions. It dances and plays music, delighting both young and old gnomes.

##### Social Gatherings

*   **Daily Life:** On regular days, the statue serves as a picturesque and tranquil spot for social interactions and communal gatherings.
*   **Special Events:** During special events, the statue's magical enchantments enhance the celebrations, creating an immersive and joyous experience for all attendees.

#### Conclusion

The Keenleene Family Statue is a cherished landmark in Sheernobb, embodying the village's history, culture, and community spirit. Its enchanting presence in the festival social grounds continues to inspire and bring joy to the gnomes of Sheernobb, ensuring that the legacy of Keenleene lives on for generations to come.
