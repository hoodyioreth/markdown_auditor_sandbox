Penwick
=======

Penwick is a small but significant township located in the southeastern plains of the [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md). In the bustling center of Penwick, where the Harvest Road from the township of [Etterboek](etterboek-settlement-1.md)  meets the Merchants Way from the [Verbobonc, City](verbobonc2C-city-settlement.md). This large open space serves as a hub for local farmers and visiting peddlers to trade their goods. Dominating the square is a three-story guard tower, a stone keep that leans to the east, bearing the banner of House Kolgrim.

### Location and Description

Penwick is situated in the fertile lands of the southeastern plains, bordered by House Asbury and House Milinous holdings. The township benefits from numerous small creeks and natural springs that provide ample water for the region's homesteads. The township is a patchwork of farms, each delineated by rows of trees and owned by vassals of House Milinous or House Asbury.

*   **Location:** Southeastern plains of the Viscounty of Verbobonc

##### Geographic Features

*   Fertile lands
*   Small creeks and natural springs
*   Patchwork of farms
*   Rows of trees marking boundaries

### Historical Significance

Penwick's history is marked by conflict and resilience. Overrun by forces from the Temple of Elemental Evil two decades ago, the township was nearly decimated. After the Temple's defeat, Mayor Connor Grantham offered free land to militia and mercenaries, many of whom settled and rebuilt the township. This history of conflict has left Penwick economically poor but militarily strong, as nearly all freemen are veteran soldiers or their descendants.

*   **Temple of Elemental Evil:** Penwick was overrun and nearly decimated.
*   **Rebuilding Efforts:** Land offered to militia and mercenaries, leading to resettlement.
*   **Economic Status:** Economically poor but militarily strong.

### Politics and Governance

_**Governance Structure**_

Penwick is governed by Mayor Connor Grantham, who has held office for nearly thirty years. The township operates under the broader political landscape of the Viscounty of Verbobonc, with significant influence from House Asbury and House Milinous.

*   **Mayor:** Connor Grantham, aged 60, rarely appears in Verbobonc, leaving political matters to his eldest son, Gaelsich.
*   **Political Influence:** Significant influence from House Asbury and House Milinous.

##### Key Political Entities

[House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md)

*   **Location:** South of Penwick, with Asbury Manor located 10 miles southeast of the township.
*   **Influence:** House Asbury exerts control over the southern lands of Penwick.

[House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md)

*   **Location:** North of Penwick, with Griffon Manor dominating the center of the township.
*   **Influence:** House Milinous controls the northern lands and has a significant presence in Penwick.

### Key Locations

[P15. Kolgrim Manor](p15-kolgrim-manor-landmark.md)

Kolgrim Manor is the estate home of Lord [Arant Kolgrim](arant-kolgrim-person.md), dominating the top of the hill overlooking Penwick. The manor is stately and regal, constructed of timber and thick hardwood logs. It features large, stained glass windows and a private pond. A chapel dedicated to Rao is also situated on the estate.

[P20. Mayor Connor Estate Farm](p20-mayor-connor-estate-farm-landmark.md)

The Mayor Connor Estate Farm is a large, walled manor that serves as the residence of Mayor Connor Grantham, his wife Beatrix, and their eldest son Gaelsich. The manor shows signs of repair, reflecting the township's worn appearance.

[P1. Market Square and Tower](p1-market-square-and-tower-landmark.md)

The center of Penwick is dominated by a huge open space used as a market by local farmers and visiting peddlers. A three-story guard tower, a stone keep that leans to the east, dominates the square. The tower bears the dusty banner of House Kolgrim, depicting a black griffon on a field of azure. The regular militia, wearing House Asbury tabards, patrols the town and its roads.

### Relationships and Influence

_Relationships with Lords of Verbobonc_

Penwick's relationships with House Asbury and House Milinous are integral to its political landscape. Both houses exert significant influence over the township, with House Asbury controlling the southern lands and House Milinous the northern. These relationships are characterized by mutual benefit and occasional tension, reflecting the complex dynamics of feudal governance.

*   **House Asbury:** Controls southern lands, Asbury Manor is located 10 miles southeast.
*   **House Milinous:** Controls northern lands, Griffon Manor is central to Penwick.

#### Economic and Military Aspects

_Economy_

Penwick's economy is primarily agrarian, with excess foodstuffs sold to the Viscounty of Verbobonc. The township remains economically poor due to its historical conflicts, but it is known for its strong military presence.

##### Economic Activities

*   Agrarian economy
*   Excess foodstuffs sold to Verbobonc
*   Economically poor but militarily strong

##### Military Strength

Nearly all of Penwick's freemen are veteran soldiers or their descendants, giving the township a formidable militia. Mayor @connor can muster a well-armed militia of fifty in a night and three hundred by highsun the next day. The mayor also has the authority to draft mercenaries and adventurers as needed.

##### Military Strength

*   Veteran soldiers and descendants
*   Can muster a well-armed militia quickly
*   Authority to draft mercenaries and adventurers

### Current Issues and Threats

_**Local Threats  
**_

Penwick faces several threats, including river pirates, ettercaps, and roving bands of humanoids. Additionally, a green dragon has recently moved into the area, preying on livestock. Mayor Connor Grantham is actively seeking help from adventurers to deal with these threats.

*   River pirates
*   Ettercaps
*   Roving bands of humanoids
*   Green dragon preying on livestock

#### Conclusion

Penwick is a resilient township with a rich history and a complex political landscape. Governed by Mayor Connor Grantham and influenced by the powerful houses of Asbury and Milinous, Penwick continues to rebuild and strengthen its community. Despite economic challenges and ongoing threats, the township remains a significant military presence in the Viscounty of Verbobonc, poised to defend its lands and support its people.
