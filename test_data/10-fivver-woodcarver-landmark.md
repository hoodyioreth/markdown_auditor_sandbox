10\. Fivver Woodcarver
======================

On the west end of Sheernobb’s bustling trade meadow, nestled between the tranquil Peony Pond and the creaking Peony Mill, stands the quaint wood carving shop known as The Whittler’s Haven. Owned by the meticulous and artistic woodcarver Fivver Timbersnarl and his family, this shop produces some of the most intricate and beautiful carvings in all of Sheernobb. From finely detailed figurines to practical wooden tools and furnishings, the Timbersnarls have become a vital part of the village’s economy and culture.

### Description of the Cottage and Shop

The Whittler’s Haven is a modest yet charming cottage with a large, attached workshop. The building is constructed from local oak and elm wood, adorned with carved shutters and a sign that depicts a laughing fox, symbolizing the Timbersnarls’ playful and creative spirit.

*   **Workshop:** Large windows allow sunlight to flood the workshop, where Fivver and his apprentices can often be seen at work. The scent of freshly carved wood and pine resin fills the air. Tools of all sizes hang neatly on the walls, and partially completed projects line the shelves.
*   **Cottage:** The living quarters feature a cozy hearth, hand-carved furniture, and walls adorned with Fivver’s artistic works and gifts from satisfied villagers.
*   **Yard:** A small yard behind the shop boasts a tidy woodpile, a small carving shed, and a training post where the family’s eldest son once practiced archery.

#### Products and Services

The Whittler’s Haven offers a wide array of handcrafted wooden items, including:

*   **Ornamental Carvings**: Figurines, plaques, and totems featuring whimsical designs or gnomish lore.
*   **Practical Items:** Furniture, utensils, and tools for daily use, combining functionality with artistic flair.
*   **Toys and Games:** Popular with the village’s children, these include carved animals, spinning tops, and puzzle boxes.
*   **Custom Commissions:** Fivver creates personalized works for villagers and travelers, often embedding hidden meanings or humorous details.

#### Community Role and Relationships

*   **Village Life:** The Timbersnarls are well-loved in Sheernobb, not just for their craftsmanship but for their involvement in local festivals and events. Fivver’s carvings often feature prominently in celebrations, from decorative totems to festival trophies.
*   **Neighborly Bonds:** The Timbersnarls share a close friendship with the millers of Peony Mill, often trading herbal oils for flour and sharing news over a cup of tea.
*   **Support for the Guard:** Grynn’s position as a ranger ties the family to Sheernobb’s defense efforts. Fivver and Pymra regularly supply the guard with sturdy arrows and salves for injuries.

##### Quests

*   **The Stolen Figurines**: A set of valuable figurines carved by Fivver has gone missing, possibly stolen by bandits or mischievous fey. Recover the carvings before they are lost forever.
*   **A Ranger’s Call:** Grynn seeks help tracking down a bandit camp threatening the village, and he’s looking for skilled adventurers to assist in an ambush.
*   **The Enchanted Carving:** A customer claims one of Fivver’s carvings holds magical properties, but strange events begin to occur in the village. Investigate the carving’s origins and its unusual effects.
*   **Trouble at Peony Mill:** The Timbersnarls notice strange noises and shadows near Peony Mill at night. Could something—or someone—be lurking nearby?
*   **A Legacy in Danger:** A shipment of rare, enchanted wood meant for Fivver’s latest masterpiece has been delayed. Protect the caravan and ensure the materials reach Sheernobb safely.
*   The Whittler’s Haven is more than just a shop; it’s a cornerstone of Sheernobb’s culture, blending artistry, community, and tradition. The Timbersnarls embody the gnome spirit of creativity and resilience, providing endless opportunities for connection, adventure, and inspiration.

##### Fivver Timbersnarl (Patriarch, Woodcarver)

*   **Personality:** Fivver is a meticulous and imaginative craftsman with a sharp wit and a heart of gold. Though serious about his craft, he loves to laugh and often weaves humorous elements into his carvings.
*   **Background:** Born into a family of carpenters, Fivver showed an early talent for artistry rather than construction. He became renowned for blending practicality with beauty, carving everything from ornate furniture to playful toys.
*   **Motivations:** Fivver’s primary goal is to preserve and celebrate Sheernobb’s traditions through his art, while also providing for his family and inspiring future woodcarvers.

##### Pymra Timbersnarl (Matriarch, Herbalist)

*   **Personality:** Pymra is nurturing and resourceful, often seen tending to her herb garden or brewing potions for the villagers. She is known for her sharp intuition and gentle wisdom.
*   **Background:** The daughter of a healer, Pymra complements Fivver’s craft with her knowledge of nature, providing salves and oils to treat wood and invigorate the Timbersnarl children.
*   **Motivations:** Pymra aims to keep her family united and thriving while supporting Fivver’s creative endeavors with her herbal remedies.

##### Grynn Timbersnarl (Eldest Son, Ranger)

*   **Personality:** Grynn is serious and disciplined, a stark contrast to his father’s playful demeanor. Loyal and protective, he serves in Sheernobb’s guard under Captain Gnonsbim.
*   **Background:** Growing up, Grynn excelled in archery and wilderness survival, eventually joining the guard to help defend Sheernobb from bandits and creatures threatening the village.
*   **Motivations:** Grynn is driven by a sense of duty to protect his family and the village, inspired by his father’s unwavering dedication to community.

##### Pip and Millie Timbersnarl (Younger Twins, Apprentices)

*   **Personality:** Mischievous and energetic, the twins are inseparable and full of curiosity. They are just beginning to learn the art of wood carving under their father’s guidance.
*   **Background:** Though still young, Pip and Millie show promise in their craft, with Pip focusing on detailed figurines and Millie specializing in decorative patterns.
*   **Motivations:** The twins are eager to prove themselves and carry on the Timbersnarl legacy, though they often find themselves distracted by their youthful energy and schemes.
