Diocese of Whitehale
====================

#### Overview

The Diocese of Whitehale is not merely a province—it is the foundation upon which Veluna standeth.

Oldest of all the dioceses, it was forged in the hour Veluna cast off Furyondy and declared its own destiny. From that day to this, Whitehale hath remained the beating heart of the realm—feeding its people, anchoring its faith, and shaping its rule.

Here lie the endless fields that sustain the nation. Here standeth Mitrik, where the Crook of Rao was found and where the Canon ruleth in quiet majesty. And here, too, simmer the oldest rivalries… and the most dangerous ambitions.

#### Geography — Breadbasket of the Faith

Whitehale stretcheth across wide and fertile plains, where grain swayeth like a golden sea beneath the wind.

This abundance defines the land:

*   **Central Plains:** Endless farmland feeding Veluna and beyond
*   **Western Edge:** Falls of Mist and mountain-fed waters
*   **Northern Reach:** Strategic fortifications along the Fals River
*   **Southern Border:** Roads linking to the wider dioceses and Veluna City

As the land riseth toward the Yatil Mountains, it groweth harsher—but never barren. Whitehale feedeth the realm. And so long as it endureth, Veluna endureth.

#### Mitrik — Seat of the Archclericy

Mitrik is the soul of Veluna. Here standeth the great Cathedral of Rao, beneath which the Crook was found centuries ago. From this place the Archclericy governeth faith, law, and doctrine across the realm.

*   The city itself is ancient—built of stone, divided by walls, and ordered with purpose. Pilgrims walk its worn streets daily, their footsteps echoing those of generations past.
    
*   Within these walls dwell priests, scholars, and those few who whisper counsel into the ear of Canon Hazen himself.
*   To stand in Mitrik is to feel the weight of centuries pressing upon thy shoulders.
    

#### Government & Rule

Duchess Clariece Landis ruleth with fire where others ruled with comfort. Called suddenly from her life as a Knight of Veluna, she inherited not only a throne—but a nest of complacent nobles and hollow traditions.

*   She hath broken many of them.
    
*   Military offices once granted as idle honors have been stripped and reforged into true commands. Loyal men—proven, not titled—now lead her forces.
*   The old houses murmur. Some openly oppose her. Yet the people? They cheer her name.
    

#### The Church of Rao

Whitehale is the unquestioned heart of Rao’s worship in the Flanaess.

*   Bishop Roland Otjieg standeth as both shepherd and advisor—his voice calm where the Duchess’s is fierce.
    
*   Their alliance is strong… perhaps too strong for some tastes.
*   Certain nobles whisper that the Bishop hath grown too close to House Landis—that faith and rule have become entwined beyond propriety.
    

Thus far, such whispers remain only that.

#### Military Power — The Knights of Whitehale

The strength of Whitehale lieth not only in its fields—but in its steel.

*   The Knights of Whitehale, founded by Duke Yurik, serve as rapid defenders of the realm against threats from the Yatils and beyond.
    
*   Under Sir Ellard Rojan, they have become something more: a force of justice feared by brigands and beloved by the common folk.
*   Where nobles see politics, Ellard seeth right and wrong. And acteth accordingly.
    

#### Economy — The Bread of Veluna

Whitehale feedeth the realm. Its wealth is not glittering like Valkurl’s silver, nor flowing like Devarnish’s trade—but it is constant, dependable, and absolute.

*   Grain, livestock, and agricultural exports
*   Pilgrimage traffic to Mitrik</li>
*   Support industries: milling, storage, and transport</li>

Without Whitehale, Veluna would starve. And all powers within the realm know this truth.

#### Notable Locations

**Mitrik**

Ancient stone city and seat of the Archclericy; the spiritual center of Veluna and site of the Crook of Rao.

**Whitehale**

Seat of House Landis and center of regional governance; grown from a simple market into a refined noble town.

**Castle Sepher**

A rising fortress along the Fals River, built to counter threats from the north and the encroaching shadow of Iuz.

**Falls of Mist**

A towering waterfall whose waters never freeze—its surrounding caves home to dangers that must be regularly purged.
