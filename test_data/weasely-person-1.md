Weasely
=======

Weasely is no ordinary animal. No brainless rodent. But an alt-planar intelligent creature that you have bound to your will. When you first summoned him, he manifested into a form best suited to your personality. Such creatures are beings from another reality who possess immense power when they are on our plane of existence. This creatures true name only known to you is Agmokan. As the Law of Dichotomy states, that the Wizard, having once summoned a alt-planar creatures, must either control it with his mind and will, or be controlled by it. When Agmokan stepped through the summoning portal you painstakingly drew, the connection was instantaneous. He immediately submitted to be your familiar and friend. Seeing your familiar take on the form of a weasel. You smiled with invitation and named him "Weasely".   Weasely, as he does every night, keeps ever vigilant and alert while you slumber. Prowling the area, paying attention with his highly attuned hearing and smell. He takes pleasure in his material form chewing and crunching nuts while he is thankful to be enjoying the adventure that is life in the material plane of existence. He knows that his cord is directly connected to your life and would be instantly sent back to the ether that he came from if you would perish.
