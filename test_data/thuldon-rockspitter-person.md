Thuldon Rockspitter
===================

### Commander of the Mithral Guard

### Discovery in the Temple

A prisoner in **Room 20: Temple**, manacled to the wall and **stabilized** but unconscious. He revives with basic care and quickly asks for **steel** and **water**. All six human captives who shared his chains have been sacrificed; **Thuldon was next** until the PCs intervened.

**Stolen gear (to be found among the orcs):** dwarf-sized chain mail, **anvil-shield**, **fine hand axe**, and the **winged, wide-horn helm**—all Thuldon’s. He is profoundly grateful for their return.

* * *

#### Background & Current Course

*   **570 CY:** Leaves the Lortmils seeking the **lost citadel of Ulthak-Nor**, ancestral seat of Clan Rockhall. The surface citadel changed hands for 500 years, but no dwarf has walked those **smooth granite floors** in living memory.
*   **Wandering Years:** Six hard years hunting rumors across the Lortmils and Kron Hills. Ambushed by a warband; woke in chains in the **Suel Temple**.
*   **Rescued:** Owes the party (or Lady Asbury’s agents) his life and **chooses Asbury** as the place to plant his anvil.
*   **Now:** Smith for **Baroness Elinor Asbury**. She offers a **charter**—after **four years’ service** and apprentice-training, the forge becomes **his** by right.

**Muttered truth:** “I’ve always wanted to be **my own master**.” He means it. He’ll accompany the PCs back to Asbury, work, and **pay his debt in steel**.

* * *

#### At House Asbury

*   **Role:** Town armorer; repairs militia kit; forges tools, hinges, nails; saves coin for a **gift**—a mail shirt “like none she’s imagined” for **Lady** [Elinor Asbury](elinor-asbury-person.md).
*   **Obligation:** **4 years** bound by charter to train apprentices (including **Cito**, son of **Thoma**). Duty first; wanderlust second.

* * *

#### Family & Clan

*   **Father:** **Thornblade Rockspitter**, elder of **Clan Rockhall**, **priest of Moradin**, head of the **4th House of Ulthak-Nor**.
*   **Home:** The **Lortmil Mountains**; clan halls scattered; **Ulthak-Nor** (lost city) remains the Rockspitter dream.

**Social / Relationship Web**

*   [Thornblade Rocksplitter](thornblade-rocksplitter-person.md) **(Father & Clan Leader — Vital):** Proud, stern, expects deeds worthy of the forge.
*   **Thuldon Rockspitter (Eldest Son — Important):** Honest 5/5; torn between clan duty and a life he’s building.
*   **Gelten** (young dwarf, 63): Met in **Verbobonc City** while buying steel. First apprentice at Asbury—prefers **soft metals & ornament**. Thuldon supports her path; she **will travel with the PCs** to Rockhall if asked.

### Using Thuldon in Play

#### Roleplaying Cues

*   Plainspoken, practical; touches every tool before he speaks.
*   Calls the party “**masters**” until corrected; warms quickly to oaths kept.
*   One-liners:
*   _“A clean edge and a kept word—that’s civilization.”_
*   _“I’ll pay my debt_ _**in iron**__.”_
*   _“If there’s a way through, it’s through_ _**stone**__.”_

#### Table Stats (quick)

*   **Unarmed helper:** use **Commoner** (0 gp wage, better as torch/lever duty).
*   **Re-equipped smith-guard:** use **Guard** with dwarf flavor: **AC 18** (chain + shield), **HP 11**, **Speed 25 ft**, **Battleaxe +4 (1d8+2)**; **advantage** on saves vs. **poison**/**frightened** when within 10 ft of an ally (Stone-Stubborn perk).

* * *

#### Asbury Arc Hooks

1.  **Forge of Her Own** — Deliver Thuldon safely; Lady Elinor’s **charter** ceremony grants PCs **free basic repairs** in town and a **discount** (10%) on mundane metalwork.
2.  **Apprentice Troubles** — **Cito** and **Gelten** clash over technique; a minor social quest resolves into new **tool proficiencies** or a **custom trinket**.
3.  **Rockhall Envoys** — Thuldon’s duties keep him put; he begs the party to **carry a letter & gifts** to his father in the Lortmils. Success earns **clan hospitality** and access to **dwarven caches**.

* * *

#### The Rockhall Thread (mini-campaign seed)

*   **Ask:** Represent Thuldon to **Clan Rockhall** with a **letter and gift**; confirm his oath at Asbury and seek news of **Ulthak-Nor**.
*   **Why now:** He’s **homesick**, but duty binds him. “Duty is the measure of a dwarf.”
*   **Promise:** If the PCs take the road, Thuldon will **outfit** them: one **masterwork** (non-magical) metal item each, or a **single suit** of superior mail (advantage vs. rust/acid checks at DM’s option).

##### **Rewards ladder (choose what fits your table)**

*   **Return his gear:** Thuldon’s **lifelong discount** (20%) on mundane metalwork; **priority repairs**.
*   **Deliver the letter safely:** **Clan token of Rockhall** (advantage on Persuasion with Lortmil dwarves).
*   **Track a solid lead on Ulthak-Nor:** Access to **dwarven runesmith** services (downtime: silvered weapons, adamantine fittings).
