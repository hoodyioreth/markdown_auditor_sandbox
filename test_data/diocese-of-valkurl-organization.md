Diocese of Valkurl
==================

#### Overview

The Diocese of Valkurl spreadeth southward from the Velverdyva like a widening wedge of wealth and ambition. From river port to mountain mine, it bindeth trade, labor, and coin into a single relentless current.

*   Where other dioceses debate doctrine or tend fields, Valkurl diggeth, carrieth, and selleth.
*   It is a land of movement. Of wagons creaking under silver. Of barges heavy with ore. Of men who measure worth not in piety—but in profit.

#### Geography — From River to Mountain

The diocese beginneth at the Velverdyva, where trade floweth swift and constant, then stretcheth south into the rising foothills and deep into the Lortmil Mountains.

 

This progression of land defineth its nature:

*   **North:** River ports and trade hubs (Tempon)
*   **Central:** Roads, markets, and the city of Valkurl
*   **South:** Mining camps, dwarf holds, and mountain wealth

 

Every road leadeth south. Every cart returneth north laden with riches.

#### Government & Power

Baron Stephon Nital ruleth, though he was never meant to. Once a merchant of the High Roads Trading Company, he inherited power through misfortune rather than design. Now he standeth at the head of a diocese where coin and influence oft move faster than law.

*   His rule is firm—but watched.
*   Merchant houses distrust him. Guilds test him. Yet the people, thus far, accept him.
*   And behind him standeth Bishop Aniel Morlin—whose presence lendeth legitimacy, wisdom, and quiet authority to his reign.

#### The Church of Rao

Unlike Lorrish or Devarnish, Valkurl standeth in rare harmony between noble and church.

*   Bishop Aniel Morlin hath served for decades and is beloved by both common folk and rulers alike.
*   Where Baron Nital bringeth energy and ambition, she bringeth balance.
*   Many whisper that without her, the diocese would descend swiftly into merchant chaos.

#### Economy — The Silver Engine of Veluna

Valkurl is wealth made manifest.

 

Its economy is driven by three great engines:

*   **Mining:** Precious metals and gemstones from the Lortmils
*   **River Trade:** Ore flowing north via the Velverdyva
*   **Caravan Exchange:** Goods redistributed east toward Devarnish and beyond

Nearly every major merchant house in Veluna maintaineth presence here. Coin is plentiful. Competition is fierce. And fortunes are made—and lost—by the day.

#### Trade & Strategic Importance

Valkurl standeth at a critical junction within Veluna’s greater trade network:

*   Northward: Goods flow to Veluna City via the Velverdyva
*   Eastward: Trade routes connect to Devarnish and the Iron Woods corridor
*   Southward: Resource extraction from the Lortmils

With northern routes disrupted by Iuz, Valkurl’s role hath only grown. It is not merely a diocese—it is an artery of wealth.

#### Relations with the Rhennee

Tempon, upon the Velverdyva, is the only major Velunan port that openly welcomes the Rhennee. Their barges line the riverbanks, their festivals echo across the water, and their presence bringeth both opportunity and unease. Where others mistrust them, Valkurl profiteth from them.

#### Notable Locations

**Valkurl (City)**

A city divided between labor and leisure—half warehouses and trade yards, half taverns and boarding houses. It is loud, crowded, and relentlessly alive.

  **Tempon**

A river town of festivals and barges, where the Rhennee gather in great number and trade never truly ceaseth.

  **[Clan Rockhammer](clan-rockhammer-settlement.md)**

A powerful dwarven clan deep in the Lortmils, bound by exclusive trade to the High Roads Trading Company and a major source of wealth in the region.
