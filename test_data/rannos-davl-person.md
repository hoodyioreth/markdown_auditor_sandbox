Rannos Davl
===========

Rannos Davl, one of the co-proprietors of D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile in Hommlet, is a slow, complacent merchant who appears content with running a simple trading post. He is known for his laid-back demeanor and commitment to serving the needs of villagers, travelers, and adventurers alike. Despite his seemingly unremarkable presence, Rannos plays a key role in the community, providing essential goods and services while maintaining a friendly relationship with his customers. His shop is a bustling hub of commerce in the [Village of Hommlet](village-of-hommlet-settlement.md), offering everything from household essentials to adventuring gear.

#### Role in the Community

Rannos is seen as a typical merchant, one who provides essential goods and services to the community. He maintains a cordial relationship with the villagers and participates in local events, ensuring that his presence is a constant but unremarkable part of Hommlet's daily life.

### Background and Relationships

Rannos Davl established D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile alongside his partner, Gremag Gozlin, to capitalize on the growing trade opportunities in Hommlet. Over time, the shop became a central hub for commerce in the village, attracting adventurers and travelers with its wide array of goods and services.

##### Relationships

*   [Gremag Gozlin](gremag-gozlin-person.md): Rannos's business partner and co-conspirator, Gremag is both an ally and a confidant in their shared schemes. Together, they present a united front, handling the daily operations of the shop and ensuring their true motives remain hidden.
*   **Villagers and Customers:** Rannos maintains friendly relationships with his customers, ensuring he is seen as a helpful and reliable merchant. This helps mask his true intentions, allowing him to gather information and influence events subtly.

##### Motivations

*   **Profit:** Rannos is driven by a desire for wealth, seeking out opportunities to exploit and manipulate for financial gain.
*   **Influence:** He aims to increase his influence in Hommlet and beyond, using his position as a merchant to gather intelligence and sway events to his advantage.

### Current Activities

##### Shop Operations

Rannos is actively involved in the day-to-day operations of D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile, ensuring the shop is well-stocked and organized. He interacts with customers regularly, offering deals and special orders to maintain the shop's reputation as a reliable trading post.

##### Community Engagement

Rannos participates in local events and gatherings, maintaining his image as a supportive member of the community. He uses these opportunities to network and gather information, keeping a finger on the pulse of Hommlet's affairs.

##### True Allegiances

Rannos Davi is a servant of the Temple of Elemental Evil, using his position in Hommlet as a cover to further the Temple's dark goals. His true loyalty lies with the Temple, and he reports local activities to a brigand courier, ensuring that the Temple remains informed of potential threats and opportunities.

##### Evil Dealings

*   **Collaboration with Lareth:** Rannos collaborates with the bandits of Lareth, using his shop to fence stolen goods and move contraband through Hommlet. This partnership allows him to profit from banditry and chaos in the region.
*   **Manipulation of Adventurers:** Rannos is particularly interested in adventurers who uncover hidden treasures or dungeons. He gathers information from them under the guise of friendly conversation, then passes this intelligence on to evil adventurers who share a cut of their spoils with him.

##### Plans for Hommlet

Rannos intends to destabilize the region further by increasing bandit activities and fostering distrust among the villagers. He uses his influence to sow seeds of discord, all while maintaining his facade of a harmless merchant.

##### Partnership with Gremag

Together with Gremag, Rannos plots to increase their control over Hommlet, using the shop as a base of operations for their nefarious activities. They aim to extend their influence beyond the village, manipulating events to serve the Temple's interests and their own ambitions.

##### Exploitation of Bibi and Garent

Rannos uses Bibi and Garent as spies and agents, employing them to monitor adventurers and report any valuable discoveries. This allows Rannos to stay informed and maintain control over potential threats and opportunities in Hommlet.

### Temple Command Chain — Who Rannos Reports To

**Rannos Davl does not operate independently. He is a managed Temple asset under the direct oversight of **Lucius Graeme**, the Greater Temple's courier and intelligence handler.** The "brigand courier" Rannos sends reports through is part of Lucius's encrypted communication network — every piece of intelligence Rannos gathers flows up through Lucius to **Hedrack** and the Greater Temple leadership.

#### Rannos's Reporting Chain

*   **Direct handler:** **Lucius Graeme** — Greater Temple courier and intelligence handler. Lucius recruited Rannos, provides operational direction, and evaluates his intelligence product. All of Rannos's reports pass through Lucius before reaching Greater Temple leadership
*   **Communication method:** Encrypted courier network. Rannos passes written reports to a brigand courier who carries them to Lucius at his staging points along the Nulb–Hommlet corridor. Lucius evaluates the intelligence and forwards actionable items to **Hedrack**
*   **Operational partner:** [Gremag Gozlin](gremag-gozlin-person.md) — Rannos's co-proprietor at D&G Dry Goods and his co-conspirator. Both report to Lucius Graeme through the same courier network. Lucius considers them a single intelligence cell
*   **Strategic authority:** **Hedrack** (Greater Temple). Rannos does not know Hedrack personally — all orders from the Greater Temple leadership come down through Lucius. Rannos knows only that his handler serves a powerful Temple superior

#### Rannos's Place in the Hommlet Spy Network

Rannos and [Gremag Gozlin](gremag-gozlin-person.md) are two of four Temple agents embedded in [Hommlet](village-of-hommlet-settlement.md). All four report to **Lucius Graeme**:

*   **Rannos Davl & Gremag Gozlin** — D&G Dry Goods & General Mercantile. Intelligence gathering and smuggling coordination. Primary recruitment contacts for civilian sympathizers
*   **Zert** — Mercenary agent at the Inn of the Welcome Wench. Monitors adventurers, sabotages Doomwatch Keep construction. Lucius's enforcer in Hommlet
*   **[Joseph Millar](joseph-millar-person.md)** — Saboteur at the Doomwatch Keep construction site. Delays fortification construction under Lucius's direction. Also has a separate link to the [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md) through [Romag](romag-person.md)

This spy network was originally built to support [Lareth](lareth-person.md) the Beautiful's Moathouse operation. Now that Lareth has been captured or killed, the network continues to operate under Lucius Graeme's direct control — the agents' loyalty is to the Greater Temple, not to Lareth personally.

#### DM Notes: Why the Command Chain Matters

*   **If the party captures Rannos:** Interrogation (DC 14 Intimidation, DC 16 Persuasion) can extract the existence of his "handler" — a man who travels the Nulb–Hommlet road and gives Rannos his orders. Rannos knows Lucius by name but does not know Lucius's connection to **Hedrack** or the Greater Temple hierarchy. This is a breadcrumb that leads to Lucius, not a complete intelligence picture
*   **If the party kills Rannos:** Lucius loses his primary intelligence source in Hommlet. He redirects intelligence tasking to Zert and [Gremag Gozlin](gremag-gozlin-person.md), increasing their operational tempo and making them more likely to be detected
*   **If Lareth's diary is recovered:** The diary implicates Rannos, Gremag, and Zert — but does NOT name Lucius. The diary only references a "courier" who delivers Hedrack's orders. Connecting the courier to **Lucius Graeme** requires additional investigation (DC 18 Investigation to cross-reference courier descriptions from multiple sources)

##### Merchant Rannos Davl

Rannos Davl is a man of Oeridian and Flan descent, characterized by his bulky, slow-moving frame and round, sweat-slicked face. He often wears simple, functional clothes that blend in with those of other merchants, further reinforcing his image as a harmless and somewhat lazy shopkeeper.

##### Personality

Outwardly, Rannos is placid and appears somewhat clumsy, giving off an air of complacency that puts others at ease. He often engages in light conversation with customers and is quick to offer discounts and deals to encourage trade. To most, he seems content with his life in Hommlet, running a shop and serving the needs of villagers and travelers alike.
