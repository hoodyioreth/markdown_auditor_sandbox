Fernan
======

### Ranger Scout

> He wears clothing that is held on in multiple layers but all of it is in poor condition. His left side is far more bruised and cut up than his other. He typically wears a sleeveless long coat of deep yellow leather and dull green iron scale mail. His brown hair is greasy and uncared for. His face possesses mild wrinkles. His smile is extraordinarily sincere though he does not smile often.

#### History

He was born a particularly ugly child, much to the horror of his parents. Through the years after Vanuath was born, his mother became increasingly distant from his family, instead choosing to pursue power in the north. Vanuath's mother didn't care for him much, and the feeling was mutual. He was recently attacked on the road and savagely beaten by bandits. Found by the Mounted Borderers and cared for he joined the organization as a Scout. Since then, he has proven himself to be a value.

#### Motivation

To prevent unauthorized entry into the Kingdom of [Celene](celene-organization.md). Seek out and destroy Orc, giant and Gnoll incursions into the area. Guard against the Kron hills and Gnarley forest. Track their movements and setup ambushes. Work with the Elves of the Gnarley Forest and Celene border guard at Caer Ostverk. Also friends and colleague of the Ranger Knight [Wyn](wyn-person.md) of the [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md).

#### Virtues & Personality perks

He is cool and collected. He likes to make quips and puns whenever he does anything. He also makes a point to compliment his superiors and flatter them. He is harsh and unforgiving.
