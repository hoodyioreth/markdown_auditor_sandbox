D6 Bastion of the Conclave
==========================

A disciplined barracks-fortress and council-seat of the Elven Conclave, housing 64 veteran elves of Celene—spell-blades and ward-mages—who keep the pebblestone ways of the Elven Dawn quiet, watched, and inviolate. A strong part of the [Verbobonc Military](verbobonc-military-formation.md).

##### Arrival at the Bastion of the Conclave

The pebblestones beneath thy boots grow smoother, as if countless years of measured steps have polished them. Ahead, a low wall of pale stone curves in a gentle half-moon, and beyond it rises the Bastion—neither gaudy nor grim, but certain. Tall ipt trees stand as sentries, their boughs trained into living galleries and watch-ways, and along the parapets hang lanterns hooded in green glass, casting a calm, leaf-tinted light. No gate stands open; yet thou feelest—most keenly—that thou art observed. A figure in grey-green mail, cloaked in quiet dignity, lifts a hand. Sentinel: _“Name thy business, traveler. The Dawn is fair—yet fairness is not the same as permission.”_

### Sentinels of the Ancient Glades

The Bastion of the Conclave is the **martial heart** of the Elven Enclave: barracks, training grounds, archives, and council chambers in one. It is not merely a “post” within Verbobonc—rather, it is a living statement that the Enclave’s peace is **kept**, not granted. The militia here numbers **64 veteran elves from** [Celene, Faerie Kingdom of](../c/celene2C-faerie-kingdom-of-category.md), renowned for mastery of **blade and spell** alike.

The Bastion’s deeper purpose is cultural: to preserve elven discipline, memory, and autonomy within a human city—while still serving as a diplomatic bridge to Verbobonc’s Viscount and lords.

#### Ancient Origins

*   **Foundation Days:** The Enclave's roots go back to the time when Verbobonc was a modest elven outpost.
*   **Elven-Gnome Alliance:** A bond with the gnomes of the Kron Hills strengthened the elven community’s defense.

### Exterior & Grounds

**Half-Moon Wall & Gate-Court:** A graceful outer wall encloses a court of clipped grass and training sand. Visitors are received here first; deeper entry is by invitation or official summons.

**Ipt-Walks & Watch-Ways:** Living ipt boughs are shaped into elevated walks, allowing sentinels to move silently overhead—never rushing, never absent. (A perfect visual cue for players: _the quarter is watched from above._)

**Courtyard Drill:** You may show the militia as serene rather than brutal: paired drills, spell-forms practiced in silence, archers loosing in perfect cadence.

* * *

##### Interior: Key Areas of the Bastion

**Main Hall:** Strategy talk, formal greetings, and quiet instruction—where a visitor can be made to feel welcome while still being weighed.

**Living Quarters:** Spartan by elven standards—clean lines, restrained beauty, possessions few. (The message: _service first, self second._)

**Armory & Archives:** Enchanted weapons and the written record of campaigns are kept together—blade beside memory, as if each is meaningless without the other.

**Training Grounds:**

*   **Sparring Arenas** for duels and discipline
*   **Arcane Field** for spell-practice and ward-forms
*   **Obstacle Course** for agility, archery, and spell precision

### History: Chronicles of the Elven Conclave

The Elven Enclave's long and storied history reflects the trials and triumphs of Verbobonc's elves.

##### Settlement Era

*   **Founding of the Enclave:** Established as a protectorate when elves first settled in Verbobonc.
*   **Gnome Alliances:** Joint efforts with the [Free Alliance of the Kron Hills](free-alliance-of-the-kron-hills-organization.md) gnomes to repel humanoid threats from the Iron Wood and Kron Hills.

##### Temple of Elemental Evil Campaigns

*   **First Campaign (568 CY):** Elven militia supported the allied forces at Emridy Meadows.
*   **Second Campaign (574 CY):** Reinforced Hommlet during the construction of its defensive castle.

##### Post-War Era

*   **Greyhawk Wars (582-584 CY):** Defended the Elven Quarter from infiltrators during the Greyhawk Wars.
*   **Modern Enclave (Current Era):** Formed a militia to maintain stability in the Elven Quarter amidst growing threats.

### Relationships: Converging Paths

The Elven Enclave Barracks maintains intricate relationships with various factions in Verbobonc.

##### Elven Conclave

*   **Stewards of Tradition:** Guards elven culture and ensures the Elven Quarter’s peaceful coexistence with the city.
*   **Council of Elders:** The supreme decision-making body within the Enclave.

##### City of Verbobonc

*   **Viscount** [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md): The Enclave recognizes the Viscount’s authority while securing autonomy for the elves.
*   **Lords of Verbobonc:** Maintains diplomatic ties with influential [The Council of Lords of Verbobonc](the-council-of-lords-of-verbobonc-organization.md) , especially those overseeing areas adjacent to the Elven Quarter.

##### Gnomes of the Kron Hills

*   **Trade and Defense Allies:** Continued cooperation in trade and regional defense.

### Relationship Between the Elven Enclave and the Knights of Luna

##### Shared History and Lore

**Mutual Legacy of Emridy Meadows (570 CY).  
**The Bastion and the Knights of Luna both trace their living purpose to the Battle of Emridy Meadows, and they speak of that campaign as a _covenant_, not a victory. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

**Guardians of Tradition.  
**Both uphold ancient elven custom, defending Celene’s monarchy and martial discipline as a moral duty — even while dwelling within a human city. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

##### Collaborative Efforts

**Strategic Defense of Celene.  
**They coordinate to safeguard Celene’s borders and elven interests in Verbobonc — the Bastion as the city-root, the Knights as the border-bough. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

**Campaigns Against Evil.  
**Joint operations are recorded against remnants of the Temple of Elemental Evil, and later cooperation to guard Verbobonc during the Greyhawk Wars is remembered as proof that “isolation is not safety.” D6 Bastion of the Conclave

##### Political Relationships

**Advocating Against Isolationism.  
**The Enclave aligns with the Knights of Luna in arguing for a more open Celene policy, resisting the fey queen’s isolationist stance — a dangerous opinion to hold too loudly. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

**The Viscount & Shared Allies.  
**Both maintain a delicate balance with Viscount Wilfrick Verdanhart: respect shown, autonomy guarded. They also collaborate with the Knights of the Hart when enemies and objectives align, especially for the defense of the Viscounty. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

### Core Values and Creed

**Elven Leadership.  
**They share the belief that elven leadership is necessary to guide the “lesser races” toward a brighter course — a creed that can read as benevolence… or arrogance… depending on who hears it. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

**Noblesse Oblige.  
**The Enclave echoes the Knights of Luna’s sense of responsibility toward both their kin and other folk — a duty that often places them in the path of danger _before_ human institutions awaken. D6 Bastion of the Conclave

### DM Levers: How This Changes Play at the Bastion

*   **If the PCs mention the Temple**: the Bastion’s stance hardens into _professional memory_ (“We have hunted such roots before.”). Use this to justify immediate access to maps, quiet warnings, or a “measured test” rather than dismissal. D6 Bastion of the Conclave
*   **If the PCs push Celene politics**: you can split the room between _open-policy Luna sympathizers_ and _quiet loyalists_ who fear offending the Fey Court. The PCs become a thread in that argument. D6 Bastion of the Conclave
*   **If the PCs invoke the Viscount / Knights of the Hart**: it becomes a diplomacy scene, not a security scene — “We honor him. Yet we are not his wardens.”

The Elven Enclave Barracks stands as a symbol of elven unity and resilience amidst Verbobonc's diverse landscape. As the elven militia continues to guard the Pebblestone Roads, they embody both the Elven Conclave’s rich heritage and their unwavering dedication to peace and coexistence.
