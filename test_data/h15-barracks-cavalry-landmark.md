H15 Barracks – Cavalry
======================

_The Viscount’s Iron Hooves Beyond the Walls_

* * *

Just beyond Verbobonc’s city walls stands **Warmaster Eadric’s Cavalry Barracks**, a fortified complex of stone, timber, and long stables that houses one of the viscounty’s most elite formations: **fifty heavy cavalry** of the Standing Army. Their presence is both sword and signal—ready to ride at a moment’s notice, and a visible reminder that Verbobonc will not be caught unprepared again.

These horsemen are the **armored fist** of Viscount [Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md)’s military reforms, part of the 300-strong standing militia and a key piece in the broader network of Vigil Wardens, Mounted Borderers, and allied demi-humans that guard the viscounty’s borders.

* * *

##### Location & First Impressions

*   **Position:** The barracks and stables stand **just outside Verbobonc’s walls**, along one of the main approach roads. From the city towers, the banners of the cavalry can be seen snapping in the wind—close enough to support the gates, far enough to deploy without bottlenecking inside the streets.

The road bends along the outer wall until the stone gives way to open ground and the smell of horses. Ahead, a low palisade and stout gate enclose a cluster of barrack-houses and long, slate-roofed stables. Pennants bearing Verbobonc’s colors snap atop the corner towers as armored riders guide their mounts through practiced drills in a churned practice field. Grooms hurry between stalls, farriers hammer at glowing shoes beside a forge, and the clatter of hooves and shouted commands fills the cold air. This is no sleepy rural post; this is a camp that expects the next order to be ride.

#### Role in Verbobonc's Politics and Defense

*   The heavy cavalry stationed here are a key component of Viscount Wilfrick’s military strategy, reflecting his approach to the safety and sovereignty of Verbobonc.
*   They serve as a rapid response unit for threats both within and on the outskirts of the Viscounty.
*   Their presence outside the city walls symbolizes a show of force and readiness, deterring potential aggressors.

#### Historical Significance

*   The Barracks – Cavalry has been a historical constant in the defense of the city, with its origins tracing back to the earlier days of the Viscounty.
*   Throughout various conflicts, including the battles against the Temple of Elemental Evil, these cavalry forces have played a crucial role in ensuring victory and stability.

#### Safety and Security of the Viscounty

*   Under Viscount Wilfrick’s leadership, the cavalry at these barracks have been instrumental in maintaining peace and order.
*   Their strategic positioning allows for efficient monitoring of trade routes and quick deployment to troubled areas.
*   Their presence reassures the citizens of Verbobonc, who have seen their fair share of turmoil, lending a sense of stability and security.

#### Current Political Climate

*   Viscount Wilfrick’s reliance on the cavalry reflects his commitment to a strong defense policy.
*   The cavalry serves as a symbol of the Viscount's authority and his dedication to safeguarding the Viscounty.

In the complex political landscape of Verbobonc, where tensions with neighboring powers like Veluna and Furyondy simmer, the Cavalry Barracks stand as a testament to the Viscount's readiness to defend his rule and the independence of the Viscounty.

The Barracks – Cavalry outside the city walls of Verbobonc is not just a military installation; it's a symbol of the Viscount's power, the city's historical resilience, and a reassurance of safety to the people of the Viscounty. Its presence is a constant reminder of the need for vigilance and preparedness in a region that has seen its share of conflicts and political intrigues.

### Using H15 in Your Campaign

You can treat **H15 – Warmaster Eadric’s Cavalry Barracks** as:

*   A **military hub** where PCs meet officers, request escorts, or negotiate for mounted support.
*   A place to **witness drills** that show off Verbobonc’s discipline and readiness.
*   A source of **war rumors and strategic information** about threats in the Kron Hills, Gnarley Forest, or along the Velverdyva.

### **Adventure Hooks**

*   **Escort of Honor:** Eadric assigns the PCs to ride with a small troop of heavy cavalry escorting a vital envoy or relic—perfect for ambushes and political complications.
*   **Saddle Troubles:** Sabotage within the stables—tampered gear, poisoned feed—suggests spies or cult agents trying to cripple the cavalry before a looming crisis.
*   **Field Evaluation:** Eadric is considering promoting a promising young officer and wants the party to accompany him or her on a real patrol, reporting back honestly on their performance.
*   **Old Scars, New War:** Veterans in the barracks who fought at Emridy Meadows or against the Temple of Elemental Evil recognize a symbol, tactic, or cult sign the PCs bring back from the field, triggering a new investigation.
