### barkeep

Tíbor Timason, the owner and bartender of the Black Griffon Inn, is a central figure in the social and political landscape of [Nigb's Rest](nigb-s-rest-settlement.md). Beholden to [House Milinous](house-milinous-organization.md), Tíbor ensures the inn serves as a welcoming gathering place while subtly promoting the interests of his patrons. His charismatic personality and strategic acumen make him a respected and influential figure in the community.

### Description

Tíbor Timason is a sturdy and approachable man known for his warm hospitality and keen political awareness. His practical attire is always clean and well-maintained, reflecting his commitment to providing excellent service to his patrons. Despite his busy role, Tíbor always finds time to welcome guests with a smile.

#### Personality

Tíbor is friendly and charismatic, always ready with a smile and a welcoming word. He exudes confidence and takes pride in the quality of service provided at the Black Griffon Inn. Tíbor's shrewd political awareness allows him to navigate the complex landscape of Nigb's Rest, balancing his loyalty to House Milinous with his commitment to the local community.
