Village of Hommlet
==================

The Village of Hommlet, commonly referred to simply as Hommlet, is situated in the central part of the Flanaess, in the western Oerik Continent. This hamlet-sized village lies about thirty leagues southeast of Verbobonc, on the edge of the territory controlled by the noble Viscount [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md) of Verbobonc. A crucial crossroads for travelers, Hommlet sees traffic from the north to Verbobonc and south to Celene, as well as east through the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md) to Dyvers and west into the [Greenway Valley](greenway-valley-settlement.md).

### History

Hommlet has a long and storied history, particularly marked by its connection to the infamous Temple of Elemental Evil. Initially a quiet farming community, the village grew as travelers and merchants passed through. The rise of the Temple brought significant turmoil, culminating in its fall in 569 CY. The village prospered thereafter, but new threats have emerged, particularly with the resurgence of bandit activities in recent years.

##### Key Historical Events

*   Rise and Fall of the Temple of Elemental Evil: The village witnessed the rise of one of the greatest centers of corruption and its subsequent defeat.- Adventurer Influence: Post the temple's fall after [The Battle of Emridy Meadows](the-battle-of-emridy-meadows-myth.md), adventurers played a significant role in defending and enriching the village.- Bandit Resurgence: In 576 CY, bandit activities have increased, posing new challenges to Hommlet.

### Geography

Hommlet’s quiet but important position within the [Kron Hills](kron-hills-location.md) makes it a natural stopping place for trade, travel, and rumor along the borderlands. The village is surrounded by fertile farmlands, low wooded hills, and the winding roads and trails that bind this region together. [Nigb's Rest](nigb-s-rest-settlement.md) lies some 10 miles to the north, while the small mountain range called the Etter rises about 5 miles north of the village. To the south, [Sheernobb](sheernobb-settlement.md)  stands roughly 10 miles distant among the hills. Eastward, the road runs toward Nulb, some 8 miles away, passing beyond the Moathouse Fens and the old ruined moathouse before reaching that ill-famed settlement on the marsh’s edge. The farmstead itself benefits from the rich soil and ample water of the region. Around it stretch well-tended fields of wheat, corn, and vegetables, with nearby pastures for cattle and sheep. A small grove of fruit trees adds to the land’s bounty, yielding apples, pears, and cherries in their season.

### Society and Culture

The people of Hommlet are predominantly human, with a mix of [Oeridian Oerid](oeridian-oerid-ethnicity.md) and [Flannae Flan](flannae-flan-ethnicity.md) ancestry. The village is governed by Elder Kenter Sr., who follows [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md). He is advised by retired adventurers Burne and Rufus, and religious leaders from the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) and the local druidic order. The community is close-knit and cautious of outsiders, shaped by past conflicts and the need for vigilance.

### Government and Laws

Hommlet is governed by Elder Kenter Sr., who acts as the Justice of the Peace. Major decisions are made with input from Burne, Rufus, and religious representatives. Taxation is minimal, and a portion of taxes goes to Burne and Rufus for their protection services. The village maintains a strict but fair legal system, ensuring peace and order within the community.

### Politics

Political tensions have escalated between the human inhabitants of Verbobonc and the [Gnomes](gnomes-species-1.md) communities, disrupting trade and cooperation. The withdrawal of [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md) patrols and [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md) troops has exacerbated these tensions, with accusations flying from both sides regarding deceit, theft, and aggression.

##### Political Issues

*   Gnome-Human Tensions: Strained relationships affecting trade and cooperation.- Military Withdrawals: Reduction in protective forces leading to increased vulnerability.- Bandit Threats: Rising bandit activities impacting both human and gnome merchants.

### Religion

Hommlet's religious life is dominated by the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) and the local druidic tradition. The Church emphasizes law and order, while the druids focus on balance and harmony with nature. Both play vital roles in the village, providing spiritual guidance and support.

##### Key Religious Figures

*   Canon [Terjon](terjon-person.md) of St. Cuthbert: Leader of the Church of St. Cuthbert.- Druid [Jaroo Ashstaff](jaroo-ashstaff-person.md) of the Grove: Head of the local druidic order of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md).

### Military

Hommlet's defense is primarily managed by a local militia with Captain Zeb Fletcher, supported by Burne and Rufus. The militia consists of 30-40 members, equipped to handle bandits, monsters, and other threats. The recent increase in bandit raids has tested the militia's capabilities, necessitating enhanced vigilance and preparedness.

### Key Military Figures

*   Burne: Key defender and strategist.- Rufus: Military leader and warrior.- Zeb and Elmo: Leaders within the local militia.

### Current Events

##### Rising Banditry

The merchant roads around Hommlet have seen a surge in bandit attacks, disrupting trade and instilling fear among travelers. This increase in criminal activity coincides with the withdrawal of [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md) patrols and Viscounty troops, leaving the village more vulnerable.

##### Gnome-Human Tensions

Political tensions between Verbobonc's human nobles and the gnome communities have worsened, impacting trade and cooperation. Accusations of deceit and theft fly from both sides, creating an atmosphere of distrust and uncertainty.

##### Economic Strain

Local businesses, particularly vineyards and ranches, have suffered due to bandit raids. Merchants are considering arming themselves, and some are calling for increased military intervention to restore order and security.

##### Community Response

The villagers, led by Burne and Rufus, are taking steps to address these challenges. Efforts to bolster the militia, secure trade routes, and mediate between conflicting factions are underway, demonstrating the resilience and unity of Hommlet's inhabitants.

##### Conclusion

The Village of Hommlet, with its rich history and strategic location, remains a vital and vibrant community despite recent challenges. Its people, guided by strong leadership and a spirit of resilience, continue to navigate the complexities of their world, striving to maintain peace and prosperity in the face of adversity.
