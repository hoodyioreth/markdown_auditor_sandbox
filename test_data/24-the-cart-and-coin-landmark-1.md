24\. The Cart And Coin
======================

The Cart and Coin is a bustling wainwright and wagon shop owned by Galti Dextersson and his large family. Located in the west part of Nigb's Rest, behind the Greensward Estate, the shop provides essential cart and wagon services to the township and beyond.  

### Location

The Cart and Coin is situated on the western side of Nigb's Rest, nestled behind the expansive Greensward Estate. This strategic location places it conveniently near the main roads and the township's agricultural heart, facilitating the transport of goods and supplies.

### History

The Cart and Coin was established by Galti Dextersson after he recognized the growing need for reliable transport and wagon repair services in Nigb's Rest. Over the years, the shop has become integral to the local economy, facilitating the movement of goods and supporting the agricultural and commercial activities of the township.

##### Notable Events

*   **Establishment:** Founded by Galti Dextersson to meet the rising demand for wagon and cart services.
*   **Expansion:** Expanded services to include a wainwright and the construction of a unique crane system for efficient handling of heavy loads.
*   **Community Role:** The shop has played a vital role in supporting local farmers, merchants, and even the military operations of [1\. Fort Emridy](1-fort-emridy-landmark-1.md).

### The Shop

*   **Outside Description:** The Cart and Coin is housed in a large barn with a sturdy, practical design. The barn holds two carts, three wagons, six horses, and two guard dogs. A simple sign depicting a horse and cart hangs above the entrance.
*   **Inside Description:** Inside, the shop is a hive of activity. The main work area features a unique crane system, which includes a metal railing, a miniature cart, and a chain hook mechanism for lifting and moving heavy items. This innovative setup allows workers to reposition loads with ease.

### Relationships

Galti Dextersson has cultivated strong relationships with many influential figures in Nigb's Rest.

*   Mayor [Marcus Greensward](marcus-greensward-person.md): Galti supports the mayor’s initiatives for community development and infrastructure improvement.
*   [House Asbury](house-asbury-organization.md): Loyal to House Asbury, Galti ensures his business aligns with their interests and provides services to support their agricultural and commercial activities.
*   [Tanithil Mornala](tanithil-mornala-person.md) the Druid: Galti maintains a respectful relationship with Tanithil, discussing land use and environmental concerns.
*   Commander [Olean Rodrigo](olean-rodrigo-person.md): Provides transport services to Fort Emridy, supporting the commander’s logistical needs.
*   [Gigur Nónason](gigur-nonason-person.md): Works closely with the Vintner's Guild Hall to transport wine and related goods across the viscounty.

#### Summary

The Cart and Coin, owned by Galti Dextersson and his family, is a cornerstone of Nigb's Rest’s economy. Providing essential wagon and cart services, the shop supports local farmers, merchants, and even military operations. With strong community ties and a commitment to quality, Galti ensures that The Cart and Coin remains a vital part of the township’s infrastructure.
