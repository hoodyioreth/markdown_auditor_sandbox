Istus soothsayers plight
========================

From out of a tent draped in layers of colorful layers of silks a Baklunish woman of golden skin tones, high cheek bones, tall and slender with straight, fine-long blueish black hair. Fixes her unnerving grey eyes upon you. This NPC is from the  Istus chapel location in Verbobonc city: @[D5 Hindsight](d5-hindsight-landmark.md)   

> "Ranger Falsinor, I see your strand of fate. I see it as plain as a ships tether. It is strong and disappears east over the horizon. Come in, come in, I have much to tell. Have a seat at Istus's table. Maybe I can discern your fate...for a fee..."

  She leads you into the cramped tent dominated by a small table layers in more silks. Many tallow candles light the tent casting it into an unnatural purple light. She takes a seat across the table from you and waves her bejeweled hand at the opposite seat for you to sit. She curls her very long nails around a silvery bowl engraved with flowing runes. Then she reaches down under the table and draws out a crystal skull covered in withering silvery runes that flow around the orb. You hear a faint multitude of whispers emanating from within. But you are unable to make out the words. As if a dozen voices are speaking at once. She holds the skull over the bowl. The silvery liquid in the bowl swirls upwards to circle the skull as her eyes glow with the same color. Then she whispers her ritual and intones the words.  

> By recall's light and wisdom's might, the past shall point towards the future...Behold! The true power of Istus, the Lady of Our Fate. There are few left with the knowledge to create an artefact with such... potential.
