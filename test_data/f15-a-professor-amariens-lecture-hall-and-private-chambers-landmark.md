F15-A — Professor Amarien’s Lecture Hall & Private Chambers
===========================================================

Within the Wrinkle Academy of Magic, few spaces are as eagerly anticipated—or as quietly feared—as the lecture hall of **Professor Amarien**. Where other professors teach from chalkboards and lecterns, Amarien teaches through _experience_. Her hall is a place where magic is not merely discussed, but **felt**—where heat presses against the skin, sand grinds beneath boots, and summoned forces strain visibly against their bindings.

Students entering this chamber quickly learn that Amarien does not separate instruction from demonstration. Lessons unfold as living tableaux: deserts bloom indoors, storms break without warning, and the boundaries between the Material Plane and elsewhere grow thin—but never uncontrolled. Every display is deliberate, every danger measured, and every spectacle serves a deeper lesson about balance, restraint, and respect for power.

Beyond the public hall lies Amarien’s private domain: a study and summoning annex rarely seen by students and closely warded against intrusion. Here, Baklunish arcane traditions mingle with Flanaess scholarship, and the quiet presence of her familiar, Nelothel, watches all who enter. Though Amarien’s methods are dramatic, her discipline is absolute—those who mistake spectacle for recklessness do not remain students for long.

This location serves as both a **center of advanced instruction** and a subtle reminder of the Wrinkle Academy’s philosophy: magic is a force to be _understood_, not merely wielded.

##### General Description

The air here is warm and dry, carrying the faint scent of spice and sun-baked stone. The walls are draped in layered silks of ochre, turquoise, and deep crimson, each embroidered with geometric sigils unfamiliar to Flanaess spellcraft. Braziers burn low with smokeless flame, casting shifting shadows that feel more like desert twilight than indoor lamplight.  
  
Beneath your feet, the stone floor has been inlaid with a vast circular pattern—part lecture diagram, part summoning ring—its lines worn smooth by years of ritual use. The room feels… attentive, as though it remembers every spell ever spoken here.

### Keyed Areas

##### 1\. The Central Teaching Circle

At the heart of the chamber lies a **large circular inlay** of sandstone, lapis, and brass. This is Amarien’s primary lecture space.

*   Used for demonstrations of **conjuration and evocation**
*   Can be magically altered to simulate environments:
*   desert heat
*   shifting dunes
*   thunderous winds
*   illusionary horizons
*   The circle dampens catastrophic spell backlash; destructive spells deal **reduced collateral damage** here

**DM Note:  
**Amarien prefers to teach _inside_ the circle while students stand at its edge—forcing them to observe before acting.

* * *

##### 2\. Student Galleries

Tiered stone benches line the outer curve of the room, each etched with Baklunish numerological markings.

*   Seats 30–40 students comfortably
*   Inscriptions subtly **enhance concentration checks**
*   A hidden enchantment discourages reckless spellcasting from the benches

**Roleplay Hook:  
**Students often whisper that the carvings “count mistakes.”

* * *

##### 3\. The Oasis Alcove

A recessed archway houses a permanent conjured **illusory oasis**—palm fronds, trickling water, and cool shade.

*   Used to teach elemental balance and environmental magic
*   Can be dispelled or reshaped at will by Amarien
*   Occasionally populated with conjured creatures during examinations

**DM Note:  
**Amarien once dismissed a student for attempting to harvest conjured water for resale.

* * *

##### 4\. Private Study & Spell Library (Restricted)

Behind a silk-curtained arch lies Amarien’s **private chambers**, accessible only to staff or trusted students.

Inside:

*   Low writing desks strewn with scrolls and Baklunish treatises
*   Brass astrolabes and planar charts suspended by fine chains
*   Locked cabinets containing rare conjuration scrolls
*   Nelothel, her green lizard familiar, is almost always present—watching

**Security:**

*   Warded against teleportation
*   Unauthorized entry triggers a nonlethal but humiliating banishment spell
*   Repeat offenders are barred from Academy grounds

* * *

##### 5\. The Sand Circle (Summoning Annex)

A secondary chamber connected to the study contains a **deep sand-filled pit**, ringed with runes.

*   Used for safe summoning of elementals and extraplanar creatures
*   Sand absorbs magical shock and anchors unstable conjurations
*   Evidence of past earth elemental bindings is clearly visible

**DM Hook:  
**One rune here is subtly cracked—recently, and not by Amarien.

* * *

##### 6\. Halamin’s Stall (External Reference)

Though not within the chamber itself, Amarien’s **celestial camel Halamin** is stabled nearby in a meticulously crafted **sandalwood stall**, in compliance with Academy regulations.

*   Frequently visited by Amarien between lectures
*   Students consider it good luck to glimpse Halamin before examinations

* * *

#### Atmosphere & Use in Play

This location reinforces:

*   Amarien’s **theatrical but disciplined teaching style**
*   Baklunish magical philosophy within a Flanaess academy
*   The Wrinkle Academy’s willingness to tolerate dangerous brilliance—under rules

It is an ideal site for:

*   Magical examinations gone awry
*   Political tension between professors
*   Student mishaps involving summoned creatures
*   Quiet clues about deeper planar disturbances tied to the wider campaign

* * *
