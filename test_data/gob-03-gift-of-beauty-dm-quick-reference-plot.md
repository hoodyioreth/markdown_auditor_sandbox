### Key NPCs Quick Reference — Every Faction at a Glance

**This section lists every NPC who has a stake in the Gift of Beauty, grouped by faction.** Each entry gives the NPC's role, their position on what should happen to the Gift, and the one thing the DM needs to remember about them during play. Use this as a table-side reference when the party interacts with any Gift-related NPC.

#### Earth Temple — Outpost Command (Sepulchre-Priory)

*   [Tharvok Stonehand](tharvok-stonehand-person.md) — War-Priest, Garrison Warden. **Position:** Destroy it ceremonially — "Stone outlasts beauty." Tharvok sees the Gift as a trophy that invites exactly the kind of attention the outpost cannot afford. He wants it ground to dust in a ritual offering to the Elder Elemental God. He guards it only because [Romag](romag-person.md) has not yet issued final orders
*   [Maris Blackshale](maris-blackshale-person.md) — Raid Commander. **Position:** Ship to [Nulb](nulb-settlement.md) as tribute to Romag. Maris stole the Gift. He wants credit. Sending it to the Greater Temple means Romag sees Maris as the man who delivered. Maris does not care about the Gift's symbolism — he cares about advancement
*   [Varnor Earthcaller](varnor-earthcaller-person.md) — High Priest, Geomancer. **Position:** Indifferent — resolve it fast, refocus on the excavation. Varnor's obsession is the geomantic node beneath the priory. The Gift is a distraction that diverts garrison resources from his real work. He will support whichever resolution ends the argument fastest
*   [Caidax Nightshade](caidax-nightshade-person.md) — Arcane Advisor. **Position:** Hold as a political bargaining chip. Caidax is the only outpost leader thinking strategically about the Gift's diplomatic value. She argues for keeping it as leverage — against Celene, against the Viscounty, against rival Temple factions. She is right, and she is outnumbered

#### Temple of Elemental Evil — Greater Hierarchy

*   [Romag](romag-person.md) — Earth Temple Prophet. **Position:** Exploit the Gift for prestige among the four cults. Romag wants the Gift delivered to him at the Greater Temple so he can present it as proof the Earth Temple is the most effective cult. His ambition is to use the theft to consolidate power within the TOEE
*   **Lucius Graeme** — Spy Coordinator (TOEE / Iuz). **Position:** Prolong the argument. Lucius serves two masters — the TOEE and Iuz. Internal Temple dysfunction serves Iuz's interests. As long as the four cults argue about the Gift, they are not cooperating. Lucius will subtly sabotage any resolution
*   [Lakash Quallad](lakash-quallad-person.md) — TOEE Agent in Verbobonc. **Position:** Keep it missing. Every day the Gift stays lost, the Viscount looks weaker. Lakash does not want the Gift recovered by anyone — he wants the diplomatic failure to fester
*   **[Hedrack](hedrack-person.md)** — Supreme Leader, TOEE. **Position:** Control the situation. Hedrack does not care about the Gift itself. He cares about the four cults staying in line. If the Gift becomes a source of faction infighting that threatens Temple unity, he will order it destroyed, moved, or surrendered — whatever ends the problem
*   [Lareth the Beautiful](lareth-person.md) — Rival Operative (Agent of Lolth). **Position:** Seize it to humiliate the Earth Temple. Lareth wants the Gift for leverage and prestige — proving he can take what the Earth Temple cannot hold. He is running multiple parallel schemes through the [Sepulchre-Priory](the-sepulchre-priory-of-the-azure-oath-settlement.md) and the [Moathouse](the-ruins-of-the-moathouse-settlement.md). See GOB Article 2 for his active theft schemes

#### Fire Temple

*   [Alrrem](alrrem-person.md) — High Priest, Fire Temple. **Position:** Humiliate the Earth Temple; seize the Gift if opportunity arises. Alrrem has no direct plan for the Gift, but his intelligence network (through **Cookie**) means he knows more about its status than most Temple leaders. If the Gift is loose — stolen from the priory — the Fire Temple will attempt to intercept it
*   **Cookie** — Fire Cult Priest (undercover as cook at [The Laughing Dog Inn](the-laughing-dog-inn-landmark.md)). **Position:** Intelligence asset — does not have a position on the Gift, only a mission to report movements. Cookie uses carrier pigeons to relay Long Road traffic to Alrrem. He does not know Lareth's identity. He does not know he is compromising a fellow Temple operative's intelligence network
*   **Varek the Smiler (Smiley)** — Innkeeper, [The Laughing Dog Inn](the-laughing-dog-inn-landmark.md). **Position:** Works for [Lareth the Beautiful](lareth-person.md). Bungled Lareth's first recovery attempt. His crew was too slow to intercept the Gift before [Maris Blackshale](maris-blackshale-person.md)'s raiders. Varek still reports to Lareth and the inn remains a live intelligence node

#### Viscounty of Verbobonc

*   **Viscount [Wilfrick Revepaix](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md)** — Ruler of Verbobonc. **Position:** Recover the Gift quietly using deniable assets. The Viscount cannot publicly admit the Gift was stolen — it would expose the Viscounty's failure to protect a diplomatic mission. He needs adventurers who can succeed without drawing political attention
*   [Patriarch Martiolus Legeum](martiolus-legeum-person.md) — High Priest of Rao, Verbobonc. **Position:** Recover the Gift and restore peace. Martiolus has a personal connection through the NSQ-05 quest (the Rao delegation that was attacked alongside the Gift convoy). Recovering the Gift AND restoring [Artus Kellan](artus-kellan-person-1.md) would be a double victory for the Church of Rao
*   **[Godeleva Langmuir](godeleva-langmuir-person.md)** — Iron Lady, House Langmuir. **Position:** Political leverage. Godeleva sees the Gift's loss as ammunition against the Viscount's diplomatic competence. She does not want the Gift destroyed, but she is in no hurry to see it recovered either — not until she has extracted maximum political advantage from the failure
*   **Lord [Simon Milinous](simon-milinous-person.md)** — Southern Lord. **Position:** Adds political complexity. His concerns about southern border security intersect with the Gift's transport route along the Long Road

#### Church of Rao / Veluna

*   [Artus Kellan](artus-kellan-person-1.md) — Priest of Rao (feebleminded, held at Asbury Manor). **Position:** Living witness to the ambush. Artus cannot speak — he was struck by a _feeblemind_ spell during the attack on the Rao delegation. A _greater restoration_ spell would restore him, and his testimony would confirm the Earth Temple's involvement. He is the most important witness who cannot currently testify
*   **Seranna Valeth** — Junior Priestess of Rao (Veluna). **Position:** Divinely tasked to find Artus and the Chest (NSQ-05). Seranna is an active agent in the field — she will be searching for Artus Kellan independently. If the party encounters her, she becomes either an ally or a complication depending on whether their goals align
*   **Canon [Hazen](hazen-person.md)** — Velunan Church Hierarchy. **Position:** The Gift was meant to be delivered as a multi-nation alliance symbol. Its non-arrival has been noted in Veluna. If it is recovered and delivered, the diplomatic benefit is enormous

#### Mounted Borderers and Rangers

*   [Corbin Deleveu](corbin-deleveu-person.md) — Waldgraf, Knight Captain. **Position:** Recover the Gift — his honor depends on it. Deleveu was responsible for the Gift's safe transport. The ambush happened in his jurisdiction. His reputation, his rank, and his standing with the Viscount all hinge on recovery. He is the party's most likely patron for a direct recovery mission
*   [Captain Marcones Carrio](marcones-carrio-person.md) — [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md) Commander. **Position:** Provides military context and patrol support. Carrio can give the party information about road conditions, bandit activity, and the Long Road corridor between [Sheernobb](sheernobb-settlement.md) and the [Sepulchre-Priory](the-sepulchre-priory-of-the-azure-oath-settlement.md)
*   **Corporal Janic** — Surviving Borderer (at Deleveu Manor). **Position:** Witness. Janic was in the escort when the ambush struck. He saw the attackers, can identify Earth Temple insignia, and can describe the scale of the raid force. His testimony is partial — he was knocked unconscious during the fight — but his account gives the party their first concrete intelligence
*   [Wyn](wyn-person.md) — Senior Ranger Knight. **Position:** Quest hook. Wyn is the original adventure hook for the Gift of Beauty quest — she recruits the party in Verbobonc on behalf of Deleveu. If the party enters the Gift quest through Wyn's approach, she provides the initial briefing and can answer questions about the ambush, the transport route, and the suspected cult involvement

#### Druids and Forest Factions

*   [Hrudek Oakheart](hrudek-oakheart-person.md) — Archdruid, Western Gnarley ([Druids of the Gnarley Forest](druids-of-the-gnarley-forest-organization.md)). **Position:** Blessed the Gift — spiritual obligation to see it recovered. Hrudek performed the Obad-Hai blessing on the Gift's Ipp Wood box. That blessing created a sacred bond. Recovery of the Gift restores the sanctity of the blessing. If the party brings the Gift to Hrudek, he will safeguard it and arrange delivery through druidic channels — bypassing Verbobonc's political establishment entirely
*   **Great Druid [Naimur](naimur-person.md)** — Great Druid of the Gnarley (gnome). **Position:** Spiritual authority. Naimur's involvement is indirect but significant — his authority over the Gnarley druids means any druidic involvement in the Gift's recovery would carry his implicit approval
*   **Archdruid Hildefer Paravis** — Eastern Region. **Position:** Shared concern about cult incursion. Hildefer's druids have observed [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md) activity spreading into the eastern reaches. The Gift's theft is part of a larger pattern of cult aggression that concerns the entire druidic hierarchy

#### Other Key NPCs

*   [Halven Sere](halven-sere-person.md) — Captured Custodian (held in AO-20, Sepulchre-Priory). **Position:** Knows the Gift's exact location within the priory. Halven was the Azure Oath's custodian before the Earth Temple took the site. He witnessed the Gift's arrival and placement in the reliquary. He will not speak to his captors — but he will talk to rescuers. Freeing Halven gives the party a detailed map of the priory and the Gift's exact location
*   [Gimble Jillink](gimble-jillink-person.md) — Gnome Operative (Earth Temple / Jillink clan). **Position:** Shrank the Gift for transport using gnome magic. Gimble is operating between [Sheernobb](sheernobb-settlement.md) and [Nulb](nulb-settlement.md). His involvement connects the Gift to the gnome political thread and the false-flag destabilization operation
*   **[Slikkina Jillink](slikkina-jillink-person.md)** — Clan Leader, Tulvar. **Position:** Political entanglement. Slikkina's clan connections create complications if the party investigates the gnome angle of the Gift's theft
*   **Queen [Yolande, Her Fey Majesty](yolande2C-her-fey-majesty-person.md)** — Ruler of [Celene](celene-organization.md). **Position:** Intended recipient. The Gift was meant for her. Its non-arrival has been noted by the Celene court. Yolande's isolationist faction uses the failure as evidence that dealing with humans is unreliable

 

### Table-Tested Scenarios — How the Gift Plays at Real Tables

**The following scenarios have been tested at actual tables running this campaign.** They demonstrate the sandbox's flexibility and should be considered canon-viable approaches. Each scenario is a proven path through the Gift of Beauty quest — use them as templates or inspiration for your own table.

#### Tested Scenario 1: Lareth the False Viscounty Agent

**Setup:** The party is in Hommlet. They have never seen [Lareth the Beautiful](lareth-person.md)'s face or heard his voice. Lareth approaches them posing as a representative of the Viscounty, claiming he has been authorized to recruit adventurers to recover the Gift. He provides accurate intelligence — points them toward the [Sepulchre-Priory](the-sepulchre-priory-of-the-azure-oath-settlement.md) (which he describes as a "Heironeous stronghold occupied by cultists").

 

**Why it works:** The party has no reason to doubt him. The mission sounds legitimate. Lareth's information is _true_ — the priory IS occupied by [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md) cultists, the Gift IS there. The lie is only about who Lareth is and why he wants the Gift recovered.

 

**Outcome branches:**

*   Earth Temple kills the party → Lareth loses nothing
*   Party recovers the Gift and returns it to "the Viscounty agent" → Lareth gets what he wants
*   Party succeeds AND gets suspicious AND refuses to hand over → the only path where Lareth loses

#### Tested Scenario 2: Playing Lareth's Game from the Inside

**Setup:** A party deliberately befriends [Lareth the Beautiful](lareth-person.md) and agrees to work for him — not because they trust him, but because they want to learn the [Cult of Elder Elemental God](cult-of-the-elder-elemental-god-organization.md)'s plans and leverage the factions against each other.

 

**How it plays:** Lareth tells the party about [Romag](romag-person.md)'s Reavers, the raid on the Rao Delegation, the Gift's location at the priory, and the [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md)'s garrison. He tasks them to raid the outpost and bring the Gift to him. He offers bandit support under [Gattas Void](gattas-void-person.md).

 

**The party's real play:** They are gathering intelligence — names, locations, faction rivalries, command structures. Every conversation with Lareth is an intelligence briefing he does not realize he is giving. The party plans to recover the Gift but deliver it to the real authorities — or use it as leverage themselves.

 

**DM guidance:** This is a high-wire act. The party must manage Lareth's trust (Insight vs. Deception, mutual), [Gattas Void](gattas-void-person.md)'s loyalty (do his bandits report back to Lareth during the raid?), and the risk of being in too deep if Lareth catches on. Reward player creativity. The TOEE's faction war is designed to be exploited.

#### Tested Scenario 3: The Laughing Dog Complication

**Setup:** [Lareth the Beautiful](lareth-person.md)'s original plan was to have **Varek the Smiler** and his crew at [The Laughing Dog Inn](the-laughing-dog-inn-landmark.md) intercept the Gift during transport. They bungled it — the [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md)'s professional raiders under [Maris Blackshale](maris-blackshale-person.md) were faster and more competent. But the Laughing Dog is still a live node in the intelligence web.

 

**The Cookie problem:** **Cookie**, the inn's cook, is a Fire Cult priest — an agent of [Alrrem](alrrem-person.md), High Priest of the Fire Temple. He uses carrier pigeons to report movements to his contacts. This means: (a) the Fire Temple knew about the Gift's transport, (b) the Fire Temple knows about the Earth Temple's successful raid, and (c) the Fire Temple will know about any recovery attempt that passes through Laughing Dog territory.

 

**DM guidance:** Cookie is a slow-burn revelation. The party may stay at the Laughing Dog on their way south. Cookie seems harmless — a cook. But if the party recovers the Gift and passes through again on the way back, Cookie has already sent word. A Fire Temple ambush on the return journey — when the party thinks the quest is over — is devastating and narratively powerful. The danger is not just getting the Gift out of the priory. It is getting it home.

 

### The Gift and the Broader Campaign — Why This Is the Linchpin

#### Why the Gift of Beauty Is the Campaign's Linchpin

The Gift of Beauty is not a side quest. It is the thread that connects every major faction in the campaign:

 

**Earth Temple:** The theft demonstrates their operational reach. The Gift's presence at the priory proves the [Earth Temple](specialty-cleric3A-earth-temple-cultist-of-the-black-earth-organization.md) can strike at the highest levels of Verbobonc's diplomatic apparatus.

 

**Temple of Elemental Evil:** The inter-faction rivalry over the Gift (Earth vs. Lareth/Lolth) mirrors the greater temple's internal instability. The Gift is a microcosm of the [Cult of Elder Elemental God](cult-of-the-elder-elemental-god-organization.md)'s dysfunction — four factions that should be cooperating, instead competing over a single prize.

 

**Verbobonc politics:** The Gift's loss exposes the Viscounty's vulnerability. It gives [Lakash Quallad](lakash-quallad-person.md) ammunition. It strains the Viscount's relationship with [Celene](celene-organization.md), the gnomes, and his own lords.

 

**The gnome thread:** The false-flag operation used the Gift ambush as cover for broader destabilization. Recovering the Gift AND exposing the false-flag evidence is the one-two punch that could save the human-gnome alliance.

 

**The priory itself:** The [Sepulchre-Priory](the-sepulchre-priory-of-the-azure-oath-settlement.md) exists as a dungeon _because_ of the Gift. The Earth Temple occupied the Azure Oath priory to stage Long Road raids — raids that culminated in the Gift's theft. The geomantic node, the captured custodian, the desecrated chapel — all of it exists in the shadow of the Gift's strategic significance.

#### The Sandbox Promise

The party can encounter the Gift of Beauty quest thread from _any direction_:

*   Through [Wyn](wyn-person.md)'s approach in Verbobonc (the original adventure hook)
*   Through the priory itself (discovering the Gift while clearing the dungeon)
*   Through the gnome political thread (investigating the false-flag operation leads to the ambush evidence)
*   Through [Nulb](nulb-settlement.md) (intelligence gathered in the river town points to the priory)
*   Through Veluna/Rao channels (clerical contacts mention the missing delegation gift)
*   Through Lord [Corbin Deleveu](corbin-deleveu-person.md) directly (if the party has connections to the southern nobility)

No matter which door the party enters through, the Gift connects to everything else. That is what makes it the campaign's linchpin.
