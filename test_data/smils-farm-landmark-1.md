Smils Farm
==========

> In the distance, you see the telltale column of smoke rise from a barn of a small farm.

   

> Two low buildings, built from stacked stone and roofed in thatch, and waist-high stone walls form a central courtyard. The fences separating the pens of the ranch’s livestock from the idyllic pastureland beyond are made simply but sturdily: thick planks of dried and treated wood hammered into position along stout posts. Pigs squeal and grunt in a muddy enclosure along one wall. In the open-sided barn opposite them is the hacked remains of horses. The house stands across the courtyard, through the wooden gate. It looks homely and humble with someone having taken the time to paint flowers on its shutters.   Smoking charred remnants of the fire mar the side of the barn. Thick smoke rises through the roof. Terrified sheep and goats bleat from their pen near the barn. A woman in a commoners dress knee's at a farmers corpse, her face in her hands weeping. Two young men, smudged in soot and smoke with red haggered eyes come to confront your party as you draw close. One of them is limping and nursing his arm. The other is clutching a crude broken small spear.

Questions on the goblin raid
----------------------------

#### What happened?

> "I am his wife, Hessa. My poor Smil. They murdered my husband and took my son Elthard! You must go after them! Please, I beg you. I will give you all we have. Just go find my poor son."   We thought the horses were restless or maybe some drunk had staggered here from the Anshan. My husband went out to see what was going on.   "They killed the horses...why did they kill them?! We...we heard the animals, their shrieks.   Then Smil, he shouted. Then they murdered him! I'm certain, I never heard a sound like that before, and I will never forget it. We ran outside, they were waiting for us!   They must have set the blaze when they arrived.   Not demons, Just vermin, worthless goblinoid vermin.   I saw them run towards the woods. to the North. You could see if there is a fence knocked down...or a gate."

 

#### What voices did you hear?

> Nothing human, the creatures cursing like demons. Even at each other. Sounds like gobby, gobby, gobby.

#### How many?

> Everywhere. it was chaos. I dont even know how many of them there were.   The bastards attacked at night a little before dawn.   Another useless soldier coming here to ask questions. You should have FUCKING been here last night!

#### How did you get wounded

> I heard someone talking so I went outside and saw what they did to those horses. I shouted at those bastards - caught one of them by their cloak and started beating him.   Yes, But he cut me good, that fucker! Lucky for me the others started to come out. So the raiders run. This is all that is left is its spear.

#### Loss of horse and farmhand

> They left in a hurry, carried off Elthard and limbs of the poor horses. You could see if there is a fence knocked down...or a gate.

#### Track the goblin war party

> Search the fence along the backside of the barn you find that a section of it has been pulled apart. Muddy tracks of many small booted feet lead into the woods. You follow the tracks of small booted passage of the war party through the pastures and woods behind the farm. The walls of crag slope up steeply as you approach the end of the trail-you have no doubt you can climb them, but you'd need both hands. Clusters of tree's and rock outcroppings screen the edge of a dark cave entrance in the side of a bluff. There is no evidence of what could dwell within the cave besides the fact that this trail of tracks lead directly into the mouth of the cave.
