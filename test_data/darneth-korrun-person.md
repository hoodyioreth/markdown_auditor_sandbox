Darneth Korrun
==============

### Gnarley Ranger

Darneth, is a resolute and rugged ranger hailing from the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md). At 30 years old, Darneth's recent life has been marred by tragedy and a thirst for vengeance against those who wronged him. His experiences have honed his skills and hardened his resolve, making him a formidable guardian of the forest and its outskirts.

#### Physical Description

Darneth stands tall and robust, with an appearance that speaks to months of solitary life in the wilderness. His unkempt beard and intensely focused eyes reflect a man shaped by the harsh realities of survival and personal loss. His attire, though worn, is functional, equipped for the demands of life in the wild.

### Rangers Background

Once a contented family man and proficient trapper, Darneth's life took a dark turn when bandits, led by the notorious Commander [Feldrin](feldrin-person.md), brutally attacked his family. The loss drove Darneth into the depths of the Gnarley Forest, where he vowed to fight against the corruption and lawlessness that plagues his home.

#### Personality

Darneth is defined by a quiet intensity and a relentless drive for justice. While he harbors a deep-seated anger towards those who destroyed his life, he remains guided by a moral compass that aligns with the principles of [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md). His solitude has not dampened his willingness to aid those in need, especially against common foes.

*   **Resolute and Skilled:** Known for his expertise in trapping and survival skills.
*   **Burdensome Past:** Carries the weight of personal tragedies that have hardened him.
*   **Protector:** Despite challenges, remains committed to safeguarding his family and locals.

#### Motivation

His primary motivation is to exact vengeance on Feldrin and his cohorts while disrupting the operations of bandit gangs linked to the Temple of Elemental Evil. Darneth is determined to protect the innocents of Nulb and the surrounding areas from the fate his family suffered.

*   **Survival:** Driven by the need to protect and provide for his family amidst Nulb’s harsh realities.
*   **Revenge:** Seeks justice for the brutal atrocities committed against his family.
*   **Restoration:** Desires to restore his home and reputation within the community.

### Relationships

Darneth's interactions within Nulb are complex and heavily influenced by his past and current circumstances. Known for his protective nature, he has a contentious relationship with Lady Nysera's Men due to past altercations and a neutral but cautious rapport with patrons of the Waterside Tavern, where he sometimes acts as an unsolicited guardian.
