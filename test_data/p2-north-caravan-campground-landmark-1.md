P2. North Caravan Campground
============================

The North Caravan Campground is a well-maintained, fenced-in field in Penwick, providing a safe and convenient spot for caravans to park their wagons and contain their oxen. This campground, equipped with a fresh water well and ample space for tents and campfires, is overseen by Darz Helgar, an elderly caretaker and a priest of [Fharlanghn](fharlanghn-person.md), the god of Horizons, Distance, Travel, and Roads.

### Detailed Description

_Location and Layout  
_The North Caravan Campground is located on the northern outskirts of [Penwick](penwick-settlement.md), easily accessible for caravans passing through the town. The field is surrounded by a sturdy wooden fence, ensuring the safety and security of the parked wagons and contained oxen. The spacious lot provides plenty of room for caravans to pitch tents and build campfires, creating a comfortable and communal atmosphere for travelers.

*   **Location:** Northern outskirts of [Penwick](penwick-settlement.md)

##### Layout

*   Fenced-in field for wagons and oxen
*   Ample space for tents and campfires
*   Fresh water well in the southeast corner

##### Key Features

_Fresh Water Well  
_A 30-foot-deep stone well located in the southeast corner of the campground provides fresh water for the caravans. This well is a vital resource, ensuring that both travelers and their animals have access to clean drinking water.

*   **Location:** Southeast corner of the campground
*   **Feature:** 30-foot-deep stone well

### Caretaker’s Cottage

A small cottage stands in the east corner of the lot, home to Darz Helgar, the campground's caretaker. Darz is responsible for keeping the campgrounds clean by removing garbage and burying excrement. Despite his humble duties, Darz is a vital part of the campground's operation, ensuring a clean and welcoming environment for all visitors.

*   **Location:** East corner of the lot
*   **Feature:** Caretaker's cottage

#### Community Role and Importance

_Safe Haven for Travelers  
_The North Caravan Campground plays a crucial role in the community of Penwick by providing a safe and welcoming haven for caravans passing through the town. It fosters a sense of security and community among travelers, who often share stories and resources around campfires.

*   **Role:** Safe haven for travelers
*   **Importance:** Fosters security and community

#### Contribution to Local Economy

The campground also contributes to the local economy by attracting caravans that trade goods and services with the townsfolk. This exchange supports the local market and strengthens Penwick’s connections with the broader region.

*   **Economic Contribution:** Attracts caravans that trade with townsfolk

#### Conclusion

The North Caravan Campground is an essential part of Penwick, providing a secure and convenient resting place for caravans. Overseen by Darz Helgar, a dedicated caretaker and priest of Fharlanghn, this well-maintained field offers ample space for parking, fresh water, and clean facilities, making it a valued resource for travelers and the local community alike. Despite his past, Darz has found a new purpose in maintaining the campground, contributing to the safety and well-being of Penwick’s visitors.

##### Personality

Darz Helgar is an elderly man with a quiet, contemplative demeanor. His experience and wisdom are reflected in his calm and methodical approach to his duties. Despite his past, Darz has embraced his new life in Penwick with humility and dedication.

*   **Role:** Caretaker of the North Caravan Campground

##### Personality Traits

*   Quiet and contemplative
*   Methodical and dedicated

##### Physical Description

Darz is an elderly man with a weathered face, bearing the marks of a hard life. His grey hair and beard are neatly trimmed, and he dresses in simple, practical clothing suitable for his work. Despite his age, Darz remains physically active and capable.

*   **Appearance:** Weathered face, grey hair and beard, practical clothing

##### Background and History

Darz Helgar has a colorful past. He was once a member of a thieves’ guild in Verbobonc and was imprisoned for his many crimes. After serving his sentence, Darz sought to start a new life in Penwick, far from the shadows of his past. Mayor Connor Grantham gave him a second chance by appointing him as the caretaker of the North Caravan Campground.

*   **Past:** Former member of a thieves’ guild in Verbobonc
*   **Imprisonment:** Served time for his crimes
*   **New Life:** Moved to Penwick to start anew
