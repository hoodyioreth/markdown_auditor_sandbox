Druids of the Gnarley Forest
============================

The Druids of the Gnarley Forest, guardians of one of the most revered natural sanctuaries in the Flanaess, within the expansive Gnarley Forest, the Protectors of the Gnarley are a dedicated group of druids who fervently uphold the principles of [The Old Faith](the-old-faith-organization.md). These stewards of nature are committed to preserving the ecological balance and sanctity of their verdant home, engaging in both alliances and confrontations to ensure the forest’s well-being.

### Composition and Allies

*   **Devoted Guardians**: The druids, although few in number, are intensely committed to their roles as protectors of the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md). Their dedication extends beyond the physical realm to embrace the philosophical tenets of the Old Faith, with a particular devotion to [Ehlonna](ehlonna-person.md), the goddess of the woodlands.
*   **Elven Collaboration:** Strong bonds exist between the Protectors and the Sylvan Elves residing within the forest. This relationship is built on a mutual commitment to preserving the natural environment, which both groups hold sacred.
*   **Partnership with the Gnarley Rangers:** The druids collaborate closely with [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md), forming a critical front line in defense of the forest against external threats. This partnership ensures that the forest remains a bastion of natural beauty and balance.

##### Druidic Unity and Partnership

*   Inclusive membership comprising elves, humans, and [Gnomes](gnomes-species-1.md), each contributing unique skills to forest stewardship.
*   Regular coordinated actions with the Sylvan Elves and Gnarley Rangers help maintain the forest’s integrity and biodiversity.

### Threats and Challenges

*   **The Black Thorn Orc Tribe:** Emerging from their stronghold, the Black Thorn Orc Tribe poses a grave threat to the peace and stability of the forest and its neighboring regions. Their raids disrupt the lives of local communities, affecting agricultural and trade activities significantly.
*   **Expansion Threats:** Human encroachment from nearby settlements threatens to disrupt the ecological balance of the forest. These expansions lead to habitat destruction and resource depletion, prompting the druids to act to mitigate these impacts.

##### Activities and Duties of the Druids of the Gnarley Forest

The Druids of the Gnarley Forest are deeply involved in maintaining the ecological and spiritual health of their woodland realm. Their activities are varied, ranging from conservation efforts to diplomatic engagements with the various inhabitants and factions within and around the forest.

##### Collaborative Conservation

*   **Forest Management:** The Druids work closely with the Human [Woodsmen of the Gnarley Forest](woodsmen-of-the-gnarley-forest-organization.md), teaching sustainable logging practices that ensure the health of the forest is maintained while allowing for human use. They help manage the growth of the forest, ensuring that old growth areas are protected and that new growth is managed sustainably.
*   **Habitat Restoration:** Working with local communities and [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md), the Druids lead efforts in restoring damaged parts of the forest. This includes replanting trees, rehabilitating streams and rivers, and reintroducing native flora and fauna that may have been displaced by human activities.

##### Guardianship and Environmental Stewardship

*   Regular patrols with [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md) to monitor the health of the forest and prevent illegal logging or poaching.
*   Organizing seasonal festivals with the Human Woodsmen to celebrate and educate on the importance of the forest ecosystem.

### Gnarley Forest Relations The Old Faith

The Gnarley Forest is a vibrant ecosystem, not only in terms of its flora and fauna but also in the diversity of its inhabitants. This ancient woodland is home to races and cultures that have, over the centuries, developed deep connections with the druids of the Old Faith.

##### Elves and the Old Faith

[Elves of the Gnarley Forest](elves-of-the-gnarley-forest-organization.md) have long maintained cordial relations with the Old Faith, dating back to when the first human druids encountered elvenkind.

*   **Historical Bonds:** The interaction began with the Flan tribes, who were early followers of the Old Faith, impressing the elves with their deep respect for nature.
*   **Diverse Attitudes:** Wood and snow elves show the warmest reception towards druids, appreciating their dedication to natural harmony, while grey elves remain cautious and distant.

##### Fey Relations

The fey creatures of the Gnarley Forest interact with the Old Faith druids based on a mutual understanding of nature’s intricacies.

*   **Fey Respect:** Druids’ intrinsic connection to the land makes them respected counterparts in the eyes of the fey, leading to interactions that are generally peaceful but can be unpredictably perilous by human standards.
*   **Sacred Sites:** Many druidic sacred groves include dryads' trees or are home to a nymph's pool, signifying deep spiritual connections.
*   **Transient Allies:** Satyrs and sprites, though nomadic, frequently engage with druids for counsel and companionship.

##### Old Lore Bards

This elite group of bards holds a special place within the Old Faith, serving as the druids' voice and historians.

*   **Noble Lineage:** Members are traditionally chosen from the Flan nobility, with a requirement now extended to those of human or half-elven noble descent.
*   **Academic Institutions:** The bards are organized into seven colleges across the Flanaess, focusing on preserving and disseminating the philosophical teachings of the Old Faith.
*   **Diplomatic Roles:** While druids often shun social interactions, Old Lore bards act as their envoys, ensuring their messages reach far and wide.

##### Rangers of the Gnarley

This group of [The Gnarley Rangers](the-gnarley-rangers-organization.md) works to protect the forest and maintain its autonomy against external political forces.

*   **Independent Guardians:** Approximately 200 strong, these rangers are dedicated to preserving the independence of the Gnarley Forest from cities like Greyhawk and Dyvers.
*   **Complicated Alliances:** Though their good alignment often aligns with the goals of the Old Faith, their proactive stance sometimes clashes with the druids' strict neutrality.
*   **Mutual Support:** Rangers often turn to druids for guidance, and druids rely on them to address threats that could disrupt the natural balance.

### Foes of the Old Faith

The Druids of the Old Faith, while deeply respected for their connection to the natural world, have historically faced significant opposition from various factions and ideologies. These conflicts range from doctrinal disagreements to existential threats against the natural order they strive to protect.

##### Clerical Rivalries

The Old Faith often finds itself at odds with more structured religions, particularly those with an aggressive approach to conversion or territorial control.

*   **St. Cuthbert Clerics:** There is a friendly rivalry with the followers of [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md), primarily over the devotion of the common folk. This competition, while robust, remains mostly peaceful and does not escalate into open conflict.
*   **Pholtus' Followers:** The relationship with the followers of [Church of Pholtus the One True Way](church-of-pholtus-the-one-true-way-organization.md) is strained but not openly hostile. The two faiths disagree fundamentally on several doctrinal issues, leading to a chilly but manageable coexistence.Cosmic and Supernatural Threats. The Old Faith also contends with foes that are not of this world, posing existential threats to the natural order.
*   **Elder Elementalism and Tharizdun:** Initially underestimated, the cults of [Elder Elemental God](elder-elemental-god-person.md) were later revealed to be connected to Tharizdun, the Unmaker. This discovery has led the Old Faith to actively oppose these cults, recognizing them as severe threats to the Eternal Circle.
*   **The Far Realms:** Encounters with entities from the Far Realms represent a direct affront to nature's laws, as these beings distort reality itself. The druids are committed to repelling these invasions and sealing any planar breaches to maintain the natural order.

### Druids of the Gnarley Forest

#### The Great Druid Naimur

As Great Druid, @naimuis the spiritual fulcrum of the Gnarley Forest. He holds sway over the druids of the Old Faith, maintains deep ties with the Gnarley Rangers, and is considered the unspoken voice of the forest in the great matters of balance. He served briefly and begrudgingly as Grand Druid of the Flanaess, but happily relinquished the post to return to his home soil.

#### The ArchDruid Hildefer

@hildefer the ArchDruid overseeing the eastern region of the Gnarley Forest and encompassing the Dominion of Greyhawk, epitomizes the dedication and influence wielded by the highest echelons of the Old Faith's hierarchy. In 594 cy, her ascension from Archdruid to Great Druid at the summer solstice, marked by an impressive feat involving fireflies, underscores her deep connection to nature and her innovative spirit.

*   **Residence:** Making her home within the verdant expanse of the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md), Hildefer maintains strong alliances with the rangers, the Swanmay of the Gnarley Forest, and the fey communities.

##### Observation and Diplomacy

While the Greyhawk militia remains wary of Hildefer's intentions, her current focus on rejuvenation rather than confrontation speaks to a strategy of careful observation and potential diplomacy.

*   **Cultivating Alliances:** Her relationships with the rangers, fey, and other forest denizens will be crucial in maintaining the balance and health of the Gnarley Forest, leveraging these alliances to foster a unified front in defense of the natural world.

#### Archdruid Hrudek – Guardian of the Western Gnarley

Among the esteemed High Druids of the Gnarley Forest, Archdruid Hrudek stands as the vigilant protector of the western reaches, overseeing the sacred balance of wilderness and civilization along the borderlands of the Viscounty of Verbobonc and the Wild Coast.

##### Role and Responsibilities

*   **Guardian of the Western Gnarley:** Hrudek ensures that human expansion, particularly from Verbobonc and Dyvers, does not overreach into the untamed forest.
*   **Druidic Watcher:** He maintains a network of scouts and informants, including woodsmen, Gnarley Rangers, and friendly elves, to monitor humanoid movements, banditry, and unnatural disturbances.
*   **Overseer of Druids:** He organizes rituals and seasonal gatherings to strengthen the bond between the druids, rangers, and fey of the Gnarley.

##### Current Concerns

*   **Missing Reports from Jaroo Ashstaff:** Hrudek has grown concerned about Jaroo Ashstaff, a druid stationed in the village of Hommlet, who has failed to send his expected reports.
*   **Bandit Activity Near Hommlet:** Suspecting that renewed bandit activity around the Moathouse ruins could be disrupting druidic operations, Hrudek seeks volunteers to investigate.
*   **Rising Cult Influence:** Hrudek is particularly wary of the Cult of the Elder Elemental Eye, fearing their corruption may be spreading from the ruined Temple of Elemental Evil into the western forest.

##### Notable Traits

*   **Grizzled but Wise:** An elder druid with a weathered face and piercing green eyes, he commands deep respect among both the Woodsmen and Gnarley Rangers.
*   **Strategic Thinker:** Unlike some of his kin who remain detached mystics, Hrudek is a pragmatic leader, actively coordinating with human and elven allies to counter external threats.
*   **Reluctant to Trust Outsiders:** While willing to recruit adventurers for critical tasks, Hrudek remains suspicious of city-dwellers and sellswords, believing few truly care for the forest’s fate.

### Politics and the Old Faith in Verbobonc

The intertwining of the Old Faith with the political and cultural fabric of [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md)illustrates the enduring influence of ancient traditions in shaping the identity and governance of human settlements. From its earliest days to the current Viscount's reign, the Old Faith has played a pivotal role in the life of the viscounty, weaving a rich tapestry of tradition and belief that continues to guide the people and their leaders.

##### Historical Influence

*   **Druidic Foundations:** The initial settlers of what would become Verbobonc brought with them the tenets and traditions of the Old Faith. Druidic councilors served as advisors to clan chiefs, integrating Druidic customs into the ceremonies and governance of the early communities.
*   **Continuation of Traditions:** Despite changes in political advisement, with the [Bishop Haufren](bishop-haufren-person.md) of the [Church of St Cuthbert](church-of-st-cuthbert-organization.md) now serving the current Viscount, the rural populace of Verbobonc remains deeply rooted in the practices of the Old Faith.

##### Cultural Practices

*   **Harvest and Birth Rituals:** The Old Faith is integral to key aspects of daily life in Verbobonc. Farmers seek the blessings of the Old Ones for a successful harvest, while the presence of a druid is sought for the Ritual of Days during childbirth, highlighting the faith's role in pivotal life events.

##### Political Acumen of the Viscount

*   **Recognition of Influence:** Viscount [Wilfrick Verdanhart](wilfrick-revepaix-person.md), aware of the Old Faith's deep-seated influence within his viscounty, has chosen a path of acknowledgment and cooperation rather than confrontation. This approach has allowed him to maintain peace and harmony within his domain.
*   **Alliance with Nature Folk:** By respecting the Old Faith and its followers, the Viscount has forged powerful yet subtle alliances with the _"nature folk"_ of the region. This relationship benefits both parties, ensuring the continuity of ancient practices while fostering a sense of unity and mutual respect.

### The Protectors of the Gnarley

##### Members of the Old Faith

Within the verdant expanse of the [Gnarley Forest](gnarley-forest-location.md), a dedicated group of druids, known as the Protectors of the Gnarley, uphold the tenets of the Old Faith with fervent devotion. These guardians of the natural world work tirelessly to preserve the sanctity and balance of the Great Forest, forming alliances and confronting threats to ensure its protection.

##### Composition and Allies

*   **Devoted Guardians:** Though their numbers are few, the druids of the Gnarley Forest are deeply committed to their cause. Their devotion is not only to the forest itself but also to the principles of the Old Faith, particularly the veneration of [Ehlonna](ehlonna-person.md), the goddess of woodlands.
*   **Elven Collaboration:** The Protectors maintain a cordial relationship with the elven clans residing within the Gnarley, especially the [Sylvan Elves](sylvan-elves-ethnicity.md), who share a similar reverence for the forest and its preservation.
*   **Partnership with the Gnarley Rangers:** Working alongside the Gnarley Rangers , the druids play a crucial role in safeguarding the forest from external threats, ensuring that it remains a haven of natural beauty and balance.

##### Threats and Challenges

*   **The Black Thorn Orc Tribe:** A significant threat to the peace of the Gnarley Forest and the surrounding regions comes from the Black Thorn Orc tribe. Originating from their lair known as Black Thorn, these orcs launch raids into the [Verbobonc, Viscounty](verbobonc2C-viscounty-organization.md), disrupting farming and trade with their aggressive incursions.

##### The High Druids

*   **Maiya Dewshine:** A [Sylvan Elves](sylvan-elves-ethnicity.md) of profound wisdom and grace, Maiya Dewshine stands as one of the High Druids overseeing the protection of the Gnarley. Her deep connection to the forest and its creatures, along with her knowledge of ancient magics, makes her a formidable guardian.
*   **Laric:** A human druid, Laric brings to the group a fierce determination and a broad understanding of the wider world beyond the forest's borders. His insights and strategic mind are invaluable in the ongoing efforts to protect the Gnarley.

#### Protection and Defense

*   **Emergency Response:** The Druids, in collaboration with the Gnarley Rangers and Fey allies, are the first responders to natural and unnatural threats. This includes everything from forest fires to incursions by hostile forces.
*   **Defensive Strategies:** They strategize and implement defensive measures against threats such as the expansion of human settlements, the activities of bandits from the Wild Coast, and invasive species that could disrupt the forest’s ecological balance.

##### Defense and Crisis Management

*   Training sessions with the Rangers and local militias to prepare for potential threats.
*   Creation of natural barriers and sanctuaries to protect vulnerable areas and species within the forest.

Through these diverse activities and duties, the Druids of the Gnarley Forest not only protect their beloved woodland but also teach and lead by example, showing all who dwell near or in the forest how to live in harmony with nature while respecting the delicate balance that sustains all life within it.
