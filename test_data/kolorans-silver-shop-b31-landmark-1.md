Kolorans Silver Shop B31
========================

Kororan's Silver shop is located directly on Bridgewalk Boulevard along with other shops selling gemstones, jewelry, precious metals. He specialized in very high quality silver ware, cups, plates, utensils, and ornaments. Only using the purest quality silver sourced from the gnomes in Tulvar. He does very well and has a high reputation of the highest quality.  

> You enter into a brightly lit shop of glowing magical order hanging from the wood beamed ceiling. A tiny bell above the door announces your entry. Counters run parallel in the center of the room and around the edges, and in each you see various silverware stored under glass. Several display cases shine throughout the perfumed shop, glinting amid long racks bearing elegant, expensive-looking silver. A middle-aged man sits behind the right-most one, looks up and says, “Greetings, my friends. How many I serve you?” the man says, coming towards you.   Bright baubles and pretty little things crafted in pure silver abound, dangling from hooks, laid across tables, and collected in boxes. Rings, necklaces, hair pins, bracelets, arm bands and more seem to be on offer. Although none of it looks outlandishly expensive, you do spy a few items with gems, settings of precious metal, or both.
