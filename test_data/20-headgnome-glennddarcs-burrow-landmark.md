20\. Headgnome Glennddarc's Burrow
==================================

The Keeleene Headgnome Home, named "Glennddarc's Burrow," is the residence of Glennddarc Keeleene, the long-standing Headgnome of Sheernobb. This burrow is not only the largest home in the village but also a central hub for leadership and community activities. Despite recent bandit activity disrupting trade routes, Glennddarc remains dedicated to protecting and leading his village.

##### Exterior and Ambiance

Glennddarc's Burrow is nestled into a lush hillside, with an entrance adorned with intricately carved wooden doors and a small sign that reads "Glennddarc's Burrow." The surrounding area is vibrant with greenery, and a cobblestone path leads to the entrance.

##### Interior Design

*   **Ceiling Height**: Medium-sized creatures need to bend down to avoid hitting their heads, while larger creatures must wait outside. Small creatures like halflings find everything appropriately sized.
*   **Living Quarters:** Cozy and well-decorated with tapestries and handcrafted furniture. The burrow exudes warmth and comfort, with glowing lanterns providing a soft light.

### Location and Layout

##### Central Hall

*   **Meeting Area:** A spacious room where Glennddarc holds village meetings. Comfortable chairs and sofas sized for gnomes and halflings surround a large central table.
*   **Decor:** Adorned with maps and historical artifacts, showcasing the rich history of Sheernobb.

##### Glennddarc's Office and Library

*   **Cluttered Space:** Overflowing bookcases, piles of books on the floor, and various papers and maps strewn about.
*   **Furniture:** Comfortable chairs and sofas for gnomes and halflings, with a central desk where Glennddarc works.
*   **Atmosphere:** A scholarly environment, reflecting Glennddarc's dedication to knowledge and leadership.

##### Family Quarters

*   **Private Rooms:** Separate burrows within the mound for family members, including sons, daughters, in-laws, nephews, and nieces.
*   **Community Feel:** The extended Gleddddarc family lives here, fostering a strong sense of unity and support.

### History

_Founding and Evolution  
_The Keeleene Headgnome Home has been the center of leadership in Sheernobb for generations. Glennddarc's dedication and wise leadership have been instrumental in the village's prosperity.

##### Significant Events

*   **Community Growth:** The burrow has expanded over the years to accommodate the growing Keeleene family.
*   **Crisis Management:** Glennddarc's leadership has been crucial during times of crisis, such as the recent bandit activity on the Long Road.

### Relationships

_Community Bonds  
_

*   **Paternal Care:** Glennddarc and Eldara view the villagers as their extended family, fostering a strong sense of unity.
*   **Family Unity:** The Keeleene family lives together in the burrow, supporting each other and the community.

##### External Relations

*   **Trade Partners:** Maintains positive relations with neighboring villages and traders.
*   **Political Allies:** Works with regional leaders to address security and economic concerns.

##### Staff Dynamics

*   **Glennddarc:** The experienced and knowledgeable shopkeeper, managing the grocer's operations and engaging with customers.
*   **Eldara:** The supportive and nurturing partner, ensuring the shop runs smoothly and the family stays connected.
*   **Extended Family:** Each member contributes to the family and community, supporting Glennddarc's leadership.

Glennddarc's Burrow, the Keeleene Headgnome Home, stands as a testament to leadership, unity, and community spirit in Sheernobb. With its central role in local politics, trade, and security, it remains a cornerstone of the village's prosperity and resilience.
