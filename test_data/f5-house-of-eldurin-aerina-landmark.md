F5 House of Eldurin Aerina
==========================

_A Minor Noble with Major Ambition_

A newly renovated noble townhouse in the Foreign Quarter, the **House of Eldurin Aerina** has become infamous for its lavish _midnight salons_, theatrical performances, and exclusive guest lists. To the public, it is a beacon of cultured ambition and social ascent. To the watchful and the wise, it is something far more dangerous:  
a **social pressure chamber**, quietly reshaping alliances among Verbobonc’s nobles, merchants, and foreign interests.

Unbeknownst to most, the house is also a **vampiric influence node**, where charm, secrecy, and obsession are weaponized to destabilize rival houses—most notably **House Sarcina**.

Eldurin’s residence sits along a fashionable cobblestone street, its façade newly dressed in pale stone, wrought-iron balconies, and velvet-draped windows. Lanterns burn late into the night, especially on evenings marked by discreet sigils chalked near the servants’ door—the sign of a midnight gathering. Inside, the house is arranged for flow, not comfort: rooms encourage movement, conversation, and separation into ever-shifting social knots.

### Manor Description

The House of Eldurin Aerina is a grand noble residence nestled amidst the cobblestone streets of the Foreign Quarter in Verbobonc. Reflecting Lord Eldurin Aerina's ambition and charm, the house features a richly renovated facade adorned with intricate carvings and elaborate stonework. The elegant courtyard is meticulously landscaped, with statues, flowerbeds, and lush greenery adding to the whimsical and charismatic aura of the estate.

##### Exterior

*   **Facade and Architecture:** The exterior boasts a blend of early 17th-century architectural styles, with richly decorated columns, detailed stonework, and large, inviting windows.
*   **Courtyard and Gardens:** The courtyard features statues and flowerbeds, with lush greenery creating a serene ambiance.

##### Interior

*   **Grand Ballroom:** A spacious ballroom where Eldurin hosts his famous 'midnight' balls, complete with shimmering chandeliers and polished wooden floors.
*   **Drawing Room:** Richly furnished with plush chairs and ornate tables, providing a comfortable space for private meetings and social gatherings.
*   **Library and Study:** Shelves filled with rare books and manuscripts, and a study where Eldurin plans his strategic social moves.
*   **Private Quarters:** Luxurious living quarters for Eldurin and his distinguished guests, offering privacy and comfort.

### Social Events and Atmosphere

*   **Elaborate 'Midnight' Balls:** The house is known for its lavish 'midnight' balls that attract prominent nobles and merchants.
*   **Charming Aura:** The whimsical and charismatic atmosphere reflects Eldurin's personality and ambitions, making the estate a lively hub of Verbobonc society.

Overall, the House of Eldurin Aerina stands as a testament to the young noble’s aspirations and charm, with its richly renovated facade and lively social atmosphere providing a striking presence in Verbobonc's Foreign Quarter.

#### **Public Reputation**

*   Eldurin is widely regarded as a **brilliant social climber**, rising far faster than his lineage should allow.
*   His salons attract **minor nobles, wealthy merchants, guild representatives**, and visiting dignitaries.
*   His fortune is attributed to a “private inheritance,” though no records confirm this.
*   Rumors persist that _those who leave his house often act… differently_ in the weeks that follow.

### **Running the House in Play**

##### **Tone**

Elegant, intimate, and faintly oppressive. Conversations are polite—but measured. Laughter stops just a breath too quickly when certain names are spoken.

##### **What PCs Notice**

*   Guests subtly steered away from one another
*   Servants who appear _too attentive_
*   The Maiden never eats, never drinks, never tires

##### **What PCs Can Learn**

*   Eldurin’s rise coincides with Sarcina’s growing troubles
*   Certain nobles leave gatherings _changed in priorities_
*   Eldurin is deeply, desperately in love—and vulnerable

* * *

#### **Adventure Hooks**

*   PCs are invited to a midnight salon for reasons unclear
*   A noble ally asks the party to “observe” Eldurin’s guests
*   Evidence surfaces that Eldurin is being manipulated
*   Terica mistakes a PC for a past associate—or threat

* * *

##### **If the PCs Intervene**

*   Breaking Eldurin’s enchantment earns a powerful (if shaken) ally
*   Exposing Terica risks open conflict with House Sarcina’s enemies
*   Failure allows Terica’s influence to spread into the High Quarter

* * *

##### **DM Guidance**

Use this house as a **slow-burn social dungeon**. Nothing explodes immediately. Instead, alliances bend, rumors shift, and reputations rot quietly—until the players decide _how much corruption they will tolerate in silk gloves_.

#### Meeting Lord Eldurin Aerina

You are led through the tall doors of the manor into a candle-warmed receiving hall, where velvet drapes hush the outside world and the air smells faintly of spiced wine and old stone. Polished floors gleam beneath your boots, reflecting lamplight like still water. Conversation murmurs from deeper within the house, but here—at the threshold of welcome—all sound seems to pause.  
  
A slender man steps forward from beside a carved sideboard, his movements smooth and practiced, as though he has rehearsed this moment many times. He wears noble attire of fine cut but modest heraldry, and his dark eyes weigh you with open curiosity rather than suspicion. When he smiles, it is warm—almost eager. You have the distinct sense that he has been expecting someone… if not precisely you.

Dialogue (Lord Eldurin Aerina) _“Ah. Yes… you will do nicely.”_

He inclines his head in a courtly bow, one hand resting lightly over his heart. _“Welcome, friends, to my humble house. I am Lord Eldurin Aerina, and I am most pleased that you accepted my invitation—or perhaps found your way here by fate’s gentle nudge.”_

His gaze lingers on each of you in turn, attentive, almost searching. _“Pray, forgive the hour. I find that the night encourages honesty where the day insists upon manners. Come—be at ease. Any who cross my threshold are guests, not strangers.”_

A brief laugh escapes him, soft and sincere. _“Tell me… what brings such capable souls to my door this evening? Curiosity? Coin? Or something far more interesting?”_

##### **Lord Eldurin Aerina**

A young Flan noble of modest blood, Eldurin has reinvented himself as a patron of art, wit, and refined decadence. He is charming, earnest, and utterly convinced of his own destiny.

**Public Face**

*   Gracious host, tireless conversationalist
*   Patron of actors, musicians, and playwrights
*   Earnest admirer of Verbobonc’s great houses

**Private Reality (DM-Only):** Eldurin is **enthralled**, body and will bound to a figure he believes to be his true love.

* * *

##### Terica Fourlock

Presented as a refined lady of Greyhawk’s high court, Terica is in truth a **vampire of calculated patience**, using Eldurin as a social instrument.

**Her Objectives**

*   Undermine **Lady Kathryn Sarcina** socially and politically
*   Build a web of obligations, secrets, and emotional leverage
*   Establish safe movement through the Foreign Quarter and High Quarter

**Methods**

*   Domination and emotional manipulation of Eldurin
*   Strategic appearances at salons to charm targeted nobles
*   Nightly exits in bat or mist form, avoiding suspicion

Eldurin believes he is in love.  
Terica believes he is _useful_.
