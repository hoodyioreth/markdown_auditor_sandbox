11\. Tomlyn Tailor’s shop
=========================

Tomlyn Tailor's Shop, located in the center of Hommlet across the road from the Village Hall, is a quaint establishment run by Tomlyn, a skilled tailor with a unique backstory. Despite facing physical challenges and social prejudices, Tomlyn has made a name for himself with his exceptional tailoring skills and secret prowess with the crossbow.  

### Description

Tomlyn Tailor’s Shop is a small, cozy cottage with a thatched roof, much like other buildings in Hommlet. The shop is strategically positioned across the road from the Village Hall, making it easily accessible for villagers and visitors alike.

##### Interior

Inside, the shop is neatly organized, with bolts of fabric in various colors and textures lining the walls. Shelves display finely made garments, scarves, and handbags. A sturdy wooden table holds sewing tools, needles, threads, and a crossbow discreetly tucked away. The air is filled with the scent of fresh fabric and the soft sound of scissors cutting cloth.

### Lore and History

Tomlyn moved to Hommlet after his first business in Dyvers failed due to debts. Seeking a fresh start, he set up his tailor shop in the village, hoping to rebuild his life. His physical abnormalities make him particularly weak, leading to difficulties and prejudices, but he is determined to succeed.

##### Historical Significance

*   **Move to Hommlet:** Tomlyn relocated to Hommlet to escape his past failures and debts, bringing with him a wealth of tailoring experience.
*   **Secret Training:** To defend himself against bullying and insults, Tomlyn trained to become an expert with the crossbow, a weapon he can use even while seated.

### Motivation and Relationships

_Tomlyn the Tailor_

*   **Motivation:** Tomlyn is driven by his passion for tailoring and his desire to prove himself despite his physical limitations. He aims to build a successful business and earn the respect of the community.
*   **Relationships:** Although he is not well-integrated into the militia due to his size, Tomlyn attends regular services at the Church of Saint Cuthbert.

#### Religion

Tomlyn publicly attends services at the Church of [St. Cuthbert](st-cuthbert-person.md), adhering to its teachings of law and order. However, he secretly worships Kurell, the god of thieves and revenge, seeking solace in the deity's promise of retribution against those who have wronged him.

### Current Events

##### Shop Activities

Tomlyn Tailor’s Shop is bustling with activity as Tomlyn repairs and creates garments for the villagers. His skill with fabric and attention to detail have quickly earned him a reputation for quality and craftsmanship.

##### Secret Challenges

*   **Social Prejudice:** Tomlyn continues to face prejudice and bullying due to his physical appearance, but he uses his skills and determination to rise above these challenges.
*   **Debt Recovery:** Although he left Dyvers to escape debts, Tomlyn remains cautious and works hard to ensure his financial stability.
*   **Secret Worship:** Secretly, he follows Kurell, the god of thieves and revenge, reflecting his inner struggles and desire for justice.
*   **Shop treasure:** He has 5 gp and 15 sp in his coin purse and another 26 gp, 12 sp and 131 cp in locked strongbox under his bed.

 

##### Secret: Helping the Tailor

[Show spoiler](#spoiler-7uYhVV)

Tomlyn the tailor has been secretly trying to join the local militia for months. Despite his diminutive size and physical challenges, he has become an exceptional marksman, known for his skill with the crossbow. Every evening, Tomlyn can be seen practicing his archery in the yard behind his house, visible from many parts of the Inn of the Welcome Wench.   Side quest: [2\. Helping the Tailor - Hommlet](2-helping-the-tailor---hommlet-plot.md)   Tomlyn's dedication to improving his marksmanship has not gone unnoticed by the villagers and patrons of the inn. However, due to his physical limitations, he has not been allowed to join the militia, a source of great frustration for him.

##### Interaction with Players

If players engage Tomlyn in conversation, particularly about his archery practice, he will eventually share his desire to join the militia. He will explain his situation and ask the players if they could put in a good word for him with Elmo's father, Militia Captain Zeb Fletcher.

##### Potential Outcomes

*   **Gaining Favor:** If the players successfully advocate for Tomlyn and get him accepted into the militia, they will receive experience points equivalent to Tomlyn's XP bonus.
*   **Additional Reward:** In gratitude for their assistance, Tomlyn will craft and gift the players finely made outfits of traveler’s clothes a few days after he is accepted into the militia. These outfits are not only practical but also stylish, showcasing Tomlyn's exceptional tailoring skills.

   

#### Conclusion

Tomlyn Tailor’s Shop stands as a testament to resilience and skill in the face of adversity. With its rich history, dedicated proprietor, and commitment to quality, the shop continues to thrive despite societal challenges. As Tomlyn navigates the complexities of village life and personal struggles, his shop remains a pillar of craftsmanship and determination in Hommlet.
