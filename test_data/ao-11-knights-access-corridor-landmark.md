AO-11. Knights Access Corridor
==============================

_(Now the Command Wing of Marin & Caidax)_

* * *

This rear entrance once served as a **private access corridor for the Knights and Paladins of Heironeous** who guarded the Sepulchre-Priory. The doorway allowed the defenders of the monastery to move quickly between the **courtyard, stables, and their quarters** without disturbing the sanctity of the temple halls.

In the days of the Azure Oath, the rooms beyond housed **elite knight guardians sworn to protect the tombs below**. Their chambers were simple but disciplined—stone cells arranged around a narrow corridor that connected directly to the temple complex.

Now this wing has been claimed by **Commander Marin**, Romag’s appointed military overseer of the monastery. From these rooms Marin organizes the raids across the Kron Hills and Long Road. The wizard **Caidax Nightshade** has also claimed one of the chambers, turning it into a small arcane study.

The corridor beyond the door now serves as the **command wing of the monastery garrison**.

##### Entering The Paladin & Knight Quarters

A weathered oak door reinforced with rusting iron bands stands set into the northern wall of the courtyard. The hinges creak softly in the wind.  
  
Beyond the doorway lies a narrow stone corridor, lit by a single smoking lantern hung from an iron hook. The walls are lined with faded murals of armored knights kneeling before a lightning-shaped symbol of Heironeous—though many of the faces have been scratched away or smeared with dark paint.  
  
Bootprints, mud, and the scrape of heavy armor mark the stone floor. Somewhere deeper in the hall, a man's voice mutters sharply—followed by the faint clatter of steel against stone.

### Key Features

##### Reinforced Door

The door is **thick monastery oak**, originally built for defense.

**AC:** 15  
**HP:** 30  
**Strength DC to force:** 16

The door is usually **kept barred from the inside during night hours**.

* * *

#### Defaced Murals

The corridor walls depict the **Knights of the Azure Oath** kneeling before the lightning bolt of **Heironeous**.

Black Earth cultists have partially defaced them.

Signs include:

• scratched faces on the knight carvings  
• the **Black Earth triangular sigil painted in charcoal  
**• gouges from weapon strikes

A character succeeding on:

**DC 12 Religion (Heironeous)  
**recognizes these murals as **portraits of the monastery’s original knight guardians**.

##### **Command Wing Corridor**

*   **Type:** Interior Access Corridor / Command Wing Threshold
*   **Original Use:** Private passage linking courtyard to Paladin & Knight quarters of the Azure Oath
*   **Current Use:** Secure command corridor for Marin and Caidax’s occupied chambers
*   **Connects From:** Courtyard (Location 5) via reinforced north door
*   **Leads To:** Four former knight chambers (now command wing rooms; separate location articles)
*   **Primary Users:** Commander Marin, Caidax Nightshade, raid sergeants, reaver officers
*   **Lighting:** Single smoking lantern (dim light) + occasional candles under doors. **Daylight through narrow monastery windows during daytime**
*   **Defenses:** Door commonly barred at night; corridor treated as “secure interior”
*   **Signs of Occupation:** Muddy bootprints, armor scuffs, wax drips, map scraps, charcoal markings
*   **Desecration Detail:** Defaced murals of kneeling knights; charcoal Black Earth triangles painted over lightning motifs
*   **Stealth Note:** Noise carries—occupants investigate within **1–2 rounds** if alerted
*   **Clue Value:** First “leadership zone” of the monastery; good place for raid plans/orders to be discovered
*   **Escalation:** If fighting breaks out nearby, Marin fortifies and Caidax begins spell preparations by **round 3–4**
