AO-10. Barracks of the Fallen Oath
==================================

_Former Barracks of the Knights of Heironeous — Now the Reaver Den_

This long stone structure sits along the **southwestern wall of the monastery complex**, immediately beside the guard tower. In the days when the Sepulchre-Priory stood vigilant, this hall housed the soldiers and squires who protected the sacred tombs of Heironeous. Rows of disciplined warriors once slept here beneath banners of the Invincible.

Those days are long gone.

The hall has been seized by the **Black Earth cult reavers and raiders** who now use the monastery as a forward raiding base. Mud-caked boots, blood-stained gear, and scavenged weapons now fill the space where once stood polished armor and ordered racks.

A **rear door along the north wall** leads deeper into the monastery complex. This passage once connected the common barracks to the **Paladin and Knight quarters**, but it is now used by the cult’s commanders — **Commander Marin** and the wizard **Caidax Nightshade** — who occupy those chambers as their private command area.

##### Entering the Barracks

You push open the heavy barracks door and the smell of sweat, damp leather, and smoke rolls out to meet you. Inside stretches a long stone hall lined with rough wooden bunks. Armor pieces, battered shields, and black-stained weapons lie scattered across tables and crates. A small cooking fire smolders in an iron basin near the center of the room, and bedrolls litter the floor in disorganized piles.  
  
Along the far wall stands a reinforced wooden door leading deeper into the monastery — its iron latch newly fitted and far better maintained than anything else in the room.  
  
Whatever discipline once filled this hall has been replaced by the brutal disorder of raiders who believe the fortress is theirs.

##### **Structure**

*   Rectangular hall roughly **60 ft long × 25 ft wide**
*   Low vaulted stone ceiling with timber beams
*   Two shuttered arrow windows facing the outer wall
*   One main entrance door opening to the **courtyard side**
*   **North rear door** leading toward the **Knight Quarters / Command area**

#### Barracks Garrison – Black Earth Reavers

The barracks houses the **main garrison force of the Sepulchre-Priory**, composed primarily of **Black Earth Reavers**—raiders recruited from bandit gangs, mercenary companies, and caravan brigands operating throughout the **Kron Hills and Long Road trade routes**.

These warriors serve the **Temple of the Black Earth under Romag**, functioning as raiders, scouts, and inner perimeter defenders of the monastery stronghold.

Unlike the heavily armored **Black Earth Guards**, these soldiers rely on speed, ambush tactics, and familiarity with the rugged terrain of the Kron Hills.

##### Command Structure

The barracks ultimately answers to the **Earth Temple command staff occupying the Knight Wing**.

**Local Command**

• **Sergeant Drogath “Stonejaw” Varn** – Barracks commander (Lvl 4 Fighter)  
• **Maris Blackshale** – Raider Captain and ambush trainer  
• **Commander Marin** – Overall military leader of the monastery garrison  
• **Caidax Nightshade** – Arcane advisor and cult operative

* * *

##### Earth Temple Guards (Not Stationed Here)

The **Black Earth Guards** are **elite temple warriors** and **do not live in the common barracks**.

Instead, they serve as the **personal guards of the Earth Temple priesthood**.

Their quarters are located **near the inner shrine chambers deeper in the monastery complex**.

Typical strength in this monastery:

**3–4 Black Earth Guards**

They only appear when:

• The inner temple is threatened  
• The priesthood calls for defense  
• A ritual or convoy requires protection

##### **Rear Door – Command Passage**

The **north door** leads deeper into the monastery complex.

This passage connects to the chambers now used by:

*   **Commander Marin** – field leader of the raiders
*   **Caidax Nightshade** – cult wizard advisor

Unlike the rest of the barracks, this door shows **recent maintenance and reinforcement**, suggesting it protects something important.

The door is normally:

*   **Locked**
*   Guarded when Marin or Caidax are present

#### **Hidden History**

A careful inspection reveals traces of the hall’s original purpose. Scratched into a stone column is the faint symbol of **Heironeous’ lightning bolt** — partially defaced but not fully destroyed. Under one bunk frame lies a rusted shield bearing the **azure cross of the Azure Oath Knights**. If cleaned and restored, the shield still carries faint holy power.

* * *

#### **DM Hooks**

These clues reinforce the monastery’s new role as a **raiding headquarters for the Black Earth cult**. Possible discoveries in the barracks:

*   A **raiding map** marking merchant roads within a 20-mile radius of the monastery
*   A half-burned **order scroll** referencing shipments being sent toward **Nulb**
*   A frightened captive stable hand hiding beneath a bunk

##### **Barracks of the Fallen Oath**

*   **Type:** Occupied Barracks / Raider Rest Hall
*   **Original Use:** Soldiers’ quarters for the Azure Oath guards of Heironeous
*   **Current Use:** Reaver and raider bunkhouse, staging point, quick-response reinforcement
*   **Primary Occupants:** Black Earth Reavers & Raiders (4–6 present at most times)
*   **Tactical Role:** First interior reinforcements if tower/gate raises alarm
*   **Key Feature:** **North rear door** to the old Paladin & Knight Quarters (now Marin + Caidax command wing)
*   **Condition:** Smoke-stained, muddy, looted, disordered; minimal discipline except around the rear door
*   **Loot/Clues:** Raider map of Long Road targets, partial convoy orders toward Nulb, stolen trade goods scraps
*   **Spiritual Echo:** Faint lightning sigil defaced but not fully destroyed; possible holy relic under bunks
*   **Threat Note:** If combat starts here, reavers attempt to **block the rear door** and **send a runner** to warn Marin/Caidax
*   **Stealth Note:** Loud entry likely heard in courtyard; quiet entry may catch raiders asleep (day)
*   **Escalation:** Alarm in this room triggers **courtyard response in 1–2 rounds**, command wing response in **3–4 rounds**

##### **Tactical Notes**

The narrow interior creates a **tight melee environment** where multiple combatants cannot easily surround defenders. If intruders enter the barracks:

*   Reavers grab weapons immediately
*   Two attempt to **block the rear door**
*   Others try to **alert the courtyard or tower**

#### Barracks Strength

##### Black Earth Reavers (20 total)

**Class:** Fighter / Rogue hybrid raiders  
**Level:** 3  
**Armor:** Studded leather or brigandine  
**Role:** Shock troops and interior defenders

Reavers carry **2d10 gp or minor valuables**

These warriors form the **core fighting force** of the monastery.

**Combat Role**

• Inner courtyard defense  
• Barracks garrison  
• Reinforcements for gatehouse alarm  
• Corridor choke-point defense  
• Caravan raid strike teams

These raiders are **fanatically loyal to the Earth Temple priests**, believing the rise of the Elder Elemental God will reward those strong enough to survive the coming upheaval.

* * *

##### Earth Temple Raiders (8 total)

**Class:** Ranger / Fighter raiders  
**Level:** 3–4  
**Composition:** Humans and Half-Orcs  
**Training:** Ambush specialists of the Long Road

These are the **elite raiding bands** trained under **Maris Blackshale**.

They specialize in:

• Merchant convoy ambush  
• Terrain navigation of the Kron Hills  
• Mounted raids and wagon capture  
• Long-range scouting

Many of the wagons currently stored in the monastery stables were taken by these raiders.
