Frubnip Tillien
===============

Frubnip Tillien stands as a prominent figure within the community of Tulvar, embodying the resilience and tenacity of his people. As the clan elder of House Tillien, Frubnip not only manages the hidden silver and gem mines crucial to his clan's economy but also serves as a guardian against the threats that loom over his family and the surrounding lands.

#### Physical Description

*   **Stature and Build:** Compact yet muscular, Frubnip resembles a typical Hobbit with his robust physique suitable for both mining and combat.
*   **Facial Features:** His auburn hair is kept short, practical for his dual life as a miner and warrior. His face, marked by mild wrinkles, hints at years of smiles and worries, reflecting a life fully lived.
*   **Eyes and Expression:** Glittering, gem-like eyes exhibit a keen sharpness, mirroring the precious stones he mines. His rare, sincere smile is a treasure in itself, radiating warmth when it does appear.

### Background

*   **Origins and Family:** Born into the modest yet respected House Tillien, Frubnip was raised in the mining tunnels and learned the value of hard work early. His lineage, though not wealthy, has long been integral to Tulvar’s mining sector.
*   **Transition to Leadership:** Frubnip assumed leadership following his father, steering the family through economic ups and downs with a steady hand and a clear vision for their future.

#### Personality

*   **Disposition:** Frubnip is known for his grounded, practical nature, often focusing on the well-being of his clan over political maneuvering.
*   **Leadership Style:** He leads by example, often the first to delve into the mines or stand on the front lines against threats. His leadership is paternal, viewing his clan members as extended family rather than subordinates.

#### Motivations

*   **Economic Stability:** Driven to ensure the prosperity and security of his clan through the continued success of their secret mining operations.
*   **Protection of Heritage:** Fiercely protective of his family’s legacy and the hidden wealth they possess, striving to fend off any threats from bandits, orcs, and even political adversaries.

### Political Relationships

*   **Alliances within Tulvar:** Maintains a cautious relationship with other families, balancing cooperation with competition over resources.
*   **Stance on Regional Politics:** Skeptical of broader political promises and interventions, especially from the Viscounty of Verbobonc, given recent neglect and threats to local security.

##### Challenges and Threats

*   **Raiding Goblins and Orcs:** With the depletion of the [Mounted Borderers](mounted-borderers-organization.md), Frubnip has had to enhance his clan’s defenses against increasing raids by goblins and Blackthorn Orcs.
*   **Economic Pressures:** The isolation imposed by the closed borders of [Celene](celene-organization.md) and diminishing trade due to bandit attacks has put additional pressure on the economic stability of House Tillien.

##### Role in the Community

*   **Elder and Protector:** Beyond his duties as a miner, Frubnip’s role as a protector has become increasingly significant, organizing patrols and defenses for Tulvar.
*   **Mentor and Guide:** Offers guidance and support to younger members of his clan and community, ensuring the transmission of skills and traditions essential for their survival and prosperity.

Frubnip Tillien’s life is a testament to the resilience and ingenuity required to thrive in the challenging landscapes of Tulvar. His leadership not only sustains his clan economically but also fortifies them against the myriad threats that encroach upon their secretive and precarious existence.

### Daily Routine of Frubnip Tillien

##### Morning Rituals

*   **Early Riser:** Frubnip starts his day before dawn, a habit ingrained from years of needing to maximize the daylight hours in the mines and on the fields.
*   **Breakfast with Family:** He enjoys a hearty breakfast with his family, discussing the day's plans and any concerns from the community.
*   **Inspection Rounds:** After breakfast, Frubnip dons his mining gear and makes his first round of the day through the mines, ensuring that everything is in order and safety measures are being followed.

##### Midday Activities

*   **Supervising Mining Operations:** Much of his morning is spent deep within the mines, overseeing the extraction of silver and gems, and occasionally working alongside his miners to maintain morale and camaraderie.
*   **Community Engagements:** Post mining, Frubnip often holds brief meetings with other clan leaders or visits the market to keep abreast of the economic pulse of Tulvar.
*   **Training and Mentoring:** He dedicates part of his afternoon to training younger clan members in both combat and mining skills, ensuring the next generation is well-prepared to continue the clan’s legacy.

##### Evening Responsibilities

*   **Dinner and Family Time:** Evenings are reserved for family, where Frubnip enjoys a quiet dinner with his loved ones, sharing stories and lessons from his extensive experience.
*   **Security Checks:** With the ongoing threats of raids, he personally checks the security of their homestead and the surrounding areas, often coordinating with local scouts and rangers.
*   **Strategic Planning:** Before retiring, Frubnip reviews the day’s progress and plans future endeavors or defenses, often poring over maps and reports to strategize against potential threats.

#### Personal Time

*   **Leisure and Reflection:** Despite his responsibilities, Frubnip finds time for personal reflection, often walking through the areas surrounding his home, connecting with the land that has given so much to his family.
*   **Hobbies:** He has a keen interest in gemstone carving, a hobby that not only relaxes him but also allows him to create intricate pieces that are often used as diplomatic gifts or family heirlooms.

#### Community Interaction

*   **Evening Councils:** Occasionally, Frubnip attends or hosts evening councils with other community leaders to discuss urgent issues affecting Tulvar, from trade to security.
*   **Guidance and Support:** Being a revered elder, he is approached by many for advice on various matters, from personal disputes to business decisions.

##### Mysil Tillien - Wife of Frubnip

Mysil is a compassionate gnome with a keen intellect and a warm smile that makes her a beloved figure in the community.

*   **Role:** Manages the household and assists in the administrative aspects of the mining business.
*   **Motivation:** She is committed to supporting her husband’s endeavors and ensuring the well-being of their children and the prosperity of their home.

##### Eldrin and Belba Tillien - Elder Children

Eldrin, their son, is robust and earnest, taking after his father in strength and character. Belba, their daughter, is clever and dexterous, showing an early aptitude for gem crafting.

*   **Role:** Both are involved in the family’s mining operations, with Eldrin focusing on the physical labor and Belba learning the finer aspects of gem cutting.
*   **Motivation:** They are eager to prove their worth and continue the family tradition, each finding their niche within the broader scope of the family business.

##### Lilin Tillien - Youngest Child

Lilin is a spirited and curious child, often found exploring the edges of the mine or playing among the gemstones.

*   **Role:** Currently under the tutelage of her mother and sister, learning the business and the arts associated with their trade.
*   **Motivation:** Lilin is motivated by a youthful curiosity and the desire to emulate her older siblings and parents.
